CHUDLEIGH

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHUDLEIGH (Lady Mary), was born in 1656, and married to Sir George Chudleigh, bart. by whom she had several children: her poems and essays have been much admired for delicacy of style. She died in 1710; and is said to have written several dramatic pieces, which, though not printed, are preserved in the family.
