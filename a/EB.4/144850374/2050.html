CLINCHING

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLINCHING, in the sea-language, a kind of slight caulking used at sea, in a prospect of foul weather, about the posts: it consists in driving a little oakum into their seams, to prevent the water coming in at them.
