CLARE

article · 315 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLARE, a market-town of Suffolk, 13 miles south of Bury. E. Long. 35'. N. Lat. 52. 15. It gives the title of Earl to the duke of Newcastle.

CLARE is also the capital of a county of the same name in the province of Connaught, in Ireland, situated about 17 miles north-west of Limerick. W. Long. 9. 0. N. Lat. 52. 40.
