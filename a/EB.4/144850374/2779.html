CONTRARY

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTRARY, a positive opposite, which, substituting by turns in the same subject with its opposite, is as remote from it as possible, expels it, and is mutually expelled by it. Blackness and whiteness, cold and heat, are such contraries.
