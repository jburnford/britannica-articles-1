CALLISTHENES the philosopher

sub_entry · 315 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLISTHENES the philosopher, disciple and relation of Aristotle, by whose desire he accompanied Alexander the Great in his expeditions; but proving too severe a censurer of that hero's conduct, he was put by him to the torture (on suspicion of a treasonable conspiracy), and died under it, 328 years before Christ.
