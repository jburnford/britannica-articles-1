CROSLET

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: HERALD-DAY

CROSLET, in heraldry, is when a cross is crossed again at a small distance from each of the ends. Upton says it is not so often borne by itself in arms, as other crosses are, but often in diminutives, that is, in small croplets scattered about the field. See HERALD-
DAY, no 26. exempl. 10.
