COMMODITY

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMODITY, in a general sense, denotes all sorts of wares and merchandizes whatsoever, that a person deals or trades in.

Staple Commodities, such wares and merchandizes as are commonly and readily sold in a market, or exported abroad; being, for the most part, the proper produce, or manufacture of the country.
