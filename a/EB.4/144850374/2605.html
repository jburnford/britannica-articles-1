CONDORMIENTES

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONDORMIENTES, in church-history, religious sectaries, who take their name from lying all together, men and women, young and old. They arose in the 13th century, near Cologne; where they are said to have worshipped an image of Lucifer, and to have received answers and oracles from him.
