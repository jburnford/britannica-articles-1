CALDARIUM

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALDARIUM, in the ancient baths, a certain vault or room made so as to collect the vapours, and produce sweating; whence it signifies a hot-house, bagnio, stove, or sweating-room.
