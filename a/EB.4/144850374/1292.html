CHALCIDIUS

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHALCIDIUS, a famous platonic philosopher, in the third century, who wrote a commentary, which is esteemed, on the Timæus of Plato. This work has been translated from the Greek into Latin.
