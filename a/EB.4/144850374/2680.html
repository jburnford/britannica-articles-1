CONNECTION, or Continuity

sub_entry · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONNECTION, or Continuity, in the drama, consists in the joining of the several scenes together.

The connection is said to be observed, when the scenes of an act succeed one another immediately, and are so joined as that the stage is never left empty.
