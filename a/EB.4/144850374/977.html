CAT-Harpings

sub_entry · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAT-Harpings, a purchase of ropes employed to brace in the shrouds of the lower masts behind their

yards for the double purpose of making the shrouds more tight, and of affording room to draw in the yards more obliquely, to trim the sails for a side-wind, when they are laid to be close hauled.
