COMMELINA

article · 327 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMELINA, in botany, a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the triandria class of plants. There are 10 species, all of them natives of warm climates. They are herbaceous plants, rising from two to four feet high, and adorned with blue or yellow flowers. Their culture differs in nothing from that of the common exotics.
