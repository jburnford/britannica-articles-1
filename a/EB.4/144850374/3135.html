COUPAR, the name of two towns of Scotland, the one situated about 12 miles north-east of Perth, in the shire of Angus, W. Long. 3. 0. N. Lat. 56. 30.; and the other in the county of Fife, about 10 miles west of St Andrews: W. Long. 2. 40. N. Lat. 56. 20.
