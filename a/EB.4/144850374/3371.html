CTESIPHON

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CTESIPHON, a celebrated Greek architect, who gave the designs for the famous temple of Ephesus, and invented a machine for bringing thither the columns to be used in that noble structure. He flourished 544 B. C.
