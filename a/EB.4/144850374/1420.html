CHARITY

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARITY, among divines, one of the three great theological virtues, consisting in the love of God and of our neighbour, or the habit and disposition of loving God with all our heart, and our neighbour as ourselves.
