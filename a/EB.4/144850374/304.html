CALYCIFLORÆ

Cross-references: BOTANY

CALYCIFLORÆ, in botany, the 16th order in LINNÆUS's Fragmenta methodi naturalis, consisting of plants which, as the title imports, have the stamina (the flower) inserted into the calyx. This order contains the following genera, viz. cleagnus, hippophae, osyris, and trophis. See BOTANY, sect. vi. 16.
