COUNTERFEITS

article · 285 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTERFEITS, in law, are persons that obtain any money or goods by counterfeit letters or false tokens, who being convicted before justices of assize or of the peace, &c. are to suffer such punishment as shall be thought fit to be inflicted under death, as imprisonments, pillory, &c.
