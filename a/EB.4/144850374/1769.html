CICINDELA

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CICINDELA, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera. The antennæ are falcateous; the jaws prominent, and furnished with teeth; the eyes are a little prominent; and the breast is roundish and margined. There are 14 species.
