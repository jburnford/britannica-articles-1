COEFFICIENTS

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ALGEBRA

COEFFICIENTS, in algebra, are such numbers, or known quantities, as are put before letters or quantities, whether known or unknown, and into which they are supposed to be multiplied. Thus, in 3x, ax, or bx; 3, a and b, are the coefficients of x; and in 6a, 9b; 6, and 9, are the coefficients of a and b. See ALGEBRA.
