CAMPISTRON

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMPISTRON, a celebrated French dramatic author, was born in 1656. Racine directed his poetical talents to the theatre, and assisted him in his first pieces. He died in 1723.
