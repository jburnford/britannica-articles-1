CAPTION

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPTION, in Scots law, a writ issuing under his majesty's signet, in his majesty's name, obtained at the instance of a creditor in a civil debt, commanding messengers at arms and other officers of the law to apprehend and imprison the person of the debtor until he pay the debt.
