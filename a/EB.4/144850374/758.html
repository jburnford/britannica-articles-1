CARNERO

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARNERO, in geography, a name given to that part of the gulph of Venice, which extends from the western coast of Istria to the island of Grossa and the coast of Morlachia.

CARNERO is likewise the name of the cape to the west of the mouth of the bay of Gibraltar.
