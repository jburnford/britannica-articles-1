CHANDELIER

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHANDELIER, in fortification, a kind of moveable parapet, consisting of a wooden frame, made of two upright stakes, about six feet high, with cross planks between them; serving to support fascines to cover the pioneers.
