Cross-references: MORAL

CONSCIENCE, a secret testimony of the soul, whereby it gives its approbation to things that are naturally good, and condemns those that are evil. See MORAL Philosophy, No 85, 86.

COURTS of CONSCIENCE, are courts for recovery of small debts, constituted by act of parliament in London, Westminster, &c. and other populous and trading districts.
