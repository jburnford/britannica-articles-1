COCYTUS

COCYTUS, one of the rivers of hell, according to the theology of the poets. It has its name απο τω κωκτώ, from groaning and lamenting. Hence Milton,

Cocytus nam'd of lamentation loud,
Heard on the woful stream.

It was a branch of the river Styx; and flowed, according to Horace, with a dull and languid stream.
