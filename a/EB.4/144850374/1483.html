CHATEAU-Roux

sub_entry · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHATEAU-Roux, a town of France, in Berry, with the title of a duchy. It has a cloth-manufacture, and is seated in a very large pleasant plain on the river Indre, in E. Long. 1. 47. N. Lat. 46. 49.
