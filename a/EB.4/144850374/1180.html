CENTONARI

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CENTONARI, in antiquity, certain of the Roman army, who provided different sorts of stuff called centones, made use of to quench the fire which the enemies engines threw into the camp.

These centonarii kept with the carpenters and other officers of artillery.
