CHOREPISCOPUS

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHOREPISCOPUS is also the name of a dignity in some cathedrals in Germany, signifying the same with chori-episcopus, or bishop of the choir. The first chanter in the church of Cologne is called chori-episcopus.
