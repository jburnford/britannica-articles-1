COLOCZA

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLOCZA, a town of Hungary, seated on the Danube, and capital of the county of Bath, with an archbishop's see. It was taken by the Turks in 1686, but afterwards retaken by the Imperialists. E. Long. 19. 42. N. Long. 46. 33.
