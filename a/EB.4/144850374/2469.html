COMNENA

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMNENA (Ann) daughter of Alexis Comnenus emperor of the East; memorable for her great learning and virtue, and for her history of the life and actions of her father, which is highly esteemed. She flourished about the year 1117.
