CATARO

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATARO, a town of Dalmatia, and capital of the territory of the same name, with a strong castle, and a bishop's see. It is subject to Venice, and seated on a gulph of the same name. E. Long. 19. 19. N. Lat. 42. 25.
