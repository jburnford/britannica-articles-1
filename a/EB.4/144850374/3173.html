CRAB-TAWS

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRAB-TAWS, a name in Jamaica for a kind of ulcer on the soles of the feet, with hard callous lips, so hard that it is difficult to cut them. The ungt. cœrol. fort. is their cure.
