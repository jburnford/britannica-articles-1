CRESCENT

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRESCENT, a term among farriers. Thus a horse is said to have crescents when that part of the coffinbone, which is most advanced, falls down and presses the sole outwards, and the middle of the hoof above shrinks, and becomes flat, by reason of the hollowness beneath it.
