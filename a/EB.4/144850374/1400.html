CHARACTERISTIC Triangle of a Curve

sub_entry · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CURVE

CHARACTERISTIC Triangle of a Curve, in the higher geometry, is a rectilinear right-angled triangle, whose hypothenuse makes a part of the curve, not sensibly different from a right line. It is so called, because curve lines are used to be distinguished hereby. See CURVE.
