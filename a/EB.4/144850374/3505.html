CYBELE

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYBELE, in Pagan Mythology, the daughter of Cœlus and Terra, and the wife of her brother Saturn, was also called the mother of the gods, Ops, Rhea, Vesta, Dyndimna, Berecynthia, the good goddess, &c. un- which different characters she had different representations, and different sacrifices.
