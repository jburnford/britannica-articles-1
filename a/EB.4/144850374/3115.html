COUNTER-PALED

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-PALED, in heraldry, is when the escutcheon is divided into twelve pales parted per se, the two colours being counter-changed; so that the upper are of one colour, and the lower of another.
