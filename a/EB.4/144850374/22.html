CABIDOS

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CABIDOS, or CAVIDOS, a long measure used at Goa, and other places of the East Indies belonging to the Portuguese, to measure stuffs, linens, &c. and equal to 4 of the Paris ell.
