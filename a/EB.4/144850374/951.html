CASTLETOWN

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTLETOWN, the capital of the isle of Man, seated on the south-west part of the island. It has a strong castle; but of no great importance, on account of its distance from the rocky and shallow harbour. W. Long. 4. 39. N. Lat. 53. 30.
