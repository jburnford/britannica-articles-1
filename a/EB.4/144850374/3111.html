COUNTER-GUARD

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-GUARD, in fortification, is a work raised before the point of a bastion, consisting of two long faces parallel to the faces of the bastion, making a falliant angle: they are sometimes of other shapes, or otherwise situated.
