CALEPIN

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALEPIN (Ambrosius), an Augustin monk of Calepio, whence he took his name, in the 16th century. He is author of a dictionary of eight languages, since augmented by Passerat and others.
