COMETEAN

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMETEAN, a town of Bohemia in the circle of Saltz, with a handsome town house. It was taken by storm in 1421, and all the inhabitants, men, women, and children, put to the sword. It is seated in a fertile plain, in E. Long. 13. 25. N. Lat. 50. 30.
