COPERNICUS

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COPERNICUS, the name of an astronomical instrument, invented by Mr Whiston, to exhibit the motion and phenomena of the planets, both primary and secondary. It is built upon the Copernican system, and for that reason called by his name.
