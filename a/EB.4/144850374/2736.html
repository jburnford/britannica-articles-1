CONSUMPTION

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONSUMPTION, in medicine, a word of very extensive signification, implies all disorders that bring any decay or waste upon the constitution; but is most commonly used for the phthisis pulmonalis. See (the Index subjoined to) MEDICINE.
