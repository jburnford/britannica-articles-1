CONTRAVALLATION

CONTRAVALLATION, or the Line CONTRAVALLATION, in fortification, a trench guarded with a parapet, and usually cut round about a place by the besiegers, to secure themselves on that side, and to stop the sallies of the garrison. See FORTIFICATION.
