CEMENT

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CEMENT, among engravers, jewellers, &c. is the same with the hot cement used in building*; and is used for keeping the metals to be engraved firm to the block, and also for filling up what is to be chiseled.
