CLEATS

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLEATS, in naval affairs, pieces of wood, having one or two projecting ends whereby to fasten the

ropes: some of them are fastened to the shrouds below for this purpose, and others nailed to different places of the ship's deck or sides.
