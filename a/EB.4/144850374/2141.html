COCKSWAIN

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COCKSWAIN, or COXEN; an officer on board a man of war, who has the care of the barge, and all things belonging to it, and must be also ready with his crew to man the boat on all occasions: he sits at the stern of the boat, and steers.
