CAPRARIA

article · 306 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPRARIA, in botany, a genus of the angiosperma order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants. There is but one species, the biflora, which is a native of the warm parts of America. Being a troublesome weed, and without beauty, it is never cultivated, except in botanic gardens for the sake of variety.
