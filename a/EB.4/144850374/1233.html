CERINTHE, HONEYWORT; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. There are three species, natives of Germany, Italy, and the Alps. They are low annual plants with purple, yellow and red flowers, which may be propagated by seed sown in autumn, in a warm situation.
