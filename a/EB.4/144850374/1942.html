CLARO-OBSCURO, or CLAIR-OBSCURE

sub_entry · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLARO-OBSCURO, or CLAIR-OBSCURE, in painting, the art of distributing to advantage the lights and shadows of a piece, both with respect to the easing of the eye, and the effect of the whole piece.
