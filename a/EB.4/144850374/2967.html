CORONARY Arteries, are two arteries springing out of

Coronary of the aorta, before it leaves the pericardium. See ANATOMY, no 387, b. and 386, d.

Coroner. Coronary Vein, a vein diffused over the exterior surface of the heart. Ibid. no 386, d.

Stomachic Coronary, a vein inserted into the trunk of the splenic vein, which, by uniting with the mesenteric, forms the vena porta. See ANATOMY, no 387, k.
