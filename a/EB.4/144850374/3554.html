CYTHEREA

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYTHEREA, a name of Venus, so called from Cytherea an island of Greece, where poets say she was formed of the froth of the sea. She had a sumptuous temple there consecrated to her under the name of Venus Urania.
