COBLENTZ

article · 321 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COBLENTZ, an ancient, handsome, and strong town of Germany, in the electorate of Triers or Treves, seated at the confluence of the rivers Rhine and Moselle, in a fertile country, with mountains covered with vineyards. It is the usual residence of the elector of Treves, to whom it belongs. E. Long. 7. 18. N. Lat. 50. 24.
