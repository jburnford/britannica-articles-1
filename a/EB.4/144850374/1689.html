CHOROGRAPHY, the art of making a map of some country or province.

Chorography differs from geography, as the description of a particular country does from that of the whole earth; and from topography, as the description of a country differs from that of a town or district. See the articles GEOGRAPHY, TOPOGRAPHY, and MAP.
