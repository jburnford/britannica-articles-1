CARABINE

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARABINE, a fire-arm shorter than a musket, carrying a ball of 24 in the pound, borne by the light horse, hanging at a belt over the left shoulder. The barrel is two feet and an half long; and is sometimes furrowed spirally within, which is said to add to the range of the piece.
