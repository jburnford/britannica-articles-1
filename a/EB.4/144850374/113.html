CÆSURE

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CÆSURE, in the modern poetry, denotes a rest, or pause, towards the middle of an Alexandrian verse, by which the voice and pronunciation are aided, and the verse, as it were, divided into two hemistichs.
