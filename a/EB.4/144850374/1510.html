CHEASAPEAK BAY

article · 338 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHEASAPEAK BAY, in North America, the entrance between Cape Henry and Cape Charles, running up 30 miles between Virginia and Maryland. It is navigable almost all the way for large ships, and has several navigable rivers that fall into it, by means of which, ships go up to the very doors of the planters, to take in their lading of goods.
