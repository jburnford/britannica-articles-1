CALOTTE

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALOTTE, in architecture, a round cavity or depression, in form of a cap or cup, lathed and plastered, used to diminish the rise or elevation of a moderate chapel, cabinet, alcove, &c. which, without such an expedient, would be too high for other pieces of the apartment.
