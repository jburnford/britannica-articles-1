COSMICAL

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COSMICAL, a term in astronomy, expressing one of the poetical risings of a star: thus a star is said to rise cosmically when it rises with the sun, or with that

point of the ecliptic in which the sun is at that time: Cosmical and the cosmical setting is when a star sets in the west at the same time that the sun rises in the east.
