COCCEIUS

article · 347 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COCCEIUS (John), professor of theology at Bremen, was founder of a sect called Cocceians: they held, amongst other singular opinions, that of a visible reign of Christ in this world, after a general conversion of the Jews and all other people to the true Christian faith, as laid down in the voluminous works of Cocceius. He died in 1699, aged 66.
