CORROSION, in a general sense, the action of gnawing

Corrosion gnawing away, by degrees, the continuity of the parts of bodies.

Corrosion, in chemistry, an action of bodies, by means of proper menstrua, that produces new combinations, and a change of their form, without converting them to fluidity.
