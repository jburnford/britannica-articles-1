CASTLE-Work

sub_entry · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTLE-Work, service or labour done by inferior tenants, for the building and upholding castles of defence, toward which some gave their personal assistance, and others paid their contributions. This was one of the three necessary charges to which the Anglo-Saxons were expressly subject.
