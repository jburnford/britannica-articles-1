CHIPPENHAM

CHIPPENHAM, a town of Wiltshire, seated on the river Avon. It is a good thorough-fare town; has a handsome stone-bridge over the river, consisting of 16 arches; and sends two members to parliament. W. Long. 2. 12. N. Lat. 51. 25.
