CONCRETE

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONCRETE, in natural philosophy and chemistry, signifies a body made up of different principles, or any mixed body; thus, soap is a factitious concrete, mixed together by art; and antimony is a natural concrete, or a mixed body compounded in the bowels of the earth.
