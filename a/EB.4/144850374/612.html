CAPO d'Istria

sub_entry · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPO d'Istria, a considerable town of Italy, in Istria, on the gulph of Trieste, with a bishop's see, and subject to the Venetians. The air is wholesome and temperate; its principal revenue consists in wine and salt. E. Long. 14. o. N. Lat. 45. 48.
