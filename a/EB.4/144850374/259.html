CALLIOPE

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLIOPE, in the Pagan mythology, the muse who presides over eloquence and heroic poetry. She was thus called from the sweetness of her voice, and was reckoned the first of the nine sisters.
