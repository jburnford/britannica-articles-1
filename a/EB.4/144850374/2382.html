COMA Berenices

COMA Berenices, Berenice's hair, in astronomy, a constellation in the northern hemisphere, composed of stars near the lion's tail. This constellation consists of three stars, according to Ptolemy; of 13, according to Tycho; and of 40, in the Britannic Catalogue.
