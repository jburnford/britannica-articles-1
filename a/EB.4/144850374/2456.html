COMMUNES

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMUNES, in botany, the name of a class in Linnaeus's methodus Calycina, consisting of plants which, like teasel and dandelion, have a calix or flower-cup common to many flowers or florets. These are the aggregate or compound flowers of other systems.
