CANT

CANT is also applied to words and phrases affected by particular persons or professions for low ends, and not authorised by the established language*. The difference between cant and technical seems to be this: the former is restrained to words introduced out of folly, affectation, or impotence; the latter is applied to such as are introduced for the sake of clearness, precision, and significancy.
