CAPILLARY Vessels. Many small vessels of animal bodies have been discovered by the modern invention of injecting the vessels of animals with a colouring fluid which upon cooling grows hard. But though most anatomists know the manner of filling the large trunks, few are acquainted with the art of filling the capillaries. Dr Monro has given what he after many trials has found most successful. See INJECTION.
