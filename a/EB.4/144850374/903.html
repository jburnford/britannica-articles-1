CASSUMUAR, in the Materia Medica, a root approaching to that of zedoary.

It is cardiac and sudorific, and famous in nervous cases: it is also an ingredient in many compositions, and is preferred in powders, boluses, and infusions. Its dose is from five to fifteen grains.

CAST is peculiarly used to denote a figure or small statue of bronze. See BRONZE.
