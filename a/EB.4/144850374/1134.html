CELLS

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CELLS, in botany, the hollow places between the partitions in the pods, husks, and other seed-vessels of plants; according as there is one, two, three, &c. of these cells, the vessel is said to be unilocular, bilocular, trilocular, &c.
