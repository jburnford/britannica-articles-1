CIOBAT, a sea-port town of Provence in France;
famous for Muscadine wine. It is seated on the bay
of Laquea, between Marfeilles and Toulon; and the
harbour is defended by a strong fort. E. Long. 5. 30.
N. Lat. 43. 10.
