CYBELICUM MARMOR

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYBELICUM MARMOR, a name given by the ancients to a species of marble dug in a mountain of that name in Phrygia. It was of an extremely bright white, with broad veins of bluish black.
