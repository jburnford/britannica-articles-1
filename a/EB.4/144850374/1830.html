CIRCLE of perpetual Occultation

sub_entry · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIRCLE of perpetual Occultation, is another circle at a like distance from the equator; and contains all those stars which never appear in our hemisphere. The stars situate between these circles, alternately rise and set at certain times.
