CONFECTION

Cross-references: PHARMACY

CONFECTION, in pharmacy, signifies, in general, any thing prepared with sugar: in particular it imports something preserved, especially dry substances. It also signifies a liquid or soft electuary, of which there are various sorts directed in dispensaries. See PHARMACY, no 883.
