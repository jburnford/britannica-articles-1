COUNTER-DRAWING

article · 423 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-DRAWING, in painting, is the copying a design, or painting, by means of a fine linen-cloth, an oiled paper, or other transparent matter, where the strokes appearing through are followed with a pencil, with or without colour. Sometimes it is done on glass, and with frames or nets divided into squares with silk or with thread, and also by means of instru-

ments invented for the purpose, as the parallelogram. Counter
