CRANIOLARIA

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRANIOLARIA, in botany, a genus of the angiosperma order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants. There are two species, both natives of hot climates, and neither of them possessed of any remarkable property.
