CHARISTIA

article · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARISTA, a festival of the ancient Romans, celebrated in the month of February, wherein the relations by blood and marriage met, in order to preserve a good correspondence; and that, if there happened to be any difference among them, it might be the more easily accommodated, by the good-humour and mirth of the entertainment. Ovid, Fasti. i. 617.
