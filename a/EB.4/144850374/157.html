CALAIS and Zetes

sub_entry · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALAIS and Zetes, in fabulous history, sons of Boeas and Orithia, to whom the poets attributed wings: they went on the voyage of Colchis with the Argonauts, delivered Phineus from the harpies, and were slain by Hercules.
