CASTIGLIONE

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTIGLIONE, a small, but strong town of Italy, in Mantua, with a castle. It was taken by the Germans in 1701, and the French defeated the Imperialists near it in 1706. E. Long. 10. 29. N. Lat. 43. 23.
