CARRICK-Fergus

sub_entry · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARRICK-Fergus, a town of Ireland, in the county of Antrim and province of Ulster; it is a borough and market-town, very rich and populous, with a good harbour and castle, and sends two members to parliament. It is seated on the Irish channel, on a bay of the same name.
