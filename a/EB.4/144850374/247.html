CALLÉ

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLÉ, (anc. geog.), a town of Hither Spain, situated on an eminence which hangs over the river Durius; whose port was at the mouth of the river. Now Porto, Oporto, or Port a Port. See Oporto.
