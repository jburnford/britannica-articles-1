COUPED

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUPED, in heraldry, is used to express the head, or any limb, of an animal, cut off from the trunk, smooth; distinguishing it from that which is called erased, that is, forcibly torn off, and therefore is ragged and uneven.
