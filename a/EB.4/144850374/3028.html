CORYMBUS

article · 328 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: BOTANY

CORYMBUS, properly signifies a cluster of ivy berries. Among botanists, it is a mode of flowering in which the lesser or partial flower-stalks are produced along the common stalk on both sides; and, though of unequal lengths, rise to the same height, so as to form a flat and even surface at the top. See BOTANY, p. 1294. no 32.
