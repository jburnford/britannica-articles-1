CASK

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASK, a vessel of capacity, for preserving liquors of divers kinds; and sometimes also dry goods, as sugar, almonds, &c.—A cask of sugar is a barrel of that commodity, containing from eight to eleven hundred weight.
