CLEOSTRATUS

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLEOSTRATUS, a celebrated astronomer born at Tenedos, was, according to Pliny, the first who discovered the signs of the Zodiac; others say, that he only discovered the signs Aries and Sagittarius. He also corrected the errors of the Grecian year about the 306th before Christ.
