CARUNCULÆ Myrtiformes

Cross-references: ANATOMY

CARUNCULÆ Myrtiformes, in anatomy, fleshy knobs about the size of a myrtle-berry, which owe their origin to the breaking of the hymen. See ANATOMY, no 372, d.

CARUNCLES in the urethra, proceeding from a gonorrhœa, or an ulceration of the urethra, may be reduced by introducing the ROUGIE.
