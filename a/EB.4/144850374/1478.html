CHATEAU-DAUPHIN

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHATEAU-DAUPHIN, a very strong castle of Piedmont in Italy, and in the marquisate of Saluces, belonging to the king of Sardinia. It was taken by the combined army of France and Spain in 1744, and was restored by the treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle.
