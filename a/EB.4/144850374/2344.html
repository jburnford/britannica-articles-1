COLUMBANUS

COLUMBANUS (St), a Latin poet, was born in Ireland in 560. He retired from the world, and lived a solitary life in the mountains of Wales for some years. He next went over to France and built a monastery; but, being exiled from that country, he travelled to Italy, where he founded the abbey of Bobio, and died in it in 615.
