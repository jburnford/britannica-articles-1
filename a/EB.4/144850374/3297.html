CRONSLOT, a town and fortress of Russia, in a little island of the same name, situated in the mouth of the river Neva, near the entrance of the gulph of Finland. It has a good harbour, which is the station of the Russian fleet, and where the great magazines of naval stores as well as docks and yards for building ships are. E. Long. 32. 0. N. Lat. 60.
