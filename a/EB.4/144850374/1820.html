CIRCE

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIRCE, in fabulous history, a famous sorceress, poisoned her husband, king of the Sarmatae; for which being banished, she came into Italy, where she changed Scylla into a sea-monster; she entertained Ulysses, who was cast away on the coast near her house, but metamorphosed his companions into different sorts of beasts.
