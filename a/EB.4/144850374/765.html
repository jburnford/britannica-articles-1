CAROLUS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAROLUS, an ancient English broad piece of gold, struck under Charles I. Its value has of late been at 23 shillings Sterling, though at the time it was coined it is said to have been rated at 20 Shillings.
