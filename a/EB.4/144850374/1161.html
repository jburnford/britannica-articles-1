CENSURE

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CENSURE, a judgment which condemns some book, person, or action, or, more particularly, a reprimand from a superior. Ecclesiastical censures, are penalties by which, for some remarkable misbehaviour, Christians are deprived of the communion of the church, or prohibited to execute the sacerdotal office.
