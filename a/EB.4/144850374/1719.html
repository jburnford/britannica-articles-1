CHRONOGRAM

CHRONOGRAM, a species of false wit, consisting in this, that a certain date or epocha is expressed by numeral letters of one or more verses; such is that which makes the motto of a medal struck by Gustavus Adolphus in 1622:

ChrIstVs DVX; ergo trIVmphiVs.
