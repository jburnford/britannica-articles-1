CADUSII

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CADUSII (anc. geog.), a people of Media Atropatene, situated to the west in the mountains, and reaching to the Cospian sea; between whom and the Medes, perpetual war and enmity continued down to the time of Cyrus.
