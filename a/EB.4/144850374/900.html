CASSOCK

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASSOCK, or CASSULA, a kind of robe or gown, wore over the rest of the habit, particularly by the clergy. The word cassock comes from the French casaque, an horseman's coat.
