CHALLONS-sur-Marne

sub_entry · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHALLONS-sur-Marne, a large episcopal town of France, in Champagne. It carries on a considerable trade in shalloons, and other woollen stuffs. It is seated between two fine meadows on the rivers Marne, Mau, and Nau, in E. Long. 4. 37. N. Lat. 48. 57.
