COMORRA, a handsome and large town of lower Hungary, and capital of a territory of the same name.

Comose name. It is so well fortified, that the Turks could never take it. The greatest part of the inhabitants are Hungarians or Russians, who are very rich, and are of the Greek religion. It is seated on the river Danube, in the island of Sihut, E. Lon. 18. 25. N. Lat. 47. 50.
