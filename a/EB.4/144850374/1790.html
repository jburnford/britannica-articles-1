CINALOA, a province of Mexico in South Amer-
ica, abounding in corn, cattle, and cotton; and ren-
dered extremely picturesque, by a number of beauti-
ful cascades of clear water that fall down from the
mountains. It lies on the eastern coast of the sea of
California, and has a town of the same name, situated
in N. Lat. 26°.
