CASTREL

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTREL, a kind of hawk resembling the lanner in shape, but the hobby in size. The castrel is also called kestrel, and is of a slow and cowardly kind; her game is the grouse, though she will kill a partridge.
