CORFE-CASTLE

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORFE-CASTLE, a borough-town in Dorsetshire in England. It takes its name from a strong castle, belonging to the crown, that stood there, but is now ruined. W. Long. 2. 8. N. Lat. 50. 33.
