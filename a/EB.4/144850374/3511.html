CYCLOPÆDIA

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYCLOPÆDIA, or ENCYCLOPÆDIA, denotes the Cyclopædia circle or compass of arts and sciences. A cyclopædia, say the authors of the French Encyclopædia, ought to explain as much as possible the order and connection of human knowledge.
