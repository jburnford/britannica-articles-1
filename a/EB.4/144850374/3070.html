COTULA

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COTULA, or Cotyla, in antiquity, a liquid measure among the Greeks, equal to the hemina of the Romans, containing half a sextary, or four acetabula: hence it appears that it contained 10 ounces of wine, and 9 of oil.
