CONTEMPT

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTEMPT, in law, is a disobedience to the rules and orders of a court, which hath power to punish such offence; and as this is sometimes a greater, and sometimes a lesser offence, so it is punished with greater or less punishment, by fine, and sometimes by imprisonment.
