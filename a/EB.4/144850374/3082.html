COVERT-WAY

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FORTIFICATION

COVERT-WAY, or CORRIDOR, in fortification, a space of ground, level with the field on the edge of the ditch, three or four fathoms broad, ranging quite round the half moons and other works toward the country. It has a parapet raised on a level, together with its banquets and glacis. See FORTIFICATION.
