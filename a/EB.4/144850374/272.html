CALM, the state of rest which appears in the air and sea when there is no wind stirring. That tract of the Atlantic ocean situated between the tropic of Cancer and the latitude of 29° north, or the space that lies between the trade and the variable winds, is frequently subject to calms of very long duration; and hence it has acquired amongst seamen the name of the calm latitudes.
