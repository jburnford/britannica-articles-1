CHALLON-SUR-SAONE, an ancient town of France, in Burgundy, and capital of the Chalonnois,

* Vid. Blackstone's Comm. III. 362.

with a citadel and bishop's fee. It is seated on the river Saone, in E. Long. 5. 7. N. Lat. 46. 47.
