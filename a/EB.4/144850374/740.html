CARLOCK

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARLOCK, in commerce, a sort of isinglass, made with the surgeon's bladder, imported from Archangel. The chief use of it is for clarifying wine, but it is also used by the dyers. The best carlock comes from Astracan, where a great quantity of surgeon is caught.
