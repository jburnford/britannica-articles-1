COUPLE-CLOSS

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUPLE-CLOSS, in heraldry, the fourth part of a chevron, never borne but in pairs, except there be a chevron between them, faith Guillim, though Bloom gives an instance to the contrary.
