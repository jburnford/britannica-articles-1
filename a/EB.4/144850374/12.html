CABALLIO, or CABELLIO, (anc. geog.), a town of the Cavares in Gallia Narbonensis, situated on the Druentia. One of the Latin colonies, in the Notitiæ called Civitas Cabellicorum. Now Cavaillon in Provence. See CAVAILLON.
