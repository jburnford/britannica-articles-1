CARDIAC

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARDIAC, in a general sense, signifies all medicines beneficial to the heart, whether internally or externally applied. The word comes from the Greek word καρδια, cor; the heart being reputed the immediate seat of their operation.
