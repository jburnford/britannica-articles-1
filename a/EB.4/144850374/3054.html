COSTUS

COSTUS, a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the monandria class of plants. There is but one species, viz. the arabicus, a native of the Indies. The root was formerly in some esteem as an attenuant, and serviceable in venereal complaints; but is now rarely prescribed or met with in the shops.
