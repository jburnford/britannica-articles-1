CASSIDA

CASSIDA, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera. The feeders are like threads, but thicker on the outside; the elytra are marginated; and the head is hid under the thorax. There are 31 species of this insect, distinguished principally by differences in their colour.
