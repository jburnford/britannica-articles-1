CANCHERIZANTE

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANCHERIZANTE, or CANCHERIZATO, in the Italian music, a term signifying a piece of music that begins at the end, being the retrograde motion from the end of a song, &c. to the beginning.
