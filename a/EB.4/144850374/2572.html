CONCIATOR

article · 427 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONCIATOR, in the glass art, is, for the crystal-glass, what the founders are at the green-glass houses. He is the person that weighs and proportions the salt on ashes and sand, and works them with a strong fire till they run into lumps and become white; and if the metal be too hard, and consequently brittle, he adds salt or ashes, and if too soft, sand; still mixing them to a fit temper, which is only known by the working.
