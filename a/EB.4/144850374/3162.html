COWL

COWL, or COUL, a habit worn by the Bernardines and Benedictines, of which there are two kinds; one white, very large, worn in ceremonies; the other black, worn on ordinary occasions, in the streets, &c. Friar's Cowl, in botany. See ARISARUM.
