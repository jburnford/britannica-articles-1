CONFIRMATION

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONFIRMATION, in law, a conveyance of an estate, or right in esse, from one man to another, whereby avoidable estate is made sure and unavoidable, or a particular estate is increased, or a possession made perfect.
