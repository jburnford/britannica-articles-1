IMMER

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMMER, the most easterly island of all the New Hebrides in the South Sea. It lies about four leagues from Tanna, and seems to be about five leagues in circumference; it is of a considerable height, with a flat-top.
