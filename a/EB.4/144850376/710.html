GUERICKE

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUERICKE, or GUERICHE, (Otho), the most celebrated mathematician of his time, was born in 1602. He was the inventor of the air-pump; and author of several works in natural philosophy, the chief of which is Experimenta Magdeburgica. He died in 1686.
