HALLAND

HALLAND, a country of Sweden, in the island of Schonen, lying along the sea-coast, at the entrance of the Baltic Sea, and opposite to Jutland. It is 60 miles along the coast, but is not above 12 in breadth. Halmstad is the capital town.
