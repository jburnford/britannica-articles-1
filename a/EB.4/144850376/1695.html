HYPHEN

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPHEN, an accent or character in grammar, implying that two words are to be joined, or connected into one compound word, and marked thus - ; as pre-established, free-leaved, &c. Hyphens also serve to connect the syllables of such words as are divided by the end of the line.
