HOOP

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOOP, a piece of pliant wood, or iron, bent into a circular form, commonly used for securing casks, &c.

Driving a HOOP, a boyish exercise, of good effect in rendering the limbs pliable, and for strengthening the nerves.
