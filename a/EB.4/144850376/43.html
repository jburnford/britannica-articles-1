GALLS

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLS are very strong astringents, and give out their astringent virtue very readily both to water and spirit. From two ounces of galls, 14 drachms of astringent extract were obtained by water, and between 12 and 13 with spirit. In medicine they are rarely used.
