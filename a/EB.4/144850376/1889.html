IGIS, a town of the country of the Grisons, in Caddea, with a magnificent castle, in which is a cabinet of curiosities, and a handsome library; 23 miles

south-west of Chora, and 23 south of Glaris. E. lon. 9. o. N. lat. 49. 10.
