HALT

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALT, in war, a pause or stop in the march of a military body.—Some derive the word from the Latin halitus, breath; it being a frequent occasion of halting to take breath; others from alto, because in halting they raised their pikes on end, &c.
