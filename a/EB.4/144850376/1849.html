JEDBURGH

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JEDBURGH, a town of Scotland, capital of Tiviotdale or Roxburghshire. It is well-built and populous, with a handsome church and town-hall, and a good market for corn and cattle. It gives the title of lord to the marquis of Lothian's eldest son, and is the seat of the sheriff's court and presbytery.
