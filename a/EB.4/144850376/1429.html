HOLYOAK

article · 323 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOLYOAK (Francis), author of the Latin dictionary, became rector of South-ham in Warwickshire in 1604; and being greatly esteemed, was chosen member of the convocation in the first year of Charles I.'s reign. He suffered much for the king; and died in 1653, aged 87. His son Thomas made enlargements to the said Dictionary.
