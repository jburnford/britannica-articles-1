JEBUSÆI

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JEBUSÆI, one of the seven ancient people of Canaan, descendants of Jebusi, Canaan's son; so warlike and brave, as to have flooded their ground, especially in Jebus, afterwards called Jerusalem, down to the time of David. Judges i. 21. 1 Sam. v. 6.
