JAMES IV

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JAMES IV. king of Scotland, succeeded his father in 1488. He was a pious and valiant prince; subdued his rebellious subjects; and afterwards, taking part with Louis XII. against Henry VIII. of England, he was slain in the battle of Flouden-Field in 1513, aged 41.
