HORNSEY, a town in Yorkshire, in England. It is almost surrounded by a small arm of the sea; and the church having a high steeple, is a noted sea-mark. Not many years ago there was a street here called Hornsey beck, which was washed away by the sea, except a house or two. E. Long. 0. 6. N. Lat. 54. 0.
