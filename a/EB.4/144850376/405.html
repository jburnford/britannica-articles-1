GOLDEN Rule

GOLDEN Rule, in arithmetic, a rule or praxis, of great use and extent in the art of numbers; whereby we find a fourth proportional to three quantities given.

The golden rule is also called the Rule of Three, and Rule of Proportion. See its nature and use under the article ARITHMETIC, n° 13.
