ISSOUDUN

ISSOUDUN, a considerable town of France, in Berry. It carries on a great trade in wood, cattle, cloth, hats, and stockings; is seated partly on a plain, and partly on an eminence. E. Long. 2. 5. N. Lat. 46. 57.
