INGELSHEIM

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INGELSHEIM, a town of Germany, in the palatinate of the Rhine, remarkable for having been the residence of the emperors; seated on the river Salva, on an eminence, from whence there is a charming prospect. E. Long. 8. 5. N. Lat. 49. 58.
