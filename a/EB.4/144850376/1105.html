HELEN, (in fab. hist.) the daughter of Tyndarus and Leda, was married to Menelaus king of Sparta, but was stolen from him by Theseus, 1235 B. C. She was restored soon after; but carried off again by Paris, the Trojan prince; which occasioned the famous Trojan war. See TROY.
