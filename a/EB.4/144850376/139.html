GAUZE

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GAUZE, or GAWSE, in commerce, a very slight, thin, open kind of stuff, made of silk, sometimes of thread. There are also figured gauzes, and some with gold or silver on silk ground.
