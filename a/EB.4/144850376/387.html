GOITO

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOITO, a town of Italy, in the duchy of Mantua, taken by the Germans in 1701, and by the prince of Hesse in 1706. It is seated on the river Mincio, between the lake of Mantua and that of Garda, 10 miles north-west of Mantua. E. Lon. 11. 0. N. Lat. 45. 16.
