JAMES III

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JAMES III. king of Scotland, succeeded his father in 1460. He was a tyrannical prince; and having irritated his subjects, they rebelled against him, and he was slain by them in battle in 1488, aged 35.
