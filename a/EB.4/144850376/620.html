GROTESQUE

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GROTESQUE, or GROTESSE, in sculpture and painting, something whimsical, extravagant, and monstrous; consisting either of things that are merely imaginary, and have no existence in nature; or of things so distorted, as to raise surprise and ridicule.
