HOSCHIUS

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOSCHIUS (Sidronius), a jesuit, who was born at Marke, in the diocese of Ypres, in 1596, and died at Tongres in 1653. He wrote some elegies and other poems in Latin, with great purity and elegance.
