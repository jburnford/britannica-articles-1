JAROSLOW

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JAROSLOW, a handsome town of Poland, in the palatinate of Russia, with a strong citadel. It is remarkable for its great fair, its handsome buildings, and a battle gained by the Swedes in 1656, after which they took the town. It is seated on the river Saine, in E. Long. 22. 23. N. Lat. 49. 58.
