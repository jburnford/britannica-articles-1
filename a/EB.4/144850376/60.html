GALLIOT

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLIOT, a small galley designed only for chase, carrying but one mast and two pattereroes; it can both sail and row, and has 16 or 20 oars. All the seamen on board are soldiers, and each has a musket by him on quitting his oar.
