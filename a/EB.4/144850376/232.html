GERARD

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GERARD (John), a learned Lutheran divine, was professor of divinity, and rector of the academy of Jena, the place of his birth. He wrote, 1. The harmony of the Eastern languages; 2. A treatise on the Coptic church; and other works which are esteemed. He died in 1668.
