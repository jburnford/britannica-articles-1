GUADALUPE

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUADALUPE, a handsome town in Spain, in Estramadura, with a celebrated convent, whose structure is magnificent, and is immensely rich. It is seated on a rivulet of the same name. E. Lon. 3. 50. N. Lat. 39. 15.
