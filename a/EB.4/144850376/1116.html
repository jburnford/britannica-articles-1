HELIOCOMETES

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELIOCOMETES, a phenomenon ſometimes obſerved about ſunſetting; being a large luminous tail or column of light proceeding from the body of the ſun, and dragging after it, not unlike the tail of a comet; whence the name.
