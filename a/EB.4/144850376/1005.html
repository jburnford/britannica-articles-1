HAVERILL

HAVERILL, a town of England, in the county of Suffolk, where there is a considerable manufactory of checks, cottons, and fustians. By the ruins of a church and castle still to be seen, it appears to have been formerly a place of much greater consequence than at present. It has now only about 300 poor clay-houses, and one wide street not paved.
