INTERLUDE

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INTERLUDE, an entertainment exhibited on the theatre between the acts of a play, to amuse the spectators while the actors take breath and shift their dress, or to give time for changing the scenes and decorations.
