HIPPOBOSCA

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HIPPOBOSCA, or HORSE-FLY, in zoology, a genus of insects, belonging to the order of diptera. The beak consists of two valves, is cylindrical, obtuse, and hanging; and the feet have several claws. There are four species, distinguished by their wings, &c. The equina is very troublesome to horses.
