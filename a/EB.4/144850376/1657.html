HYDROPHOBIA

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYDROPHOBIA, an aversion or dread of water: a terrible symptom of the rabies canina; and which has likewise been found to take in violent inflammations of the stomach and in hysterical fits. See (the Index subjoined to) MEDICINE.
