ISIA, feasts and sacrifices anciently solemnized in honour of the goddess Isis.

The isia were full of abominable impurities; and for that reason, those who were initiated were obliged to take an oath of secrecy: they were held for nine days successively; but were so abominable, that the senate abolished them at Rome, under the consuls of Piso and Gabinus.
