HELOISE

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ABELARD

HELOISE, famous for her unfortunate affection for her tutor Abelard, and for her Latin letters to him after they had retired from the world. She died abbess of Paraclet in 1163. See ABELARD.
