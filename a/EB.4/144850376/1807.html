JAVELLO

JAVELLO (Chrysostome), a learned Italian Dominican of the 16th century, taught philosophy and theology at Bologna, and died about the year 1540. He wrote a work on philosophy, another on politics, and another on Christian economy, which are esteemed; with notes on Pomponatius, and other works, printed in 3 vols folio.
