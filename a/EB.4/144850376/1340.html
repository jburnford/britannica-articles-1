HIGHAM FERRERS

HIGHAM FERRERS, an ancient borough of Northamptonshire in England, which has its name from the family of the Ferrers, to whom it formerly belonged, and who had a castle in its neighbourhood. It sends one member to parliament. E. Lon. 1. 40. N. Lat. 52. 20.
