HÆMAGOGOS

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HÆMAGOGOS, among physicians, a compound medicine, consisting of feud and aromatic simples mixed with black hellebore, and prescribed in order to promote the menstrua and hæmorrhoidal fluxes; as also to bring away the lochia.
