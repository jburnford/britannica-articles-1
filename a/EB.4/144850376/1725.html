JACK

article · 337 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JACK, in the sea-language, a sort of flag or colours, displayed from a mast erected on the outer end of a ship's bowsprit. In the British navy the jack is nothing more than a small union flag, composed of the intersection of the red and white crosses; but in merchant-ships this union is bordered with a red field.
