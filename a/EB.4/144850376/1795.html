JARNAC

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JARNAC, a town of France, in Orleanois and in Angmois, remarkable for a victory gained by Henry III. over the Huguenots in 1569. It is seated on the river Charente, in W. Long. 0. 13. N. Lat. 45. 40.
