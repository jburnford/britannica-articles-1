IOLAUS

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IOLAUS, in fabulous history, the son of Iphicles, assisted Hercules in overcoming the hydra, by procuring and lighting firebrands, as fast as Hercules cut off its heads. As a reward for this piece of service, Hercules prevailed on Hebe to restore him to youth, when decrepit with age.
