IVREA

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IVREA, an ancient and strong town of Italy, in Piedmont, and capital of Canaveze, with a strong fort, a bishop's see, the title of a marquise, and an ancient castle. It is subject to the king of Sardinia, and seated on the river Doria between two hills, in E. Long. 7. 48. N. Lat. 45. 12.
