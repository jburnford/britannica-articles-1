HALIOTIS

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALIOTIS, the EAR-SHELL, a genus of insects belonging to the order of vermes testacea. This is an animal of the snail-kind, with an open shell resembling an ear. There are seven species, distinguished by the figure of their shells. See Plate CLVIII.
