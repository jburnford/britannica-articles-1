HALLE

article · 175 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALLE, a free and imperial town of Germany, in Suabia, famous for its salt-pits. It is seated on the river Kocher, among rocks and mountains, in E. Lon. 10. 50. N. Lat. 49. 6.
