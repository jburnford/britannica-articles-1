GOTTENBURG

GOTTENBURG, a rich and strong town of West Gothland, in Sweden, with a good harbour, at the mouth of the river Gothelba; which is the best situated for foreign trade of any in Sweden, as it lies without the sound. E. Lon. 11. 50. N. Lat. 57. 44.
