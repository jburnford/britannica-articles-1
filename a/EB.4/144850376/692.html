GUARDIA

GUARDIA, or GUARDA, a town of Portugal, in the province of Beira, with a bishop's see. It contains about 2300 inhabitants, is fortified both by art and nature, and has a stately cathedral. W. Lon. 5. 17. N. Lat. 40. 20.
