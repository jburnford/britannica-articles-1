GOOSE-Neck

sub_entry · 161 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOOSE-Neck, in a ship, a piece of iron fixed on the one end of the tiller, to which the laniard of the whip-staff or the wheel-robe comes, for steering the ship.
