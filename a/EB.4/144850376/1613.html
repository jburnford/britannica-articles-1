HURST-Castle

sub_entry · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HURST-Castle, a fortress of Hampshire in England, not far from Lymington. It is seated on the extreme point of a neck of land which shoots into the sea, towards the isle of Wight, from which it is distant two miles.
