Tempi di GAVOTTA

sub_entry · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Tempi di GAVOTTA, is when only the time or movement of a gavotte is imitated, without any regard to the measure, or number of bars or strains.—Little airs are often found in sonatas, which have this phrase to regulate their motions.
