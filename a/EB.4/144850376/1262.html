HEROIC VERSE

HEROIC VERSE, that wherein heroic poems are usually composed, or it is that proper for such poems. In the Greek and Latin, hexameter verses are usually denominated heroic verses, as being those only used by mer, Virgil, &c. See POETRY, no 116—124.
