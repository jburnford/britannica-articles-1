HECATOMPYLOS

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HECATOMPYLOS, (anc. geog.) the metropolis of Parthia, and royal residence of Arsaces, situated at the springs of the Araxes. Thebes, in Egypt, had also the same name, from its 100 gates.
