HAM

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAM is a Saxon word used for "a place of dwelling;" a village or town: hence the termination of some of our towns, Nottingham, Buckingham, &c.

Also a home close, or little narrow meadow, is called a ham.
