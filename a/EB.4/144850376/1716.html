HYSTERON PROTERON

article · 372 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYSTERON PROTERON, in grammar and rhetoric, a species of the hyperbaton, wherein the proper order of construction is so inverted, that the part of any sentence which should naturally come first is placed last: as in this of Terence, Valet et vivit, for vicit et valet; and in the following of Virgil, Moriamur, & in media arma ruamus, for In media arma ruamus, & moriamur.
