GILBERTINES

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GILBERTINES, a religious order founded in England by St Gilbert, in the reign of Henry I. The nuns followed the rule St Benedict, and the monks that of Augustin. There were many monasteries of this order in different parts of England.
