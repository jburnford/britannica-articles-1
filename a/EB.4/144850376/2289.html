ISAURIA

article · 165 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISAURIA, (anc. geog.) a country touching Pamphylia and Cilicia on the north, rugged and mountainous, situate almost in mount Taurus, and taking its name from Ifaura.
