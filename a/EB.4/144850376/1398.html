HOGUE, a town and cape on the north-west point of Normandy in France; near which admiral Rook burnt the French admiral's ship called the Rising Sun, with 12 more large men of war, the day after the victory obtained by admiral Russell near Cherburgh, in May 1692. W. Long. 2. o. N. Lat. 49. 50.
