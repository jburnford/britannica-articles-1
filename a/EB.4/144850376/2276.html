IROQUOIS

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IROQUOIS, the name of five nations in North America, in alliance with the British colonies. They are bounded by Canada on the north, by the British plantations of New York and Pensylvania on the east and south, and by the lake Ontario on the west.
