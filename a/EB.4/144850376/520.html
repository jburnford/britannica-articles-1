GRAPHOMETER, a mathematical instrument, otherwise called a Semicircle; the use of which is to observe any angle whose vertex is at the centre of the instrument in any plane, though it is most commonly

Grappling, horizontal, or nearly so), and to find how many degrees it contains. See GEOMETRY, p. [12.]
