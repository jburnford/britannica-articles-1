HYPOTHESIS

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPOTHESIS, in general, denotes something supposed to be true, or taken for granted, in order to prove or illustrate a point in question. Hypotheses, however elegant or artful, ought to be first proved by repeated observations and constant experience, before they are received as truths.
