IRTIS

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IRTIS, a large river of Asia, in Siberia, which rises among the hills of the country of the Kalmucks, and, running north-east, falls into the Oby near Tobolsk. It abounds with fish, particularly sturgeon, and delicate salmon.
