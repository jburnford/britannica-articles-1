HUBNER

HUBNER (John), a learned geographer of Germany, taught geography at Leipzig and Hamburg with extraordinary reputation; and died at Hamburg in 1732, aged 63. His principal work is A Geographical treatise, printed at Basil in 1746, in 6 vols 12mo.
