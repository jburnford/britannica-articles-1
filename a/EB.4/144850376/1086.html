HEEM, (John David,) an able painter, born at Utrecht in 1604. He excelled in painting flowers, fruit, vases, and instruments of music, which he performed in such a perfect manner, that a person was apt to attempt taking them in his hand. His colouring is agreeable, and the insects in his pictures appear alive. He died at Antwerp in 1674.
