HELLESPONT

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELLESPONT, the entrance of the straits which divides Asia from Europe, and passes from the Archipelago to Constantinople. It is now called the Dardanelles, and is about two miles wide.
