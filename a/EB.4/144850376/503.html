GRANADILLOES

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRANADILLOES, the name of some islands of the Caribbees, in America, having St. Vincent to the N. and Granada to the S. They are so inconsiderable that they are quite neglected; but were ceded to England by the treaty of peace in 1763.
