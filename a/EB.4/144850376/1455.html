HOOD-Island

sub_entry · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOOD-Island, one of the Marquess Islands in the South Sea. It was discovered in April 1774 by captain Cook, who gave it that name from the person who first saw the land. It is the most northerly of the cluster, and lies in S. Lat. 9. 26. W. Long. 139. 13.
