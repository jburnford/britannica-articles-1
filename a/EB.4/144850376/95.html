GARBLE, a word used to signify the action of separating the dross and dust from spice, drugs, &c. Garbling is the cleansing and purifying the good from the bad; and may come from the Italian garbo, i. e. finery or neatness: and hence, probably, we say, when we see a man in a neat habit, that he is in handsome garb.
