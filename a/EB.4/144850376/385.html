GOES

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOES, or TER GOES, a strong and considerable town of the United Provinces, in Zealand, and capital of the island of South-Beveland. It communicates with the sea by a canal; and is 10 miles east of Middleburg, and 30 north of Ghent. E. Lon. 3. 50. N. Lat. 51. 33.
