HOMAGE

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOMAGE, in law, is the submission, loyalty, and service, which a tenant promised to his lord, when he was first admitted to the land which he held of the lord in fee: also that owing to a king, or to any superior.
