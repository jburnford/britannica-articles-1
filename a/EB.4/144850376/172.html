Grand GENDARMES

sub_entry · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Grand GENDARMES, at present are a troop composed of 250 gentlemen; the king himself is their captain, and one of the first peers their captain-lieutenant, who has under him two lieutenants, three ensigns, three guidons, and other officers.
