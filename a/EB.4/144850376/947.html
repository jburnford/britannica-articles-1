HARP. They therefore supposed, that the moon, as being the lowest of the planets, corresponded to mi; Mercury, to fa; Venus to sol; the Sun, to la; Mars, to si; Jupiter, to ut; Saturn, to re; and the orb of the fixed stars, as being the highest of all, to mi, or the octave.
