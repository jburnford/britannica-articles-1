GEDDES

article · 306 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GEDDES (James), born of a respectable family in Scotland in 1710, was educated for and practiced at the bar several years; but died of a consumption before he arrived at the age of 40. He published An essay on the composition and manner of writing of the ancients; and left behind him several other tracts.
