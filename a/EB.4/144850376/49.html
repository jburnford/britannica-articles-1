GALLERY

Cross-references: MINE

GALLERY, in fortification, a covered walk a-cross the ditch of a town, made of strong beams, covered over with planks, and loaded with earth: sometimes it is covered with raw hides, to defend it from the artificial fires of the besieged.

GALLERY of a Mine, is a narrow passage or branch of a mine carried on under ground to a work designed to be blown up. See MINE.
