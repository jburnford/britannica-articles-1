HIRSCHFELD

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HIRSCHFELD, a town of Germany in the circle of the upper Rhine, and capital of a principality of the same name, depending on a famous abbey which was secularized in favour of the house of Cassel. It is seated on the river Fulda, in E. Long. 9. 52. N. Lat. 51. 46.
