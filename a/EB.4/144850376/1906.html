ILERDA

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ILERDA, (anc. geog.) the capital of the Iligeres; situated on an eminence between the rivers Sicoris and Cinga; an unhappy city, often besieged, and often taken, because lying exposed to the incursions from Gaul; and under Gallienus it was destroyed by the Germans. Now LEXIDA, in Catalonia, on the river Segra.
