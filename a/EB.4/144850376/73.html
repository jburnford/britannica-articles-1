GAMELION

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GAMELION, in the ancient chronology, was the eighth month of the Athenian year, containing 29 days, and answering to the latter part of our January and beginning of February. It was thus called, as being, in the opinion of the Athenians, the most proper season of the year for marriage.
