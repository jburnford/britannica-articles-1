GLACIS

GLACIS, in building, an easy insensible slope or declivity.

The descent of the glacis is less steep than that of the talus. In gardening, a descent sometimes begins in talus, and ends in glacis.

The glacis of the corniche, is an easy imperceptible slope in the cymatium, to promote the descent and draining off the rain-water.
