JOEL

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JOEL, the son of Phatuel, and the second of the lesser prophets, 800 B. C. foretold the captivity of Babylon, the descent of the Holy Ghost on the apostles, and the last judgment. His prophecy is in Hebrew, and contains only three chapters. The style is strong, expressive, and figurative.
