INTENTION

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INTENTION, in metaphysics, denotes an exertion of the intellectual faculties with more than ordinary vigour; when the mind with earnestness fixes its view on any idea, considers it on all sides, and will not be called off by any solicitation.
