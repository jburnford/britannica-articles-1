Mort-GAGE

sub_entry · 324 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Mort-GAGE, is that which is left in the hands of the proprietor, so that he reaps the fruits thereof.

In opposition to vif-gage, where the fruits or revenues are reaped by the creditor, and reckoned on the foot of the debt, which diminishes in proportion thereto. The second acquits or discharges itself; the first does not.
