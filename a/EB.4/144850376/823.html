HAGIOGRAPHIA

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAGIOGRAPHIA, or "holy writings;" a name given to a particular division of the Old Testament, as containing hymns to God, and moral precepts for the conduct of life. The books distinguished by this term were the Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and the Song of Solomon.
