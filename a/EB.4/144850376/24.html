GALATEA, in fabulous history, a nymph and marine deity, the daughter of Nereus and Doris. She was beloved by Polyphemus, whom she despised for the shepherd Acis; at which that Cyclops was so enraged, that he crushed Acis to pieces with a rock. See ACIS.
