GUELDRS

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUELDRS, a strong town of the Netherlands, in the duchy of the same name. It was ceded to the king of Prussia by the peace of Utrecht, and is seated among marshes. E. Lon. 6. 21. N. Lat. 51. 30.
