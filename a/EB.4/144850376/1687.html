HYPERCATALECTIC

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPERCATALECTIC, in the Greek and Latin poetry is applied to a verfe that has one or two fyllables too much, or beyond the regular and juft meafure; as,

Mufe forores funt Minervae:

Alfo,

Mufe forores Palladis lugent.
