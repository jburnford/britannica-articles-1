HOVEDEN

article · 339 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOVEDEN (Roger de), born of an illustrious family, became chaplain to king Henry II. and left behind him two books of English Annals, carried on from the year 732, where Bede ended, to the 4th of king John, 1201. Sir H. Saville and Mr. Selden reckon him among the most considerable historians who flourished in the 11th and 12th centuries.
