GRUINALES

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRUINALES, (from grus, a crane); the name of the fourteenth order in Linnaeus's fragments of a natural method, consisting of geranium, and a few other genera which the author considers as allied to it in their habit and external structure.
