JABLONSKI (Daniel Ernest), a learned Polish Protestant divine, born at Dantzick in 1660. He became successively minister of Magdeburg, Lissa, Königsberg, and Berlin; and was at length ecclesiastical counsellor, and president of the academy of sciences at the latter. He took great pains to effect an union between the Lutherans and Calvinists; and wrote some works which are in good esteem, particularly Meditations on the origin of the Scriptures, &c. He died in 1741.
