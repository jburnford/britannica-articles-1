KETTLE-Drummer

sub_entry · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KETTLE-Drummer, a man on horseback appointed to beat the kettle-drums, from which he takes his name. He marches always at the head of the squadron, and his post is on the right when the squadron is drawn up.
