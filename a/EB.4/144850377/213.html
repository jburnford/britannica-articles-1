LABEL

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LABEL, a long, thin brass ruler, with a small fight at one end, and a centre-hole at the other; commonly used with a tangent-line on the edge of a circumferentor, to take altitudes, &c.
