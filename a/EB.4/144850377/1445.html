MARSH

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARSH, signifies a piece of ground flow'd with water, yet so that the grass and other vegetables rise above the surface of the water, and, by their decaying, give rise to putrid effluvia, which are very pernicious to the human body.
