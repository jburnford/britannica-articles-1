MARITAGIUM

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARITAGIUM, or Marriage, strictly taken, is that right which the lord of the fee had, to marry the daughters of his vassals after their death: Others tell us, it was that profit which might accrue to the lord, by the marriage of one under age, who held his lands of him by knight's service.
