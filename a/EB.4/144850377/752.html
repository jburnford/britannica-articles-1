LIMBURG

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIMBURG, a town of Germany, in the electorate of Triers or Treves, and in Wetteravia, formerly free and imperial, but now subject to the electorate of Treves. It is seated on the river Lhon. E. Long. 8. 13. N. Lat. 50. 18.
