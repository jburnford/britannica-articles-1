LISMORE

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LISMORE, one of the Western islands of Scotland, seated at the mouth of the bay of Lochyol in Argyleshire. It is eight miles long, and two broad; and the soil is pretty fertile. It was formerly the residence of the bishops of Argyle.
