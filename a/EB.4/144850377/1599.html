MEAUX

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEAUX, an ancient town of France, in Brie, with a bishop's see, seated in a place abounding in corn and cattle, on the river Marne, which divides it into two parts, and its trade consists in corn, wool, and cheese. E. Long. 2. 58. N. Lat. 48. 58.
