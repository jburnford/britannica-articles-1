MACROTELOSTYLA

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MACROTELOSTYLA, in natural history, the name of a genus of crystals, which are composed of two pyramids joined to the end of a column; both the pyramids, as also the column, being hexangular, and the whole body consequently composed of 18 planes.
