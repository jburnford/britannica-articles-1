LOMBARD (Lambert), an eminent painter, born at Liege in 1500; who, after a diligent study of the antique at Rome, introduced that style of painting among his countrymen, instead of the Gothic. He painted history, architecture, and perspective; and though he could never altogether free himself from his national goût, he is ranked among the best painters of his time. He died in 1560.
