LENEICIA

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LENEICIA, a strong town of Poland, and capital of a palatinate of the same name, with a fort seated on a rock. The nobility of the province hold their diet here. It stands in a morass on the banks of the river Bfura, in E. Long. 19. 25. N. Lat. 52. 12.
