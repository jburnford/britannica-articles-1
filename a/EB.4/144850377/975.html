LUCRINUS LACUS

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUCRINUS LACUS (anc. geogr.), a lake of Campania between Baiae and Puteoli, famous for its oysters, (Hórace, Martial, Juvenal); Lucrinenses (Cicero), the people dwelling on it. Now a perfect bay since the earthquake in 1538.
