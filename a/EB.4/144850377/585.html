LESKARD

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LESKARD, a town in Cornwall, seated in a level, is a corporation, and sends to members to parliament. It had formerly a castle, now in ruins; and has a good free-school, and a considerable manufacture of yarns, which is chiefly sold at Exeter. W. Lat. 4. 45. N. Lat. 50. 34.
