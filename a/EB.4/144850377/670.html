LIBETHRIUS mons

LIBETHRIUS mons, (anc. geog.) a mountain of Bœotia, distant from Coronea 40 stadia; where stood the statues of the Muses, and of the nymphs, furnished Libethrine. A mountain probably conjoined with, or at least very near to, Helicon.
