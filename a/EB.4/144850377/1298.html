MANNER

article · 363 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MANNER, in painting, a habitude that a man acquires in the three principal parts of painting, the management of colours, lights and shadows; which is either good or bad according as the painter has practised more or less after the truth, with judgment and study. But the best painter is he who has no manner at all. The good or bad choice he makes is called gout.
