LIBITINA

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIBITINA, in Pagan worship, the goddess of funerals, is believed by some to have been the same with Proserpine. She had a temple at Rome, in which every thing proper for funerals was kept; and which were either bought or borrowed of her priests, called libitinarii, as every one had occasion.
