MARIOTTE

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARIOTTE (Edme), an eminent physician and mathematician, was born in Burgundy, and was made a member of the academy of sciences. He died in 1684. His works, which are much esteemed, were printed at Leyden in 1717, 2 vols. 4to.
