MAIDSTONE, a town of Kent, in England, seated on the river Medway, a branch of which runs through it. It is a large, populous, and agreeable place; and the assizes for the county are held here. It is a corporation, has a free school, and sends two members to parliament. E. Long. o. 37. N. Lat. 51. 20.
