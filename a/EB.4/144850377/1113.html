MACROBIUS

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MACROBIUS (Aurelius), lived about the end of the fourth century. He was one of Theodosius's chamberlains, or one of his wardrobe. His Saturnalia is a pleasant mixture of criticism and antiquity. He also composed some commentaries upon that part of Cicero called Scipio's dream.
