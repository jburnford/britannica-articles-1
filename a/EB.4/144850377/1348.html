MARANS

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARANS, a rich town of France, in the territory of Aunis and diocese of Rochelle, seated among salt marshes, near the river Sevre, three miles from the sea. It carries on a very great trade in corn; and is seated in W. Long. 0. 55. N. Lat. 46. 20.
