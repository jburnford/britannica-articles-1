Astronomical KALENDAR

sub_entry · 318 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Astronomical KALENDAR, an instrument engraved upon copper-plates, printed on paper, and pasted on board, with a brass slider which carries a hair, and shews by inspection the sun's meridian altitude, right ascension, declination, rising, setting, amplitude, &c. to a greater exactness than our common globes will shew.
