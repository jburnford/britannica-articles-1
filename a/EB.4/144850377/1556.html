MAUBEUGE

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAUBEUGE, a town of the Netherlands in Hainault, with an illustrious abbey of canonesses, who must be noble both by the father and mother's side. This place was ceded to France in 1678; and fortified after the manner of Vauban. It is seated on the river Sambre, in E. Long. 5. 0. N. Lat. 50. 15.
