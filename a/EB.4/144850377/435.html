LAWRENCE

LAWRENCE (St), the largest river in north America, proceeding from the lake Ontario, from which it runs a course of 700 miles to the Atlantic ocean. It is navigable as far as Quebec, which is above 400 miles; but beyond Montreal it is so full of shoals and rocks, that it will not admit large vessels without danger, unless the channel be very well known.
