MAMERTUM

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAMERTUM, or MAMERTIUM, (anc. geog.), an inland town of the Bruttii. Mamertini, the people; said to have been expelled their country, and to have been hospitably received by the people of Messana in Sicily; and thus the Messanenses were called Mamertini. Mamertinum Fretum, the strait between Italy and Sicily.
