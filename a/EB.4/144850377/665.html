LIBERTUS

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIBERTUS, in Roman antiquity, a person who from being a slave had obtained his freedom.—The difference between the liberti and libertini was this: the liberti were such as had been actually made free themselves, and the libertini were the children of such persons.
