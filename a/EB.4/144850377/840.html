LIVONICA TERRA

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIVONICA TERRA, a kind of fine bole used in the shops of Germany and Italy. It is found in Livonia, from whence it takes its name, and also in some other parts of the world. It is generally brought to us in little cakes, sealed with the impression of a church and an escutcheon, with two cross keys.
