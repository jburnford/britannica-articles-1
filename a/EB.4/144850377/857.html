LOBO

LOBO (Rodriguez Francis), a celebrated Portuguese poet, was born at Leiria, a small town of Estramadura. He wrote an heroic poem, some eclogues, and a piece entitled Euphrosyne, which is the favourite comedy of the Portuguese. His works were collected and printed together in Portuguese in 1721, in folio. He flourished about 1610.
