MAGAZINE

article · 364 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAGAZINE, on ship-board, a close room or storehouse, built in the fore or after part of the hold, to contain the gun-powder used in battle. This apartment is strongly secured against fire, and no person is allowed to enter it with a lamp or candle: it is therefore lighted, as occasion requires, by means of the candles or lamps in the light-room contiguous to it.
