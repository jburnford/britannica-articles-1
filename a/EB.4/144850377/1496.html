MASILLON

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASILLON (John Baptist) bishop of Clermont, and one of the most eloquent preachers of his time, was born at Hieres, in Provence, in 1663, and died in 1742. His sermons and other works are published in 14 vols 12mo.
