LANDERNEAU

LANDERNEAU, a town of France, in Lower Bretagne, seated on the river Elboro, 20 miles east of Brest. In an inn here is a well which ebbs and flows like the sea, but at contrary times. E. Long. 4. 13. N. Lat. 48. 25.
