MARANO

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARANO, a town of Italy, in the territory of Venice and province of Friuli, with a strong citadel; seated in a marsh at the bottom of the Gölph of Venice, which renders it difficult of access.
