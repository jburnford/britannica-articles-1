KIRCH

KIRCH (Christian Frederic), of Berlin, a celebrated astronomer, was born at Guben in 1694; and acquired great reputation in the observatories of Danzig and Berlin. Godfrey Kirch his father, and Mary his mother, acquired considerable reputation by their astronomical observations. This family corresponded with all the learned societies of Europe; and their astronomical works are in high repute.
