MÆONIA

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MÆONIA, (anc. geog.), a town of Lydia, situate at the foot of mount Tmolus. Mæonis, the people. Mæonis, (Homer, Ovid), the feminine gentilitions name: hence Mæonides, the mules, (Ovid). Mæonides denotes also Homer, (Ovid).
