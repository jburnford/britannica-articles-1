MATTHIOLUS

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MATTHIOLUS (Peter Andrew), an eminent physician in the 16th century, born at Siena, was well skilled in the Greek and Latin tongues. He wrote learned commentaries on Dioscorides, and other works which are esteemed; and died in 1577.
