Scythian LAMB

sub_entry · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Scythian LAMB, a kind of moss, which grows about the roots of fern in some of the northern parts of Europe and Asia, and sometimes assumes the form of a quadruped. See Plate CLVIII. fig. 8. A particular description of it may be seen in Philos. Trans. No. 398.
