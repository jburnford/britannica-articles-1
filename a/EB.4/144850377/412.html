LAUNCESTON

LAUNCESTON, a town of Cornwall in England, seated on the river Tamar on the top of a small hill, and is a large corporation sending two members to parliament. It was formerly defended by a castle, which is now in ruins. W. Long. 4. 55. N. Lat. 50. 40.
