LANDAFF, a town or village of Glamorgan-shire in South-Wales, with a bishop's see, and on that account has the title of a city. It is seated upon an ascent on the river Taff, or Tave, near Cardiff; but the cathedral stands on a low ground, and is a large, stately building. W. Long. 3. 20. N. Lat. 51. 33.
