LEUCIPPUS

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEUCIPPUS, a celebrated Greek philosopher and mathematician; first author of the famous system of atoms and vacuums, and of the hypothesis of storms; since attributed to the moderns. He flourished about 428 B. C.
