LUGDUNUM

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUGDUNUM, (anc. geog.), the capital of the Segusiuni in Gallia Celtica, situated at the conflux of the Arar and Rhodanus, on an eminence, as the Celtic term dune signifies; built by Manotius Plancus under Augustus, while commanding in that part of Gaul; and whither he led a colony. Now Lyons, capital of the Lyonois.
