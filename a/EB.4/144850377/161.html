KNAPSACK

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KNAPSACK, in a military sense, a rough leather bag which a soldier carries on his back, and which contains all his necessaries. Square knapsacks are most convenient; and should be made with a division to hold the shoes, black-ball and brushes, separate from the linen. White goat-skins are the best.
