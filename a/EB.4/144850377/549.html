LEOMINSTER, a town of Herefordshire, in England, seated on the river Lug; which waters the north and east sides of the town. It contains one parish, about 400 houses, six wards, and the principal officer is a bailiff. It is of great note for its fine wool, has several good inns, and sends two members to parliament. W. Long. 2. 45. N. Lat. 52. 20.
