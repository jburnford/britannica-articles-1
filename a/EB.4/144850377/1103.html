MACHIAN

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MACHIAN, one of the Molucca islands, in the East Indian Ocean, about 20 miles in circumference, and the most fertile of them all. It likewise produces the best cloves; and is in possession of the Dutch, who have three strong forts built on it.
