LAUNCH

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAUNCH, in the sea-language, signifies to put out: as, Launch the ship, that is, Put her out of dock; launch aft, or forward, speaking of things that are

stowed in the hold, is, put them more forward; launch hoist is a term used when a yard is hoisted high enough, and signifies, hoist no more.
