Cross-references: HOUSE

MANSE, Mansus, Mansa, or Mansum; in ancient law-books, denotes an house, or habitation, either with or without land. See HOUSE, and MANSION. The word is formed à manendo, "abiding;" as being the place of dwelling or residence.
