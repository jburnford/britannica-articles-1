KEISERSBERG

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KEISERSBERG, a town of Alsace in France, and in the bailiwick of Haguenau, which has belonged to the French ever since the year 1548. It is seated in a pleasant country, in E. Long. 7. 25. N. Lat. 48. 10.
