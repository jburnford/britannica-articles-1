LISSA

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LISSA, a village of Silesia, 16 miles from Breslau, remarkable for a battle fought between the Prussians and the Austrians on the 15th of December 1757, when the latter were entirely defeated.
