LOOMING

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LOOMING, in the sea-language, an indistinct ap-
pearance of any distant object, as the sea-coast, ships,
mountains, &c. as, "the looms large before the wind;"
"the looming of the land is high above the water, &c."
