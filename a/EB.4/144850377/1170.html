MAGNUS PORTUS

MAGNUS PORTUS, (anc. geog.), a port of the Belge, in Britain, on the Channel. Now thought to be Portsmouth, in Hampshire, (Camden).—Another Portus Magnus of Bætica in Spain, (Ptolemy); a port to the east of Abdera.
