LEECH-ROPE

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: BOLT-ROPE

LEECH-ROPE, a name given to that part of the bolt-robe to which the border or skirt of a sail is sewed. In all sails whose opposite leeches are of the same length, it is terminated above the earring, and below the clue. See BOLT-ROPE, CLUE, and EARING.
