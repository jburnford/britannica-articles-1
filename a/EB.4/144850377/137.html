KINROSS

article · 458 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KINROSS, a town of the county of Fife in Scotland, situated in W. Long. 3. 7. N. Lat. 56. 15. on the west side of Lochleven, a fresh-water lake about 10 miles in compass, abounding with pike, trout, perch, and water-fowl. In the lake are two islands; on one of which appear the ruins of a priory, heretofore possessed by the Culdees; the other is famous for the castle in which queen Mary was imprisoned by her rebellious subjects.
