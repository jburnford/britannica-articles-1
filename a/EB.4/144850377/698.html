LIEGE-POUSTIE

article · 165 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIEGE-POUSSE, in Scots law, is opposed to death-bed; and signifies a person's enjoying that state of health in which only he can dispose of his property at pleasure.
