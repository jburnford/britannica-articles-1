MAPPARIUS

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAPPARIUS, in Roman antiquity, the officer who gave the signal to the gladiators to begin fighting; which he did by throwing an handkerchief that he had received from the emperor or other magistrate.
