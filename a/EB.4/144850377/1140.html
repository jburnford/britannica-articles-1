MAGADOXO

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAGADOXO, the capital town of a kingdom of the same name, in Africa, and on the coast of Ajan; seated near the mouth of a river of the same name, defended by a citadel, and has a good harbour. The inhabitants are Mohammedans. E. Long. 45. 15. N. Lat. 3. 0.
