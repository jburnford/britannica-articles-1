LIST

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIST, in commerce, the border of cloth or stuff; serving not only to shew their quality, but to preserve them from being torn in the operations of fulling, dyeing, &c.—List is used on various occasions; but chiefly by gardeners for securing their wall-trees.
