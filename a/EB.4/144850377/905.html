LONGWY

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LONGWY, a town of France, on the frontiers of the duchy of Luxemburg, with a castle, divided into the old and new towns. This last was built and fortified by Lewis XIV. It is seated on an eminence. E. Long. 5. 51. N. Lat. 40. 32.
