LEVANT

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEVANT, is also a name given to the eastern part of the Mediterranean sea, bounded by Natolia or the lesser Asia on the north, by Syria and Palestine on the east, by Egypt and Barca on the south, and by the island of Candia and the other part of the Mediterranean on the west.
