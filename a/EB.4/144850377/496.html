LEECH-LINES

article · 281 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: BRAILS

LEECH-LINES, certain ropes fastened to the middle of the leeches of the main-sail and fore-sail, and communicating with blocks under the opposite sides of the top, whence they pass downwards to the deck, serving to truss up those sails to the yard as occasion requires. See BRAILS.
