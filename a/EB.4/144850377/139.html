KINTORE

KINTORE, a royal borough of Aberdeenshire in Scotland, situated on the river Don, in W. Long. 2. 5. N. Lat. 57. 38. It gives the title of earl to a branch of the noble family of Keith, but in other respects is inconsiderable.
