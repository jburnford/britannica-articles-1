Clarenceux KING at Arms

Cross-references: CLARENCEUX

Clarenceux KING at Arms, is so called from the duke of Clarence, to whom he first belonged. His office is to marshal and dispose the funerals of all the inferior nobility, as baronets, knights, esquires, and gentlemen, on the south side of the Trent. See CLARENCEUX.
