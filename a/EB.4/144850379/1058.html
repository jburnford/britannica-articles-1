PISISTRATUS

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PISISTRATUS, a famous general of the Athenians. He usurped the supreme power, and, in order to obtain it, was a cruel tyrant; but having gained it, he became a wise legislator, and an excellent governor. He died 327 B. C.
