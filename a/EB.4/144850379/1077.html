PIZARRO

Cross-references: PERU

PIZARRO (Francis), a celebrated Spanish general, the discoverer and conqueror of Peru, in conjunction with Diego Almagro, a Spanish navigator. They are both charged with horrid cruelties to the inhabitants; and they fell victims to their own ambition, jealousy, and avarice. Almagro revolting, was defeated, and beheaded by Pizarro, who was assassinated by Almagro's friends, in 1541. See PERU.
