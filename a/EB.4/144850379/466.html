PASIPHAE

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PASIPHAE, in fabulous history, the daughter of Apollo, and the wife of Minos, king of Crete, by whom she had Androgeos, Ariadne, and Phaedra. She conceived a violent passion for a bull; and had by him the Minotaur, which was kept in the labyrinth, where it was killed by Theseus.
