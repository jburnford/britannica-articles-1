PERIGEUX

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERIGEUX, an ancient episcopal town of France, capital of the province of Perigord, seated on the river Isle, in E. Long. 0. 33. N. Lat. 45. 18. It is remarkable for the ruins of the temple of Venus, and an amphitheatre.
