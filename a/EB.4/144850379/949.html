PHYSIOGNOMONICS

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHYSIOGNOMONICS, among physicians, denote such signs as, being taken from the countenance, serve to indicate the state, disposition, &c. both of the body and mind: and hence the art of reducing these signs to practice is termed physiognomy.
