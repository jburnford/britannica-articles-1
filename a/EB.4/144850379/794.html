PESCHIERA

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PESCHIERA, a small but strong town of Italy, in the Veronese, with a castle, and a strong fort; seated on the river Mincio, or Menzo, which proceeds from the lake Garda. E. Long. 10. 37. N. Lat. 45. 23.
