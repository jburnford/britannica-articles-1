PETRA Secktael

PETRA Secktael, (2 Kings xiv.), a town of the Amalekites; near the Adscensus Scorpionis (Judg. i.) and the Valley of Salt in the south of Judea: afterwards in the possession of the Edomites, after destroying the Amalekites.
