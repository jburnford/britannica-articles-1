PERNAMBUCO

article · 367 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERNAMBUCO, a province of Brasil in South America, bounded on the north by Tamera, on the east by the ocean; on the south by Seregippa, and on the west by Tapuyers. It is about 200 miles in length, and 150 in breadth. The Dutch became masters of it in 1630, but the Portuguese soon retook it from them. It produces a great quantity of sugar, and the best Brasil wood.
