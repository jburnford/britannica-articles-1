ORGUES

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORGUES, in the military art, are thick long pieces of wood, pointed at one end, and shod with iron, clear one of another; hanging each by a particular rope or cord, over the gateway of a strong place, perpendicularly, to be let fall in case of the approach of an enemy.
