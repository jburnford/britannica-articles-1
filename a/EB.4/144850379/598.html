PEDARIAN

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PEDARIAN, in Roman antiquity, those senators who signified their votes by their feet, not their tongues; that is, such as walked over to the side of those whose opinion they approved of, in divisions of the house.
