PLACENTA

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLACENTA, in anatomy and midwifery, a soft roundish mass, found in the womb of pregnant women; which, from its resemblance to the liver, was called by the ancients hepar uterinum, uterine liver.
