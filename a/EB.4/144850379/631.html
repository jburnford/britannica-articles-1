PELLETIER

PELLETIER (JAMES), a doctor of physic, and an eminent mathematician, was born in 1517. He was an excellent Latin and French poet, a good orator, physician, and grammarian. He wrote, Oeuvres Poétiques, Commentaires Latins sur Euclide, &c.
