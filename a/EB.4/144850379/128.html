OVARIUM

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OVARIUM, in botany, a name by which botanists, who are fond of assimilating the animal and vegetable kingdoms, have distinguished the germ or seed-bud, as containing the rudiments of the future seed.
