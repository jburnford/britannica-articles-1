PARIS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARIS, the son of Priam king of Troy, memorable in history for carrying off Helena the wife of Menelaus king of Sparta in his absence, which occasioned the famous siege of Troy. Slain there about 1188 B.C.
