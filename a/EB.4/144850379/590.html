PECULATE

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PECULATE, in civil law, the crime of embezzling the public money, by a person entrusted with the receipt, management, or custody thereof. This term is also used by civilians for a theft, whether the thing be public, fiscal, sacred, or religious.
