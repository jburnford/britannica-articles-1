ORIOLUS

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORIOLUS, in ornithology, a genus belonging to the order of picæ. The bill is conical, convex, very sharp and straight; the superior mandible being much longer than the under one; and the tongue is forked and sharp. There are 20 species, principally distinguished by their colour.
