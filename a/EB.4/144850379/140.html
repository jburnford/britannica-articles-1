OVIEDO, a town of Spain, and capital of Asturias
d'Oviedo, with a bishop's see, and an university; seat-
ed at the confluence of the rivers Ove and Deva, which
form the Asta, 50 miles north-west of Leon, and 208
north-west of Madrid. W. Long. 5. 47. N. Lat. 43.
