PANDOURS

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANDOURS, are Hungarian infantry: they wear a loose garment fixed tight to their bodies by a girdle, with great sleeves, and large breeches hanging down to their ancles. They use fire-arms, and are excellent marksmen: they also use a kind of sabre near four feet long, which they use with great dexterity.
