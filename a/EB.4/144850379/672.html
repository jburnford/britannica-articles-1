PENSACOLA

PENSACOLA, a settlement in North America, situated at the mouth of a river on the gulf of Mexico. It was established by the French, and ceded to Great Britain in 1763; but has lately been taken by the Spaniards. W. Long. 87. 20. N. Lat. 30. 22.
