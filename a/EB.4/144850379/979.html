Cross-references: HERALDRY

PICES, in heraldry, denotes an ordinary or charge. —The honourable pieces of the shield are the chief, fess, bend, pale, bar, cross, saltier, chevron, and in general all those which may take up one-third of the field, when alone, and in what manner soever it be. See HERALDRY.
