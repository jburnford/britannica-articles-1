PENULTIMA

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PENULTIMA, or PENULTIMATE Syllable, in grammar, denotes the last syllable but one of a word; and hence the antipenultimate syllable is the last but two, or that immediately before the penultima.
