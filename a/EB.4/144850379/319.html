PANORMUS, (Ptolemy), a port of Attica; its name denoting it to be capacious. — Another, of Epirus, (Strabo, Ptolemy); a large harbour in the heart of the Montes Cerauni, below the citadel Chimera. — A third, of Ionia, (Strabo); near Ephesus, with the temple of the Ephesian Diana.
