PANEGYRICUM, in church-history, an ecclesiastical book, used by the Greek church, containing the panegyrical orations of various authors, on the solemnities of Jesus Christ and the saints.

Among the principal authors of this work are Athanasius, Cyril, Basil, Chrysostom, &c.
