PELASGICUM, (Pausanias, Pliny); the north
wall of Athens; so called from the builders, the Pe-
lasgi. There was an execution pronounced on any
that should build houses under this wall; because the
Pelasgi, while dwelling there, entered into a conspiracy
against the Athenians, (Thucydides).
