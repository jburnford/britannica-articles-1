ORGAN

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORGAN, in general, is an instrument or machine designed for the production of some certain action or operation; in which sense the mechanic powers, machines, and even the veins, arteries, nerves, muscles, and bones of the human body, may be called organs.
