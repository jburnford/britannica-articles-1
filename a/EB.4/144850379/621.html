PELASGIA, (Pliny) the ancient name of Lef-
bor; so called from the Pelasgi, its first inhabitants,
(Diodorus Siculus). Also the ancient name of Pelo-
ponnesus, from Pelasgius, a native of the country,
(Nicolaus Damascenus, Ephorus).
