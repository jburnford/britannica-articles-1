PETROSA ossa

sub_entry · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PETROSA ossa, in anatomy, a name given to the fourth and fifth bones of the cranium, called also ossia temporum, and ossia squamosa; the substance whereof, as their first and last names express, is squamous and very hard.
