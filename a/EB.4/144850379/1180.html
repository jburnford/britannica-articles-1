PLUMOSE, something formed in the manner of a feather, with a stem and fibres issuing from it on each

Plural each side; such are the antennæ of certain moths, butterflies, &c.

Plutarch. PLURAL, in grammar, an epithet applied to that number of nouns and verbs which is used when we speak of more than one thing. See GRAMMAR.
