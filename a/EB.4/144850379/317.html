PANNONIA

article · 467 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANNONIA, (Pliny, Strabo, Dio), an extensive country of Europe, having the Danube on the north, Dalmatia on the south, Noricum on the west, and Moesia on the east. It is divided in superior and inferior, (Ptolemy, Dio). The common boundary between both were the river Arabo and mount Cestus, having the superior to the west, and the inferior on the east side. This division is thought to be no older than the times of the Antonines. Pannonicus the epithet, (Martial).
