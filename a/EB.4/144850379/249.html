PALINURI PROMONTORIUM

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALINURI PROMONTORIUM, (Virgil, Velleius,) with a cognominal port, was situated at the south extremity of the Sinus Pæstanus, on the coast of Lucania; so called from Palinurus, Æneas's steersman, who there perished. (Mela, Dionysius Halicarnassus.)
