PISSAPHALTUM

article · 349 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PISSAPHALTUM, EARTH-PITCH; a fluid, opaque, mineral body, of a thick consistence, strong smell, readily inflammable, but leaving a residuum of greyish ashes after burning. It arises out of the cracks of the rocks, in several places in the island of Sumatra, and some other places in the East Indies, where it is much esteemed in paralytic disorders.
