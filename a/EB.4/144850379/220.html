PALÆMON

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALÆMON (Q. Rhemnius), a Latin grammarian, born at Vicenza, was the son of slave. He taught at Rome with great applause under Tiberius and Claudius, and Juvenal mentions him with praises. We have only some fragments of his works.
