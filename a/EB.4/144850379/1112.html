PLANUDES

PLANUDES (Maximus), a Greek monk of Constantinople, towards the end of the 14th century, who published a collection of epigrams intitled Anthologia; a Greek translation of Ovid's Metamorphoses; a Life of Æsop, which is rather a romance than a history; and some other works. We know nothing more of him, than that he suffered some persecution on account of his attachment to the Latin church.
