PATRICIAN

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PATRICIAN, among the ancient Romans, a title given to the descendants of the hundred, or, according to others, of the two hundred first senators chosen by Romulus, and by him called patres, "fathers."
