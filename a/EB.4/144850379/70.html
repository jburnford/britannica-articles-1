ORPIMENT

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORPIMENT, in natural history, a fossil substance usually found in copper-mines, composed of thin flakes like the tales; these easily split, are flexible, not elastic, soluble in oil, fusible in a moderate fire, and yield in burning an offensive smell like garlic.
