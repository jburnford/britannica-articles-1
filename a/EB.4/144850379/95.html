OSSA

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OSSA, a mountain of Theffaly, near the Peneus, which runs between this mountain and Olympus; famous in the fabulous story of the giants, (Homer, Virgil, Horace, Seneca, Ovid.) The bending and unbending of its pines, on the blowing of a strong north wind, formed a clashing sound like thunder, (Lucan.)
