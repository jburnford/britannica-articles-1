PIERIS

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PIERIS (anc. geog.), a mountain which is thought to have given name to Pieria of Macedonia; taking its name from Pierus a poet, who was the first that sacrificed to the Muses, thence called Pierides, if credit may be given to an ancient scholiast on Juvenal.
