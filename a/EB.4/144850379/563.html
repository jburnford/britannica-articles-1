PAULICIANS

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAULICIANS, Christian heretics of the seventh century, disciples of one Constantine a native of Armenia, and a favourer of the errors of Manes; who, as the name Manichee was become odious, gave those of his sect the name of Paulicians, on the pretence that they followed only the doctrine of St Paul.
