PHALEUCIAN VERSE

article · 160 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHALEUCIAN VERSE, in ancient poetry, a kind of verse consisting of five feet, the first of which is a spondee, the second a dactyl, and the three last trochees.
