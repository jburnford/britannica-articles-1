ORVIETO

article · 341 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORVIETO, a town of Italy in the patrimony of St Peter, with a bishop's see and a magnificent palace. In this place there is a deep well, into which mules descend by one pair of stairs to fetch up water, and ascend by another. It is seated on a craggy rock, near the confluence of the rivers Pagli and Chiana. E. Long. 12. 10. N. Lat. 42. 42.
