ORLOPE

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORLOPE, in the sea-language, the uppermost space
or deck in a great ship, reaching from the main to
the mizen-mast. In three-deck ships, the second and
lowest decks are sometimes called orlopes.
