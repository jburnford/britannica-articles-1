PLOUGH

Cross-references: AGRICULTURE

PLOUGH, in agriculture, a machine for turning up the soil, contrived to save the time, labour, and expences, that, without this instrument, must have been employed in digging land, to prepare for the sowing of all kinds of grain. See AGRICULTURE, no 77, &c.
