PERITONEUM

Cross-references: ANATOMY

PERITONEUM, in anatomy, is a thin, smooth, and lubricous membrane, investing the whole internal surface of the abdomen, and containing most of the viscera of that part as it were in a bag. See ANATOMY, n° 350.
