PATRON

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PATRON, in the church of Rome, a saint, whose name a person bears, or under whose protection he is put, and whom he takes particular care to invoke: or

Patronage. a faint, in whose name a church or order is founded.
