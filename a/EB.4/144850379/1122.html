PLATALEA, the SPOONBILL, in ornithology, a genus belonging to the order of grallæ. The beak is plain, and dilates towards the point into an orbicular form; the feet have three toes, and are half-palmated. There are three species, distinguished by their colour. They inhabit the continent of Europe. See Plate CCXLIV. fig 3.
