PANEGYRIC, an oration in praise of some extraordinary thing, person, or virtue.

The name is Greek, πανηγυρις; formed of παν, "all," and αγυρα, "I assemble;" because anciently held in public and solemn assemblies of the Greeks, either at their games, their feasts, fairs, or religious meetings.
