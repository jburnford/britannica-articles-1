PATANCE

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FLORY

PATANCE, in heraldry, is a cross, flory at the ends; from which it differs only in this, that the ends, instead of turning down like a fleur de lis, are extended somewhat in the pattee form. See FLORY.
