PALESTRINA

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALESTRINA, is one of the largest and most populous of the islands called the Lagunes, near Venice, and where the most considerable of the noblemen have houses of pleasure. It is 15,000 paces in length, and 400 in breadth; the principal harbour has also the same name.
