ORTIVE

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORTIVE, in astronomy, the same with eastern. The ortive or eastern amplitude, is an arch of the horizon intercepted between where a star rises, and the east point of the horizon, or point where the horizon and equator intersect.
