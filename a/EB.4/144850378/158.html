MESSUAGE

MESSUAGE, MESSUAGIUM, in law, a dwelling-house, with some land adjoining assigned for its use. By the name of messuage may a garden, shop, mill, cottage, chamber, cellar, or the like, pass.—In Scotland, messuage denotes what is called in England the manor-house; viz. the principal dwelling-house within any barony.
