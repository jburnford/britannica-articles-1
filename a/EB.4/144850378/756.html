NATURAL

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NATURAL, in heraldry, is used where animals, fruits, flowers, &c. are blazoned with the colours they naturally have, though different from the common colours of heraldry: and this is to prevent the armories being accused of falsity, when blazoned with the names of colours unknown in heraldry.
