JAMES SMALL. ROB. RAMSAY.

Ballantyne house, 4th February 1780.

The within interrogatories were put to David Tullo tenant in Auchertyre, anent he mule he had, and the foal she produced; to which he gave the answers subjoined to each query, and signed them; as did James Small and Robert Ramsay, attesting the truth thereof, in presence of GEORGE WATSON, J. P.

The original attestation is in the possession of the translator; and he lately transmitted notarial or authenticated copies of it to the count de Buffon, and to Thomas Pennant, Esq; of Downing, in Flintshire.
