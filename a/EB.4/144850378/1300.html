ONOMANCY

article · 344 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ONOMANCY, or rather ONOMAMANCY, a branch of divination, which foretells the good or bad fortune of a man, from the letters in his name. See the article DIVINATION.

From much the same principle the young Romans toasted their mistresses as often as there were letters in their names: hence Martial says,

Navis sex cyathis, septem Justina bibatur.
