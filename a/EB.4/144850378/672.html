MUTULE

MUTULE, in architecture, a kind of square modillion set under the cornice of the Doric order.

MUZZLE of a GUN or MORTAR, the extremity at

which the powder and ball is put in; and hence the muzzle-ring is the metalline circle or moulding that surrounds the mouth of the piece.
