Orderly non-commissioned OFFICERS

sub_entry · 403 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Orderly non-commissioned OFFICERS, are those who are orderly, or on duty for that week; who, on hearing the drum beat for orders, are to repair to the place appointed to receive them, and to take down in writing, in the orderly book, what is dictated by the adjutant, or sergeant-major: they are then immediately to shew these orders to the officers of the company, and afterwards warn the men for duty.
