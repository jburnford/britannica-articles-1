MENNITH, or MANNITH, Judges xi. 33. a town near Heshbon, (Jerome), in Arabia Petraea; in a district named Ecesipolis, or twenty-towns, (Cellarius). There is also a Minnith mentioned Ezekiel xxvii. as being in a good wheat country: but whether the same with the foregoing is uncertain; though some think, that the first Minnith lay in the country of Ammon, (Wells).
