OLERON

OLERON, an island of France, on the coast of Aunis and Saintonge, about five miles from the continent. It is 12 miles in length, and five in breadth; and is very fertile, containing about 12,000 inhabitants, who are excellent seamen. It is defended by a castle, which is well fortified; and there is a light-house placed here for the direction of ships. It is 14

miles south-east of Rochelle. W. Long. 1. 26. N. Lat. 46. 10. Olfactory
