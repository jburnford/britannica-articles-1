MUSONIUS

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MUSONIUS, (Caius Rufus) a Stoic philosopher of the second century, was banished into the island of Gyare, under the reign of Nero, for criticising the manners of that prince; but was recalled by the emperor Vespasian. He was the friend of Apollonius Tyaneus, and the letters that passed between them are still extant.
