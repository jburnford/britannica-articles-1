OCHRE

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OCHRE, in natural history, a genus of earths, slightly coherent, and composed of fine, smooth, soft, argillaceous particles, rough to the touch, and readily diffusible in water. Ochres are of various colours, as red, blue, yellow, brown, green, &c.
