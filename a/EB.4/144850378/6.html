MEDIOLANUM Aulercorum (anc. geogr.), a town of Gallia Celtica, which afterwards took the name of the Ebroicorum Civitas (Antonine); corrupted to Civitas Ebroicorum, and this last to Ebroica; whence the modern appellation Evreux, a city of Normandy. E. Long. 1. 12. N. Lat. 49. 21.
