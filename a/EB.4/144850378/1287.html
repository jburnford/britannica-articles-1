ON, (anc. geog.), a town of Egypt towards Arabia, to the south-east of Babylon, and of the eastern branch of the Nile. The prophet Jeremiah calls it the house or town of the sun, in the land of Egypt. The high-priest Onias built a temple here, held in great esteem by the Hellenists.
