OBLIQUE

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OBLIQUE, in geometry, something asslant, or that deviates from the perpendicular. Thus an oblique angle, is either an acute or obtuse one, i. e. any angle except a right one.
