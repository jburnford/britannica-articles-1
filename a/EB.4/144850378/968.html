NOBILIARY

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOBILIARY, in literary history, a book containing the history of the noble families of a nation, or province: such are Chorier's Nobiliary of Dauphine, and Caumartin's Nobiliary of Provence. The Germans are said to be particularly careful of their Nobiliaries, in order to keep up the dignity of their families.
