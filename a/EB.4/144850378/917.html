NICOSIA

NICOSIA, the capital of the island of Cyprus, where a Turkish bashaw resides. It is delightfully situated between the mountains of Olympus, and a chain of others; and was formerly well fortified by the Venetians; but the works are now in ruins. E. Long. 33. 35. N. Lat. 35. 1.
