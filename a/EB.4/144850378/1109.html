NYON

article · 282 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NYON, a considerable town of Switzerland, in the canton of Bern, and capital of a bailiwick of the same name, with a castle. There are a great many Roman inscriptions here; and it is a trading place, seated in a good country, near the lake of Geneva. E. Long. 5. 10. N. Lat. 46. 24.
