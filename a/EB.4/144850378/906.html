NICHE

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NICHE, in architecture, a hollow sunk into a wall, for the commodious and agreeable placing of a statue. The word comes from the Italian necchia, "sea-shell;" in regard the statue is here inclosed in a shell, or perhaps on account of the shell wherewith the tops of some of them are adorned.
