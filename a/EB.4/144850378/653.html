MUSLIN

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MUSLIN, a fine sort of cotton-cloth, which bears a doway knot on its surface. There are several sorts of muslins brought from the East Indies, and more particularly from Bengal; such as doreas, betelles, mulmula, tanjebas, &c.
