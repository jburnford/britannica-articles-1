NOTTEBURG

article · 282 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOTTEBURG, a town of Russia, in the province of Ingria, seated on an island in the lake Ladoga, at the place where the river Niewa proceeds from this lake. It is strong, has a good citadel, and was capital of the province before Petersburg was built. E. Long. 31. 40. N. Lat. 60. 0.
