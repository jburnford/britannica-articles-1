MELPOMENE

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELPOMENE, in fabulous history, one of the nine muses, and the inventress of tragedy. She is commonly represented with a serious countenance, and in a theatrical dress, holding crowns and sceptres in one hand, and a dagger in the other.
