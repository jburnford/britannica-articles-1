MELCOMB-REGIS

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELCOMB-REGIS, a town of Dorsetshire, in England, situated in W. Long. 2. 32. N. Lat. 50. 40. The streets are broad and paved; and there is an excellent harbour, by which they carry on a pretty good foreign trade.
