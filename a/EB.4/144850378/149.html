MESOPOTAMIA

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MESOPOTAMIA, the ancient name of the province of DIARBECK, in Turkey in Asia. It is situated between the rivers Euphrates and Tigris; having Assyria on the east, Armenia on the north, Syria on the west, and Arabia Deserta with Babylonia on the south. In Scripture it is called Padan Aram.
