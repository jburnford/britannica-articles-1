NECYDALIS

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NECYDALIS, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera. The feelers are falcateous; the elytra are shorter and narrower than the wings; the tail is simple. There are 11 species, chiefly distinguished by the size and figure of their elytra.
