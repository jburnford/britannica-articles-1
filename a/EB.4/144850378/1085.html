NUNDINA

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NUNDINA, a goddess among the ancient Heathens, supposed to have the care of the purification of infants. And because male-infants were purified nine days after their birth, her name is derived from nonus, or the ninth, though female-infants were purified the eighth day; which purification was called lustration by the Romans.
