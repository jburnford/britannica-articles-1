MUTILATION

MUTILATION, the retrenching or cutting away any member of the body.

This word is also extended to statues and buildings, where any part is wanting, or the projection of any member, as a cornice or an impost, is broken off. It is sometimes also used, in a more immediate manner, for CASTRATION.
