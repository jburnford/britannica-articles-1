MELIA

MELIA, AZADERACH, or the Bead-tree; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the decandria class of plants. There are three species, all of them exotic trees of the Indies, rising near 20 feet high; adorned with large pinnated or winged leaves, and clusters of pentapetalous flowers. They are all propagated by seeds sown on hot-beds.
