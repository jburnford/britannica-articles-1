OCTAETARIDES

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OCTAETARIDES, in chronology, denotes a cycle of eight years, at the end of which three entire lunar months were added. This cycle was used at Athens till Meton discovered the golden number.
