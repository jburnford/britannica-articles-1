OBLIQUE Sailing

sub_entry · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: NAVIGATION

OBLIQUE Sailing, in navigation, is when a ship sails upon some rhumb between the four cardinal points, making an oblique angle with the meridian; in which case, she continually changes both latitude and longitude. See NAVIGATION, §. 6.
