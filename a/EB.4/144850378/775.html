NAXUS (anc. geogr.), the most remarkable of the Cyclades, 18 miles to the east of Delos; called Strongyle, then Dia, and Dionysias. Some have called it Sicily the Left. Now called Naxia, Nixia, or Naxos. E. Long. 26. 5. N. Lat. 36. 30.
