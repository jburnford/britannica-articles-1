MONUMENT

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONUMENT, in architecture, a building destined to preserve the memory, &c. of the person who raised it, or the person for whom it was raised; such are a mausoleum, a triumphal arch, a pyramid, &c.
