NOMARCHA

article · 338 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOMARCHA, in antiquity, the governor or commander of a nome, or nomos.—Egypt was anciently divided into several regions or quarters, called nomes, from the Greek nomos, taken in the sense of a division; and the officer who had the administration of each nome, or nomos, from the king, was called monarcha, from nomos and arche, "command."
