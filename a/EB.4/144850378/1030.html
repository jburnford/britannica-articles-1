NOTHUS

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOTHUS, signifies spurious, or bastard; whence it is figuratively applied by physicians to such diseases as, though in a respect of a similitude of symptoms, &c. they have the same denomination as some others, yet are of a different origin, seat, or the like, from the same.
