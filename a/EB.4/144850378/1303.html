ONTARIO

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ONTARIO, a lake of North America, in the country of the Iroquois, 180 miles in length and 60 in breadth. There are many rivers that run into it; and from it the great river St Lawrence proceeds. It communicates with lake Erie by a river 33 miles in length, in which is the remarkable cataract of NIAGARA.
