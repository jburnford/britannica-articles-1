OLAUS MAGNUS

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OLAUS MAGNUS, archbishop of Upsal in Sweden, succeeded his brother John Magnus in 1544. He appeared with great credit at the council of Trent in 1546, and suffered much afterward for the Catholic religion. We have of his writing, A History of the Manners, Customs, and Wars of the Northern Nations of Europe.
