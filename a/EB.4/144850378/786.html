NEBO

article · 416 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEBO (anc. geogr.), a very high mountain, a part of the mountains Abarim, and their highest top, whither Moses was ordered to ascend to take a view of the land of Canaan, and there die. Situate in the land of Moab, over-against Jericho: with a cognominal town at its foot (Isaiah) belonging to the Reubenites, which afterwards returned to the Moabites; in Jerome's time desolate; eight miles to the south of Heshbon.
