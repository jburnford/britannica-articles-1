MIRANDA-DE-EBRO

MIRANDA-DE-EBRO, a town of Spain in Old Castile, with a strong castle; seated in a country that produces excellent wine. The town is divided into two parts by the river, over which there is a handsome bridge. W. Long. 3. 10. N. Lat. 42. 52.
