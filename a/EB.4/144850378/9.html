MEDIOLANUM Santonum (anc. geogr.), which afterwards taking the name of the people, was called Santonica Urbs; also Santonies and Santoni: A town of Aquitain. Now Saintes, capital of Saintonge in Guienne, on the river Charente. W. Long. 36. 0. N. Lat. 45. 50.
