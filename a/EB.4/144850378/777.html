NAZARENES

article · 347 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NAZARENES, in church-history, a name originally given to all Christians in general, on account that Jesus Christ was of the city of Nazareth; but afterwards restrained to a set of heretics, whose religion consisted of a strange jumble of Judaism and Christianity, observing at the same time the Mosaic law, and the rites of the Christian religion.
