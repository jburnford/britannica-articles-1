MORENA

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MORENA, (anc. geog.), a district or division of Mysia, in the Hither Asia. A part of which was occupied by Cleon, formerly at the head of a band of robbers; but afterwards priest of Jupiter Abrettenus, and enriched with possessions, first by Antony, and then by Cæsar.
