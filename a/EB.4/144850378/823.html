NEMOURS

article · 149 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEMOURS, a town of the Isle of France in the Gâtinois, with the title of a duchy. It is seated on the river Loing, in E. long. 2. 45. N. lat. 48. 15.
