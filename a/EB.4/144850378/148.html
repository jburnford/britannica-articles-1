MESOLOGARITHMS

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MESOLOGARITHMS, according to Kepler, are the logarithms of the co-sines and co-tangents; the former of which were called by Lord Napier anti-logarithms, and the latter differentials.
