MERIDIAN

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MERIDIAN, in geography, a great circle supposed to be drawn through any part on the surface of the earth, and the two poles; and to which the sun is always perpendicular at noon.—In astronomy, the circle is supposed to be in the heavens, and exactly perpendicular to the terrestrial one.
