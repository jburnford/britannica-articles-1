MULLERAS, a town of Germany, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and marquise of Brandenburg, seated 38 miles south of Berlin, upon a canal which joins the Oder and the Spree. This canal is 15 miles in length, 10 yards in breadth, and seven feet in depth. It was eight years in making; and since that time the cities of Hamburg and Breslaw have carried on great trade by water. E. Long. 14. 50. N. Lat. 52. 21.
