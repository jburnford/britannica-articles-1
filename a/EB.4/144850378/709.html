NAGERA, or NAGARA, a town of Spain, in Old
Caſtile, and the territory of Rioja, with the title of a
duchy and fortreſs; famous for a battle fought in its
neighbourhood in 1369. It is ſituated in a fertile
country, on a brook called Naſerilla. W. Long. 2.20.
