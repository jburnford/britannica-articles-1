OPHIDIUM

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OPHIDIUM, a genus of fishes belonging to the order of apodes. The head is somewhat naked; the teeth are in the jaws, palate, and fauces; there are seven rays in the gill-membranes; and the body is shaped like a sword. There are two species.
