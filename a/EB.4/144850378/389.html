MOMBAZA

MOMBAZA, or MONBAZA, a town of Africa, in an island of the same name, with a castle and a fort; seated on the eastern coast, opposite to the country of Mombaza in Zanguebar, 70 miles south of Melinda, and subject to Portugal. E. Long. 48. 0. N. Lat. 44. 0.
