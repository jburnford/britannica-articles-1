NORTH-Foreland, a cape or promontory of Kent, in the isle of Thanet, four miles east of Margate. Between this and the South-Foreland are the Downs, through which all ships pass that are bound to or from the

North the west. E. Long. 1. 25. N. Lat. 51. 25.
