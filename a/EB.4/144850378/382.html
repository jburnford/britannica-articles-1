MOLOSSES

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOLOSSSES, in commerce, the thick fluid matter remaining after sugar is made, like syrup. In Holland molosses are much used in the manufacture of tobacco, and by the poor people for sugar. A brandy is also distilled from them, but it is said to be unwholesome.
