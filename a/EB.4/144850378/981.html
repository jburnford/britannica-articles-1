NOEOMAGUS LEXUVIORUM

NOEOMAGUS LEXUVIORUM, (Ptol.); thought
to be the Civitas Lexuviorum of the lower age. Now
Lisieux, a city in Normandy.—Another of the Trica-
flini; a town of Gallia Narbonensis; thought to be
S. Pol de Trois Châteaux, six miles to the west of Ny-
ons in Dauphiné.
