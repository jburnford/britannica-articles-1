Letters of ADVOCATION

sub_entry · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Letters of ADVOCATION, in Scots law, the decree or warrant of the court of Session upon cognisance of the facts set forth in the bill, drawn up in the form of a summons, and passing under the signet, discharging the inferior judge and all others from further procedure in the cause, and advocating it to itself*.
