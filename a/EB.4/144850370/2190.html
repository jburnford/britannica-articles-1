ANTEUS

article · 356 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTEUS, in fabulous history, a giant of Libya, son of Neptune and Terra. Designing to build a temple to his father, of mens sculls, he slew all he met; but Hercules fighting him, and perceiving the assistance he received from his mother (for by a touch of the earth he refreshed himself when weary), lifted him up from the earth, and squeezed him to death.
