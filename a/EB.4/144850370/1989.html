ANDREW (St), the apostle, born at Bethsaida in Galilee, brother to Simon Peter: he was a zealous preacher of the gospel in several countries; and sealed it with his blood at Patre, a city of Achaia, suffering martyrdom with great heroism, A. D. 69.
