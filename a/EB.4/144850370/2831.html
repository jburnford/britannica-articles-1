ARMADA

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMADA, a Spanish term, signifying a fleet of men of war, as armadilla does a squadron.—The armada, which attempted to invade England in the time of Queen Elizabeth, is famous in history.
