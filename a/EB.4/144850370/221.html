ABSORBENT Vessels, a name given promiscuously to the lacteal vessels, lymphatics, and inhalent arteries†. Naturalists speak of the like absorbents in plants, the fibrous or hairy roots of which are as a kind of vasa absorbentia, which attract and imbibe the nutritious juices from the earth. See PLANTS, no 21, 50.
