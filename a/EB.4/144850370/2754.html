ARGENTEUIL, a town of the isle of France, seated on the river Seine, five miles north-west of Paris. It is a very beautiful place, with fine vineyards. On the environs

* Origin de l'imprimerie de Paris, p. 214.

Argentiere environs are quarries of the plaster of Paris. In the Benedictine priory they pretend to have the seamless coat of Christ. E. Long. 2. 28. N. Lat. 48. 52.
