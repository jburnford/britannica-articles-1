AIR

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AIR, in mythology, was adored by the heathens under the names of Jupiter and Juno; the former representing the superior and finer part of the atmosphere, and the latter the inferior and grosser part. The augurs also drew presages from the clouds, thunder, lightning, &c.
