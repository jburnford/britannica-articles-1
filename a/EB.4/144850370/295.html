ACANTHOS

ACANTHOS, a town of Egypt, near Memphis, (Pliny); now Bisalta. Also a maritime town of Macedonia, to the west of mount Athos, a colony of Andrians, (Thucydides, Ptolemy); now Eriss; near which was shewn Xerxes's ditch, of seven stadia, in order to separate mount Athos from the continent, and convey his ships, without doubling Athos, into the Singitic Bay. Acanthos, is also a town of Epirus.
