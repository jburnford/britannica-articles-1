ARBON

ARBON, an ancient town in Switzerland, on the south banks of the lake Constance, in Thurgau. It has a castle built by the Romans, and is under the ju-

sisdiction of the bishop of Constance. In the time of war, the Swiss have a right to put in a garrison. The Popish and Protestant religions are equally tolerated in this town. E. Long. 9. 30. N. Lat. 4. 38.
