ACMONIA

article · 344 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACMONIA, and AGMONIA, in Peutinger's map, a town of Phrygia Major, now in ruins. The inhabitants are called Acmonenses by Cicero, and the city Gi-vitas Acmonensis. Also a city of Dacia, (Ptolemy,) on the Danube, near the ruins of Trajan's bridge, built by Severus, and called Severicum; distant 12 German miles from Temeswar, to the south-east.
