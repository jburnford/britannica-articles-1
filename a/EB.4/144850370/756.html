ADOSCULATION

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADOSCULATION, a term used by Dr Grew, to imply a kind of impregnation, without intromission; and in this manner he supposes the impregnation of plants is effected by the falling of the farina fecundans on the pistil.
