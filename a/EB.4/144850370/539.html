ACRISIUS

article · 346 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACRISIUS, king of Argos, (fab. hist.) being told by the oracle he that should be killed by his grandchild, shut up his only daughter Danae in a brazen tower: but Jupiter coming down in a golden shower, begot Perseus upon her: after Perseus had slain the Gorgons, he carried Medusa's head to Argos; which Acrisius seeing, was turned into a statue.
