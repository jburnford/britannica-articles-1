AMPHIBRACHYS

article · 169 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPHIBRACHYS, in ancient poetry, the name of a foot consisting of three syllables, whereof that in the middle is long, and the other two short; such is the word [ἀβίρε].
