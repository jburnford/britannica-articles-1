ABACUS

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABACUS is also used by modern writers for a table of numbers ready cast up, to expedite the operations of arithmetic. In this sense we have Abaci of addition, of multiplication, of division; an Abacus logisticus; Abacus of squares, of cubes, &c.
