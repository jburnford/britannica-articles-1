ALEGAMBE

article · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALEGAMBE (Philip), a celebrated Jesuit, born at Brussels in 1592, distinguished himself by publishing a Bibliotheque of the writers of his order, and died at Rome in 1652.
