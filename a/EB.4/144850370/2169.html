ANSÆ

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANSÆ, in astronomy, implies the parts of Saturn's ring projecting beyond the disk of the planet.—The word is Latin, and properly signifies handles; these parts of the ring appearing like handles to the body of the planet.
