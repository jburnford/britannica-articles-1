ADMINISTRATOR

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADMINISTRATOR, is sometimes used for the president of a province; for a person appointed to receive, manage, and distribute, the revenues of an hospital or religious house; for a prince who enjoys the revenues of a secularized bishopric; and for the regent of a kingdom, during a minority of the prince, or a vacancy of the throne.
