AMASIA

AMASIA, an ancient town of Turkey, in Natolia, remarkable for the birth of Strabo, the geographer. It is the residence of a baslaw, and gives its name to the province it stands in, where there are the best wines and the best fruits in Natolia. It is seated near the river Cafalneck. E. Long. 36. 10. N. Lat. 39. 33.
