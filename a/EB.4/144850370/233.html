ABSTRACT Numbers

sub_entry · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSTRACT Numbers, are assemblages of units, considered in themselves, without denoting any particular and determined particulars. Thus 6 is an abstract number, when not applied to any thing; but, if we say 6 feet, 6 becomes a concrete number. See the article NUMBER.
