ACTÆON

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACTÆON, in fabulous history, the son of Aristæus and Autonoe; a great hunter. He was turned by Diana into a stag, for looking on her while bathing; and died by his own dogs.
