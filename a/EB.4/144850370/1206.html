ALBA HELVIORUM

ALBA HELVIORUM, or Albaugusta, in ancient geography, (Pliny, Ptolemy); afterwards called Vivarium; now Viviers, in the south-east of Languedoc, on the Rhone. In the lower age the inhabitants were called Albenes, and their city, Civitas Albenfium, in the Notitia Galliae. E. long. 4. 45. lat. 44. 50.
