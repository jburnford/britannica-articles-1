AMBERG

article · 309 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMBERG, a city of Germany, the capital of the palatinate of Bavaria, with a good castle, ramparts, bastions, and deep ditches. It is seated near the confines of Franconia, on the river Wils. It drives a great trade in iron and other metals, found in the neighbouring mountains. E. Long. 12. 4. N. Lat. 29. 46.
