ACHAÏC PRESBYTERI

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHAÏC PRESBYTERI, or the Presbyters of Achæia, were those who were present at the martyrdom of St Andrew the apostle, A. D. 59; and are said to have written an epistle in relation to it. Bellarmin, and several other eminent writers in the church of Rome, allow it to be genuine; while Du Pin, and some others, expressly reject it.
