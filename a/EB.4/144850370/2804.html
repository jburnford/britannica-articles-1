ARISTÆUS

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARISTÆUS, son of Apollo and Cyrene, whom, for the many services he had rendered to mankind by his knowledge of all profitable arts, the gods placed amongst the stars; so that he is the Aquarius in the zodiac. The resemblance of his history to that of Moses has been curiously discussed by Huettius.
