ACATHISTUS

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACATHISTUS, the name of a solemn hymn anciently sung in the Greek church, on the Saturday of the fifth week of Lent, in honour of the Virgin, for having thrice delivered Constantinople from the invasions of the barbarous nations.
