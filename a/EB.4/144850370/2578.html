AQUÆ AUGUSTÆ, (Ptolemy); AQUÆ TARBELLICÆ, (Antonine); AQUENSIS CIVITAS, in the Notitia. Now Aqqs, or Dax, a town in Gascony, on the river Adour, famous for its baths. W. Long. 1° 40. Lat. 43. 56.
