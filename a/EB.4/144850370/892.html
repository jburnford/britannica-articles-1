AERUGO

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AERUGO, in natural history, properly signifies the rust of copper, whether natural or artificial. The former is found about copper mines, and the latter made by corroding copper plates with acids†. See Materis Medica, 74.
