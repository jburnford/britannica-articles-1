ARCHIPELAGO

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCHIPELAGO, in geography, a general term signifying a sea interrupted with islands; it is however more especially applied to that lying between Europe and Asia, which contains the islands anciently called Cyclades and Sporades. See these two words.
