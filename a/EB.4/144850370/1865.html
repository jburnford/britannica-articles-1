ANAGRAMMATIST

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANAGRAMMATIST, a maker or composer of anagrams. Thomas Billon, a provincial, was a celebrated anagrammatist, and retained by Lewis XIII. with a pension of 1200 livres, in quality of anagrammatist to the king.
