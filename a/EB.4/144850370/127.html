ABESTA

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABESTA, the name of one of the sacred books of the Persian magi, which they ascribe to their great founder Zoroaster. The abesta is a commentary on two others of their religious books called Zend and Pazend; the three together including the whole system of the Ignicoid, or worshippers of fire.
