ACCUMULATION

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCUMULATION, in a general sense, the act of heaping or amassing things together. Among lawyers, it is used in speaking of the concurrence of several titles to the same thing, or of several circumstances to the same proof.
