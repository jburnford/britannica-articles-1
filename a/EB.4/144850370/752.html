ADORATION

article · 345 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADORATION is also used among Roman writers for a high species of applause given to persons, who had spoken or performed well in public. We meet with adoration paid to orators, actors, musicians, &c. The method of expressing it was, by rising, putting both hands to their mouth, and then returning them towards the person intended to be honoured.
