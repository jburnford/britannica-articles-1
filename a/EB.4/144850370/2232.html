ANTHEM

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTHEM, a church-song performed in cathedral-service by choristers, who sing alternately. It was used to denote both psalms and hymns, when performed in this manner. But, at present, anthem is used in a more confined sense, being applied to certain passages taken out of the scriptures, and adapted to a particular solemnity.
