ARVIL-SUPPER

article · 342 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARVIL-SUPPER, a feast or entertainment made at funerals, in the north part of England. Arvil-bread is the bread delivered to the poor at funeral solemnities; and arvil, arval, arsal, are used for the burial or funeral rites; as,

Come, bring my jarkin, Tibb, I'll to the arvil,
Yon man's dea scuy feoun, it makes me marvil.

Yorksh. Dial. p. 38.
