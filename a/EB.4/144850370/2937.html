ARROW

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARROW, a missile weapon of offence, slender, pointed, and barbed, to be cast or shot with a bow*. Arrows are also called shafsts.

ARROW-MAKERS are called fletchers; and were formerly, as well as bowyers, persons of great consequence in the commonwealth.
