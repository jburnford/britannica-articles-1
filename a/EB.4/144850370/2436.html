APION

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APION, a famous grammarian, born in Egypt,
was a professor at Rome in the reign of Tiberius. He
had all the arrogance of a mere pedant, and amused
himself with difficult and insignificant inquiries. One
of his principal works was his Antiquities of E-
gypt.
