ALCALA DE GUADÉIRA

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCALA DE GUADÉIRA, a small town of Spain, in Andalusia, upon the river Guadeira. Here are a abundance of springs, from whence they convey water to Seville by an aqueduct. W. long. 6. 16. N. lat. 37. 15.
