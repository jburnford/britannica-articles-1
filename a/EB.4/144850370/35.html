ABANGA, the name of the fruit of the palm-tree, in the island of St Thomas. The tree is the Palma Aÿ

Insula S. Thomas, C. B. The fruit is like a lemon externally; and the inhabitants give three or four of the kernels two or three times a-day as a restorative*.
