Cross-references: LAW

ACCESSORY, in law, is the subject acquired by accession: Or, in crimes, it signifies the person by whose assistance, advice, or command, the crime was committed; in which sense, it is the same with accomplice, act and part, &c. See LAW, Part III. no clxxxiv. 4, 45, 50.
