ALBISOLA

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALBISOLA, a small town belonging to the republic of Genoa: here is a porcelain manufacture, and several country-houses of the Genoese nobility. It was bombarded in 1745, by the English. E. Long. 8. 20. N. Lat. 44. 15.
