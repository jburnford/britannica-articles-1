AMPELUSIA

AMPELUSIA, a promontory of Mauritania Tingitana, called Cottes by the natives, which is of the same signification, (Mela); with a town of the same name, (Pliny); not far from the river Lixus, near the straits of Gibraltar: now Cape Spartel. W. Long. 6. 30. Lat. 36. 0.
