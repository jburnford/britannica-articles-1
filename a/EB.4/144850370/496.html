ACOMINATUS

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACOMINATUS (Nicetas), was secretary to Alexis Comnenus and to Isaac Angelus successively: he wrote an history from the death of Alexis Comnenus in 1118, where Zonaras ended his, to the year 1203, which has undergone many impressions, and is much applauded by the best critics.
