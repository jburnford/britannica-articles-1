AMANICÆ PYLÆ

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMANICÆ PYLÆ, (Ptolemy); AMANIDES PYLÆ, (Strabo); AMANI PORTÆ, (Pliny): straits or defiles in mount Amanus, through which Darius entered Cilicia; at a greater distance from the sea than the Pylæ Ciliciz or Syriz, through which Alexander passed.
