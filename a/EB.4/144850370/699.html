ADIGE

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADIGE, a river in Italy, which taking its rise south of the lake Glace among the Alps, runs south by Trent, then east by Verona in the territory of Venice, and falls into the gulph of Venice, north of the mouth of the Po.
