ASINIUS

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASINIUS (Pollio), consul and Roman orator, distinguished himself under Augustus by his exploits and his literary works. He is frequently mentioned with praises by Horace and Virgil, and is said to have collected the first library at Rome. He died at Frecati, at 80 years of age.
