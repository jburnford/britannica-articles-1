AGGREGATE

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGGREGATE, in a general sense, denotes the sum of several things added together, or the collection of them into one whole. Thus, a house is an aggregate of stones, wood, mortar, &c. It differs from a mixed or compound, inasmuch as the union in these last is more intimate than between the parts of an aggregate.
