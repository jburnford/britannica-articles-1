APULIA

article · 465 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APULIA, now PUGLIA, a territory of Italy, bordering on the Adriatic, and extending from the river Frento to Tarentum in length, and from the Adriatic to the Lucani in breadth. Apuli the people, (Horrace), divided into the Apulia Daunia, now called Puglia Pinna, or the Capitanata; and into the Apulia Peucetia, now Terra di Bari, (Pliny, Ptolemy). Apulia abounded in sheep, which yielded the finest wool, (Martial). It is now the east side of the kingdom of Naples.
