AGRIPPINA

AGRIPPINA, daughter of Germanicus, sister of Caligula, and mother of Nero; a woman of wit, but excessively lewd: she was thrice married, the last time to Claudius her own uncle, whom she poisoned to make way for Nero her son. Nero afterward caused her to be murdered in her chamber, when she bid the executioner stab her first in the belly, that had brought forth such a monster.
