ANTRIM, the capital town of the county of Antrim, in Ireland, seated at the north end of the lake Lough-Neagh. It is but a poor place, 13 miles west of Carrickfergus. W. Long. 6. 26. N. Lat. 54. 45. It sends two members to parliament.
