AMPHILOCHIA

AMPHILOCHIA, the territory of the city of Argos in Acarnania; Amphilocheum, (Thucydides); called Amphilochei (from the people,) in the lower age, (Stephanus.) A town also of Spain, in Galicia, built by Teucer, and denominated from Amphilocheus one of his companions, (Strabo:) now Orense. W. long. 8. 20. lat. 42. 36.
