AOSTA

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AOSTA, a territory of Piedmont, with the title of a duchy. It is a valley 30 miles in length, and extends from the pass of St Martin's, near the frontiers of Yvree, to St Bernard. It abounds in pastures, and all sorts of fruits; the capital is of the same name.
