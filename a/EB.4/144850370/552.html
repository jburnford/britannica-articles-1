ACRONICAL, ACHRONICAL, or ACHRONICAL

sub_entry · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACRONICAL, ACHRONICAL, or ACHRONICAL, in astronomy, is a term applied to the rising of a star, when the sun is set in the evening; but has been promiscuously used to express a star's rising at sunset, or setting at sunrise.
