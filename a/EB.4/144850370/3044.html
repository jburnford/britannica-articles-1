ASCYRUM

ASCYRUM, PETERS-WORT; a genus of the polyanthria order, belonging to the polydelphia class of plants. Of this genus there are three species; but they have

no property worthy of notice, and therefore are never cultivated but in botanic gardens for the sake of variety.
