ANATHOTH

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANATHOTH, a hamlet of Palestine, very near Jerusalem, (Josephus;) about three miles and a half to the north; the ruins of which are still to be seen. It was the birth-place of the prophet Jeremiah, and one of the Levitical towns in the tribe of Benjamin.
