ACOMA

ACOMA, a town of North America, in New Mexico, seated on a hill, with a good castle. To go into the town, you must walk up 50 steps cut out of the rock. It is the capital of that province, and was taken by the Spaniards in 1599. W. Long. 104. 15. Lat. 35. 0.
