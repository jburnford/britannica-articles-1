AARON HARISCHON

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AARON HARISCHON, a learned rabbi and caraité* in the 13th century, wrote an Hebrew grammar, printed at Constantinople 1581; probably the same with Aaron the caraité, who wrote a commentary on the five books of Moses, which is in MS. in the French king's library.
