AACH, a little town in Germany, in the circle of Suabia, near the source of the river Aach, and almost equally distant from the Danube and the lake Constance. It belongs to the house of Austria; and is twelve miles north-east of Schaffhausen, and twenty-five northwest of Constance. E. Long. 9. o. Lat. 47. 55.
