ABYLA

ABYLA, (Ptolemy, Mela); one of Hercules's pillars, on the African side, called by the Spaniards Sierra de las Monas, over against Calpe in Spain, the other pillar; supposed to have been formerly joined, but separated by Hercules, and thus to have given entrance to the sea now called the Mediterranean: the limits of the labours of Hercules, (Pliny.)
