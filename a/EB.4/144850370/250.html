ABUTILON

Cross-references: SIDA

ABUTILON, in botany, the trivial name of several species of the fida. See SIDA. Abutilon is also a synonyme of the melochia tomentosa and melochia depressa, two American plants of the monadelphia pentandria class. It is likewise a synonyme of the lavatora, malva, and hibiscus.
