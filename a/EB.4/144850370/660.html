ADDITIONS

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADDITIONS, in heraldry, some things added to a coat of arms, as marks of honour; and therefore directly opposite to abatement. Among additions we reckon BORDURE, QUARTER, CANTON, GYRON, PILE, &c. See these articles.
