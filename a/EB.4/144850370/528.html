ACRATH, (anc. geog.) a place in Mauritania Tingitana, (Ptolemy;) now supposed to be Velez de Gomara; a fortified town in the kingdom of Fez, with a citadel and commodious harbour on the Mediterranean, scarce a mile distant from Penon de Velez, a Spanish fort. W. long. 5. lat. 34. 45.
