AMBROSIA

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMBROSIA, in heathen antiquity, denotes the solid food of the gods, in contradistinction from the drink, which was called nectar. It had the appellation ambrosia, (composed of the particle a. and bro, immortal,) as being supposed to render those immortal who fed on it.
