ABSORBENT Medicines

sub_entry · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSORBENT Medicines, testaceous powders, as chalk, crab-eyes, &c. which are taken inwardly for drying up or absorbing any acrid or redundant humours in the stomach or intestines. They are likewise applied outwardly to ulcers or sores with the same intention*.
