ABEL-MEHOLA

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL-MEHOLA, the country of the prophet Elisha, situate in Manasseh, on this side Jordan, between the valley of Jezreel and the village Bethmaela in the plains of Jordan, where the Midianites were defeated by Gideon. Judges, vii. 22.
