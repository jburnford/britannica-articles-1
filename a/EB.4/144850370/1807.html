AMSBURY

article · 387 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMSBURY, or AMBERSBURY, a town in Wiltshire, lying in W. Long. 1. 20. N. Lat. 51. 29. It is the Pagus Ambri, famous for a monastery built by one Ambrus, and afterwards for a nunnery of noble women. There is a nobleman's seat here, built by Inigo Jones, to which new works were added under the direction of Lord Burlington. It is 80 miles west of London, and six miles north of Salisbury.
