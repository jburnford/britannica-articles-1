AGRIPPINA COLONIA VEIORUM

AGRIPPINA COLONIA VEIORUM, (Pliny, Suetonius); now Cologne: so called from Agrippina, the daughter of Germanicus, and mother of Nero, who had a colony sent thither at her request by the emperor Claudius, to honour the place of her birth. See COLOGNE.
