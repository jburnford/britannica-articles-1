ARISTIDES

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARISTIDES, a painter contemporary with Apelles, flourished at Thebes about the 122d Olympiad. He is said to have been the first who attempted to delineate the passions of the mind in colours. His Bacchus was so excellent a piece, as to become proverbial.
