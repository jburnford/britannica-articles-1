APARTISMENUS

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APARTISMENUS, in the ancient poetry, an appellation given to a verse, which comprehended an entire sense or sentence in itself. This is sometimes also written, apartemenus, i. e. suspended, as not needing any following verse.
