ACROTHYMION

ACROTHYMION, from ακρο extreme, and θυμω thyme. A sort of wart described by Celsus, as hard, rough, with a narrow basis, and broad top; the top is of the colour of thyme, it easily splits and bleeds. This tumour is also called thymus.
