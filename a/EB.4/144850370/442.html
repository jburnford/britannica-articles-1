ACHERON

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHERON, a river of Epirus. The poets feigned it to have been the son of Ceres, whom she hid in hell for fear of the Titans, and turned into a river, over which souls departed were ferried in their way to Elysiun.
