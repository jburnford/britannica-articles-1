AROPH

article · 366 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AROPH, a contraction of aroma philosophorum; a name given to saffron.

AROPH Paracelsi; a name given to a kind of chemical flowers, probably of the same nature with the Eus Veneris, elegantly prepared by sublimation from equal quantities of lapis hæmatitis and sal ammoniac.

AROPH is also a term used frequently by Paracelsus in a sense synonymous with lithontriptic.
