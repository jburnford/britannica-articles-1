ARCHILOCHIAN

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCHILOCHIAN, a term in poetry, applied to a sort of verses, of which Archilochus was the inventor, consisting of seven feet, the four first whereof are ordinarily dactyls, though sometimes spondees, the three last trochees; as in Horace,

Solutor ceteris hyenis, grata vice veris & Pavoni.
