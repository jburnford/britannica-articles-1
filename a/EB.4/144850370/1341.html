ALEIUS CAMPUS

ALEIUS CAMPUS, in ancient geography, (Homer, Strabo, Pliny); a plain in Cilicia, on this side the river Pyramus, near the mountain Chimera, famous for Bellerophon's wandering and perishing there, after being thrown off Pegasus; which is the reason of the appellation.
