ASCOLI

ASCOLI, formerly Asculum Apulum, a pretty large and populous town of Italy, in the marche of Ancona, and territory of the church; it is a bishop's see, and seated on a mountain, at the bottom of which runs the river Fronto. E. Long. 15. 20. N. Lat. 42. 47.
