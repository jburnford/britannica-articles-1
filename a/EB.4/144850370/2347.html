ANTLIA

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTLIA, an ancient machine, supposed to be the same with our pump. Hence the phrase, in antliam condemnari, according to the critics, denotes a kind of punishment, whereby criminals were condemned to drain ponds, ditches, or the like.
