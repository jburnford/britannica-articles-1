ADOPTIANI

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADOPTIANI, in church-history, a sect of ancient heretics, followers of Felix of Urgel, and Elipand of Toledo, who, towards the end of the eighth century, advanced the notion, that Jesus Christ, in his human nature, is the son of God, not by nature, but by adoption.
