ANDRON

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANDRON, in Grecian antiquity, denotes the apartment in houses designed for the use of men; in which sense it stands opposed to Gymnaceum.—The Greeks also gave their dining-rooms the title of andron, because the women had no admittance to feasts with the men.
