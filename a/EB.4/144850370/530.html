ACRE

article · 371 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACRE, in the Mogul's dominions, the same with lack, and signifies the sum of 100,000 rupees; the rupee is of the value of the French crown of 3 livres, or 30 sols of Holland; an 100 lacks of rupees make a couron in Indostan, or 10,000,000 rupees: the pound Sterling is about eight rupees; according to which proportion, a lack of rupees amounts to 12,500 pounds Sterling.
