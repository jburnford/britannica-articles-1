ALCAID, ALCAIDE, OR ALCALDE

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCAID, ALCAIDE, OR ALCALDE, in the polity of the Moors, Spaniards, and Portuguese, a magistrate, or officer of justice, answering nearly to the French provost, and the British justice of peace.—The alcaid among the Moors is vested with supreme jurisdiction, both in civil and criminal cases.
