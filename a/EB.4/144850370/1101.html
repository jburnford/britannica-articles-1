AJALON

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AJALON, (anc. geogr.) a town of the tribe of Dan, one of the Levitical. Another in the tribe of Benjamin, in whose valley Joshua commanded the moon to stand still, being then in her decrease, and consequently to be seen at the same time with the sun.
