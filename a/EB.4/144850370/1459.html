ALLOBROGES

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALLOBROGES, (Inscriptions, Livy, Velleius, Florus); from Allobrox, (Horace): a people of Gallia Narbonensis, situated between the rivers Isara and Rhodanus, and the Lacus Lemanus; commended by Cicero for their fidelity, discommended by Horace on account of their fondness for novelty.
