AMBURBIUM

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMBURBIUM, in Roman antiquity, a procession made by the Romans round the city and pomerium, in which they led a victim, and afterwards sacrificed it, in order to avert some calamity that threatened the city.
