ARGEMONE

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGEMONE, PRICKLY POPPY; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the polyandria class of plants. Of this genus there are three species, which are common in many parts of the West Indies, and called by the Spaniards the devil's fig; but they are of no use, and have very little beauty.
