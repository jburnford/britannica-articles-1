ARACHIS

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARACHIS, in botany, a genus of the diadelphia decandria class. There is only one species, viz. the hypogaea, a native of America. The calix is divided into two parts; and the capsule or pod is cylindrical, and contains two seeds.
