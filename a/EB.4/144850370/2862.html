ARMOZA

ARMOZA, or HARMOZIA, a town in Carmania, at the mouth of the Anamis, which falls into the Persian Gulf, (Arrian); Armuza, (Ptolemy); and from this the neighbouring island, and a small kingdom, take the modern name of Ormus. E. Long. 56. 17. N. Lat. 27. 20.
