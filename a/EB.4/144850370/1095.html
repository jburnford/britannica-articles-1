AHICCYATLI

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AHICCYATLI, in zoology, the Indian name of a serpent resembling the rattle-snake, only it wants the rattles. It is as fatal in the effect of its poison as any known species of serpent.
