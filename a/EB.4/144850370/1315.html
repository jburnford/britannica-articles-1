ALDEBARAN

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALDEBARAN, in astronomy, a star of the first magnitude, called in English the bull's-eye, as making the eye of the constellation Taurus. Its longitude is 6 deg. 32 min. 9 sec. of Gemini, and its latitude 5 deg. 29 min. 40 sec. south.
