ADJUNCT

article · 346 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADJUNCT, in music; a word which is employed to denominate the connection or relation between the principal mode and the modes of its two-fifths, which, from the intervals that constitute the relation between them and it, are called its adjuncts.

ADJUNCT is also used to signify a colleague, or some person associated with another as an assistant.
