ARMENTIERS

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMENTIERS, a small handsome town of the Netherlands, in the county of Flanders, and district of Ypres. It was taken by Lewis XIV. in 1667, who dismantled it; and it now belongs to the French. It is seated on the river Lis. E. Long. 3. 3. N. Lat. 50. 40.
