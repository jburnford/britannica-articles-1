ABSINTHIUM, in botany, the trivial name of the common wormwood or artemisia. It is also a synonyme of the tanacetum incanum, the senecio incanum, the anthemis montana, the achilleagyptiaca, and of the parthenium hysterophorus. See ARTEMISIA, &c. and MATERIA MEDICA, no 64, 65, 66.
