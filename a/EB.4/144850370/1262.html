ALBUM Pretoris

sub_entry · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALBUM Pretoris, that wherein the formulae of all actions, and the names of such judges as the prator had chosen to decide causes, were written.

The high-priest entered the chief transactions of each year into an album, or table, which was hung up in his house for the public use.
