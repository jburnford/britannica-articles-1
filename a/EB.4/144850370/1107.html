AJAZZO

AJAZZO, a sea-port town of Natolia, in the province of Caramania, anciently Silefia, seated on the coast of the Mediterranean, 30 miles north of Antioch, and 50 west of Aleppo, where the city of Issus anciently stood, and near which Alexander fought his second battle with Darius. Long. 33. 10. Lat. 37. 0.
