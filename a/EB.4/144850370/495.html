ACOMAC, the name of a county in Virginia. It is on the eastern side of Chesapeake bay, on a slip of land, by the Virginians called the eastern shore. It is

Aconitum, a large county, and yet contains but one parish, the inhabitants being but thin at present, and scattered up and down in distinct settlements.
