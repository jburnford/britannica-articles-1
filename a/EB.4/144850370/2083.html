ANGUSTICLAVIA

article · 153 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGUSTICLAVIA, in Roman antiquity, a tunic embroidered with little purple studs. It was worn by the Roman knights, as the laticlavia was by the senators.
