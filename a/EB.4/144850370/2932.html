ARRIERE Fee or Fief

sub_entry · 359 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARRIERE Fee or Fief, is a fee dependant on a superior one. These fees commenced, when the dukes and counts, rendering their governments hereditary in their families, distributed to their officers parts of the royal domains which they found in their respective provinces, and even permitted those officers to gratify the soldiers under them in the same manner.
