ADIAPHORISTS

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADIAPHORISTS, in church-history, a name importing lukewarmness, given, in the 16th century, to the moderate Lutherans, who embraced the opinions of Melancthon, whose disposition was vastly more pacific than that of Luther.
