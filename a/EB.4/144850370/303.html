ACARON

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACARON, or ACCARON, a town of Palestine, called Edron in scripture. It was the boundary of the Philistines to the north; stood at some distance from the sea, near Bethithemeth; and was famous for the idol of Baalzebub.
