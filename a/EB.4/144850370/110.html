ABEL-SATTIM

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL-SATTIM, or SATTIM, a town in the plains of Moab, to the N. E. of the Dead Sea, not far from Jordan, where the Israelites committed fornication with the daughters of Moab: So called, probably, from the great number of sittim-trees there.
