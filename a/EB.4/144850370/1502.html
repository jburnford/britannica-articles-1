ALMUCANTARS-STAFF

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALMUCANTARS-STAFF, is an instrument usually made of pear-tree or box, having an arch of fifteen degrees; used to take observations of the sun, about the time of its rising and setting; in order to find the amplitude, and consequently the variation of the compass.
