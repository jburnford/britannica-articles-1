AHUYS

AHUYS, a town of Sweden. It is small, but very strong by its situation, and has a good port. It is in the principality of Gothland, in the territory of Blekingy, near the Baltic sea, about 18 miles from Christianstadt. E. Long. 14. 10. N. Lat. 56. 20.

AI, (anc. geog.) a town in Judea, to the north of Jericho, called Aiz by Josephus, and the inhabitants Ainats.
