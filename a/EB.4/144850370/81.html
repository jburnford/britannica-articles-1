ABBREVIATURE

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABBREVIATURE, or ABBREVIATION, properly signifies the substitution of a syllable or letter for a whole word: thus, M. stands for municipus, a handful; and Cong. for congius, a gallon.
