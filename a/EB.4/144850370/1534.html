ALSFELD

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALSFELD, a town of Germany, in the landgraviate of Hesse Cassel, ten miles north-west of Marburg, and thirty-five south of Hesse Cassel. It is an ancient town, and well-built; and the inhabitants were the first of this country who embraced the Reformation. E. Long. 9. 5. N. Lat. 50. 40.
