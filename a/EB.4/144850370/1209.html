ALBA POMPEIA, (anc. geog.) on the river Ceba,

now Ceva, in Liguria, the birth-place of the emperor Pertinax; a colony either established at first by Pompey, or re-established by him after having been before settled by Scipio. The inhabitants were called Alpenfes Pompeiani. At this day the town is simply called Alba, without any epithet.
