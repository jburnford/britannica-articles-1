ALTEA

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALTEA, a sea-port town of Valencia, in Spain. It was taken in 1705, in favour of the archduke Charles; but lost, after the battle of Almanza. W. long. 0. 15. N. lat. 46. 34.
