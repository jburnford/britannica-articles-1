ALKAHEST

article · 405 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALKAHEST, or ALCAHEST, in chemistry, an universal menstruum capable of resolving all bodies into their first principles. Van Helmont pretended he was possessed of such a menstruum; but, however credulous people might be imposed on in his days, the notion is now become as ridiculous as the philosopher's stone, the perpetuum mobile, &c.—It is likewise used by some authors for all fixed salts volatilized.
