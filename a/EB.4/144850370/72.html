ABBEY-HOLM

ABBEY-HOLM, a town in Cumberland, so called from an abbey built there by David king of Scots. It stands on an arm of the sea, and had a market on Saturdays; it has now a fair on October 29, for horses and horned cattle: it is sixteen miles south-west of Carlisle. W. Long. 2. 38. Lat. 54. 45.
