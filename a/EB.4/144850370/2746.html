ARGEII

article · 163 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGEII, a people of Greece, so called by the Greeks, from Argis, or Argos; Argivi, by the Romans: Homer seems to call the Greeks in general Argeti, as also Achaei.
