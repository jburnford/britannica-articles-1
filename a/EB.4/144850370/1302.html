ALCOHOL

ALCOHOL, or ALKOOL, in chemistry, spirit of wine highly rectified*. It is also used for any highly rectified spirit.—Alcohol is extremely light and inflammable: It is a strong antiseptic, and therefore employed to preserve animal substances.
