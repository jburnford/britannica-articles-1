ACHELOUS, a rivulet of Thessaly, running by the city Larnia, (Strabo, Paufanias.) Also a river of Peloponnesus, running by Dymæ, in Achaia, (Strabo); and by mount Lycæus in Arcadia, (Paufanias.)
