ABEN MELLER

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEN MELLER, a learned rabbin, who wrote a commentary on the Old Testament in Hebrew, intitled The Perfection of Beauty. This rabbin generally follows the grammatical sense and the opinions of Kimchi. The best edition is that of Holland.
