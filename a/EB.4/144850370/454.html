AHITOPHEL

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AHITOPHEL, a counsellor, who, revolting from David king of Israel, sided with his rebellious son Absalom; to whom he gave crafty advice, which not being complied with, he hanged himself.
