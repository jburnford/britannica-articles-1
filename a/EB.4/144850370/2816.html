ARISTOGITON

article · 169 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARISTOGITON, a famous Athenian, who, with Armodius, killed Hipparchus, tyrant of Athens, about 513 years before the Christian æra. The Athenians erected a statue to him.
