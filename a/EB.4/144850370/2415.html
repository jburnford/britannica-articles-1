APHELION

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APHELION, or APHELIUM, in astronomy, is that point in any planet's orbit, in which it is furthest distant from the sun, being that end of the greater axis of the elliptical orbit of the planet most remote from the focus where the sun is.
