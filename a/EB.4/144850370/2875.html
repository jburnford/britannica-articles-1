ARNAUD DE MEYREUIL

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARNAUD DE MEYREUIL, or MEREUIL, a poet of Provence, who lived at the beginning of the 13th century. He wrote a book intitled Las recastenas de sa comtesse; and a collection of poems and sonnets. He died in the year 1220. Petrarch mentions him in his Triumph of Love.
