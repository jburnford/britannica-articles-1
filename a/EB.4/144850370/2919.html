ARREARS

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARREARS, the remainder of a sum due, or money remaining in the hands of an accountant. It likewise signifies the money due for rent, wages, &c. or what remains unpaid of pensions, taxes, &c.
