AMERICUS VESPUCIUS

AMERICUS VESPUCIUS, by the encouragement of Emanuel king of Portugal, made, in 1497, some additional discoveries of that part of the world, which from him is called America, tho' first discovered by Columbus, a Genesee, in 1492, as narrated in the preceding article.
