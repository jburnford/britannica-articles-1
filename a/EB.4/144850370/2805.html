ARISTANDER, a famous soothsayer under Alexander the Great, over whom he gained a wonder-

Aristarchus ful influence by the good success of his art. He had already had the same employment at the court of king Philip; and it was he who explained better than his brethren the dream that this prince had after having married Olympias.
