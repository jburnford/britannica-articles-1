ABSURD

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSURD, an epithet applied to any thing that opposes the human apprehension, and contradicts a manifest truth. Thus, it would be absurd to say that six and six make only 10, or to deny that twice six make 12. When the term absurd is applied to actions, it has the same import as ridiculous.
