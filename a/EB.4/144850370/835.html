ÆGINA

ÆGINA, in fabulous history, the daughter of Æsopus, king of Bæotia, was beloved by Jupiter, who debauched her in the similitude of a lambent flame, and then carried her from Epidaurus to a desert island called Oenope, which afterwards obtained her own name.
