AERSHOT

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AERSHOT, a town in the Netherlands, in the duchy of Brabant, and capital of the duchy of Aershot. It is seated on the river Demur, ten miles east of Malines or Mechlin, and eight north of Louvain. E. Lon. 5. 4. N. lat. 51. 15.
