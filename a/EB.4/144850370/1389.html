ALGOL

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALGOL, a fixed star of the third magnitude, called Medusa's Head, in the constellation Perseus; its longitude is 21°, 50', 42", of Taurus, and its latitude 23°, 23', 47", north; according to Flamstead's catalogue.
