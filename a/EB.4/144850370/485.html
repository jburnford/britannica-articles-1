ACLOWA

article · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACLOWA, in botany, a barbarous name of a species of colutea; see COLUTEA. It is used by the natives of Guinea to cure the itch: They rub it on the body, as we do unguents.
