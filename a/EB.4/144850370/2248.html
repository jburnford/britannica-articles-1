ANTHOXANTHUM

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTHOXANTHUM, or VERNAL-GRASS; a genus of the digynia order, belonging to the diandria class of plants, is one of the earliest spring grasses, and is extremely common in our fertile pastures. The delightful smell of new-mown hay is chiefly from this plant. Cows, horses, sheep, and goats eat it.
