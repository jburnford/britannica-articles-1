ACRA JAPYGIA

ACRA JAPYGIA, (Pliny); Salentina, (Ptolemy); now Capo di San Maria di Leuca; a promontory in the kingdom of Naples, to the south-east of Otranto, where formerly was a town, now lying in ruins, on the Ionian sea, over against the Montes Acroceraunii of Epirus.
