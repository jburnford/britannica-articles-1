ALOPECIA

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MEDICINE

ALOPECIA, in medicine, signifies a falling off of the hair, occasioned either by want of nourishment, or by a bad state of the humours. It is also used by Galen for a change in the colour of the hair.—See MEDICINE, n° 853.
