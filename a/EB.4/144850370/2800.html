ARIPO

ARIPO, a strong town of Asia, on the western coast of the island of Ceylon, at the mouth of the river Sarunda. It belongs to the Dutch; and to the east of it is a bank, where they fish for pearls. E. Long. 80. 25. N. Lat. 8. 42.
