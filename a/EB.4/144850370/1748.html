AMORÆANS

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMORÆANS, a sect or order of gematic doctors, or commentators on the Jerusalem Talmud. The Amoræans succeeded the Mithnic doctors. They subsisted 250 years; and were succeeded by the Seburæans.
