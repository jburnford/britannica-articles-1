ANKLE

article · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANKLE, in anatomy, the joint which joins the foot to the leg.—We have an account of the menes being regularly evacuated at an ulcer of the ankle, Edin. Med. Obs. vol. iii. art. 29.
