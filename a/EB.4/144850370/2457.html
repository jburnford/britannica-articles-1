APODOSIS

article · 459 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APODOSIS, in rhetoric, makes the third part of a complete exordium, being properly the application, or restriction of the protaxis. The apodosis is the same with what is otherwise called axiosis; and stands opposed to protaxis: e. gr. protaxis, all branches of history are necessary for a student; catastasis, so that, without these, he can never make any considerable figure; apodosis, but literary history is of a more especial use, which recommends it, &c.
