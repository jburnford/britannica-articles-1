Abstemious ABSTEMIOUS, ABSTEMIS, and ABSTEMIUM, (Strabo, Mela, Ptolemy;) islands in the Adriatic, in the gulf of Carnero; so called from Abystus, Medea's brother, there slain. They are either one island, or two, separated by a narrow channel, and joined by a bridge; and are now called Cherso and Osoro.
