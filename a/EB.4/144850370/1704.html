AMHAR, or AMHARA, a province of Abyssinia, said to extend forty leagues from east to west. It is considered as the most noble in the whole empire, both on account of its being the usual residence of the Abyssinian monarchs, and having a particular dialect different from all the rest, which, by reason of the emperors being brought up in this province, is become the language of the court and of the politer people. Here is the famed rock Ambar-geshen, where the young monarchs were formerly confined. See AMBA.
