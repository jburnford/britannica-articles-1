ANGIOSPERMIA

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGIOSPERMIA, in the Linnæan system of botany, the second order in the class Didynamia. It consists of those plants, of that class, whose seeds are inclosed in a pericarpium. In this order the stigma is generally obtuse. These are the persnati of Tournefort.
