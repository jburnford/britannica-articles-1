ANDRAPODISMUS

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANDRAPODISMUS, in ancient writers, the selling of persons for slaves. Hence also andrapodifles, a dealer in slaves, more particularly a kidnapper, who steals men or children to sell them; a crime for which the Thessalians were noted.
