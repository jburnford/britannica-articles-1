AMICABLE Numbers

AMICABLE Numbers, such as are mutually equal to the sum of one another's aliquot parts. Thus the num-

bers 284 and 220 are amicable numbers: for the aliquot parts 1, 2, 4, 5, 10, 11, 20, 22, 44, 55, 110, of 220, are together equal to the other number 284; and the aliquot parts 1, 2, 4, 71, 142, of 284, are together equal to 220.
