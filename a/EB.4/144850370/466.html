ACHYR

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHYR, a strong town and castle of the Ukarin, subject to the Russians since 1667. It stands on the river Uorsklo near the frontiers of Russia, 127 miles W. of Kiow, Long. 36. o. Lat. 49. 32.
