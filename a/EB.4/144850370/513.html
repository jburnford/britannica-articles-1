ACQS

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACQS, a town at the foot of the Pyrenean mountains, in the government of Foix in France. It takes its name from the hot waters in these parts. E. long. 1. 40. lat. 43. 0.
