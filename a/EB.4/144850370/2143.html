ANNUAL RENT

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANNUAL RENT, in Scots law, an yearly profit due by a debtor in a sum of money to a creditor for the use of it.

Right of ANNUAL RENT, in Scots law, the original method of burdening lands with an yearly payment for the loan of money, before the taking of interest for money was allowed by statute.
