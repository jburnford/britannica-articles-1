ANNUAL, in a general sense, an appellation given to whatever returns every year, or is always performed within that space of time.

ANNUAL Motion of the Earth. See ASTRONOMY.
ANNUAL Leaves, are such leaves as come up afresh in the spring, and perish in winter. These stand opposed to Ever-greens.
