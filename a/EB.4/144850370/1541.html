ALSUM

ALSUM, a city of ancient Etruria, occupying (according to Cluverius) the spot on which Pala now stands. We are told by Dionysius Halicarnassensis, that Alsum was built by the Aborigines, long before the Tyrsenians invaded Italy. In this case it must have been founded not long after the dispersion in the days of Peleg. Its founder is said to have been one Alesus, Alesus, or Alisa; whom some conjecture to have been Alisah, or Eliha, the son of Javan, mentioned in Scripture.
