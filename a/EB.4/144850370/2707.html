ARDAMON

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARDAMON, or ARDAMA, in antiquity, a vessel of water placed at the door of a person deceased, till the time of burial, as a token that the family was in mourning, and to serve to sprinkle and purify persons as they came out of the house.
