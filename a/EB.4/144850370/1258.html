ALBUM

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALBUM, in antiquity, a kind of white table, or register, wherein the names of certain magistrates, public transactions, &c. were entered. Of these there were various sorts; as the album decurionum, album senatorum, album judicium, album pretoris, &c.
