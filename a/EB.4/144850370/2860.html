ARMOUR

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMOUR denotes such habiliments as serve to defend the body from wounds, especially of darts, a sword, a lance, &c. A complete suit of armour formerly consisted of a helmet, a shield, a cuirass, a coat of mail, a gauntlet, &c. all now laid aside.
