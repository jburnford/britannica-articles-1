ASCLEPIA

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASCLEPIA, a festival of Esculapius the god of physic, observed particularly at Epidaurus, where it was attended with a contest between the poets and musicians, whence it was likewise called ἵππος ἄστυ, the sacred contention.
