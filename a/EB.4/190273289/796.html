RAMATH-MIZPE, (Joshua xiii.); Ramoth-Maspha, (Septuagint, Vulgate); Ramoth in Gilead, or Ramath Galaad, (Seventy); a town in that tract of Gilead called Maspha, or Mizpe, one of the cities of refuge.
