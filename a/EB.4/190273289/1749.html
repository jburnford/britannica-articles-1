SCHEDULE

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCHEDULE, a roll of paper or parchment, annexed to a will, lease, or other deed; containing an inventory of goods, or some other matter omitted in the body of the deed.—The word is a diminutive of the Latin scheda, or Greek σχέδα, a leaf or piece of paper.
