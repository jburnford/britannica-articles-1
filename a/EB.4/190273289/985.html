REGARDER

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REGARDER, an ancient officer of the king's forest, sworn to make the regard of the forest every year; that is, to take a view of its limits, to inquire into all offences and defaults committed by the foresters within the forest, and to observe whether all the other officers executed their respective duties.
