RULE

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RULE, or Ruler, an instrument of wood or metal, with several lines delineated on it; of great use in practical mensuration. When a ruler has the lines of chords, tangents, lines, &c. it is called a plane scale.
