PRESCIENCE

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRESCIENCE, in theology, prevision, or foreknowledge; that knowledge which God has of things to come.—The doctrine of predestination is founded on the prescience of God, and on the supposition of all futurity's being present to him.
