PRODUCT, in arithmetic and geometry, the factum of two or more numbers, or lines, &c. into one another: thus 5 \times 4 = 20 the product required.

Profanation PROFANATION, the acting disrespectfully to sacred things.

Projectiles. PROFANE, a term used in opposition to holy; and in general is applied to all persons who have not the sacred character, and to things which do not belong to the service of religion.
