RHEADEÆ (rhæa, Linnaeus's name after, Discoideæ, for the red poppy), the name of the 27th order in Linnaeus's fragments of a natural method, consisting of poppy and a few genera which resemble it in habit and structure. See BOTANY, p. 1309.
