SCALPING

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCALPING, in military history, a barbarous custom, in practice among the Indian warriors, of taking off the tops of the scalps of the enemies skulls with their hair. They preserve them as trophies of their victories, and are rewarded by their chiefs according to the number of scalps they bring in.
