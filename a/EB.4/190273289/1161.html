REUTLINGEN

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REUTLINGEN, a handsome, free, and imperial town of Germany, in the circle of Suabia and duchy of Wirtemberg; seated in a plain on the river Eschz near the Neckar, adorned with handsome public buildings, and has a well frequented college. E. Long. 9. 20. N. Lat. 48. 31.
