SCUDERY (George de), a French writer of eminence in his day, descended from an ancient family of Apt in Provence, was born at Havre-de-Grace in 1603. The greatest part of his life was spent in writing; and his works consist of prose, dramatic pieces, and poetry of all kinds; but which are now little read. He died in 1667.
