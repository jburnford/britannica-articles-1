Head QUARTERS of an Army

sub_entry · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Head QUARTERS of an Army, the place where the commander in chief has his quarters. The quarters of generals of horse are, if possible, in villages behind the right and left wings, and the generals of foot are of-

ten in the same place: but the commander in chief should be near the centre of the army.
