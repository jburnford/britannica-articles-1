PORTO-SEGURO

article · 471 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORTO-SEGARO, a government of South America, on the eastern coast of Brasil; bounded on the north by the government of Rio-dos-Hilios, on the east by the North Sea, on the south by the government of Spiritu-Santo, and on the west by the Tupicks. It is a very fertile country, and the capital town is of the same name. It is built on the top of a rock, at the mouth of a river, on the coast of the North Sea, and is inhabited by Portuguese. W. Long. 35. 50. S. Lat. 17. 0.
