ROSIERS-AUX-SALINES

ROSIERS-AUX-SALINES, a town of France, in
Lorraine, and in the bailiwick of Nancy, famous
for its salt-works. The works that king Stanislaus
made here are much admired. It is seated on the river
Muert, in E. Long. 6. 27. N. Lat. 48. 32.
