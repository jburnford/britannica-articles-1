RECEPTACULUM CHYLI

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RECEPTACULUM CHYLI, or Pecquet's reservoir, the reservoir or receptacle for the chyle, situated in the left side of the upper vertebra of the loins, under the aorta and the vessels of the left kidney.
