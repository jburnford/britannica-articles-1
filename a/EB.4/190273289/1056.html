RENCOUNTER

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RENCOUNTER, in single combats, is used by way of contradistinction to duel.—When two persons fall out, and fight on the spot, without having premeditated the combat, it is called a reencounter.
