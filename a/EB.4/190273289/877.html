REALITY

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REALITY, in the schools, a diminutive of res, "thing," first used by the Scotists, to denote a thing which may exist of itself; or which has a full and absolute being of itself, and is not considered as a part of any other.
