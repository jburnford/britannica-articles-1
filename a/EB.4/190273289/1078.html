REPLEVY

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REPLEVY, in law, is a tenant's bringing a writ of replevin, or replegiari facias, where his goods are taken by distress for rent; which must be done within five days after the distress, otherwise at the five days and they are to be appraised and sold.

This

This word is also used for bailing a person, as in the case of a homine repeliendo.
