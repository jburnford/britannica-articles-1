PORT-Grayon

sub_entry · 366 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORT-Grayon, a pencil-case, which is usually four or five inches long, and contrived so as that the pencil may slide up and down. Its inside is round, and its outside is sometimes filed into eight sides or faces, on which are drawn the sector-lines; sometimes it is made round both without side and within, and has its length divided into inches and parts of inches.
