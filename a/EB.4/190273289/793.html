RAM's Head

sub_entry · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RAM's Head, in a ship, is a great block belonging to the fore and main halliards. It has three shives in it, in which the halliards are put; and in a hole at the end are reeved the ties.
