SALUZZO

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SALUZZO, called by the French Saluces, a town and castle of Italy, in Piedmont, and capital of a marquise of the same name, with a bishop's see. It is situated on an eminence at the foot of the Alps near the river Po, in E. Long. 18. 27. N. Lat. 44. 35. It is subject to the king of Sardinia.
