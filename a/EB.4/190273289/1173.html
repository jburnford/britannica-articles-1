REVEREND, a title of respect given to eccle-
siastics.—The religious abroad are called reverend

fathers; and abbesses, prioresses, &c. reverend mothers. Reverend,
With us, bishops are right reverend; and archbishops
most reverend. In France, their bishops, archbishops,
and abbots, are all alike most reverend. Reverend.
