POSTERIOR

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POSTERIOR, a term of relation, implying something behind, or that comes after another. In which sense it is used in opposition to prior and anterior.

The back and hips are the posterior parts of man. Aristotle has given prior and posterior analytics. A date is posterior to another, when it is later or fresher.
