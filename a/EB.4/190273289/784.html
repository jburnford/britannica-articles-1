Marine RAINBOW, the Sea-bew

sub_entry · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Marine RAINBOW, the Sea-bew, is a phenomenon sometimes observed in a much agitated sea, when the wind, sweeping part of the tops of the waves, carries them aloft; so that the rays of the sun are refracted, &c. as in a common shower.
