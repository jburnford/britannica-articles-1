SCARLET, a beautiful bright red colour.

In painting in water-colours, minium mixed with a little vermilion produces a good scarlet; but if a flower in a print is to be painted a scarlet colour, the lights as well as the shades should be covered with minium, and the shaded parts finished with carmine, which will produce an admirable scarlet.
