POINT

article · 359 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POINT, in the manufactories, is a general term, used for all kinds of laces wrought with the needle; such are the point le Venice, point de France, point de Genoa, &c. which are distinguished by the particular economy and arrangement of their points. Point is sometimes used for lace woven with bobbins; as English point, point de Malines, point d'Elavre, &c.
