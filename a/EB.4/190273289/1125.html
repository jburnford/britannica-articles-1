RESSORT

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RESSORT, a French word, sometimes used by English authors to signify the jurisdiction of a court, and particularly one from which there is no appeal.—Thus it is said, that the house of lords judge en dernier ressort, or in the last resort.
