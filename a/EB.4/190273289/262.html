PRECIPITATE

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRECIPATE, in chemistry, a substance which, having been dissolved in a proper menstruum, is again separated from its solvent, and thrown down to the bottom of the vessel, by pouring some other liquor upon it.
