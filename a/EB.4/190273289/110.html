PONTON-CARRIAGE

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PONTON-CARRIAGE, is made with two wheels only, and two long side-pieces, whose fore ends are supported by a limber; and serves to carry the pontoon, boards, cross timbers, anchors, and every other thing necessary for making a bridge.
