RAVENSBURG

RAVENSBURG, a free and imperial town of Germany in Algov in the circle of Swabia. It is well built, and the public structures are handsome. The inhabitants are partly Protestants and partly Papists. It is seated on the river Chens, in E. Long. 9. 46. N. Lat. 47. 44.
