PURFLEW

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PURFLEW, a term in heraldry, expressing ermins, peans, or any of the furs, when they compose a bordure round a coat of arms: thus they say, he beareth gules a bordure, purflew, vairy; meaning, that the bordure is vairy.
