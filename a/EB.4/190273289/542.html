PULSE

PULSE, in botany, a term applied to all those grains or seeds which are gathered with the hand; in contradistinction to corn, &c. which are reaped, or mowed: or, It is the seed of the leguminous kind of plants, as beans, vetches, &c. but is by some used for artichokes, asparagus, &c.
