RHINE, Upper Circle of

sub_entry · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHINE, Upper Circle of, consisted of the landgraviates of Alsace and Hesse, comprehending the Wetteraw: but now only Hesse can be accounted a part of Germany, Alsace being long ago united to France.
