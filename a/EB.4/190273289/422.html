PROPONTIS

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PROPONTIS, or SEA of Marmora, divides Europe from Asia; having to Bosphorus on the north-east, by which it has a communication with the Euxine Sea; and the Hellespont on the south-west, by which it communicates with the Archipelago. It is 120 miles long, and in some places upwards of 40 miles broad.
