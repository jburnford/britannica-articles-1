REGGIO, an ancient and considerable town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in the Farther Calabria, with an archbishop's see, and a woollen manufactory. It is seated in a country which produces plenty of dates, on the freight or pharos of Messina, and is a large populous place. E. Long. 16. 3. N. Lat. 38. 6.
