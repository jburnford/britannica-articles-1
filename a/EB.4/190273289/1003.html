REGISTER Ships

sub_entry · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REGISTER Ships, in commerce, are vessels which obtain a permission either from the king of Spain, or the council of the Indies, to traffic in the ports of the Spanish West Indies; which are thus called, from their being registered before they set sail from Cadiz for Buenos Ayres.
