SANICULA, SANICLE, or Self heal; a genus of the digynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. There is but one species, viz. the europæa, found in many parts both of Scotland and England. This plant was long celebrated for its healing virtues, both internally and externally; but is now totally disregarded.
