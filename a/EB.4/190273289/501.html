PSAMMETICUS

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PSAMMETICUS, or PSAMMITICHUS, a renowned conqueror, who, subduing 14 other petty kings of Egypt, became the founder of the kingdom of Egypt, about 670 B. C. He is memorable likewise for taking the city of Azot, after a siege of 29 years; and for discovering the sources of the river Nile.
