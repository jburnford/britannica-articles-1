RAMMER of a Gun

sub_entry · 160 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RAMMER of a Gun, the Gun-stick; a rod used in charging of a gun, to drive home the powder, as also the shot, and the wad, which keeps the shot from rolling out.
