REFLECTION of the Rays of Light

sub_entry · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REFLECTION of the Rays of Light, in catoptrics, is their return, after approaching so near the surface of bodies as to be thereby repelled or driven backwards. For the causes of reflection, see OPTICS, p. 5544.
