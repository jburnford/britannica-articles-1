PRIMUM MOBILE

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRIMUM MOBILE, in the Ptolemaic astronomy, the ninth or highest sphere of the heavens, whose centre is that of the world, and in comparison of which the earth is but a point. This they will have to contain all other spheres within it, and to give motion to them, turning itself, and all them, quite round in 24 hours.
