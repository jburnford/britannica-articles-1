POLYHYMNIA

article · 341 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLYHYMNIA, in the pagan mythology, one of the nine muses, thus named from the Greek words πολυ, "much;" and μνησις, "memory." She presided over history, or rather rhetoric; and is represented with a crown of pearls and a white robe; her right hand in action as if haranguing, and holding in her left a caduceus or sceptre to show her power.
