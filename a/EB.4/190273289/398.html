PROLIFER FLOS

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PROLIFER FLOS; (proles, an "offspring;" and flos, to "bear;") a prolific flower, or flower which from its own substance produces another; a singular degree of luxuriance, to which full flowers are chiefly incident.
