SCEPTICISM

article · 349 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCEPTICISM, the doctrines and opinions of the sceptics: whose distinguishing tenet was, that all things are uncertain and incomprehensible; and that the mind is never to assent to any thing, but to remain in perpetual doubt and suspense. This doctrine was also called pyrrhonism, from the name of its author. See the article PYRRHONISM and SCEPTICS.
