PYRENEAN MOUNTAINS

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PYRENEAN MOUNTAINS, are some of the most

celebrated in Europe. They divide France from Spain, and extend from the Mediterranean sea to the ocean; being above 200 miles in length, and are overspread with high trees. There are but five passages over them, which are all extremely difficult.
