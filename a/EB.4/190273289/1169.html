REVENUE

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REVENUE, in hunting, a fleshy lump formed chiefly
by a cluster of whitish worms on the head of the deer,
supposed to occasion the casting of their horns by
gnawing them at the root.
