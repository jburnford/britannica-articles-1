RE-EXCHANGE

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RE-EXCHANGE, in commerce, a second payment of the price of exchange, or rather the price of a new exchange due upon a bill of exchange that comes to be protested and to be refunded the bearer by the drawer or indorser.
