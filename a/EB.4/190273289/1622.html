SANTAREN, a handsome town of Portugal, in Estremadura, seated on a mountain near the river Ta-

jo, in a country very fertile in wheat, wine, and oil. They get in their harvest here two months after they have sown their corn. It was taken from the Moors in 1447. W. Long. 7. 45. N. Lat. 39. 12.
