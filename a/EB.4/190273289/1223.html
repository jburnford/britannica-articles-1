RHYTHM

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHYTHM, in music, the variety in the movement, as to the quickness or slowness, length or shortness of the notes. Or it may be defined more generally, the proportion which the parts of the motion have to each other.
