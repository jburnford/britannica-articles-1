PUNCH

PUNCH, an instrument of iron or steel, used in several arts, for the piercing or stamping holes in plates of metal, &c. being so contrived as not only to perforate, but to cut out and take away the piece. The punch is a principal instrument of the metal-button makers, shoe-makers, &c.
