SACKS OF EARTH

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SACKS OF EARTH, in fortification, are canvas bags filled with earth. They are used in making retracements in haste, to place on parapets, or the head of the breaches, &c. to repair them, when beaten down.
