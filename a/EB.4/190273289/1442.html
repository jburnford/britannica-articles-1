RUPIN, or RAPIN, a town of Germany, in the
marquisate of Brandenburg, and capital of a duchy of
the same name; it is divided into the Old and the New.
The Old was nothing but an ancient castle, very well
furnished, the present king of Prussia, before his father's
death, residing there. New Rupin is seated on a lake,
and become a considerable place of trade, with a ma-
nufactory of cloth. It is also noted for brewers. E.
Long. 13. 23. N. Lat. 53. 0.
