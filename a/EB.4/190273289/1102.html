Mental RESERVATION

sub_entry · 439 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Mental RESERVATION, a proposition, which strictly taken, and according to the natural import of the terms, is false; but, if qualified by something concealed in the mind, becomes true.

Mental reservations are the great refuge of religious hypocrites, who use them to accommodate their consciences with their interests: the Jesuits are zealous advocates for mental reservations; yet are they real lies, as including an intention to deceive.
