SCANDALUM MAGNATUM

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCANDALUM MAGNATUM, in law, is a defamatory speech or writing to the injury of a person of dignity; for which a writ that bears the same name is granted for the recovery of damages.
