BALTIC SEA

BALTIC SEA, a great gulph between Germany and Poland; from which run several other gulphs, particularly those of Bothnia, Finland, Livonia, and Dantzick. It is remarkable that this sea neither ebbs nor flows, and there is always a current sets through the found into the ocean. It is generally frozen over three or four months in the year. Yellow amber is found in plenty on this coast.
