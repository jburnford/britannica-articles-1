AUBAGNE

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUBAGNE, a town of Provence in France, situated on the river Veaune, on the road from Marseilles to Toulon. The flats sometimes hold their sessions at this place. E. Long. 5. 52. N. Lat. 43. 17.
