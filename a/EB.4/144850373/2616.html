BRIZA

BRIZA, QUAKING-GRASS; a genus of the digynia order, belonging to the triandria class of plants. There are five species of briza; two of which are natives of Britain, viz. the media or middle quaking-grass, and the minor or small quaking-grass. They grow in pasture grounds.
