AUGUSTA Emerita, a town of Lusitania on the river Anas, the capital of the province; a colony of the Emeriti, or such soldiers as had served out their legal time, were men of experience, or had received marks of favour. The colony was founded by Augustus; and is now called Merida, a city of Spain, in Estremadura, on the river Guadiana. See MERIDAN.
