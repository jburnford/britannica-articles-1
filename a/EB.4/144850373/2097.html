BONDAGE by the Forelock

sub_entry · 525 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONDAGE by the Forelock, or Bondagium per anteriorem capitis, was when a freeman renounced his liberty,

Bondman liberty, and became slave to some great man: which was done by the ceremony of cutting off a lock of hair from the forehead, and delivering it to his lord; denoting that he was to be maintained by him for the future. Such a bondman, if he reclaimed his liberty, or were fugitive from his master, might be drawn again to his servitude by the nose; whence the origin of the popular menace to pull a man by the nose.
