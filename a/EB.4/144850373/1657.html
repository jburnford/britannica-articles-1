BILL

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BILL, in trade, both wholesale and retail, as also among workmen, signifies an account of merchandizes or goods delivered to a person, or of work done for one.

Bank-Bill, a private instrument whereby private persons become intitled to a part in the bank-stock.
