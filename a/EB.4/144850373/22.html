ATEGUA

ATEGUA, or ATREGUA, an ancient town of Spain, placed by some in the road from Antiquara, now Antequera, to Hispalis, or Seville; by others near Alcala Real; which last is the more probable situation, because the flumen salum, now the Salado, was in its neighbourhood. Now Tebala Vieja, or Teivela.
