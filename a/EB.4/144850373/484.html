BAFFETAS

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAFFETAS, or BASTAS, a cloth made of coarse white cotton-thread, which comes from the East Indies. That of Surat is the best.

BAFFIN'S BAY, a gulph of North America, running north-east from Cape Farewell in West Greenland, from 60° to 80° of north latitude.
