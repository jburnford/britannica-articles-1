BRESCIA

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRESCIA, a strong and handsome town of Italy, with a bishop's see and a good citadel. It is the capital of Bresciano in the territory of Venice, and is seated in an agreeable plain on the river Garza, in E. Long. 10. 5. N. Lat. 45. 31.
