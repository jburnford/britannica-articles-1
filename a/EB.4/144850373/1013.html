BASTION

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASTION, in the modern fortification, a huge mass of earth, faced usually with sods, sometimes with brick, and rarely with stone, standing out from a rampart whereof it is a principal part, and is what, in the ancient fortification, was called a bulwark.
