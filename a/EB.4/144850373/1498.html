BERWICK (North), a town of Scotland, in the county of Lothian, seated on the Frith of Forth. It was near this place that general Cope was defeated by the rebels in 1745, and made his escape to Berwick-upon-Tweed. W. Long. 2. 29. N. Lat. 5. 56.
