BOUTE'

sub_entry · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOUTE', in the menage. A horse is called boute', when his legs are in a straight line from the knee to the coronet: short-jointed horses are apt to be boute', and on the other hand long-jointed horses are not.
