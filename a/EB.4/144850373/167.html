AVELLANE

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVELLANE, in heraldry, a cross, the quarters of which somewhat resemble a filbert-nut. Sylvanus Morgan says, that it is the cross which ensigns the mound of authority, or the sovereign's globe.
