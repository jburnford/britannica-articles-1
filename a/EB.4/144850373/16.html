ATARNEA

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATARNEA, an ancient town of Mysia, situated between Adramyttium and Pitane, remarkable for the marriage of Aristotle with the sister or concubine of the tyrant Hermias; also for the dotage of that philosopher.
