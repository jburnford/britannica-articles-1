AZOTH

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AZOTH, in ancient chemistry, the first matter of metals, or the mercury of a metal; more particularly that which they call the mercury of philosophers, which they pretended to draw from all sorts of metallic bodies.
