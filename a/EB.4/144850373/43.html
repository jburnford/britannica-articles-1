ATHENOPOLIS

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATHENOPOLIS, a town of the Massilienses, an ancient nation of Gaul. It is conjectured by Harduin to be the same with Telo Martius, now Toulon; by others to be the same with Antipolis or Antibes.
