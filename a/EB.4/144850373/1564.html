BEZOAR-mineral. See PHARMACY, n° 794.—From this preparation, mixed with other metals various ways, arise other compound bezoardits; as, the bezoardicum solare, lunale, martiale, joviale, &c. or bezoar with gold, with silver, with iron, with tin, &c. in disrepute. The last differs very little from the antibollicum Poterii, an exploded medicine.
