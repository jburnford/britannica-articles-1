BISIGNANO, a town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in the hither Calabria. It hath a strong fort, a bishop's see, and the title of a principality. It is seated on a mountain near the river Boccona, in E. Long. 16. 40. N. Lat. 39. 37.
