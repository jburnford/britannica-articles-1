BRANCHIÆ

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRANCHIÆ, or GILLS, in the anatomy of fishes, the parts corresponding to the lungs of land-animals. All fishes, except the cetaceous ones, and the pteromyxum, which have lungs, are furnished with these organs of respiration. See Comparative Anatomy, no 152.
