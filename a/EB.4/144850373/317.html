AUTOLITHOTOMUS

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUTOLITHOTOMUS, he who cuts himself for the stone. Of this we have a very extraordinary instance given by Reifelius, in the Ephemerides of the Academy Naturæ Curioforum, dec. 1. an. 3. obs. 192.
