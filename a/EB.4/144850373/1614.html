BIDIS

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIDIS, (anc. geog.), a small city of Sicily not far from Syracuse, whose ruins are still to be seen in the territory of Syracuse, about fifteen miles to the south-west, with a church called S. Giovanni di Bidini.
