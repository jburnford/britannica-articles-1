BALE-Goods

sub_entry · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALE-Goods, among the English merchants, are all such as are imported or exported in bales; but the French give that name to certain hard-wares, and other sorts of merchandize, which come to Paris, and are commonly made by bad workmen, of indifferent materials.
