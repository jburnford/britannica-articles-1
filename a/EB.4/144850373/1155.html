BEAN-Flour

sub_entry · 155 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEAN-Flour, called by the Romans lomentum, was of some repute among the ancient ladies as a cosmetic, wherewith to smooth the skin, and take away wrinkles.
