BUNIAS

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUNIAS, a genus of the filiquosa order, belonging to the tetradymania class of plants, for which there is no English name. There are three species; all of them annual plants, but none of them possessed of any remarkable property.
