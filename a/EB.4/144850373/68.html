ATOCK, the capital of a province of the same name in the dominions of the Great Mogul. It is seated on a point of land where two large rivers meet, and is one of the best fortresses the Mogul has; but formerly nobody was permitted to enter it without a passport from the Mogul himself. E. Long. 72. 10. N. Lat. 32. 20.
