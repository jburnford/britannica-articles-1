BULLIALDUS

BULLIALDUS (Ismael), an eminent astronomer, was born at Laon in the isle of France in 1605. He travelled in his youth for the sake of improvement; and afterwards published several works, among which are, 1. De natura lucis. 2. Philolaus. 3. Astronomia philolaica, opus novum, in quo motus planetarum per novam et veram hypothesim demonstrantur. 4. Astronomia philolaica fundamenta clarius explicata et asserta adversus Zethi Wardi impugnationem. He also wrote a piece or two upon Geometry and Arithmetic.
