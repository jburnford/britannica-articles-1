BAR

Cross-references: APPUI

BAR, in the menage, the highest part of that place of a horse's mouth situated between the grinders and tusks, so that the part of the mouth which lies under and at the side of the bars retains the name of the gum. A horse with sensible bars has a fine light mouth, with an even and firm appui. See APPUI.
