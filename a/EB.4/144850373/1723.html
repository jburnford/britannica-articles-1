BIROTA

article · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIROTA, or BIROTUM, in Roman antiquity, a kind of vehicle, so denominated from its moving upon two wheels. It carried about 200 pound weight, and was drawn by three mules.
