BURROWS

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURROWS, holes in a warren, serving as a covert for rabbits, &c. A coney's coming out of her burrow is called bolting. To catch coney's, they sometimes lay purse-nets over the burrows, then put in a terrier close muzzled, which making the creature bolt, she is caught in the net.
