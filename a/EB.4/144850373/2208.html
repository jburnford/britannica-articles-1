BOSCAGE

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOSCAGE, in a law sense, is that food which trees yield to cattle; as mast, &c. But Manwood says, to be quit of boscage is to be discharged of paying any duty for windfall wood in the forest.
