BRIHUEGA

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRIHUEGA, a town of Spain, in New Castile, where general Stanhope with the English army were taken prisoners, after they had separated themselves from that commanded by count Staremberg. It is seated at the foot of the mountain Tajuna, 43 miles north-east of Madrid. W. Long. 3. 20. N. Lat. 41. 0.
