BONA Notabilia

sub_entry · 369 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONA Notabilia, are such goods as a person dying has in another diocese than that wherein he dies, amounting to the value of 5 l. at least; in which case the will of the deceased must be proved, or administration granted in the court of the archbishop of the province, unless by composition, or custom, any dioceses are authorized to do it, when rated at a greater sum.
