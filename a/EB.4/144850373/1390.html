Nuptial BENEDICTION

sub_entry · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Nuptial BENEDICTION, the external ceremony performed by the priest in the office of matrimony. This is also called sacerdotal and matrimonial benediction, by the Greeks προδοσία and προδοσία. The nuptial benediction is not essential to, but the confirmation of, a marriage in the civil law.
