AVENES

article · 467 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVENES, a small but strong town in French Flanders, in the county of Hainault, seated on the river Thespes. It contains about 2500 inhabitants; but the houses are wretchedly built, and the streets irregular. It was fortified by M. Vauban in a strong regular manner. About this place are a prodigious number of white stones proper for building, and used by sculptors for statues: they are known by the name of Stones of Avenes.

Avenues. E. Long. 3. 40. N. Lat. 50. 10.
