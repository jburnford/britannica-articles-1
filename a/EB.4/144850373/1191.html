BEAUJOLOIS

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEAUJOLOIS, a district of France, bounded on the south by Lionnois proper, on the west by Forez, on the north by Burgundy, and on the west by the principality of Dombes. It is 25 miles in length, and 20 in breadth: Ville Franche is the capital town.
