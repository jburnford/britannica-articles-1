BRESCICATE

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRESCICATE, in commerce, a kind of bays, of which there is some trade carried on with the negroes, between the river Gambia and Sierra Leona. The best forts for that purpose are the blue and the red.
