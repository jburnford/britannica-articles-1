BOWSPRIT

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOWSPRIT, or BOLTSPRIT, a kind of mast, resting slopewise on the head of the main stern, and having its lower end fastened to the partuers of the fore-mast, and farther supported by the fore-stay. It carries the sprit-sail, sprit-top-sail, and jak-staff; and its length is usually the same with that of the fore-mast.
