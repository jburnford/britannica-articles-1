BUDZIAC TARTARY

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUDZIAC TARTARY, lies on the rivers Neister, Bog, and Nieper; having Poland and Russia on the north, Little Tartary on the east, the Black sea on the south, and Bessarabia on the west. The chief town is Oczakow. It is subject to Turkey.
