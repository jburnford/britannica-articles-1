BARATHRUM

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARATHRUM, in antiquity, a deep dark pit at Athens, into which condemned persons were cast headlong. It had sharp spikes at the top, that no man might escape out; and others at the bottom, to pierce and torment such as were cast in.
