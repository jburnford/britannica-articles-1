BRIVES-LE-GALLARD

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRIVES-LE-GALLARD, a town of France, in lower Limousin. It stands in a fruitful plain, opposite to an island formed by the river Coreze, over which there are two handsome bridges. E. Long. 1. 45. N. Lat. 45. 15.
