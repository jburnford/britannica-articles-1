BANDALEER

article · 375 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BANDALEER, or BANDELEER, in military affairs, a large leathern belt, thrown over the right shoulder, and hanging under the left arm; worn by the ancient musqueteers, both for the sustaining of their fire-arms, and for the carriage of their musquet-charges, which being put up in little wooden cases, coated with leather, were hung, to the number of twelve, to each bandaleer.
