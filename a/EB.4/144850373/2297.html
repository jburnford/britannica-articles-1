BOUTANT, or ARCH-BOUTANT

sub_entry · 136 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOUTANT, or ARCH-BOUTANT, in architecture, an arch, or part of an arch, abutting against the reins of a vault to prevent its giving way.
