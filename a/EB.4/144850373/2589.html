BRINGING-IN A HORSE

article · 164 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRINGING-IN A HORSE, in the menage, the same as to say, keep down the nose of a horse that boars and tosses his nose in the wind: this is done by means of a branch.
