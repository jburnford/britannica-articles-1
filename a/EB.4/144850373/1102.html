BAUTZEN

BAUTZEN, or BUDISSEN, a considerable town of Germany and capital of Upper Lusatia, subject to the elector of Saxony, with a strong citadel. The Protestants as well as Papists have here the free exercise of their religion. E. Long. 14. 42. N. Lat. 51. 10.
