BEDWIN-MAGNA

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEDWIN-MAGNA, a village five miles south of Hungerford in Berkshire in England. It has neither market nor fair; but is a borough by prescription, and sends two members to parliament. It is said to have been a considerable place in the time of the Saxons, and that the traces of its fortifications are still extant.
