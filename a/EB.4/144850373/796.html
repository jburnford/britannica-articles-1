BARBLES

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBLES, or BARBS, in farriery, the knots or superfluous flesh that grow up in the channels of a horse's mouth; that is, in the intervals that separate the bars, and lie under tongue.
