ATHEROMA

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATHEROMA, in surgery, a tumour without pain or discoloration of the skin, containing, in a membranous bag, matter resembling pap, intermixed with hard and stony particles. These tumours are easily cured by incision.
