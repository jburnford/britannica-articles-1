BARANGI

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARANGI, officers among the Greeks of the lower empire. Cujas calls them in Latin protestores, and others give them the name of securigeri. It was their business to keep the keys of the city-gates, where the emperor resided.
