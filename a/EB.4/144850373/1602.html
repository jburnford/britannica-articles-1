BICHET

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BICHIET, a quantity or measure of corn, which differs according to the places where it is used. The bichet is not a wooden measure, as the minot at Paris, or the bushel at London; but is compounded of several certain measures. It is used in many parts of France, &c.
