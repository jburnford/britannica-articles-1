BURG-Castle

sub_entry · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURG-Castle, or Borough-Castle, a fortress on the edge of the county of Suffolk, three miles west of Yarmouth, where the rivers Yore and Waveny meet. It was formerly a delightful place; but now only the ruins of its walls remain, near which Roman coins are often dug up.
