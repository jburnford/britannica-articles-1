ATRI

ATRI, a town of Italy, in the farther Abruzzo in the kingdom of Naples, with the title of a duchy; it is the see of a bishop, and is seated on a craggy mountain, four miles from the Adriatic Sea. E. Long. 13. 8. N. Lat. 42. 45.
