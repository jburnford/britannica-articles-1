BALLATOONS

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALLATOONS, large heavy luggage-boats used for carrying wood by the river from Ayr and the Caspian sea to Moscow. These will carry from 100 to 200 ton, and have from 100 to 120 men employed to row and tow them along.
