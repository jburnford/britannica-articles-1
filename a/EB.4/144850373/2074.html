BOMBAX

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOMBAX, in zoology, a synonyme of a species of conust.—Bombax is also used sometimes for silk or cotton; but the true botanic name of cotton is Gossypium. It is likewise applied by Linnaeus to signify such insects as have incumbent wings, and feelers resembling a comb.
