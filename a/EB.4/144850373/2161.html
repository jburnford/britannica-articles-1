BORD-Service

sub_entry · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BORD-Service, the tenure of bord-lands, by which some lands in certain places are held of the bishop of London, and the tenants now pay sixpence per acre, in lieu of sending provision anciently for their lord's table.
