BRICK-MAKER

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRICK-MAKER, is he who undertakes the making of BRICKS. This is mostly performed at some small distance from cities and towns; and though some, thro' ignorance, look upon it as a very mean employ, because laborious, yet the masters about London, and other capital cities, are generally men of substance.
