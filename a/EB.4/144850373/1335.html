BELLUNESE

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BELLUNESE. a territory of Italy, belonging to the Venetians. It lies between Friuli, Cadorino, Feltrino, the bishopric of Trent and Tirol. It has good iron mines, but the only considerable place is Belluno.
