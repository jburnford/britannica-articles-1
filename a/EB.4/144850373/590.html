BALL

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALL, in pyrotechnics, is also a composition of various combustible ingredients, serving to burn, smoke, give light, &c. In this sense we read of fire-balls, light-balls, smoke-balls, flink-balls, sky-balls, water-balls, land-balls.
