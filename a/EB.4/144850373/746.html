BARs of Iron

sub_entry · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARs of Iron, are made of the metal of the sows and pigs as they come from the furnace. These pass thro' two forges called the finery and the chaufery; where, undergoing five several heats, they are formed into bars.
