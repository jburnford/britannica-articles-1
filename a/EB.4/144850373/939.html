BASARTSCHICK

BASARTSCHICK, a considerable town of Romania in Turkey of Europe. It is pretty well built, and hath clean and broad streets; has a great trade; and is situated on the river Meritz, in E. Long. 24. 30. N. Lat. 41. 49.
