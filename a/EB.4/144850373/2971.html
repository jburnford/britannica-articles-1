BUTTERIS

article · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUTTERIS, in the menage, an instrument of steel, fitted to a wooden handle, wherewith they pare the foot, or cut off the hoof of a horse.

BUTTOCK of a SHIP, is that part of her which is her breadth right aftern, from the tack upwards; and a ship is said to have a broad or a narrow buttock, according as she is built broad or narrow at the transom.
