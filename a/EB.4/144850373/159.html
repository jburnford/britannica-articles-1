AUDITORIUM

article · 396 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUDITORIUM, in the ancient churches, was that part of the church where the audientes stood to hear and be instructed.

The auditorium was that part now called nave &c. † See Nave. clesie †. In the primitive times, the church was so strict in keeping the people together in that place, that the person who went from thence in sermon-time was ordered by the council of Carthage to be excommunicated.
