ATTACK of a siege

sub_entry · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SIEGE

ATTACK of a siege, is a furious assault made by the besiegers with trenches, covers, mines, &c. in order to make themselves masters of a fortrefs, by storming one of its sides. If there are two or three attacks made at the same time, there should be a communication betwixt them. See SIEGE.
