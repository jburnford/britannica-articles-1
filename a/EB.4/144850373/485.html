BAFFO

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAFFO, a considerable town in the island of Cyprus, with a fort built near ancient Paphos, of which some considerable ruins yet remain, particularly some broken columns, which probably belonged to the temple of Venus. E. Long. 32. 20. N. Lat. 34. 50.
