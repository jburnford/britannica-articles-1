AURANCHES, the capital of a territory called Auranchin, about 30 miles in length, in Lower Normandy in France. The air is mild and temperate, and the best cyder in Normandy is produced here.

The city stands on a hill, near which the river See runs, and is about a mile and a half from the ocean. W. Long. 1. 20. N. Lat. 48. 51.
