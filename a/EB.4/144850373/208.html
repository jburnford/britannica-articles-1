AUGURALE

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUGURALE, the place in a camp where the general took auspicia. This answered to the Auguratorium in the city.

AUGURALE is also used in Seneca, for the ensign or badge of an augur, as the lituus.
