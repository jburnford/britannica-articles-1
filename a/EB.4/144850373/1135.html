BEAD-MAKERS

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEAD-MAKERS, called by the French paternostriers, are those employed in the making, stringing, and selling of beads. At Paris there are three companies of paternostriers, or bead-makers; one who make them of glass or crystal; another in wood and horn; and the third in amber, coral, jet, &c.
