BONIS NON AMOVENDIS

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONIS NON AMOVENDIS, in law, is a writ directed to the sheriffs of London, &c. charging them, that a person against whom judgment is obtained, and prosecuting a writ of error, be not suffered to remove his goods until the error is determined.
