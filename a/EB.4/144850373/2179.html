BORING, in mineralogy, a method of piercing the
earth with scooping irons, which being drawn back at
proper times, bring up with them samples of the dif-
ferent strata through which they have passed; by the
examination of which the skilful mineralogist will be
able to guess whereabouts a vein of ore may lie, or
whether it will be worth while to open a mine there or no.
