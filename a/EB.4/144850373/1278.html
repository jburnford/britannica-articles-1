BELEM

BELEM, a town of Estremadura in Portugal, about a mile from Lisbon. It is seated on the north side of the river Tajo, and is designed to defend the entrance

Belemnites to Lisbon; and here all the ships that sail up the river
Belesis. must bring to. In this place they inter the kings and
queens of Portugal.
