BELLATRIX

article · 344 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BELLATRIX, in astronomy, a ruddy glittering star of the second magnitude, in the left shoulder of Orion. It takes its name from bellum, as being anciently

supposed to have a great influence in kindling wars, and forming warriors. Its longitude, according to Helvelius, for the year 1700, was 16° 47' 20"; and its latitude southward 16° 52' 11".
