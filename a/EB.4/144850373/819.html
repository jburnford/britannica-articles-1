BARE-FOOT Carmelites and Augustines

sub_entry · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARE-FOOT Carmelites and Augustines, are religious of the order of St Carmel and St Austin, who live under a strict observance, and go without shoes, like the capuchins. There are also barefoot fathers of mercy. Formerly there were barefoot dominicans, and even barefoot nuns of the order of St Augustin.
