BAYEUX

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAYEUX, a considerable town of France in Normandy, and capital of Bessin, with a rich bishop's see. The cathedral church is accounted the finest in that province; and its front and three high steeples are said to be the best in France. W. Long. o. 33. N. Lat. 49. 16.
