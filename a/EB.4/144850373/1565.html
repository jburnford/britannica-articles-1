Fossil BEZOAR

sub_entry · 577 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Fossil BEZOAR, is a kind of figured stone, formed, like the animal bezoar, of several coats or strata ranged round some extraneous body which forms a nucleus, and supposed to have the same virtues. It is found chiefly in Sicily, in sand and clay pits. It is of a purple colour, with a rough surface, the size of a walnut, and light. When broken, it is found to be an irony craft, containing in its hollow a fine greenish white earth resembling pale bezoar. The earth is used, and not the shells. It seems to be of the nature of bole armeniacæ. It is also called Sicilian earth.
