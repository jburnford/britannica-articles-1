BRENTFORD, a town of Middlesex, in the great
London road to the west. It is divided into old and
new Brentford, in which last are the church and mar-
ket-house. It is a long place, well stocked with pub-
lic houses, and is seated on the river Thames, in W.
Long. 0. 10. N. Lat. 51. 26.
