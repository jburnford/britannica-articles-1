BLOSSOM

article · 282 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLOSSOM, or Peach-coloured, in the menage, a term applied to a horse that has his hair white, but intermixed all over with forrel and bay hairs. Such horses are so insensible and hard both in the mouth and the flanks, that they are scarce valued; besides they are apt to turn blind.
