AVANIA

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVANIA, in the Turkish legislature, a fine for crimes, and, on deaths, paid to the governor of the place. In the places wherein several nations live together under a Turkish governor, he takes this profitable method of punishing all crimes among the Christians, or Jews, unless it be the murder of a Turk.
