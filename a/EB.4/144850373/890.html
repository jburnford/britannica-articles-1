BARRAUX

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRAUX, a fortress of Dauphiny belonging to France. It stands in the valley of Gresivaudan, and was built by a duke of Savoy in 1597. The French took it in 1598, and have kept it ever since. It is seated on the river Iser, in E. Long. 4. 35. N. Lat. 45. 0.
