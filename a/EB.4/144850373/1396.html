BENEFICIARY

article · 502 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BENEFICIARY, beneficiarius, is more particularly used for a beneficed person, or him who receives and enjoys one or more benefices. A beneficiary is not the proprietor of the revenues of his church; he has only the administration of them, tho' unaccountable for the same to any, but God.

BENEFICIARY is also used, in middle-age writers, for a feudatory or vassal. The denomination was also given to the clerks or officers who kept the accounts of the beneficia, and made the writings necessary thereto.
