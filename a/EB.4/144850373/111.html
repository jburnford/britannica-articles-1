ATTORNEY General

sub_entry · 632 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATTORNEY General, is a great officer under the king, made by letters patent. It is his place to exhibit informations, and prosecute for the crown, in matters criminal; and to file bills in the exchequer, for any thing concerning the king in inheritance or profits; and others may bring bills against the king's attorney. His proper place in court, upon any special matters of a criminal nature, wherein his attendance is required, is under the judges on the left hand of the clerk of the crown: but this is only upon solemn and extraordinary occasions; for usually he does not sit there, but within the bar in the face of the court.
