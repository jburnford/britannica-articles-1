BAC

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAC, in brewing, a large flat kind of tub, or vessel, wherein the wort is put to stand and cool before boiling. The ingredients of beer pass through three kinds of vessels. They are mashed in one, worked in another, and cooled in a third called bacs or coolers.
