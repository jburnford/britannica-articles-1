BIMEDIAL

BIMEDIAL, in mathematics. If two medial lines, as AB and BC, commensurable only in power, containing a rational rectangle, are compounded, the whole line AC will be irrational, and is called a first bimedral line.

See Euclid. lib. X. prop. 38.
