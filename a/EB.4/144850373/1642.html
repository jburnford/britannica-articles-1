BILBOWS

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BILBOWS, a punishment at sea, answering to the stocks at land. The offender is laid in irons, or stocks, which are more or less ponderous according to the quality of the offence of which he is guilty.
