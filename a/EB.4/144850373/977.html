BASKET

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASKET, a machine made of twigs interwoven together, in order to hold fruit, earth, &c. It denotes an uncertain quantity; as, a basket of medlars is two bushels, of assa fetida from 20 to 50 pound weight.
