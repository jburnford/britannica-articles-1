BRAMBLE-NET

article · 355 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRAMBLE-NET, otherwise called ballier, is a net to
catch birds in of several sizes: the great meshes must
be four inches square; those of the least size are three
inches square; and those of the biggest, five. In the
depth they should not be above three or four inches:
but as for the length, they may be enlarged at plea-
sure; the shortest being 18 feet long.
