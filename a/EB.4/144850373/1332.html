BONE-BELLOWS

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONE-BELLOWS, parvulus bellus, occur in Herodotus for those applied by the Scythians to the genitals of mares, in order to distend the uterus, and by this compression make them yield a greater quantity of milk.
