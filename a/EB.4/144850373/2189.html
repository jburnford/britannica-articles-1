BOROMÆUS

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOROMÆUS (Frederic), cardinal and archbishop of Milan. He, in 1609, celebrated the council of Milan. He founded the Ambrosian library, which he enriched with 9000 manuscripts. He left several works behind him, and died in 1632.
