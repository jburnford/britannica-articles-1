EPENTHESIS

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPENTHESIS, in grammar, the interposition or insertion of a letter or syllable in the middle of a word; as alitum, for alitum; relligio, for religio; induperator, for imperator, &c.
