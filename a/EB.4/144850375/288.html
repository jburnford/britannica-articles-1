DEMI-CULVERIN of the least size

sub_entry · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEMI-CULVERIN of the least size, is 4¼ inches bore, ten feet long, and 2000 pounds weight. It carries a ball of 4 inches diameter, and of 9 pounds weight, and its level range is 174 paces.
