ESCHRAKITES

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESCHRAKITES, in matters of religion, a sect of Mahometans, who believe that man's sovereign good consists in the contemplation of God. They avoid all manner of vice, and appear always in good-humour, despising the sensual paradise of Mahomet. The most able preachers in the royal mosques are of this sect.
