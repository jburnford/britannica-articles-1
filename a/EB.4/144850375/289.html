DEMI-CULVERIN of the largest sort

sub_entry · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEMI-CULVERIN of the largest sort, is 4½ inches bore, 10½ feet long, and weighs 3000 pounds weight. It carries a ball 4½ inches diameter, weighing 12 pounds 11 ounces, point blank 178 paces.
