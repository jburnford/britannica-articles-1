FREDERICKSBURG, a fort and colony of Brandenburg, on the gold-coast of Guinea, in Africa, near Cape Three-points, and about 75 miles from Cape Coast. It mounts 46 pieces of cannon on four batteries; and formerly belonged to the Prussians, but is now subject to Denmark. W. Long. 1. 15. N. Lat. 4. 30.
