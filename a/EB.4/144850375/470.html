DIAL

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIAL, or SUN-DIAL, an instrument serving to measure time, by means of the shadow of the sun. The word is formed from the Latin die, "day," because indicating the hour of the day.

The ancients also called it scithericum, from its doing it by the shadow.
