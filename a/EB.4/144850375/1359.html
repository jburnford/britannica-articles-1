ENDOWMENT

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENDOWMENT, in law, denotes the settling a dower on a woman: though sometimes it is used, figuratively, for settling a provision upon a parson, on the building of a church; or the severing a sufficient portion of tithes for a vicar, when the benefice is appropriated.
