DEPRECATORY, or DEPRECATIVE, in theology, a term applied to the manner of performing some ceremonies in the form of prayer.

The

Depression
Deputatus. The form of absolution is deprecative in the Greek church, being conceived in these terms, May God absolve you; whereas it is in the declarative form in the Latin church, and in some of the reformed churches, I absolve you.
