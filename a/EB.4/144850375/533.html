LONG. adj. A relative term, denoting the distance between the extremes of any body, which is extended more in one of its geometrical dimensions than another. Opposed to short.

position, being considered as of a middle nature between erect and prostrate bodies, admit of either of the terms long, tall, or high; as, a long or tall rush or willow wand, or a long, tall, or high stalk of corn. See HIGH and TALL.
