FEVERSHAM

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FEVERSHAM, a town of the county of Kent in England, situated on a branch of the river Thames, which is navigable for hoys. It is a member of the cinque-port of Dover. Oysters, and many other articles, are sent from hence to the London market. E. Long. 0. 38. N. Lat. 51. 20.
