FRIEDBURG

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRIEDBURG, an imperial town of Germany, in Wetteravia. It is seated on a mountain, in E. Long. 8. 50. N. Lat. 50. 14. It was formerly much more considerable than at present.
