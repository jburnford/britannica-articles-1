DWINA

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DWINA, the name of two large rivers; one of which rises in Lithuania, and, dividing Livonia from Courland, falls into the Baltic sea a little below Riga: the other gives name to the province of Dwina, in Russia, discharging itself into the White Sea, a little below Archangel.
