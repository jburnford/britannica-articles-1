FAGARA

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FAGARA, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The calix consists of four segments, and the corolla of four petals; and the capsule has four cells, two valves, and contains one seed. There are three species, none of them natives of Britain.
