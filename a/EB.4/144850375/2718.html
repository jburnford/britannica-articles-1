FUNGI

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FUNGI (from fungus, fungus), one of the seven families or tribes of the vegetable kingdom, according to Linnæus, comprehending all those who are of the mushroom kind, and which in Tournefort constitute the 2d, 3d, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th genera of the first section in the class xvii.
