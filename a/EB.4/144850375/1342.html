ENCANTHIS

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SURGERY

ENCANTHIS, in surgery, a tubercle arising either from the caruncula lachrymalis, or from the adjacent red skin; sometimes so large, as to obstruct not only the puncta lacrymalia, but also part of the sight or pupil itself. See SURGERY.
