FORMOSA

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORMOSA, an island in the Pacific ocean, between 119° and 122° of E. Long. and between 22° and 25° N. Lat. about 100 miles east of Canton in China. It is subject to the Chinese.
