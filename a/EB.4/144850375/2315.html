FLOOR of a Ship

sub_entry · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLOOR of a Ship, as much of her bottom as she rests upon when she rests on the ground.—Such ships as have long and broad floors, lie on the ground with most security; and those that are narrow in the floor, cannot be grounded without danger either of being overset, or at least of hurting their sides.
