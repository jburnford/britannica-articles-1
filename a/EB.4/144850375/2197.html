FINISTERRA

article · 167 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FINISTERRA, the most westerly cape or promontory of Spain, in 10. 15. W. Long. and 43° N. Lat. This cape is likewise the most westerly part of the continent of Europe.
