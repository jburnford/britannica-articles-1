DUPLE

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUPLÉ, among mathematicians, denotes the ratio of 2 to 1. Thus the ratio of 8 to 4 is double, or as 2 to 1.

Sub-Duple Ratio, is just the reverse of the former, or as 1 to 2. Such is 4 to 8, or 6 to 12.
