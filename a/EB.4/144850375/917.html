DRAGONNEE

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRAGONNEE, in heraldry. A lion dragonée is where the upper half resembles a lion, the other half going off like the hinder part of a dragon. The fume may be said of any other beast as well as a lion.
