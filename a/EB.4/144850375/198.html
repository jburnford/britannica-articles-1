DEDHAM

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEDHAM, a town of Essex in England, consisting of about 400 lofty houses. The streets are not paved, but very clean, occasioned by their lying pretty high. It has one large old church, remarkable for a fine Gothic steeple, with a great deal of carved work about it, but much injured by time. E. Long. 1. 10. N. Lat. 52. 5.
