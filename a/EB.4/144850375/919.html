DRAGS

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRAGS, in the sea-language, are whatever hangs over the ship in the sea, as shirts, coats, or the like; and boats, when towed, or whatever else that after this manner may hinder the ship's way when she sails, are called drags.
