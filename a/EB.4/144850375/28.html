DALECHAMP

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DALECHAMP (James), a physician in Normandy, in the 16th century, wrote a history of plants, and was well skilled in polite learning. He wrote notes on Pliny's natural history, and translated Athenæus into Latin.
