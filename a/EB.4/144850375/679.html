DISPENSARY

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISPENSARY, or DISPENSATORY, denotes a book containing the method of preparing the various kinds of medicines used in pharmacy. Such are those of Bauderon, Quercetan, Zweller, Charas, Bates, Mesue, Salmon, Lemery, Quiney, &c. but the latest and most esteemed are the Edinburgh and London dispensaries, and Dr Lewis's dispensatory.
