EUBAGES

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUBAGES, an order of priests, or philosophers, among the ancient Celts or Gauls: some will have the eubages to be the same with the druids and faro-niads of Diodorus; and others, that they were the same with what Strabo calls EVATES.
