EXPERIENCE, a kind of knowledge acquired by long use, without any teacher. See METAPHYSICS, n° 26, 28. Experiment. EXPERIMENT, in philosophy, is the trial of the result or effect of the applications and motions of certain natural bodies, in order to discover something of their motions and relations, whereby to ascertain some of their phenomena, or causes.
