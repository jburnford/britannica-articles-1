FAGARA

article · 296 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FAGARA, Iron-wood, a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants. There are two species, both natives of the warm parts of America, rising with woody stems more than 20 feet high. They are propagated by seeds; but in this country must be kept continually in a stove.
