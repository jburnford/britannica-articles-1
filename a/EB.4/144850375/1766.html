EXEDRÆ

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXEDRÆ, in antiquity, a general name for such buildings as were distinct from the main body of the churches, and yet within the limits of the church taken in its largest sense. Among the exedrae the chief was the BAPTISTERY.
