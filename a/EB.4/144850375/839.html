DORCHESTER

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DORCHESTER, the capital of Dorsetshire, situated on the river Froom, six miles north of Weymouth: W. Long. 2. 35. N. Lat. 50. 40. It gives the title of marquis to the noble family of Pierpoint, dukes of Kingston; and sends two members to parliament.
