FIRE-Lock

sub_entry · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FIRE-Lock, or Fasil, a small gun which fires with a flint. It is distinguished from an old musquet, or match-lock, which was fired with a match. The firelock is now in common use in the European armies.
