DALMATIA

article · 395 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DALMATIA, a province of Europe; bounded on the north by Bosnia, on the south by the gulph of Venice, on the east by Servia, and on the west by Morachia. Spalatro is the capital of that part belonging to the Venetians; and Ragusa, of a republic of that name: the Turks have a third, whose capital is Herzegovina. The air is wholesome, and the soil fruitful; and it abounds in wine, corn, and oil.
