White EAGLE

sub_entry · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

White EAGLE, a like order in Poland, instituted in 1325, by Uladislavus V. on occasion of the marriage of his son Casimir to the daughter of the great duke of Lithuania.

The knights of this order wear a chain of gold, suspending a silver eagle crowned.
