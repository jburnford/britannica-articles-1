DARTOS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DARTOS, in anatomy, one of the coats which form the scrotum. It is called the dartos muscle; but Dr Hunter says, that no such muscle can be found, and Albinus takes no notice of it in his tables.
