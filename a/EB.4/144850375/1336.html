ENAMELLING

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENAMELLING, the art of laying enamel upon metals, as gold, silver, copper, &c. and of melting it at the fire, or of making divers curious works in it at a lamp. It signifies also to paint in enamel.
