DEXTROCHERE

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEXTROCHERE, or DESTROCHERE, in heraldry, is applied to the right arm painted in a shield, sometimes naked; sometimes clothed, or adorned with a bracelet; and sometimes armed, or holding some moveable or member used in the arms.
