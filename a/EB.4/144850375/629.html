DIPLOMA. See DIPLOMATICS.

In a peculiar sense, it is used for an instrument or licence given by colleges, societies, &c. to a clergyman to exercise the ministerial function, or to a physician to practise the profession, &c. after passing examination, or admitting him to a degree.
