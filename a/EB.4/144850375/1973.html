FATES, in mythology. See PARCE. FATHEMITES, FATEMITES, or FATHIMITES, the descendants of Mahomet by Fatima, or Fatima, his daughter. They never enjoyed the khalifat of Mecca or Bagdad, but reigned in Barbary and Egypt. See the history of these countries.
