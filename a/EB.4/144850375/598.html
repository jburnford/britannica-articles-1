DIMENSION, in geometry, is either length, breadth,

* See Logic,
n° 100, 101.

Diminution breadth, or thickness: hence, a line hath one dimension, viz. length; a superficies two, viz. length and breadth; and a body, or solid, has three, viz. length, breadth, and thickness.
