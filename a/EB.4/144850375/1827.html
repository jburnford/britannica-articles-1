EYE, in anatomy. See ANATOMY, no 406.

Bull's Eye, in astronomy. See ALDEBARAN.

Eye of a Block, in naval affairs, that part of the rope-strop which is fastened to some necessary place in the ship: the strop is a sort of wreath or rope formed into a ring, and fixed round the block for the double convenience of strengthening the block, and fastening it in any place where it is wanted.
