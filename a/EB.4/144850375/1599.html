ESPOUSALS

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESPOUSALS, in law, signify a contract or promise made between a man and a woman, to marry each other; and in cases where marriages may be consummated espousals go before. Marriage is termed an espousal de presenti.
