EDITOR, a person of learning, who has the care

of an impression of any work, particularly that of an ancient author: thus, Erasmus was a great editor; the Louvain doctors, Scaliger, Petavius, F. Sirmond, bishop Walton, Mr Hearne, Mr Ruddiman, &c. are likewise famous editors.
