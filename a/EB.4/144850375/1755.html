LETTERS of EXCULPATION

sub_entry · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LETTERS of EXCULPATION, in Scots law, a writ or summons issued by authority of the court of justice, at the instance of a pannel, for citing witnesses to prove his defences, or his objections to any of the jury or witnesses cited against him.
