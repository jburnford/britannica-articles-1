DIOMEDES

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIOMEDES, king of Etolia, the son of Tydeus, and the most valiant of the Grecian heroes, next to Achilles and Ajax, signalized himself at the siege of Troy against Æneas and Hector, and carried off the Palladium.
