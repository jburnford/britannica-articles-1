PEOFFMENT

Cross-references: FEE

PEOFFMENT, in law, is a gift or grant of any manors, messuages, lands, or tenements, to another in fee; that is, to him and his heirs for ever, by delivery of seisin, and possession of the estate granted. See FEE.
