FULLER (Nicholas), prebendary of Salisbury, and a learned English critic; who published, in 1617, Miscellanea Theologica, in four books; and afterward two more of Miscellanea Sacra. He died in 1623; and there are some MSS of his remaining in the Bodleian library, that shew his great skill in Hebrew and philology.
