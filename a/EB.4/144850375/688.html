DISPOSITION

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISPOSITION, in Scots law, is that deed or writing which contains the scale or grant of any subject: when applied to heritable subjects, it in some cases gets the name of charter, which differs from a disposition in nothing else than a few immaterial forms.
