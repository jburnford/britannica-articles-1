FURLING

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FURLING, in the sea language, signifies the wrapping up and binding any sail close to the yard; which is done by hawling upon the clew-lines, bunt-lines, &c. which wraps the sail close together, and being bound fast to the yard the sail is furled.
