EDDY TIDE

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EDDY TIDE, or EDDY WATER, among seamen, is where the water runs back contrary to the tide; or that which hinders the free passage of the stream, and so causes it to return again.

EDDY-WIND is that which returns or is beat back from a sail, mountain, or any thing that may hinder its passage.
