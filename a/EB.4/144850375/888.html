DOUGLAS, the principal town of the Isle of Man, and which has lately increased both in trade and buildings. The harbour, for ships of a tolerable burden, is

the safest in the island, and is much mended by a fine mole that has lately been built. It is seated on the eastern side. W. Long. 4. 25. N. Lat. 54. 7.
