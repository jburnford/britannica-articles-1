DRACO

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRACO, an Athenian lawgiver, prior to Solon, so extremely severe, that he punished all faults, small or great, with death: hence his laws were proverbially said to have been written with blood. He flourished 624 B. C.
