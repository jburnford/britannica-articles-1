FISSURES

Cross-references: SURGERY

FISSURES, in the history of the earth, certain interruptions, that in an horizontal or parallel manner divide the several strata of which the body of our terrestrial globe is composed.

Fissure of the Bones, in surgery, is when they are divided either transversely or longitudinally, not quite through, but cracked after the manner of glass, by any external force. See SURGERY.
