EQUIVOCAL Generation

sub_entry · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EQUIVOCAL Generation, the production of animals without the intercourse between the sexes, by the influence of the sun or stars, &c.

This kind of generation is now quite exploded by the learned.
