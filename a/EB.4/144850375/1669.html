EUMENES II

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUMENES II. king of Pergamus, a valiant general, patron of learning, and founder of the famous library at Pergamus, on the model of that at Alexandria. He died 159 B. C. after a reign of 39 years.
