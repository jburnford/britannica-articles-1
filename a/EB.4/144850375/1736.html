Books and accounts are kept in piasstras, soldi, and denari. The piastra here consists of 6 lires, and the lire contains 20 soldi, and the soldi 12 denari, and consequently exchange money is 6 times better than lire money. The course of exchange is from 47 d. to 58 d. Sterling per piastra.
