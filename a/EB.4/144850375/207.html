DEFAULT

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEFAULT, in law, is generally taken for non-appearance in court, at a day assigned; but imports any omission of that which we ought to do, for which

Defeasance judgment may be given against the defaulter.
