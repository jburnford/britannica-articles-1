FOUNDER, also implies an artist who casts metals, in various forms, for different uses, as guns, bells, statues, printing-characters, candlesticks, buckles, &c. whence they are denominatd gun-founders, bell-founders, figure-founders, letter-founders, founders of small works, &c. See FOUNDRY.
