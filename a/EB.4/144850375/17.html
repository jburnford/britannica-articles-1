DAGNO, a town of Turkey in Europe, in Albania, with a bishop's see. It is the capital of the district of Ducagini, and it is seated on the rivers Drino and Nero, near their confluence. It is 15 miles south-east of Scutari, and 15 north-east of Alessio. E. Long. 19. 48. N. Lat. 42. 0.
