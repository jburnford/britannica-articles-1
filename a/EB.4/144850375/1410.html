ENTREPAS

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENTREPAS, in the manège, a broken pace or going, that is neither walk nor trot, but has somewhat of an amble.

This is a pace or gait of such horses as have no reins or lack, and go upon their shoulders; or, of such as are spoiled in their limbs.
