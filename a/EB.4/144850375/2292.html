FLESH

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLESH, in anatomy, a similar, fibrous part of an animal body, soft and bloody, being that whereof most of the other parts are composed, and whereby they are connected together: or more properly, it is such parts of the body where the blood-vessels are so small, as only to retain blood enough to preserve their colour red.
