FERRARS (Henry)

sub_entry · 460 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FERRARS (Henry), a Warwickshire gentleman of a good family, was eminent in antiquities, genealogies, and heraldry. Mr Wood says, that out of the collections of this gentleman, Sir William Dugdale laid part of the foundation of his celebrated Antiquities of Warwickshire. Camden also makes honourable mention of his assistance in relation to Coventry. Some scattered poems of his were published among others in the reign of queen Elizabeth; and he died in 1633.
