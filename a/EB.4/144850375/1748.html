EXCLUSIVE

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXCLUSIVE, is sometimes used adjectively, thus; A patent carries with it an exclusive privilege; and sometimes adverbially; as, he sent him all the numbers from no 145 to no 247 exclusive; that is, all between these two numbers, which themselves were excepted.
