DIFFUSION

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIFFUSION, the dispersion of the subtle effluvia of bodies into a kind of atmosphere all round them. Thus the light diffused by the rays of the sun, issues all round from that amazing body of fire.
