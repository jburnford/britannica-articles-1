DONATION

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DONATION, an act whereby a person transfers to another either the property or the use of something as a free gift. In order to be valid, it supposes a capa-

city both in the donor and the donee; and requires consent, acceptance, and delivery, and by the French also registry.
