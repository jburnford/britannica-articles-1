DELIQUIUM

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DELIQUIUM, a term frequently employed by chemists to characterize a body which is resolved into a liquor by exposure to the air. In this sense they talk of the deliquium of a salt, as of salt of tartar for in-

stance. This word is also frequently used instead of DELIRIUM.
