DIGNITY, (from dic twice, and gyn a woman),

the name of an order or secondary division in each of the first 13 classes, except the 9th, in Linnaeus's sexual method; consisting of plants, which to the classic character, whatever it is, add the circumstance of having two styles or female organs.
