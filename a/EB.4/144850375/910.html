DRAGOMAN, DROGMAN, or Druggerman

sub_entry · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRAGOMAN, DROGMAN, or Druggerman, a name given in the Levant to the interpreters kept by the ambassadors of Christian nations, residing at the Porte, to assist them in treating of their master's affairs.
