FIDIUS

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FIDIUS, in Pagan worship, a god who presided over alliances and promises. This deity, which the Romans borrowed from the Sabines, was also called Sanctus, Semon, and Semi-pater.
