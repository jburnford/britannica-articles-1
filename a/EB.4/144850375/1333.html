EMUNCTORY

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EMUNCTORY, in anatomy, a general term for all those parts which serve to carry off the excrementitious parts of the blood and other humours of the body.

* See Pharmacy, no 910.

dy. Such more especially are the kidneys, bladder, and most of the glands.
