ERROR Loci. Boerhaave is said to have introduced this term, from the opinion that the vessels were of different sizes for the circulation of blood, serum, and lymph; and that when the larger-sized globules were forced into the lesser vessels by an error of place, they were obstructed. But this opinion does not seem well grounded.
