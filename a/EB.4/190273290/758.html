SPRING

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPRING, in mechanics, denotes a thin piece of tempered steel, or other elastic substance, which being wound up, serves to put several machines in motion by its elasticity, or endeavour to unbend itself: such is the spring of a watch, clock, or the like.
