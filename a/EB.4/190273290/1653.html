TRAIN

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRAIN, a line of gun-powder laid to give fire to a quantity thereof, in order to do execution by blowing up earth, works, buildings, &c.

TRAIN of Artillery, includes the great guns and other pieces of ordnance belonging to an army in the field.
