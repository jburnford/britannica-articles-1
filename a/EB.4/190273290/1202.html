SYSTEM

SYSTEM, in music, an assemblage of the rules for harmony deduced from some common principle by which they are re-united; by which their connection one with another is formed; from whence, as from their genuine source, they natively flow; and to which, if we would account for them, we must have recourse. See the articles CHROMATIC, DIATONIC, ENHARMONIC, HARMONY, INTERVAL, and MUSIC.
