SEGEBERG

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEGEBERG, a town of Germany, in the duchy of Holstein, and in Wagria; with a castle standing on a high mountain, consisting of limestone, large quantities of which are carried to Hamburg and Lubeck. It belongs to Denmark, and is seated on the river Treve; in E. Long. 10. 23. N. Lat. 54. 5.
