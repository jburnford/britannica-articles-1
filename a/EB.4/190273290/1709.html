TRES TAFERNÆ

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRES TAFERNÆ, (anc. geog.) a place in Latium, lying on the Via Appia, on the left or south side of the river Astura, to the north of the Paludes Pompinæ. Its ruins now seen near Cisterna, a village in the Campagna di Roma, twenty-one miles from Rome, whence the Christians went out to meet St Paul.
