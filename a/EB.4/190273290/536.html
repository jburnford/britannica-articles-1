SODOR

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SODOR, a little village in Columbkill, one of the western isles of Scotland, near that of Mull. It was formerly a bishop's see, which comprehended all the islands, together with the isle of Man; for which reason the bishop is still called the bishop of Sodor and Man.
