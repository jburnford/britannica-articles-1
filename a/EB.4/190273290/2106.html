VISCIDITY

article · 168 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VISCIDITY, or VISCOSITY, the quality of something that is viscid or viscous; that is, glutinous, and sticky like bird-lime, which the Latins call by the name of viscus.
