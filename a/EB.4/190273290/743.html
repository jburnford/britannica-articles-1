SPONGIA

SPONGIA, SPUNGE; a genus belonging to the order of vermes zoophyta. The whole texture is a congeries of bibulous cells. There are six species, all found in the bottom of the sea, and on submarine rocks. Spunges are much used by surgeons and others for sucking up superfluous moisture, which they easily part with by pressure.
