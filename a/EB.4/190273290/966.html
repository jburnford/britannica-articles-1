STRYMON

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STRYMON, (anc. geog.), formerly Coronus; a river constituting the ancient limits of Macedonia and Thrace; rising in mount Scombrus, (Aristotle). Authors differ as to the modern name of this river.
