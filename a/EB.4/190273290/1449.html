THEODOSIUS I

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CONSTANTINOPLE

THEODOSIUS I. called the Great, was a native of Spain. The valour he had shown, and the great services he had done to the empire, made Gratian, attacked by the Goths and Germans, to admit him as a partner in the government. He received the purple in 379, aged 43. See CONSTANTINOPLE, n° 81—92.
