TUBAL CAIN

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TUBAL CAIN, the son of Lamech and Sella, was born about the 2975th year before the Christian era. He invented the art of working in brass, iron, and other metals; and many have imagined that from him the pagan authors took the idea of their Vulcan.
