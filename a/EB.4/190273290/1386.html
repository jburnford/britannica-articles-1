TESSON

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TESSON, or TESTOON, commonly called Tefter, a sort of money which among the French bore the value of 18d.; but being made of brass lightly gilt with silver, in the reign of king Henry VIII. it was reduced to 12d. and afterwards to 6d.
