SOCOTORA

article · 435 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOCOTORA, an island lying between Asia and Arabia Felix; about 150 miles in length, and 22 in breadth. It is particularly noted for its fine aloes, known by the name of Socotrine aloes. The religion of the natives is a mixture of Mahometanism and Paganism; but they are civil to strangers who call there in their passage to the East Indies. It abounds in fruit and cattle; and they have a king of their own, who is dependent on Arabia.
