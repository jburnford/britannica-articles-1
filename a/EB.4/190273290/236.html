SEVERINA

SEVERINA (St), a town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in Lower Calabria, with an archbishop's see. It is very well fortified, and seated on a craggy rock, on the river Neeto; in E. Long. 17. 20. N. Lat. 39. 15.
