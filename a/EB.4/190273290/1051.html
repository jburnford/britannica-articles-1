SUGILLATION

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUGILLATION, in medicine, an extravasation of blood in the coats of the eye, which at first appears of a reddish colour, and afterwards livid or black. If the disorder is great, bleeding and purging are proper, as are also discontents.
