SYCOPHANT

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SYCOPHANT, an appellation given by the ancient Athenians to those who informed of the exportation of figs contrary to law: and hence it is still used in general for all informers, parasites, flatterers, cheats, &c.
