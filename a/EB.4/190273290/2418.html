WHIP

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WHIP, or WHIP-Staff, in a ship, a piece of timber, in form of a strong staff, fastened into the helm, for the steersman, in small ships, to hold in his hand, in order to move the rudder and direct the ship.
