SEM

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEM, or SHEM, the son of Noah, memorable for his filial piety in concealing the folly and disgrace of his father; for which he received a remarkable benediction; about 2476 B. C. He lived to the age of 600 years.
