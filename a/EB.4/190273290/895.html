STIMULATING

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STIMULATING, a property in bodies by which they vibrate, and cause vibrations and inflections of the fibres of the nerves, and a greater derivation of nervous fluid into the parts affected. Stimulants produce pain, heat, redness, &c.
