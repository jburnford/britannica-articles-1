VOLUNTEERS

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOLUNTEERS, persons who, of their own accord, either for the service of their prince, or out of the esteem they have for their general, serve in the army without being enlisted, to gain honour and preferment, by exposing themselves in the service.
