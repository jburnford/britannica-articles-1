TAVISTOCK

TAVISTOCK, a town of Devonshire in England, situated on the river Tavey or Tave, W. Long. 4. 26. N. 50. 37. It sends two members to parliament, and gives the title of marquis to the noble family of Ruffels dukes of Bedford.
