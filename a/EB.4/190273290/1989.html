VERD, the islands of Cape de Verd, are seated on the Atlantic Ocean, about 400 miles west of the Cape. They are between the 13th and 19th degree of latitude; and the principal are ten in number, lying in

Verden a semi-circle. Their names are, St Antony, St Vincent, St Lucia, St Nicholas, the Isle of Sal, Bona Vista, Mayo, St Jago, Fuego, and Brava.
