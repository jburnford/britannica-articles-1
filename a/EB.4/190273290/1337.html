TENAILLES

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TENAILLES, in fortification, are low works made in the ditch before the curtains. There three sorts. The first are the faces of the bastions produced till they meet, but much lower; the second have faces, flanks, and a curtain; and the third have only faces and flanks.
