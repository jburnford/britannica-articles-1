TIDE-WAITERS

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TIDE-WAITERS, or Tidesmen, are inferior officers belonging to the custom-house, whose employment is to watch or attend upon ships until the customs be paid: they get this name from their going on board ships on their arrival in the mouth of the Thames or other ports, and so come up with the tide.
