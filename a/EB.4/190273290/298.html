SHELF

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHELF, among miners, the same with what they otherwise call fast ground or fast country; being that part of the internal structure of the earth which they find lying even and in an orderly manner, and evidently having retained its primitive form and situation.
