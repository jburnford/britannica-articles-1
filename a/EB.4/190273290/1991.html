VERDUN, a town of France, in Lorraine, and capital of Verdun, with a bishop's see. There are several abbeys, collegiate and parish churches; but the principal structures are the bishop's palace and the town-house. They make large quantities of sweetmeats here, which are exported to several places. E. Long. 5. 24. N. Lat. 49. 0.
