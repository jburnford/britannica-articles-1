SCUTE, (scutum) a French gold coin of 3s. 4d. in the reign of king Henry V. And Catharine queen of England had an assurance made her of sundry castles, manors, lands, &c. valued at the sum of 40,000 scutes, every two whereof were worth a noble. Rot. Parl. 1. Hen. VI.
