SEDAN

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEDAN, a strong town of France in the province of Champagne, on the confines of Luxemburg. It is one of the keys to the kingdom; and there is a well-furnished arsenal, and a fine manufactory of woolen clothes and stuffs. It is seated on the river Marne, in E. Long. 5. 2. N. Lat. 49. 12.
