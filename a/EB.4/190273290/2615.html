ZABULON

ZABULON (anc. geog.), a very strong town in the tribe of that name, on the Mediterranean, firmament of men, near Ptolemais: its vicinity to which makes it probable that it was also Chabulon, unless either name is a faulty reading in Josephus; distant about 60 stadia from Ptolemais.
