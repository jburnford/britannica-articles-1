SETTING

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SETTING, in astronomy, the withdrawing of a star or planet, or its sinking below the horizon. Astronomers and poets make three different kinds of setting of the stars, viz. the COSMICAL, ACRONYCAL, and HELICAL. See these articles.
