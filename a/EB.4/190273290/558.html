SOLE

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOLE, in the manège, a nail or sort of horn under a horse's foot, which is much more tender than the other horn that encompasses the foot, and by reason of its hardness is properly called the horn or hoof.
