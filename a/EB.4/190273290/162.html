SEPTICS

article · 376 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEPTICS, are those substances which promote putrefaction, chiefly the calcareous earths, magnesia, and testaceous powders. Dr Pringle has reckoned common salt among the septic substances; but later observations give reason to believe, that it is only in consequence of its impurities that salt acts in this manner,

it being highly probable that all pure salts are anti-septic.
