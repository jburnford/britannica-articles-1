SUBALTERN

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUBALTERN, a subordinate officer, or one who discharges his post under the command and subject to the direction of another; such are lieutenants, sublieutenants, coronets, and ensigns, who serve under the captain.
