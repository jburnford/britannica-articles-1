SESAMOIDEA ossa

sub_entry · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SESAMOIDEA ossa, certain small bones somewhat resembling the seeds of sesamum, whence their name. They are placed at the under part of the bones of the last joints of the fingers and toes.
