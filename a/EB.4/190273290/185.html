SERGEANT at Arms

sub_entry · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SERGEANT at Arms, or Mace, an officer appointed to attend the person of the king; to arrest traitors, and such persons of quality as offend; and to attend the lord high steward, when sitting in judgment on a traitor.—The number of these officers is by statute limited to 30.
