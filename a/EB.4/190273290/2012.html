VERROCHIO

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERROCHIO (Andrea), a Florentine, born in 1432. He was well skilled in mathematics, music, architecture, sculpture, and painting; and was the first who discovered the art of moulding off the features of the face in plaster of Paris. He died in 1488.
