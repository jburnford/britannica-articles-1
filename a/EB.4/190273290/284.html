SHEATS

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHEATS, in a ship, are ropes bent to the clews of the sails; serving in the lower sails to haul aft the clews of the sail; but in top sails they serve to haul home the clew of the sail close to the yard-arm.
