TRANSLATION

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRANSLATION, the act of transferring or removing a thing from one place to another; as we say, the translation of a bishop's see, a council, a seat of justice, &c.

TRANSLATION is also used for the version of a book or writing, out of one language into another.
