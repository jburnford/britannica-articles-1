Composition for Marking SHEEP. For this purpose Dr Lewis recommends tallow mixed with a certain proportion of tar, and the mixture thickened by powder of charcoal. The proportions he tried were an eighth, a sixth, and a fourth part of tar. None of these could be discharged by any washing or rubbing with water, but all of them completely by soap; that which had the smallest proportion, easily enough; the others more difficultly.
