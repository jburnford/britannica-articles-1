THEOGNIS

THEOGNIS, an ancient Greek poet of Megara in Achaia; flourished about the 59th Olympiad, 144 B. C. We have a moral work of his extant, containing a usual summary of precepts and reflections, to be found in the collections of the Greek minor poets.
