TARAZONA, a strong town of Spain, in the kingdom of Arragon, and on the frontiers of Old Castile, with a bishop's see. It is seated partly on a rock, and partly in a fertile plain, on the river Chiles. It was

taken from the Moors in 1110. W. Long. 1. 29. N. Lat. 41. 55.
