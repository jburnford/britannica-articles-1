STELLIONATE

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STELLIONATE, in the civil law, a kind of crime committed by a fraudulent bargain, where one of the parties sells a thing for what it is not; as if I sell an estate for my own which belongs to another, or convey a thing as free and clear which is already engaged to another, or put off copper for gold, &c.
