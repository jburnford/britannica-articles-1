ZICLAG

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZICLAG, or ZIKLAG (anc. geog.), a town of the tribe of Simoon, on the borders of the Philistines, (Joshua xv. and xix.), but in the hands of the Philistines till David's time, (1 Sam. xxvii. and xxx.)
