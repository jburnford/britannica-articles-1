TITLE

article · 337 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TITLE, an appellation of dignity or quality, given to princes and other persons of distinction. Thus the title of his Britannic majesty is, King of Great Britain, France, and Ireland; that of the French king is,

King of France and Navarre; and so of others. The pope assumes the title of Holiness, and the cardinals that of Eminence, &c.
