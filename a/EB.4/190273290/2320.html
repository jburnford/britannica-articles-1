WATCHING

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WATCHING, in medicine, is when, through too great a determination of blood to the head, or emptiness of the vessels, and an exhausted state of the body, the patient cannot sleep. In fevers it is a dangerous symptom, and if long continued ends in a delirium.
