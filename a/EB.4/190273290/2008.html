VERONA

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERONA, a city of Italy, capital of the Veronese in the territory of Venice, situated near the mountains on the river Adige, in E. Long. 11. 15. N. Lat. 45. 20. It is seven miles in compass; and has been so fortified by the Venetians, that it is now looked upon as impregnable.
