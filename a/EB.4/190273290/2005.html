VERMIN

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERMIN, a collective name, including all kinds of little animals or insects, which are hurtful or troublesome to mankind, beasts, or fruits, &c. as worms, lice, fleas, caterpillars, ants, flies, &c.
