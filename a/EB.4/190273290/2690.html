ORDER V. The HYMENOPTERA, have the same characters with the former, only the anus is armed with a sting. But this mark is peculiar to the females and neuters; for the males have no sting.—This order comprehends 10 genera, viz. Apis, Formica, Vespa, &c. See these articles.
