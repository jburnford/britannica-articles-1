TABBYING

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TABBYING, the passing a silk or stuff under a calender the rolls of which are made of iron or copper variously engraved, which bearing unequally on the stuff renders the surface thereof unequal, so as to reflect the rays of light differently, making the representation of waves thereon.
