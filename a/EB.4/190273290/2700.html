ZOROASTER

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZOROASTER, or ZERDUSHT, a celebrated Persian philosopher, said to have been the reformer of the religion of the magi, and to have lived in the reign of Darius Hystaspes. Others place him above 500 years before that æra: in short, authors differ so much with respect to him, that nothing certain can be affirmed on the subject.
