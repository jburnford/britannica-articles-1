VIVIPAROUS

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VIVIPAROUS, in natural history, an epithet applied to such animals as bring forth their young alive and perfect; in contradistinction to them that lay eggs, which are called oviparous animals.
