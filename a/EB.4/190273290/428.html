SINOPLÉ

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SINOPE, in heraldry, denotes vert, or green colour.

Sinuosity. In armories.—Sinople is used to signify love, youth, beauty, rejoicing, and liberty; whence it is that letters of grace, ambition, legitimation, &c. are always used to be sealed with green wax.
