WATER-Line of a Ship

Cross-references: SHIP-

WATER-Line of a Ship, certain horizontal lines supposed to be drawn about the outside of a ship's bottom, close to the surface of the water in which she floats. They are accordingly higher or lower upon the bottom, in proportion to the depth of the column of water required to float her. See SHIP-building, Part I.
