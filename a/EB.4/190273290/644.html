SPAHIS

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPAHIS, horsemen in the Ottoman army, chiefly raised in Asia. The great strength of the grand feignior's army consists in the janissaries, who are the foot; and the spahis, who are the horse.
