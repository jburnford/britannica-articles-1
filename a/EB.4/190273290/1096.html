SUPPOSITORY

article · 339 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUPPOSITORY, a kind of medicated cone or ball, which is introduced into the anus for opening of the belly. Suppositories are usually made of soap, sugar, alum, or a piece of tallow-candle about the length of a man's thumb and the breadth of a finger; though they may be made smaller for children, and sometimes a little thicker for adults.
