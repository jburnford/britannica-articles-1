VORTEX

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VORTEX, in meteorology, a whirlwind, or sudden, rapid, and violent motion of the air in gyres, or circles.

Vortex is also used for an eddy or whirlpool; or a body of water, in certain seas or rivers, which run rapidly around, forming a sort of cavity in the middle.
