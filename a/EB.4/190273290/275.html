SHAMMY

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHAMMY, or CHAMOIS-LEATHER, a kind of leather, either dressed in oil or tanned; and much esteemed for its softness, pliancy, and being capable of bearing soap without hurt.—The true shammy is prepared of the skin of the chamois-goat, a species of CAPRA.
