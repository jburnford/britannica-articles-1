SHEERING

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHEERING, in the sea-language. When a ship is not steered readily, they say she sheers, or goes sheering; or when, at anchor, she goes in and out by means of the current of the tide, they also say she sheers.
