LYMINGTON

LYMINGTON, a town of Hampshire in England situated on a hill near the sea, in W. Long. 1.

Macbride. 45. N. Lat. 50. 45. It sends two members to parliament, and is remarkable for the great quantities of sea-salt made in the neighbourhood. At these salt-works are prepared great quantities of sal catharticus amarus, which being shot into large crystals is sold for true Glauber's salt, under the name of Ly-mington's salts.
