CLASS I. MAMMALIA

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLASS I. MAMMALIA, includes all animals that suckle their young. The characters of this class are these:—The heart has two ventricles and two auricles; the blood is red and

warm; and the animals belonging to it are viviparous. Mammalia.
