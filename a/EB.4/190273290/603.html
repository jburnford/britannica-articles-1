SOPORIFIC

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOPORIFIC, or SOPORIFEROUS, a medicine that has the faculty of procuring sleep. Such are opium, laudanum, &c. The word is formed from the Latin sopor, "sleep." The Greeks in lieu hereof use the word hypnotic.
