STATUTE

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STATUTE, in our laws and customs, more immediately signifies an act of parliament made by the three estates of the realm; and such statutes are either ge-

neral, of which the courts at Westminster must take notice without pleading them; or they are special and private, which last must be pleaded.
