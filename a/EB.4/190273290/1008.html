SUBSCRIPTION

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUBSCRIPTION, in general, signifies the signature put at the bottom of a letter, writing, or instrument.

In commerce, it is used for the share or interest which particular persons take in a public stock or a trading-company, by writing their names, and the shares they require, in the books or register thereof.
