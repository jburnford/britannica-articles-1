VERJUICE

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERJUICE, a liquor obtained from grapes or apples, unfit for wine or cyder; or from sweet ones, whilst yet acid and unripe. Its chief use is in sauces, ragouts, &c, tho' it is also an ingredient in some medicinal compositions, and is used by the wax-chandlers to purify their wax.
