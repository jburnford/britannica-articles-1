BOTANICAL TABLE, (Encycl.) In this table it was omitted to mark with their proper numbers, as proposed, the genera included in the 58 Orders belonging to Linnæus's Fragments of a Natural Method of Classification. In the following recapitulation of those genera, that omission is supplied; the figures added to each genus denoting the natural order to which it belongs: and these numbers or figures may be marked with the pen at the corresponding genera in the Table itself.
