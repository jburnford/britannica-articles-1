SYNGENESIA

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SYNGENESIA, (avis, together, and γένεσις, generation), the name of the 19th class in Linnaeus's Sexual Method, consisting of plants in which the antheræ or male organs of generation are united into a cylinder; the filaments on which they are supported being separate and distinct.
