POTSDAM

article · 359 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POTSDAM, or POSTDAM, a town of Germany in the circle of Upper Saxony, with a palace, belonging to the king of Prussia. It is seated in an island ten miles in circumference, formed by the rivers Sprae and Havel.

Predestinarian. Havel. The palace is very curious, and finely built upon a delightful spot 12 miles west of Berlin. E. Long. 13. 42. N. Lat. 52. 34.
