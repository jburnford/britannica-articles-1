SEINE

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEINE, a river of France, which, arising near Dijon in Burgundy, runs north-west through Champagne and the Isle of France, through Paris, &c. and, crossing Normandy, falls into the British Channel between Havre de Grace and Honfleur.
