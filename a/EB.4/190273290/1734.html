TRIESTE

TRIESTE, a sea-port town of Idria, seated on the gulph of Venice, and belonging to the house of Austria. It was formerly a free city, and is situated on the declivity of a rock, eight miles north of Capo d'Idria, and seventy-two miles north-east of Venice. E. Long. 14. 12. N. Lat. 46. 10.
