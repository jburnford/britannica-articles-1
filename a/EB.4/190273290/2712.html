ZYGOPHYLLUM

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZYGOPHYLLUM, BEAN CAPER; a genus of the monogynia order belonging to the decandria class of plants. There are eight species, partly shrubby and partly herbaceous plants, all natives of warm climates, though some of them are hardy enough to endure the open air in this country.
