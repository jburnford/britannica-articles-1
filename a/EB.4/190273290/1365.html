TERMINI

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TERMINI, in architecture, denote a kind of statues or columns, adorned a-top with the figure of a man's, woman's, or satyr's head, as a capital; and the lower part ending in a kind of sheath or scabbard.
