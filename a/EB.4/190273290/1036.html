SUESSIONES

SUESSIONES, a branch of the Remi, a people of Gallia Belgica, (Pliny); called sometimes Sueffones, in the lower age Sueffi; situated between the Remi to

the east, the Nervii to the north, the Veromandui to the west, and the Meldae to the south, in the tract now called la Soissonnois.—Sueffones, Sueffones, and Sueffone, the name of their city in the lower age; thought to have been formerly called Noviodunum, (Cæsar.) Now Soissons.
