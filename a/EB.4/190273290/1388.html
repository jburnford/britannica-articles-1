TEST-ACT

article · 342 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TEST-AT, a statute 25 Car. II. cap. 2. which requires all officers, both civil and military, to take the oaths and test, viz. the sacrament according to the rites and ceremonies of the church of England; for the neglect whereof, a person executing any office mentioned in that statute, forfeits the sum of 500l. recoverable by action of debt.
