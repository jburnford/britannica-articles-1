WORT, the infusion of malt, of which beer is made.—The uses of this infusion in common affairs is well known. By Dr McBride it has lately been found to have a strong antiseptic virtue, and to be useful in preventing the scurvy and other diseases to which sailors are liable; which was confirmed by capt. Cook in his late voyages. See Means of preserving the health of SEAMEN.
