VERMEYEN

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERMEYEN (John Cornelius), a Dutch historian, and portrait-painter. He attended the emperor Charles V. and drew his encampments, sieges, and battles, on the spot, from which designs the famous tapestries at the Escorial at Madrid were made. He died in 1559.
