TRINITY SUNDAY

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRINITY SUNDAY, a festival observed on the Sunday next after Whitsunday, in honour of the holy Trinity. The observation of this festival was first enjoined in the council of Arles, anno 1260.
