SERGE

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SERGE, a woollen stuff, manufactured in a loom; of which there are various kinds, denominated either from their different qualities, or from the places where they are wrought. The most considerable is the London serge, which is highly valued abroad; and of which a manufacture has for some years been carried on in France.
