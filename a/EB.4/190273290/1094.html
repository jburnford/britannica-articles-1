SUPPORTERS

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUPPORTERS, in heraldry, figures in an achievement placed by the side of the shield, and seeming to support or hold up the same. Supporters are chiefly figures of beasts: figures of human creatures for the like purpose are called tenants.
