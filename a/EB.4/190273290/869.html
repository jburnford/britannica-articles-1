GGK, the lower counter, with HH, its gun-ports.

G, the rail which separates the lower counter from the second or upper counter; which last is included between G and C.

KK, the wing-transom.

LL, the deck-transom.

M, N, O, first, second, and third transoms; the 4th, 5th, and 6th transoms are placed immediately under these: and that which lies between the wing and deck-transoms, is called the filling transom.
