TEXEL, a town of the United Provinces, in N. Holland, seated at the mouth of the Zuyder-Zee, with a good harbour, and a strong fort. It is seated in an island, which is separated from the continent of Holland by a narrow channel, through which most of the ships pass that are bound to Amsterdam. E. Long. 4. 25. N. Lat. 53. 12.
