VERDEN

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERDEN, a considerable town of Germany, in the circle of Westphalia, and capital of a duchy of the same name, subject to the elector of Hanover. It is seated on the river Aller near the Weier, in W. Long. 9. 10. N. Lat. 53. 30.
