SYNAGOGUE

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SYNAGOGUE, a particular assembly of the Jews met to perform the offices of their religion: Also the place wherein they meet. The word is Greek, συναγωγή, which literally signifies an assembly or congregation.
