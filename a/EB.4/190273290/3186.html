PEZIZA

article · 164 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PEZIZA, or MUSHROOM, in botany, a genus of the class of cryptogamia, and order of fungi. This mushroom is bell-shaped and sessile. Linnæus enumerates eight species.
