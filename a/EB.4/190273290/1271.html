TARACON, an ancient, populous, and large town of France, in Provence, with a well-built castle, and a chapter composed of 15 canons; seated on the river Rhone, in a pleasant fertile country, over-against Beaucaire, with which it communicates by a bridge of boats. E. Long. 4. 45. N. Lat. 43. 48.
