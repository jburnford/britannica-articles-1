URINAL

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

URINAL, in medicine, a vessel fit to receive and hold urine, and used accordingly for the convenience of sick persons. It is usually of glass, and crooked; and sometimes it is filled with milk, to allay the pain of the gravel.
