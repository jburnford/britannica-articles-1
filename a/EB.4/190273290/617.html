SOVEREIGN

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOVEREIGN, in matters of government, is applied to the supreme magistrate or magistrates of an independent government or state; by reason their authority is only bounded by the laws of God, of nature, and the fundamental laws of the state: such are kings, princes, &c.
