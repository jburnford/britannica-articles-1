STEM

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STEM, in botany, that part of a plant arising out of the root, and which sustains the leaves, flowers, fruits, &c. By washing and rubbing the stems of trees their annual increase is promoted; for the method of doing which, see the article TREE.
