VOLATILISATION

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOLATILISATION, the art of rendering fixed bodies volatile, or of resolving them by fire into a fine subtle vapour or spirit which easily dissipates and flies away. All bodies, even the most fixed, as gold, may be volatilized, either of themselves, or with the admixture of some volatile substance or spirit, by distillation or sublimation.
