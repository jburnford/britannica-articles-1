SUCCOTH

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUCCOTH, (anc. geog.); a place beyond Jordan, over-against Zortan on this side; so called from Jacob's fixing his tents there.—Another Succoth, the first mansion of the Israelites after their departure from Rameses towards the Red Sea.
