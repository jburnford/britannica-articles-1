VENERY

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VENERY, is used for the act of copulation, or coition, of the two sexes.

VENERY also denotes the act or exercise of hunting wild beasts, which are also called beasts of venery, and beasts of the forest.
