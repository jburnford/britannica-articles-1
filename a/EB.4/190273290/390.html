SILPHIUM, BASTARD CHRYSANTHEMUM; a genus of the polygamia necessaria order, belonging to the syngenesia clas of plants. There are six species; all of them herbaceous perennials, rising from three to nine feet high, with compound radiated flowers of a yellow colour. They are all very hardy, and easily propagated by parting their roots in autumn.
