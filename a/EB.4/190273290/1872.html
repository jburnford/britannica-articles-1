TYPE, a copy, image, or resemblance of some model. The word is much used among divines, to signify a symbol, sign, or figure of something to come; in which sense it is commonly used with relation to antitype,

Type
Tyrral. titype, which is the thing itself, whereof the other is a type or figure.
