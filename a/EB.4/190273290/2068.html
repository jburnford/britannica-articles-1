VILLAGE

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VILLAGE, an assemblage of houses inhabited chiefly by peasants and farmers, and having no market, whereby it is distinguished from a town. The word is French, formed of vill, or villis, "low, mean, contemptible;" or rather, from the Latin villa, a country-house or farm.
