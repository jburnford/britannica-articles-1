WAVED

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WAVED, in heraldry, is said of a bordure, or any ordinary or charge, in a coat of arms, having its outlines indented in manner of the rising and falling of waves: it is used to denote, that the first of the family

in whose arms it stands, acquired its honours for sea-service.
