VELLEITY

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VELLEITY, in the school-philosophy, is usually defined a languid, cold, and remiss will. Others say it denotes an impotence of acquiring what we desire.

VELOCITY of a BODY, denotes the quantity of motion necessary to make it run over a certain space in a given time.
