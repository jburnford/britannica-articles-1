TEUTONIC

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TEUTONIC, something belonging to the Teutones. The Teutonic is supposed to have been the language of the ancient Germans, and hence is reckoned amongst the mother-tongues. The German or Dutch is still called the Teutonic.
