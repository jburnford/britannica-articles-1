SEQUANI

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEQUANI, a people anciently forming a part of Gallia Celtica, but annexed to Belgica by Augustus, separated from the Helvetii by mount Jura, with the Rhine on the east (Strabo), bordering on the Ædui, and Segustiano to the south, and Lingones to the west, (Tacitus). Now the Franche Comte.
