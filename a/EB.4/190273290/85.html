SEGNA

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEGNA, or SEGNI, a strong town of Croatia, with a strong fort, a good harbour, and a bishop's see. It belongs to the house of Austria, and is seated on the coast of the Gulf of Venice. E. Long. 15. 2. N. Lat. 45. 4.
