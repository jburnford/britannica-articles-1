USHER

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

USHER, an officer or servant who has the care and direction of the door of a court, hall, chamber, or the like.

USHER of the Black Rod, the eldest of the gentlemen ushers, daily waiters at court, whose duty is to bear the rod before the king at the feast of St George and other solemnities.
