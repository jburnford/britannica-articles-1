ANEMONE, (Encycl.) Horned cattle, when removed from the higher grounds into woods and woody pastures, frequently eat the wood-anemone; and, according to Linnæus and Gunner, many observations have proved that it causes the bloody flux among them.

Sea-ANEMONE. See ANIMAL-Flower and SEA-ANEMONE, (Encycl.)
