HARMATTAN, a very extraordinary kind of wind and fog on the coast of Guinea, of which an account, taken from Dr Lind's works, is given under the article MEDICINE, p. 4657. col. 2. An account remarkably different, however, from that of the Doctor, may be seen in the Philosophical Transactions, vol. lxxi.
