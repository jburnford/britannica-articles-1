SESTOS

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SESTOS, a noted fortress of European Turkey, situated at the entrance of the Hellespont or Dardanelles, 24 miles south-west of Gallipoli. This place is famous for the loves of HERO and LEANDER, sung by the poet MUSEUS.
