BEREANS, in ancient church history, the inhabitants of Berea. They are highly commended in Scripture for their ready reception of the gospel, upon a fair and impartial examination of its agreement with the Old Testament prophecies. Soper, a Berean, attended the apostle Paul to Asia. Acts xvii. 10—13, and xx. 4.
