EXPONENTIAL Curve

sub_entry · 139 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXPONENTIAL Curve, is that whose nature is defined or expressed by an exponential equation; as the curve denoted by a^x = y, or by x^x = y.
