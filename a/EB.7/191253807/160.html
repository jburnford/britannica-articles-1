CATHARINE II

article · 375 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATHARINE II. empress of all the Russias, acted so conspicuous a part on the theatre of the world; possessed such uncommon powers of mind, highly cultivated by science and literature; and was such a patroness of science and literature in others—that it cannot be deemed foreign from a work of this nature to give some account of the principal events of her more private life.
