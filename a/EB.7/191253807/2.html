ABBREVIATION OF FRACTIONS

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABBREVIATION OF FRACTIONS, in arithmetic and algebra, is the reducing of them to lower terms; which is done by dividing the numerator and denominator by some number or quantity which will divide both without leaving a remainder of either.
