FOLIATE

article · 301 chars · page image at NLS ↗

FOLIATE, a name given by some to a curve of the 2d order, expressed by the equation x^2 + y^2 = axy, being one species of defective hyperbolas, with one asymptote, and consisting of two infinite legs crossing each other, forming a sort of leaf. It is the 42d species of Newton's Lines of the 3d Order.
