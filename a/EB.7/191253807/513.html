IMAGINARY ROOTS

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMAGINARY ROOTS of an equation, are those roots or values of the unknown quantity, which contain some imaginary quantity. Thus, the roots of the equation xx + aa = 0, are the two imaginary quantities \pm\sqrt{-aa} and \pm\sqrt{-aa}, or \pm a\sqrt{-1} and \mp a\sqrt{-1}.
