DEFERENS

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEFERENS, or DEFERENT, in the ancient astronomy, an imaginary circle, which, as it were, carries about the body of a planet, and is the same with the eccentric; being invented to account for the eccentricity, perigee, and apogee of the planets.
