APOCATASTASIS

article · 227 chars · page image at NLS ↗

APOCATASTASIS, or, as it should be written, APOKATASTASIS, is a Greek word employed in the language of astronomers, to denote the period of a planet, or the time it takes to return to that point of the zodiac whence it set out.
