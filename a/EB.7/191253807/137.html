CANABAC

article · 410 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANABAC, an island lying contiguous to BULAM on the western coast of Africa, and inhabited by a fierce people, governed by two kings or chiefs. It would appear that the Canabacs had been very troublesome to their neighbours, since the inhabitants of some other islands in that cluster rejoiced at the settlement of the English in Bulam, hoping to find in them a defence against the usurpations of the Canabacs.
