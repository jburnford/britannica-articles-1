HORSE-SHORE

article · 304 chars · page image at NLS ↗

HORSE-SHORE, in fortification, is a work sometimes of a round, sometimes of an oval figure, inclosed with a parapet, raised in the ditch of a marshy place, or in low grounds; sometimes also to cover a gate; or to serve as a lodgment for soldiers, to prevent surprises, or relieve an over tedious defence.
