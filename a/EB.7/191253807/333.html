EMINENTIAL EQUATION

article · 234 chars · page image at NLS ↗

EMINENTIAL EQUATION, a term used by some algebraists, in the investigation of the areas of curvilinear figures, for a kind of assumed equation that contains another equation eminently, the latter being a particular case of the former.
