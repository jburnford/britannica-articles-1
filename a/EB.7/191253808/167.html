MORION

article · 277 chars · page image at NLS ↗

MORION, in ancient mineralogy, a name given to one of the semipellucid gems, more commonly called prassion. It is a stone appearing externally of a fine deep black; but when held up against a candle, or against the sun-beams, it gives a very beautiful red in different degrees.
