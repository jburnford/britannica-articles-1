Arithmetical MEAN

sub_entry · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Arithmetical MEAN, is half the sum of the extremes. So 4 is an arithmetical mean between 2 and 6, or between 3 and 5, or between 1 and 7; also an arithmetical mean between a and b is \frac{a+b}{2}, or \frac{1}{2}a + \frac{1}{2}b.
