TACQUET

article · 204 chars · page image at NLS ↗

TACQUET (Andrew), a Jesuit of Antwerp, who died in 1660. He was a most laborious and voluminous writer in mathematics. His works were collected, and printed at Antwerp, in one large volume in folio, 1669.
