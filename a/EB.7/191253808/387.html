REPETEND

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REPETEND, in arithmetic, denotes that part of an infinite decimal fraction, which is continually repeated ad infinitum. Thus in the numbers 2.13 13 13 &c. the figures 13 are the repetend, and marked thus 13.
