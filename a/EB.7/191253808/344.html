PROSTHAPHERESIS

article · 354 chars · page image at NLS ↗

PROSTHAPHERESIS, in astronomy, the difference between the true and mean motion, or between the true and mean place, of a planet, or between the true and equated anomaly; called also equation of the orbit, or equation of the centre, or simply the equation; and it is equal to the angle formed at the planet, and subtended by the eccentricity of its orbit.
