MOINEAU

article · 283 chars · page image at NLS ↗

MOINEAU, a flat bastion raised before a curtain when it is too long, and the bastions of the angles too remote to be able to defend one another. Sometimes the moineau is joined to the curtain, and sometimes it is divided from it by a moat. Here musquetry are placed to fire each way.
