PYRAMIDOID

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PYRAMIDOID, is sometimes used for the parabolic spindle, or the solid formed by the rotation of a semiparabola about its base or greatest ordinate. See Parabolic Spindle.
