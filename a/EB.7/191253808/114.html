MASUAH (See MASSUAH, Encycl.) is in latitude 15^{\circ} 35' 5'' north, and in longitude 39^{\circ} 36' 30'' east of Greenwich. On the 22d of September 1769 Mr Bruce found the variation of the needle at Masuah to be 12^{\circ} 48' west.
