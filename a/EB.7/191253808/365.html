RATIONAL

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RATIONAL, in arithmetic, &c. the quality of numbers, fractions, quantities, &c. when they can be expressed by common numbers; in contradistinction to irrational or surd ones, which cannot be expressed in common numbers.
