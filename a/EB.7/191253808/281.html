PARALLAX

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARALLAX (see Encycl.) is used, not only in astronomy, but also in levelling, for the angle contained between the line of true level, and that of apparent level. And, in other branches of science, for the difference between the true and apparent places.
