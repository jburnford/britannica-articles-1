LOXODROMIC CURVE

article · 304 chars · page image at NLS ↗

LOXODROMIC CURVE, or Spiral, is the same as the rhumb line, or path of a ship sailing always on the same course in an oblique direction, or making always the same angle with every meridian. It is a species of logarithmic spiral, described on the surface of the sphere, having the meridians for its radii.
