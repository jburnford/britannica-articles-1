THUS

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THUS, in sea language, a word used by the pilot in directing the helmsman or steersman to keep the ship in her present situation when sailing with a scant wind, so that she may not approach too near the direction of the wind, which would shiver her sails, nor fall to leeward, and run farther out of her course.
