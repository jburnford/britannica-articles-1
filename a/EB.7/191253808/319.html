PHOSPHORUS

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHOSPHORUS, in astroonomy, is the morning star, or the planet Venus, when she rises before the sun. The Latins call it Lucifer, the French Etoile de berger, and the Greeks Phosphorus.
