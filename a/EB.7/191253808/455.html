SEIL

article · 278 chars · page image at NLS ↗

SEIL, in building, is of two kinds, viz. Ground Sell, which denotes the lowest piece of timber in a wooden building, and that upon which the whole superstructure is raised; and Sell of a Window, or of a Door, which is the bottom piece in the frame of them, upon which they rest.
