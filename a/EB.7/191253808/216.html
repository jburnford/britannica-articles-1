OBLONG SPHEROID

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OBLONG SPHEROID, is that which is formed by an ellipse revolved about its longer or transverse axis; in contradistinction from the oblate spheroid, or that which is flattened at its poles, being generated by the revolution of the ellipse about its conjugate or shorter axis.
