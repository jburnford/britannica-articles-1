PARABOLOIDES

article · 229 chars · page image at NLS ↗

PARABOLOIDES, parabolas of the higher orders. The equation for all curves of this kind being x^{m-n} x^n = y^m, the proportion of the area of any one to the complement of it to the circumscribing parallelogram, will be as m to n.
