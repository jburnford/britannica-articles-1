KING-POST

article · 217 chars · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ROOF

KING-POST, or KING-PIECE, is a piece of timber set upright in the middle, between two principal rafters, and having struts or braces going from it to the middle of each rafter. See ROOF, Encycl.; and CARPENTRY, Suppl.
