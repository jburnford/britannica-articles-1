SCIOPTIC

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCIOPTIC, or SCIOPTIC BALL, a sphere or globe of wood, with a circular hole or perforation, where a lens is placed. It is so fitted, that, like the eye of an animal, it may be turned round every way, to be used in making experiments of the darkened room.
