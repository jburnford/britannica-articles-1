SUNNUD, a grant, patent, or charter, in Bengal. SUPERPARTICULAR PROPORTION, or RATIO, is that in which the greater term exceeds the less by unit or 1. As the ratio of 1 to 2, or 2 to 3, or 3 to 4, &c.
