RECTIFICATION

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RECTIFICATION, in geometry, is the finding of a right line equal to a curve. The rectification of curves is a branch of the higher geometry, a branch in which the use of the inverse method of fluxions is especially useful.
