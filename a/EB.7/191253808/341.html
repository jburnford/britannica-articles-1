PRISMOID

Cross-references: CORRECTION-HOUSE

PRISMOID, is a solid or body, somewhat resembling a prism, but that its ends are any dissimilar parallel plane figures of the same number of sides; the upright sides being trapezoids.—If the ends of the prismoid be bounded by dissimilar curves, it is sometimes called a cylindroid.
