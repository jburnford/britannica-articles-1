NEBULOUS

article · 371 chars · page image at NLS ↗

NEBULOUS, or CLOUDY, a term applied to certain fixed stars which show a dim hazy light; being less than those of the sixth magnitude, and therefore scarcely visible to the naked eye, to which at best they only appear like little dusky specks or clouds. Through a moderate telescope, these nebulous stars plainly appear to be congeries or clusters of several little stars.
