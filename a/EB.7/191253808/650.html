VITALITY

article · 328 chars · page image at NLS ↗

VITALITY, the power of subsisting in life, which the fashionable philosophers of the French and German schools attribute to chemistry. For a confutation of their absurd and impious jargon on this subject, we refer our readers, with some degree of confidence, to the articles PHYSIOLOGY (Encycl.), and Animal Substances (Suppl.).
