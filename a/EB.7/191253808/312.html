PERPENDICULAR

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERPENDICULAR, in gunnery, is a small instrument, used for finding the centre line of a piece in the operation of pointing it to a given object.

PERSIAN or PERSIC, in architecture, a name common to all statues of men, serving instead of columns to support entablatures.
