REGULUS, in astronomy, a star of the first mag-

nitude, in the constellation Leo; called also, from its situation, Cor Leonis, or the Lion's Heart; by the Arabs, Alhabor; and by the Chaldeans, Kalbeled, or Karbeleed; from an opinion of its influencing the affairs of the heavens.
