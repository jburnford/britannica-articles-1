SARRASIN

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SARRASIN, or SARRAZIN, in fortification, a kind of port-cullis, otherwise called a herse, which is hung with ropes over the gate of a town or fortrefs, to be let fall in case of a surprise.
