BUGIE

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUGIE, a port-town of Egypt, situated on the western shore of the Red-sea, almost opposite to Ziden, the port-town to Mecca, and about 100 miles west of it; E. long. 36^{\circ}, N. lat. 22^{\circ}.
