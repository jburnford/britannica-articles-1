AFFORAGE

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AFFORAGE, in the French customs, a duty paid to the lord of a district, for permission to sell wine or other liquors within his seignory. It is also used for the rate or price of provisions fixed by the provost of Paris, or by the sheriffs.
