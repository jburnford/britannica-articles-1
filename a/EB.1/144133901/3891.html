BARTON

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARTON, a market-town in Lincolnshire, situated on the southern shore of the Humber, 30 miles south-east of York, in 15° W. long. and 53° 40' N. lat.

BARTON is also used, in the west of England, for the demesne lands of a manor; also for the manor-house; and in some parts for out-houses, &c.
