ACHILLEA, in botany, a genus of plants belonging to the syngenesia polygamia superflua class. Of this genus there are 21 species, only two of which are natives of Britain, viz. the achillea millefolium, or common yarrow, (see Plate II. fig. 1.) and the achillea ptarmica, or sneezewort.
