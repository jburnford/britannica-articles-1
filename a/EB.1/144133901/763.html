ADJUTANTS-general

sub_entry · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADJUTANTS-general, among the Jesuits, a select number of fathers, residing with the general of the order, each of whom has a province or country assigned him, as England, Holland, &c. and their business is to inform the father-general of state-occurrences in such countries.
