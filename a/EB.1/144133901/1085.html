AGISTMENT

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGISTMENT, AGISTAGE, or AGISTATION, in law, the taking in other people's cattle to graze at so much per week. It is also used in a metaphorical sense, for any tax, burthen, or charge; thus, the tax levied for repairing the banks of Romney marsh was called agistamentum.
