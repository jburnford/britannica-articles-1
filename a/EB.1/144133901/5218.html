BRIGANTINE

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRIGANTINE, a small light vessel, which can both row and sail well, and is either for fighting or giving chase. It hath about twelve or fifteen benches for the rowers, one man to a bench; all the hands aboard are soldiers, and each man hath his musquet lying ready under his oar.
