BLUE-Japan. Take gum-water, what quantity you please, and white lead a sufficient quantity; grind them well upon a porphyry; then take isinglass size what quantity you please, of the finest and best smalt a sufficient quantity, mix them well; to which add, of your white lead, before ground, so much as may give it a sufficient body; mix all these together to the consistence of a paint.
