BRONCHOTOMY

Cross-references: SURGERY

BRONCHOTOMY, in surgery, an incision made in the aspera arteria, or wind-pipe, which is necessary in many cases, and especially in a violent quinsy, to prevent suffocation from the great inflammation or tumor of the parts. It is also called laryngotomy and tracheotomy. See SURGERY.
