AXYRIS

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AXYRIS, in botany, a genus of the monœcia triandra class. The calix of the male is tripartite; it has no corolla. The calix of the female consists of two leaves; it has two styli, and one seed. The species are 4, none of them natives of Britain.
