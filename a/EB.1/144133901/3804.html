BARDESANISTS

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARDESANISTS, in church-history, Christian heretics of the second century, who maintained that the devil was a self-existent independent being; that Jesus Christ was not born of a woman, but brought his body with him from heaven; and denied the resurrection of the body.
