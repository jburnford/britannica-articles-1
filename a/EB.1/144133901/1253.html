AJUGA

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AJUGA, in botany, a genus of the didynamia gymnosperma class. There are four species of the ajuga, of which the reptans or bugle, and the pyramidalis or mountain bugle, are natives of Britain.
