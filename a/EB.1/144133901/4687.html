Turnsole BLUE

sub_entry · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Turnsole BLUE is used in painting on wood, and is made of the seed of the turnsole: The way of preparing it is, to boil four ounces of turnsole in a pint and half of water in which lime has been slacked.
