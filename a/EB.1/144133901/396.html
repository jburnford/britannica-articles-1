ACCOUNTING-HOUSE

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCOUNTING-HOUSE, counting-house, or compting-house, is a house, or office, set apart by a merchant, or trading-company, for transacting their business, as well as keeping their books, accounts, vouchers, &c.
