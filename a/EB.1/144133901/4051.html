BAY

BAY, in geography, an arm of the sea shooting up into the land, and terminating in a nook. It is a kind of lesser gulf, bigger than a creek, and is larger in its middle within than at its entrance. The largest and most noted bays in the world are those of Biscay, Bengal, Hudson's, Panama, &c.
