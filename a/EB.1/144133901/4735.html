BOERHAAVIA

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOERHAAVIA, in botany, a genus of the monandria monogynia class. It has no calix; the corolla consists of one bell-shaped plaited petal; and there is but one naked seed. There are six species, all natives of the Indies.
