BOATSWAIN'S mats

sub_entry · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOATSWAIN'S mats has the peculiar command of the long boat, for the setting forth of anchors, weighing or fetching home an anchor, warping, towing, or mooring; and is to give an account of his store.
