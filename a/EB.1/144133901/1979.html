AMOEBÆUM

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMOEBÆUM, in ancient poetry, a kind of poem, representing a dispute between two persons who are made to answer each other alternately; such are the third and seventh of Virgil's eclogues.
