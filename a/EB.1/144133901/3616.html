BALI

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALI, an island in the East Indies, situated in 114° E. long. and 7° 30' S. lat. This island, and the east end of the island of Java, form a straight about a mile over, of extremely difficult passage.
