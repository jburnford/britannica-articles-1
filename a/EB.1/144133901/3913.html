BASELLA

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASELLA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria trigynia class. It has no calix; the corolla has 6 divisions; and there is but one seed in the capsule. The species are 3, viz. the rubra, alba, and lucida, all natives of India.
