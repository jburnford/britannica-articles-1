BALLOTADE

article · 306 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALLOTADE, in the menage, the leap of a horse between two pillars, or upon a straight line, made with justness of time, with the aid of the hand, and the calves of the legs; and in such a manner, that when his fore-feet are in the air, he shews nothing but the shoes of his hinder-feet without jerking out.
