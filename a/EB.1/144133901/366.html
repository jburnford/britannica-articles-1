ACCOMPAGNAGE

article · 360 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCOMPAGNAGE, a term in the silk manufactures, signifying a fine woof of the same colour with the gilding, helping to enrich the ground under which it passes, and to hinder it from striking cross the gilding itself, which would diminish its gloss and lustre. All rich stuffs, the warps whereof are of a colour different from the gilding, should be accompanied.
