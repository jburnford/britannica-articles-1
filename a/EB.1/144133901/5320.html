BRUNIA

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRUNIA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria monogynia class. The flowers are aggregated; the filaments of the stamens are inserted into the ungues of the petals; the stigma is bifid, and the seeds are single. There are six species, all natives of Ethiopia.
