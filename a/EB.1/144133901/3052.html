ARTEDIA

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARTEDIA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria digynia class. The involucre is pinnatifid; the floccules of the disk are masculine, and the fruit is rough. There is only one species, viz. the squamata, a native of Libanium.
