BRITAIN

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRITAIN, or GREAT-BRITAIN, the most considerable of all the European islands, lies between 50^{\circ} and 60^{\circ} N. lat. and between 2^{\circ} E. long. and 6^{\circ} W. long.

The general division of Britain, is into South and North Britain, or England and Scotland.
