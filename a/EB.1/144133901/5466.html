BUNTZLAU

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUNTZLAU, or BUNTZEL, the name of two towns in Germany: the old town is situated on the river Elbe, and new town, which is become the most considerable, upon the Gizare, eight leagues from Lignitz, in 16° 26' E. long. and 51° 12' N. latitude. There is likewise a town of that name in Silesia.
