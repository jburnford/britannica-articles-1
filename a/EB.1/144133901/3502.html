AZURE

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AZURE, in heraldry, the blue colour in the arms of any person below the rank of a baron. In the escutcheon of a nobleman, it is called sapphire; and in that of a sovereign prince, Jupiter. In engraving, this colour is expressed by lines, or strokes drawn horizontally.
