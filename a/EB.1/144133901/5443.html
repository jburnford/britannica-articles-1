BULL in cena Domini

sub_entry · 388 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BULL in cena Domini, a particular bull read every year, on the day of the Lord's supper, or Maundy Thursday, in the pope's presence, containing excommunications and anathemas against heretics, and all who disturb or oppose the jurisdiction of the holy see.

After

After the reading of the bull, the pope throws a burning torch into the public place, to denote the thunder of this anathema.
