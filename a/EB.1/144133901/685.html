ADDA

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADDA, in geography, a river of Switzerland and Italy, which rises in mount Braulio, in the country of the Grisons, and passing through the Valteline, traverses the lake Como and the Milanese, and falls into the Po, near Cremona.
