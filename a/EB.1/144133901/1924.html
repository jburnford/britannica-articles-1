AMERSHAM

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMERSHAM, a market-town of Buckinghamshire, about twenty-seven miles westward of London. It is situated in 40^{\circ} W. long. and 51^{\circ} 40' N. lat. and sends two members to parliament.
