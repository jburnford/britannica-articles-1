ABSCEDENTIA

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSCEDENTIA, in surgery, a term applied to decayed parts of the body, which, in a morbid state, are separated from the sound, or lose that union which was preserved in a natural state.
