BELGRADE

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BELGRADE, the capital of the province of Servia, in European Turkey, situated on the south side of the Danube, in E. long. 21^{\circ} 20', and N. lat. 45^{\circ}. It was yielded to the Turks in 1739.
