ADOUR

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADOUR, the name of a river of France, which rises in the mountains of Bigorre, and running N. by Tarbes through Gascogne, afterwards turns E. and, passing by Dax, falls into the bay of Biscay, below Bayonne.
