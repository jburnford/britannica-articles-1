BOARD-ode

sub_entry · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOARD-ode, a service required of tenants to carry timber out of the woods of the lord to his house. It is also used to signify the quantity of provision which the bordarii or bordmen paid for their bord lands.
