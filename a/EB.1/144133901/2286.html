ANGERONALIA

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGERONALIA, in antiquity, feasts celebrated at Rome in honour of Angerona, the goddess of silence and patience. They were instituted, according to Macrobius, in consequence of a vow, when the people were afflicted with the quinz. They were held on the 21st of December.
