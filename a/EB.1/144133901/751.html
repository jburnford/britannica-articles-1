ADIANTHUM

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADIANTHUM, in botany, a genus of the cryptogamia filices, of which there are 19 species, and only two of them natives of Britain, viz. the adianthum capillus veneris, or true maiden-hair, and the trapeziforme, or shining maiden-hair.
