ALLA

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALLA, or ALLAH, the name by which all the professors of Mahometanism call the Supreme Being. The term alla is Arabic, derived from the verb alah, to

to adore. It is the same with the Hebrew elohim, which signifies the adorable Being.
