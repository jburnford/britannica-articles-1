AMSTERDAM, is also the name of a town of the Curacoas, in America: likewise the name of three islands, one of which lies in the Indian ocean, between New Holland and Madagascar; the second between Peru and

and the islands of Solomon; and the third in the Chinese sea, between Japan, and the island Formosa.
