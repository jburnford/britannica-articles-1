ANGEL

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGEL, in commerce, the name of an ancient gold coin in England, of which some are still to be seen in the cabinets of the curious. It had its name from the figure of an angel represented upon it. It was 23½ carats, and weighed four penny-weights. Its value differed in different reigns.
