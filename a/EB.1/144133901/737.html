ADERBIJAN

ADERBIGAN, a province of Persia, bounded on the N. by Armenia Proper, on the S. by Irak-Agemi, on the E. by Ghilan, and on the W. by Curdistan. The principal town is Tauris, from 42. to 48. long. from 36. to 39. lat.
