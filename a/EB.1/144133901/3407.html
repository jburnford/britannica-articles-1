AVOWEE

Cross-references: ADVOWSON · INTRUSION

AVOWEE, one who has a right to present to a benefice. See ADVOWSON.

He is thus called in contradistinction to those who only have the lands to which the advowson belongs for a term of years, or by virtue of intrusion or disseisin. See INTRUSION, &c.
