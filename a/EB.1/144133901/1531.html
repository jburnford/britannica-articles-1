Oath of ALLEGIANCE

sub_entry · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Oath of ALLEGIANCE, in the British policy, that taken in acknowledgment of the king as a temporal prince; as the oath of supremacy acknowledges him for the supreme head of the church.
