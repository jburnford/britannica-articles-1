BARBED

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBED, and CRESTED, in heraldry, an appellation given to the combs and gills of a cock, when particularized for being of a different tincture from the body.

A barbed cross, is a cross, the extremities whereof are like the barbed irons used for striking of fish. See Plate LI. fig. 6.
