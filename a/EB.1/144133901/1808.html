AMAND

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMAND, or ST AMAND, the name of two towns, one situated in the duchy of Bourbon, in the province of Lyonnais in France; and the other in French Flanders, about six miles N. of Valenciennes.
