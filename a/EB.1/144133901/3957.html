BASSOON

article · 351 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASSOON, a musical instrument of the wind-sort, blown with a reed, furnished with eleven holes, and used as a bass in a concert of hautboys, flutes, &c.

To render this instrument more portable, it is divided into two parts, whence it is also called a fagot. Its diameter at bottom is nine inches, and its holes are stopped like those of a large flute.
