BUGGASINS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUGGASINS, in commerce, a name given to buckrams made of callico: these pay a duty, on importation, of 1s. 2. \frac{1}{2}d. the half piece; whereof 1s. \frac{1}{2}d. is drawn back on exportation.
