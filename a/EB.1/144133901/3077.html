ARUNDEL

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARUNDEL, a town of Sussex, situated on a river of the same name, in 30^{\circ} W. long. and 50^{\circ} 45' N. lat. It gives the title of earl to the noble family of the Howards, and sends two members to parliament.
