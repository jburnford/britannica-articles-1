BLANKENBURG

article · 146 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLANKENBURG is also the name of a town in lower Saxony, about forty-five miles south-east of Wolfembattle, in 11° 15' E. long. and 51° 50' N. lat.
