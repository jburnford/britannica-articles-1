ANINGA

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANINGA, in commerce, a root which grows in the Antilles islands, and is pretty much like the China plant. It is used by sugar-bakers, for refining the sugar; and is more effectual, and less dangerous, than the sublimate of mercury and arsenic.
