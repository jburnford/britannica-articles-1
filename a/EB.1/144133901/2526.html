ANTI

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTI, a Greek preposition, which enters into the composition of several words, both Latin, French, and English, in different senses. Sometimes it signifies before, as in anti-chamber; and sometimes opposite or contrary, as in the names of these medicines, anti-scorbutics, anti-venereal.
