ASPALATHUS

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASPALATHUS, or ROSE-WOOD, in botany, a genus of the diadelphia decandria class. The calix consists of five divisions; the pod is oval, and contains two seeds. There are 19 species, none of them natives of Britain.
