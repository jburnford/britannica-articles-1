ANN

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

ANN, or ANNAT, in Scots law, is half a year's stipend, which the law gives to the executors of ministers of the church of Scotland, over and above what was due to the minister himself, for his incumbency. See SCOTS LAW, title, Ecclesiastical persons.
