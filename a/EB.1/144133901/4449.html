BIGNONIA

BIGNONIA, in botany, a genus of the didynamia angiospermia class. The calix consists of five segments, and is shaped like a cup; the faux of the corolla is bell-shaped, and divided into five segments; the pod has two cells, and the seeds are membranaceous, and alated. The species are seventeen, all natives of America and the Indies.
