BODMIN

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BODMIN, a borough-town of Cornwall, about twenty-six miles north-east of Falmouth, in 5° 10' W. long. and 50° 32' N. lat. It sends two members to parliament, and gives the title of viscount to the earl of Radnor.
