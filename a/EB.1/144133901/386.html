Chamber of ACCOUNTS

sub_entry · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Chamber of ACCOUNTS, in the French polity, is a sovereign court of great antiquity, which takes cognizance of, and registers the accounts of the king's revenue. It is nearly the same with the English Court of EXCHEQUER; which see.
