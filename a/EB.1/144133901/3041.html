ARRIERE fee

sub_entry · 356 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARRIERE fee, or sef, is a fee dependant on a superior one. These fees commenced, when the dukes and counts, under their governments hereditary in their families, distributed to their officers parts of the royal domains, which they found in their respective provinces; and even permitted those officers to gratify the soldiers under them in the same manner.
