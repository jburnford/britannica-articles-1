BARBACAN

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBACAN, in architecture, a canal, or opening left in the wall, for water to come in and go out, when buildings are erected in places liable to be overflowed, or to drain off the water from a terrace, or the like.
