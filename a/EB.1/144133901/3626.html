BALL and facket

sub_entry · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALL and facket is an instrument made of brass, with a perpetual screw, so as to move horizontally, vertically, and obliquely; and is generally used for the managing of surveying, and astronomical instruments.
