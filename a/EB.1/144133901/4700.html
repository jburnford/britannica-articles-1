Barrel-BOARDS

sub_entry · 420 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Barrel-BOARDS, imported from Ireland, Asia, or Africa, pay only 11. 2. 6d. the hundred; but if imported from elsewhere, they pay 1s. 5. 6d. Clap-boards pay 4s. 9. 6d. the hundred; but if imported from Ireland, Asia, or Africa, only 2s. 10. 6d. Pipe-boards pay 5s. 8. 6d. the hundred; but if from Ireland, &c. only 3s. 10. 6d. Scale-boards pay 8s. 5. 6d. the hundred weight; and 1/4d. more if imported in foreign bottoms.
