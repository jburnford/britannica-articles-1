BOROZAIL

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOROZAIL, or the zail of the Ethiopians, a disease epidemic in the countries about the river Senega. It principally affects the pudenda, but is different from the loes venerea. It owes its rise to excessive venery: In the men this dissemper is called asab, and in the women assabatus.
