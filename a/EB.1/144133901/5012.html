BRACE

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRACE, in architecture, a piece of timber framed in with bevel joints, the use of which is to keep the building from swerving either way. When the brace is framed into the kingbeams or principal rafters, it is by some called a strut.
