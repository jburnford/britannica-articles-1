BARRICADE

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRICADE, a warlike defence, consisting of empty barrels and such like vessels, filled with earth, stones, carts, trees cut down, against an enemy's shot, or as-

fault; but generally trees cut with six faces, which are crossed with battoons as long as a half-pike, bound about with iron at the feet.
