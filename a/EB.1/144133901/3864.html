BARRACAN

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRACAN, in commerce, a sort of stuff, not dispersed, something like camblet, but of a coarser grain. It is used to make cloaks, surtouts, and such other garments, to keep off the rain.
