BETHLEHEMITES

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETHLEHEMITES, in church-history, a religious order, called also star-bearers, because they were distinguished by a red star with five rays, which they wore on their breast, in memory of the star that appeared to the wise men, and conducted them to Bethlehem.
