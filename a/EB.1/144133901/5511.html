BURLINGTON

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURLINGTON, a sea-port town in the East Riding of Yorkshire, situated on the German ocean, about thirty-seven miles north-east of York: E. long. 10^{\circ}, and N. lat. 54^{\circ} 15'.

It gives the title of earl to a branch of the noble family of Boyle.
