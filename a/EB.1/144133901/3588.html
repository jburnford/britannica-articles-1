BAJULUS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAJULUS, an ancient officer in the court of the Greek emperors. There were several degrees of bajuli, as the grand bajulus, who was preceptor to the emperor; and the simple bajuli, who were sub-preceptors.
