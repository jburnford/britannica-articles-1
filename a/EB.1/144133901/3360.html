AUGMENTATION

AUGMENTATION, in heraldry, are additional charges

to a coat-armour, frequently given as particular marks of honour, and generally borne either in the escutcheon or a conton; as have all the baronets of England, who have borne the arms of the Province of Ulster in Ireland.
