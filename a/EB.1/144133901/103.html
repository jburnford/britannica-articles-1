ABEYANCE

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEYANCE, in law, the expectancy of an estate. Thus if lands be leased to one person for life, with reversion to another for years, the remainder for years is an abeyance till the death of the lessee.
