BUCCINUM

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUCCINUM or TRUMPET-SHELL, a genus of shell-fish belonging to the order of vermes testaceæ. This animal is one of the snail kind. The shell is univalve, spiral, and gibbous. The aperture is oval, ending in a small strait canal. Linnæus enumerates about sixty species, most of which are found in the southern seas.
