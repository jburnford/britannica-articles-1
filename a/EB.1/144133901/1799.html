AMAIN

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMAIN, or AMAYNE, in the sea-language, a term importing to lower something at once. Thus, to strike amain, is to lower, or let fall, the top-sails; to wave amain, is to make a signal, by waving a drawn sword, or the like, as a demand that the enemy strike their top-sails.
