BEWDLEY

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEWDLEY, a borough-town of Worcestershire, situated on the river Severn, about twelve miles north of Worcester, in 2^{\circ} 20' W. long. and 52^{\circ} 25' N. lat. It sends only one member to parliament.
