ANGUS

article · 345 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGUS, a shire or county of Scotland, bounded on the north by the shire of Merns, on the east by the German ocean, on the south by the frith of Tay, which divides it from the shire of Fife, and on the west by the shire of Perth.

This county, which for the most part is exceeding fertile, is otherwise called Forfarshire, from its capital Forfar.
