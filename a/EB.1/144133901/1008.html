AGA

Cross-references: JANISSARIES

AGA, in the Turkish language, signifies a great lord or commander. Hence the Aga of the janissaries is the commander in chief of that corps; as the general of the horse is denominated spahiclar aga. See JANISSARIES, and SPAH.
