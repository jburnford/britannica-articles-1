ALMEHRAB

article · 164 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: KEBLA

ALMEHRAB, in the Mahometan customs, a niche in their mosques, pointing towards the kebla or temple of Mecca, to which they are obliged to bow in praying. See KEBLA.
