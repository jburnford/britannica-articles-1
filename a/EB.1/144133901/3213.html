ASTROLABE

Cross-references: ASTRONOMY

ASTROLABE, the name for a stereographic projection of the sphere, either upon the plane of the equator, the eye being supposed to be in the pole of the world; or upon the plane of the meridian, when the eye is supposed in the point of the intersection of the equinoxial and horizon.

ASTROLABE is also an instrument for taking the altitude of the sun or stars at sea. See ASTRONOMY.
