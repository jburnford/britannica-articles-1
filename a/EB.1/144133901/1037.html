AGE-PRIER

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGE-PRIER, actorem precari, in law, is when an action being brought against a person under age, for lands descended to him, he, by motion or petition, shews the matter to the court, praying the action may be stayed till his full age; which the court generally agrees to.
