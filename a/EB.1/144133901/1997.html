AMPHIBIA, in zoology, the name of Linnaeus's third class of animals, including all those which live partly in water, and partly on land. This class is subdivided into three orders, viz. 1. The amphibia reptiles; the amphibia serpentes; and the amphibia nantes. See NATURAL HISTORY.
