AVERRUNCI

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVERRUNCI, in the ancient heathen theology, an order of deities among the Romans, whose peculiar office it was to avert danger and exile. Apollo and Hercules are supposed to be of this order.
