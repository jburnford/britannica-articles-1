ANGOLA

ANGOLA, a large maritime country on the south-west side of Africa, lying between 10° and 15° E. long. and 5° and 16° S. lat.

The Portuguese have several colonies and considerable settlements on this coast, which does not hinder the other nations of Europe from driving a traffic in slaves with the natives, who are all negroes.
