ARGEMONE

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGEMONE, in botany, a genus of the polyandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of six petals; the calix of three leaves; and the capsule is semi-valved. There are three species of argemone, none of which are natives of Britain. They are all a kind of poppies.
