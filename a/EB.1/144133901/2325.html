ANGUSTICLAVIA

article · 154 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGUSTIOLAVIA, in Roman antiquity, a tunica embroidered with little purple studs. It was worn by the Roman knights, as the laticlavia was by the senators.
