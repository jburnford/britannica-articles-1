Fiscal ADVOCATE

sub_entry · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Fiscal ADVOCATE, fisci advocatus, in Roman antiquity, an officer of state under the Roman Emperors, who pleaded in all causes wherein the fiscus, or private treasury, was concerned.
