BILLET

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BILLET, in heraldry, a bearing in form of a long square. They are supposed to represent pieces of cloth of gold or silver; but Guillim thinks they represent a letter sealed up; and other authors take them for bricks.

Billeté signifies that the escutcheon is all over-strewned with billets, the number not ascertained. See Plate LI. fig. 15.
