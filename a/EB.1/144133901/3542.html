BADEN

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADEN, the name of several towns: 1. Of one about 20 miles north of Strasbourg, capital of the margraviate of the same name, and remarkable for its hot baths. 2. Of another town of Swabia, in the Brisgow; where are likewise several hot baths. 3. Of one in Switzerland, about 14 miles north-west of Zurich. 4. Of one in the circle of Austria, about 15 miles south of Vienna.
