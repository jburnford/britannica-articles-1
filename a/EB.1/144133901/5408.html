BUGIA

article · 156 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUGIA, a port-town of the kingdom of Algiers, in Africa, situated about sixty miles east of the city of Algiers; E. long. 4^{\circ}, N. lat. 35^{\circ} 30'.
