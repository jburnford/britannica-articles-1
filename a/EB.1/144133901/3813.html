BARGE

article · 408 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARGE, in naval affairs, a boat of state and pleasure, adorned with various ornaments, having bales and tilts, and seats covered with cushions and carpets, and benches for many oars; as the lord-mayor's barge, a company's barge, an admiral's barge, &c. It is also the name of a flat-bottomed vessel employed for carrying goods in a navigable river; as those upon the river Thames, called west-country barges.
