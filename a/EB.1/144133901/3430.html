AURILLAC

article · 172 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AURILLAC, a neat and well-built city of France, in the Upper Aveyron, noted for its trade in bone-lace: it is situated in 30^{\circ} 31' E. long. and 54^{\circ} 44' N. lat.
