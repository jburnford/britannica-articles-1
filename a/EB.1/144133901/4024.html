BATTERY sunk or buried

sub_entry · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BATTERY sunk or buried, is when its platform is sunk, or let down into the ground, so that there must be trenches cut in the earth, against the muzzles of the guns, for them to fire out at, and to serve for embrassures.
