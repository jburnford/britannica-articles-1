BORNEO, a large island in the Indian ocean, situated between 107^{\circ} and 117^{\circ} E. long. and between 7^{\circ} 30' N. lat. and 4^{\circ} S. lat.
Its figure is almost round, and computed to be 2500 miles in circumference, and consequently containing a greater number of square acres than any island in the known world.
