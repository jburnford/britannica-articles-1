ACARNA

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACARNA, in botany, a synonyme of the carduus caesariensis, of the caucis, of the carlina lanata, corymbosa, racemosa, and cancellata. Acarna is also used by Vaillant as a term for cynaracephalous or artichoke-headed plants.
