AIX-LA-CHAPELLE

AIX-LA-CHAPELLE, otherwise called Aach, Ach, and Aken, an imperial city of Germany, in the duchy of Juliers. It is large and populous; being much resorted to by foreigners, as well as by the Germans, on account of its hot baths.
