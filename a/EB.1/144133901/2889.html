ARCHITRAVE

Cross-references: ARCHITECTURE

ARCHITRAVE, in architecture, that part of a column which lies immediately upon the capital, being the lowest member of the entablature, and so called from its representing the principal beam in timber-buildings. See ARCHITECTURE.

Over a chimney, this member is called the mantle-piece; and over doors or windows, the hyperthyron.
