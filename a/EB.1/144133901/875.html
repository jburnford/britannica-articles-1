ÆDILE

article · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ÆDILE, in Roman antiquity, a magistrate whose business it was to superintend buildings of all kinds, but more especially public ones, as temples, aquæducts, high-ways, bridges, &c.
