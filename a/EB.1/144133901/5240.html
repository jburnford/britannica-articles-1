BRISGAW

article · 176 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRISGOW, a territory of the circle of Swabia, in Germany, situated on the east side of the Rhine, opposite to the Upper Alsace, whereof Fribourg and Brisac are the chief towns.
