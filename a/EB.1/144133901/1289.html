ALANORARIUS

article · 165 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALANORARIUS, in our old customs, was a keeper of spaniels, setting-dogs, &c. for the use of sportsmen. The word is derived from alan, a gothic term for a grey-bound.
