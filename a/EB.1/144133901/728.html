ADENOS

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADENOS, a kind of cotton otherwise called marine cotton. It comes from Aleppo by the way of Marfeilles, where it pays 20 per cent. duty, according to the tariff of the year 1766. Its valuation, according to the same tariff, is 76 livres 16 sols.
