ALIMENTARY debt

Cross-references: LAW

ALIMENTARY debt, in Scots law, an obligation come under by one person to pay a certain sum annually for the maintenance of another, either gratuitously, in consideration of a sum of money sunk, or by way of wages. See LAW, title, Arrestment and poinding.
