AREHA

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AREHA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria monogynia class. The corolla is divided into five parts; the tube of the corolla is ovated; and the capsule is globular, and consists of but one cell. There is only one species, viz. the alpina.
