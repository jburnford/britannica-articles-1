ARETHUSA, in botany, a genus of the gynandria diandria class. The generic character is taken from the nectarium, which is tubular, situated at the bottom of the corolla; and the inferior labium of it is fixed to the stylus. There are four species of the arethusa, all natives of America, except the capensis, which is only found at the Cape of Good Hope.
