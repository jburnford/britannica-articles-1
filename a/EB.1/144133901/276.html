ACANTHOPTERYGIOUS fishes

sub_entry · 124 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACANTHOPTERYGIOUS fishes, a term used by Linnæus and others for those fishes whose back-fins are hard, osseous, and prickly.
