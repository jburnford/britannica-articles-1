AZOGA SHIPS

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AZOGA SHIPS, are those Spanish ships commonly called the quick-silver ships, from their carrying quick-silver to the Spanish W. Indies, in order to extract the silver out of the mines of Mexico and Peru. These ships, strictly speaking, are not to carry any goods unless for the king of Spain's account.
