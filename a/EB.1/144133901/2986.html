ARMAGH

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMAGH, once a considerable city of Ireland, but now much reduced, situated about thirty miles south of Londonderry, in 6^{\circ} 45' W. long. and 54^{\circ} 30' N. lat. It is still the see of the primate of Ireland, and gives name to the county of Armagh.
