ACCOUNT of sales

Cross-references: BOOK-KEEPING

ACCOUNT of sales, is an account given by one merchant to another, or by a factor to his principal, of the disposal, charges, commission, and nett proceeds of certain merchandises sent for the proper or company account of him that consigned them to such factor or vender. See BOOK-KEEPING.
