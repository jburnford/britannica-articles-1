BARBELICOTÆ

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBELICOTÆ, in church history, a sect of gnostics, who affirmed that an immortal Eon had commerce with a virgin called Barbelath, to whom he granted successively the gift of prophecy, incorruptibility, and eternal life.
