ARGYLESHIRE

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGYLESHIRE, a county of Scotland, lying westward of Glasgow, and comprehending the countries of Lorn, Cowal, Knapdale, Kintyre, together with the islands Mull, Jura, Isla, &c. It gives the title of duke to the noble family of Campbell.
