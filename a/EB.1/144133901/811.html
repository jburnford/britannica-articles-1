ADOPTIVE arms

sub_entry · 159 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADOPTIVE arms, in heraldry, or, arms of adoption, those which a person enjoys by the gift or concession of another, and to which he was not otherwise intitled.
