BABYLON

BABYLON, a celebrated city of antiquity, supposed to have been situated on the river Euphrates, though not on its present channel, in 44° E. long. and 32° N. lat. But of this once so flourishing city, there are now no remains; nor is even the place where it stood certainly known.
