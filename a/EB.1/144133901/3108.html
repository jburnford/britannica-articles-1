ASCOLI

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASCOLI, a city in the marquise of Ancona in Italy, situated on the river Tronto, in 15° E. long. and 42° 50' N. lat. It is also a city of the kingdom of Naples, situated in the province of Capitanata, in 16° 30' E. long. and 41° 15' N. lat.
