BAY

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAY denotes likewise a pound head, made to keep in store of water for driving the wheels of the furnace or hammer belonging to an iron-mill, by the stream that comes thence through a flood-gate, called the pen-stock.
