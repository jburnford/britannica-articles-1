BERKSHIRE

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BERKSHIRE, a county in England, lying on the south side of the river Thames, opposite to Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire. It gives the title of earl to a branch of the Howard family.
