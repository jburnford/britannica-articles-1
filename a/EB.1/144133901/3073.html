ARVALES fratres

sub_entry · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARVALES fratres, in Roman antiquity, a college of twelve priests, instituted by Romulus, who himself made one of the body: they assisted in the sacrifices of the ambervalia, offered annually to Ceres and Bacchus, for the prosperity of the principal fruits of the earth, viz. those of corn and wine.
