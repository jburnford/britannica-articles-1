ABACUS logisticius

sub_entry · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABACUS logisticius, a right-angled triangle, whose sides forming the right angle contain the numbers from 1 to 60, and its area the facta of every two of the numbers perpendicularly opposite. This is also called a canon of sexagesimals.
