BORBONIA

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BORBONIA, in botany, a genus of the diadelphia decandria class. The calix is pointed and prickly; and the stygma is emarginated. There are six species of borbonia, which is a kind of broom, all natives of America.
