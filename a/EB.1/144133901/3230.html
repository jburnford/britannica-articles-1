ATHANATI

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATHANATI, in Persian antiquity, a body of cavalry, consisting of ten thousand men, always complete. They were called athanati, because, when one of them happened to die, another was immediately appointed to succeed him.
