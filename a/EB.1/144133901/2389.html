Right of ANNUALRENT

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

Right of ANNUALRENT, in Scots law, the original method of burdening lands with an yearly payment for the loan of money, before the taking of interest for money was allowed by statute. See SCOTS LAW, title, Heritable and moveable rights.
