Native ALUM

Native ALUM, or Fossile ALUM, that formed by nature, without the assistance of art.

There are still mines of native alum in the island of Chio, consisting of a kind of vaults, or apartments crusted over with alum, which may be looked upon as exfoliations from the rock.
