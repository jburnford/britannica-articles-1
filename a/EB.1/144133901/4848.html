BORASSUS

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BORASSUS, in botany, a genus belonging to the order of palmæ flabellifoliaz. The borassus, of which there is but one species, has palmated and plaited leaves, and is a native of India.
