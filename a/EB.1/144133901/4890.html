BORNEO

BORNEO, or BOURNOU, the name of a town and country of Nigritia, in Africa. This country abounds in cattle, millet, and cotton. It lies between 15^{\circ} and 24^{\circ} E. long. and between 10^{\circ} and 20^{\circ} N. lat.
