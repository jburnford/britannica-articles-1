BLASIA

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLASIA, in botany, a genus of the cryptogamia algae class. The calix of what is called the male is cylindrical, and full of grains; the calix of the female is naked, and inclosing a roundish seed sunk in the leaves. There is but one species, viz. the pusilla, or dwarf blasia, a native of Britain.
