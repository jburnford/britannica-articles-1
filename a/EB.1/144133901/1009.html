AGADES

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGADES, or AGDES, a people or kingdom of Africa, lying on the northern bank of the river Niger, betwixt the kingdoms of Cano on the E. and Tombut on the W. with that of Zaara on the N.
