BOOTES, a constellation of the northern hemisphere, consisting of 23 stars, according to Ptolemy's catalogue, of 18 in Tycho's, of 34 in Bayer's, of 52 in Helvelius's, and of 54 in Mr Flamstead's catalogue. See ASTRONOMY, p. 486.
