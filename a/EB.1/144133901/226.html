ABYSSINIAN church

sub_entry · 167 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABYSSINIAN church, that established in the empire of Abyssinia. It is a branch of the Copts or Jacobites; a sect of heretics, who admit but one nature in Jesus Christ.
