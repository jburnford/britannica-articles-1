AMMI

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMMI, in botany, a genus of the pentandria digynia class. The involucrum is pinnated; and the flowers are all hermophrodite, with radiated petals. There are two species of the ammi, viz. the majus and glaucifolium, both natives of Europe.
