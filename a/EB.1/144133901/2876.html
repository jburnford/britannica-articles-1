ARCHDUKE, a title given to dukes of greater authority and power than other dukes. The archduke of Austria is among the most ancient: His principal privileges are, that he shall distribute justice in his own country, without appeal; that he cannot be deprived of his countries, even by the emperor and the states of the empire; and that he have a power of creating counts, barons, &c. throughout the whole empire.
