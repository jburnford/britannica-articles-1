ANIMACHA

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANIMACHA, a river of India, in the kingdom of Malabar. It rises in the kingdom of Calicut, and falls into the ocean six leagues from Cranganor. It is also the name of a town upon the river.
