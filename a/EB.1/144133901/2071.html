ANACALYPTERIA

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANACALYPTERIA, in antiquity, festivals among the Greeks on the day that the bride was permitted to lay aside her veil, and appear in public. The word is derived from a verb which signifies to uncover.
