BASILISK

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASILISK, in military affairs, a large piece of ordnance, being a 48-pounder, and weighing about 7200 pounds. Those of the French were 10 feet long, and those of the Dutch 15. The French do not call any more of that calibre.
