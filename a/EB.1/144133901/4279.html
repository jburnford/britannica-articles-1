BENEDICTUS

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BENEDICTUS, among physicians, an epithet given to several medicines, on account of their lenitive qualities; thus we meet with aqua benedicta, benedictum laxativum, vinum benedictum, &c.
