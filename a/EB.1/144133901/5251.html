BRITTLENESS

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRITTLENESS, that quality of bodies, on account of which they are denominated brittle, or which subjects them to be easily broken.

Brittle bodies are likewise very hard and durable, barring accidents; and it is remarkable, that tin, though tough in itself, makes all other metals brittle, when mixed with them.
