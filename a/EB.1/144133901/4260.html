BENCOOLEN

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BENCOOLEN, a town and fort on the south-west coast of Sumatra, belonging to the E. India company, from whence great quantities of pepper are imported. It is situated in 101° E. long. and 4° S. lat.
