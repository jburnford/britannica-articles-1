BRASIDIA

article · 324 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRASIDIA, an anniversary solemnity at Sparta, in memory of Brasidas, a Lacedæmonian captain, famous for his achievements at Methone, Pylos, and Amphipolis. It was celebrated with sacrifices and games, wherein none were permitted to contend, but free-born Spartans. Whoever neglected to be present at the solemnity was fined.
