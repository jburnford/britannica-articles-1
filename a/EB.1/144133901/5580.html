BUTTOCK of a ship

sub_entry · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUTTOCK of a ship, is that part of her which is her breadth right astern, from the tack upwards; and a ship is said to have a broad or a narrow buttock, according as she is built broad or narrow at the transom.
