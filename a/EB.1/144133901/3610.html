BALDACHIN

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALDACHIN, or BALDAQIN, in architecture, a building in form of a canopy, supported by pillars, and fre-

quently used as a covering to insulated altars. Some also use the term baldachin for the shell over a door.
