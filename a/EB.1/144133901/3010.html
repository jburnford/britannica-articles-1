ARMS of course

Cross-references: TOURNAMENT

ARMS of course, or parade, were lances not shod, swords without edge or point, &c. used in the ancient tournaments. See TOURNAMENT.

Pass of Arms, a kind of combat, when anciently two or more cavaliers undertook to defend a pass against all attacks.
