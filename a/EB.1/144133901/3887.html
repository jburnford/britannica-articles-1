BARRY-BENDY

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRY-BENDY is when an escutcheon is divided evenly, bar and bend-ways, by lines drawn transverse and diagonal, interchangeably varying the tinctures of which it consists. See Plate LI. fig. 8.
