BOSSINEY

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOSSINEY, a borough-town of Cornwall, situated on the Irish channel, about fifteen miles north-west of Launceston: W. long. 5^{\circ}, and N. lat. 50^{\circ} 40'.

It sends two members to parliament.
