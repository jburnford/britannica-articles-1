ABAISED

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABAISED, Abaissé, in heraldry, an epithet applied to the wings of eagles, &c. when the tip looks downwards to the point of the shield, or when the wings are shut; the natural way of bearing them being extended.
