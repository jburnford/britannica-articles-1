BARREL

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARREL, in mechanics, a term given by watch-makers to the cylinder about which the spring is wrapped: And by gunsmiths to the cylindrical tube of a gun, pistol, &c. through which the ball is discharged.
