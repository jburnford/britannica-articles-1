BEHEADING, a capital punishment, inflicted by cutting off the head with an ax, sword, &c.

Among the Romans, beheading was a military punishment, performed at first with an ax, but afterwards with a sword, as done at present in Holland and France. In England the ax is preferred; and in Scotland they use, for this purpose, a machine called a maiden.
