BRUNSBUTTEL

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRUNSBUTTEL, a port-town of Holstein, in the circle of Lower Saxony, in Germany, situated at the mouth of the river Elbe: E. lon. 8^{\circ} 42', and N. lat. 54^{\circ} 10'. It is subject to Denmark.
