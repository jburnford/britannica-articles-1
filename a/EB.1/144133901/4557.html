BIXA

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIXA, in botany, a genus of the polyandria mongynia class. The corolla consists of 10 petals; the calix has five teeth; and the capsule is rough, and double-valved. There is but one species, viz. the orellana, a native of America.
