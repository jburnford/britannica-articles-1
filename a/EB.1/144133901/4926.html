BOTE

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOTE, in our old law books, signifies recompence or amends: Thus man-bote, is a compensation for a man slain.

There are likewise house-bote and plough-bote, privileges to tenants, of cutting wood for making ploughs, repairing tenements, and likewise for fuel.
