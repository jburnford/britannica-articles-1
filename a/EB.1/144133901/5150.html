BRENTE

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRENTE, in geography, a river which, taking its rise in the bishopric of Trent, in Germany, runs south-east through the Venetian territories, and falls into the Adriatic sea, opposite to Venice.
