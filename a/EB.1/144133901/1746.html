ALUDELS, in chemistry, earthen pots ranged one above another, for retaining the flowers which ascend in the process of sublimation. The lowest aludel is fitted to the pot which contains the matter to be sublimed, and at the top is a close head for collecting the flowers which ascend highest. See CHEMISTRY.
