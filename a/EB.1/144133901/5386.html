BUDGE-barrels

sub_entry · 372 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUDGE-barrels, among engineers, small barrels well hooped, with only one head; on the other end is nailed a piece of leather, to draw together upon strings like a purse. Their use is for carrying powder along with

with a gun or mortar, being less dangerous, and easier carried, than whole barrels. They are likewise used upon a battery of mortars, for holding meal-powder.
