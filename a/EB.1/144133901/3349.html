AVERRHOA

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVERRHOA, in botany, a genus of the decandria pentagynia class. The calix has five leaves; the petals are five, open at top; and the apple or fruit is pentagonal, and divided into five cells. The species are three, all natives of India.
