ACCEPTANCE

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCEPTANCE, in Scots law, denotes either a person's admitting his subscription to a bill or draught, by which he subjects himself to the payment of it; or accepting or agreeing to offers made in bargaining, by which the bargain is concluded.
