AUDE

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUDE, a river of France, which, taking its rise in the Pyrenees, runs northwards by Alet and Carcassone; and from thence turning eastward through Languedoc, falls into the Mediterranean, a little to the north-east of Narbonne.
