ACANTHINE

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACANTHINE, any thing resembling or belonging to the herb acanthus. Acanthine garments, among the ancients, are said to be made of the down of thistles; others think they were garments embroidered in imitation of the acanthus.
