ABLAY

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABLAY, or ABLAI, a country of Great Tartary, whose inhabitants, called Bochars, are vassals of the Russians. It lies to the east of the Irtysh, and extends 500 leagues along the southern frontiers of Siberia.
