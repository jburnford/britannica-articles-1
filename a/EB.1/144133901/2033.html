AMPULLA

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPULLA, in antiquity, a round big-bellied vessel which the ancients used in their baths, to contain oil for anointing their bodies. It was also a cup made of glass, and sometimes of leather, for drinking out of at table.
