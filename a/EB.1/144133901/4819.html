BONGO

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONGO, or BUNGO, the capital of one of the islands of Japan, to which it gives name; in 132^{\circ} E. long. and 32^{\circ} 30' N. lat. It is a sea-port town, situated on the east side of the island, opposite to the island of Tonfa, from which it is separated by a narrow channel.
