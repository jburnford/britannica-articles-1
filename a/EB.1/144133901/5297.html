BROOM-FLOWER

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BROOM-FLOWER, ordre de la geniffe, an order instituted by St. Louis, king of France, to shew the esteem which he had for the queen his wife, who, the evening before his queen's coronation, received this order himself.
