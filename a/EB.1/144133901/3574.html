BAIL

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAIL, or BALE, in the sea-language. The seamen call throwing the water by hand, out of the ship or boat's-hold, bailing. They also call those hoops that bear up the tilt of a boat, its bails.
