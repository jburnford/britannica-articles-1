BUFONIA

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUFONIA, in botany, a genus of the diandria monogynia class. The calix is four-leaved; the petals are four; and the capsule is unilocular, and contains two seeds. There is but one species, viz. the tenuifolia or bastard chick-weed, a native of Britain.
