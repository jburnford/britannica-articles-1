BRINE-water, a salt water, which being boiled, turns into salt.

Brine taken out of brine-pits, or brine-pans, used by some for curing or pickling of fish, without boiling the same into salt, and rock-salt without refining it into white-salt, are prohibited by 1 Anne, cap. xxi.
