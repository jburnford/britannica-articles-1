ABADIR

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABADIR, a title which the Carthaginians gave to gods of the first order. In the Roman mythology, it is the name of a stone which Saturn swallowed, believing it to be his new-born son Jupiter: hence it became the object of religious worship.
