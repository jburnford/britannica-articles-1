BARBARIAN

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBARIAN, a name given by the ancient Greeks and Romans to all who were not of their own country, or were not initiated in their language, manners, and customs.

In this sense the word signified with them no more than foreigner; not signifying, as among us, a wild, rude, or uncivilized person.
