ARGENTIERE

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGENTIERE, a small island in the Archipelago, situated about 60 miles east of Morea, in 25^{\circ} E. long. and 37^{\circ} N. lat.

ARGENTIERE is also the name of a small town of Languedoc in France, in 4^{\circ} E. long. and 44^{\circ} 30' N. lat.
