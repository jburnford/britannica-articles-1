CHIN BEAK

sub_entry · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHIN BEAK, a moulding the same as the quarter-round, except that its situation is inverted: This is very frequent in modern buildings, though few examples of it are found in the ancient.
