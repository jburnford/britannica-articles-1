ALTIN

ALTIN, a kingdom of Asia, in great Tartary, between the sources of the Irrich and the Oby. It is bounded on the north by the Kirgises, on the east by the Amaduners, on the south by the kingdom of Eluth, and on the west by the Irrich, which separates it from Barabinskoi.
