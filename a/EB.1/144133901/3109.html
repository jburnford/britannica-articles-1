ASCOLIA

article · 176 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASCOLIA, in Grecian antiquity, a festival celebrated by the Athenian husbandmen in honour of Bacchus, to whom they sacrificed a he-goat, because that animal destroys the vines.
