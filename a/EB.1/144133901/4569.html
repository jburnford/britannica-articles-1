Earth-BLACK

sub_entry · 328 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Earth-BLACK, a sort of coals found in the ground, with which the painters and limners use to paint in fresco, after it has been well ground.

There.

There is also a black made with gall nuts, copperas, or vitriol, such as common ink. And a black made with silver and lead, which serves to fill up the cavities of engraved things.
