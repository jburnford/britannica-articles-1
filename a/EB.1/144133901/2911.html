ARCUATIO

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCUATIO, a machine consisting of hoops, used in Florence by nurses, in order to prevent the child from being overlaid. Every nurse is obliged to lay her child in an arcuatio, under the pain of excommunication.
