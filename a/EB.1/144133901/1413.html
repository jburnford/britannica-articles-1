ALCOVE

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCOVE, among builders, a recess, or part of a chamber separated by an eltrade, or partition of columns, and other corresponding ornaments, in which is placed a bed of state, and sometimes seats to entertain company.

These alcoves are frequent in Spain, and the bed raised two or three ascents, with a rail at the foot.
