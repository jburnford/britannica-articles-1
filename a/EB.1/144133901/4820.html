BONIFACIO

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONIFACIO, in geography, a port-town of Corsica, situated at its south end, in 9^{\circ} 20' E. long. and 41^{\circ} 20' N. lat. It is one of the best towns in the whole island, and gives name to the strait between Corsica and Sardinia.
