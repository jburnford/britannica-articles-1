ANTHROPOPHAGY

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTHROPOPHAGY, the act of eating human flesh. This horrid practice is said to prevail in some parts of Africa and America. But it is greatly to be doubted if ever such a custom existed.
