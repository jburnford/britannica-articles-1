ABELIANS

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABELIANS, ABELITES, or ABELONIANS, a sect of heretics that sprung up near Hippo in Africa during the reign of Arcadius. They had one distinguishing and extraordinary tenet, which was to marry, but never to consummate.
