Succharine ALUM

sub_entry · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Succharine ALUM, is a composition of common alum with rose-water, and the whites of eggs, which being boiled to the consistence of a paste, is formed in the shape of a sugar-loaf; it is used as a cosmetic.
