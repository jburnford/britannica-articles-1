ACRON

ACRON, a territory on the gold coast of Guinea in Africa, bordering on the Fantee country. The Dutch have a fort here, called Fort Patience. The inhabitants apply themselves principally to husbandry. They are a very ignorant people, and go naked like the rest of the negroes.
