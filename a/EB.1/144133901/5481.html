BURDEN

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURDEN, or BURDON, in music, the drone or bals, and the pipe or string which plays it: Hence that part of a song, that is repeated at the end of every stanza, is called the burden of it.

A chord which is to be divided, to perform the intervals of music, when open and undivided, is also called the burden.
