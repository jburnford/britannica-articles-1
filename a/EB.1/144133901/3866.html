BARRATOR

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRATOR, in law, a common mover or maintainer of suits and quarrels, either in courts, or elsewhere in the country. A man cannot be adjudged a barrator for bringing any number of suits in his own right, though they are vexatious. Barrators are punished by fine and imprisonment.
