ATTAINT

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATTAINT, among farriers, a knock or hurt in a horse's leg, proceeding either from a blow with another horse's foot, or from an over-reach in frosty weather, when a horse being rough-shod, or having shoes with long calkers, strikes his hinder feet against his fore-leg.
