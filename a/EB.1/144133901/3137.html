ASPERULA

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASPERULA, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The corolla is infundibuliform, and the capsule contains two globular seeds. There are six species, of which the odorata, or wood-rose, and the cynachica, or squinancy-wort, are natives of Britain.
