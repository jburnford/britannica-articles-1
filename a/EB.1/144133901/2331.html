ANIAN

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANIAN, a large maritime country on the eastern coast of Africa, lying between the equator, and 12° N. lat. and between 40° and 50° E. long.

ANIAN is also the name of a strait, supposed to lie between the north-east of Asia and north-west of America.
