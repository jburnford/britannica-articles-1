ACOMA

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACOMA, a town of N. America, in New-Mexico, seated on a high mountain, with a strong castle. It is the capital of the province, and was taken by the Spaniards in 1599, 108. 35. W. long. 35. o. lat.
