AGNO

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGNO, a river of Naples, which, taking its rise in the mountainous parts of Terra di Lavoro, washes the town of Acerra, and, passing between Capua and Aversa, falls into the Mediterranean, about seven miles N. of Puzzoli.
