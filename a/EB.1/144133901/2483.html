ANTHESTERIA

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTHESTERIA, in Grecian antiquity, festivals celebrated in the spring, by the ancient Athenians, in honour of Bacchus, during which the masters feasted their slaves, as the Romans did in the time of the Saturnalia.
