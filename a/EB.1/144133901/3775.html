BARBE

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBE, in commerce, a barbarian horse, greatly esteemed for its beauty, strength, and swiftness. Barbs are commonly of a slim shape, and have very thin legs; they retain their vigour to the last, and are therefore much prized for stallions. They are used both for the saddle and the coach.
