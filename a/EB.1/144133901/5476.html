BUPRESTIS

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUPRESTIS, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera. The feelers are like bristles, and about the length of the breast; the head is half retracted into the thorax. There are twenty-seven species of this insect, most of them natives of the Indies.
