ACIDS

article · 296 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACIDS, in the materia medica, are such medicines as possess an acid quality; such as vinegar, spirit of vitriol, &c. These being powerful antiseptics, are esteemed good in all purrid and malignant diseases, and, by their cooling virtue, are no less efficacious in feverish and inflammatory cases.
