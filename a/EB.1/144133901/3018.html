ARNOLDISTS

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARNOLDISTS, in church-history, a sectary, so called from their leader Arnold of Breffe, who was a great declaimer against the wealth and vices of the clergy; and who is also charged with preaching against baptism and the eucharist.
