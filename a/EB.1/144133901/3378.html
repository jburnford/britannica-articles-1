AVIGNON

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVIGNON, a large city of Provence in France, situated on the east side of the river Rhone, about 20 miles south of Orange, in 4^{\circ} 40' E. long. and 43^{\circ} 50' N. lat. It is an archbishop's see, and, with the whole district of Venaissine, subject to the pope.
