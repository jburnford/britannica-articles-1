BOMB-CHEST

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOMB-CHEST, a kind of chest usually filled with bombs, sometimes only with gunpowder, placed under ground to tear it and blow it up in the air, with those who stand on it. It was set on fire by means of a fauciffé fastened at one end, but is now much disused.
