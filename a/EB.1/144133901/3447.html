AUTOMATUM

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUTOMATUM, or AUTOMATON, an instrument, or rather machine, which by means of springs, weights, &c. seems to move itself, as a watch, clock, &c. Such also were Archytus's flying dove, Regiomontanus's wooden-eagle, &c.
