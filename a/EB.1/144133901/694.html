ADDICTION

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADDICTION, among the Romans, was the making over goods to another, either by sale, or by legal sen-

tence; the goods so delivered were called bona addicte. Debtors were sometimes delivered over in the same manner; and thence called servi additi.
