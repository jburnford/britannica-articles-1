Cross-references: CRESCENT

DECREMENT, in heraldry, signifies the wane of the moon from the full to the new. The moon in this state is called moon decrecent, or in decours; and when borne in coat-armor, faces to the left side of the escutcheon, as she does to the right side when in the increment. See CRESCENT.
