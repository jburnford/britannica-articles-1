HOMBERG

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOMBERG, a town of Germany, in the circle of the upper Rhine, and landgraviate of Hesse, situated ten miles north of Francfort: E. long. 8^{\circ} 24', N. lat. 50^{\circ} 20'.

HOMBERG is also a town of Germany, in the palatinate of the Rhine, and dukedom of Deuxponts: E. long. 7^{\circ} 6', and N. lat. 49^{\circ} 20'.
