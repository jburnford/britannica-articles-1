LORETTO

article · 329 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LORETTO, a city of Italy, in the marquise of Ancona, in the pope's territories, 145 miles east of Rome. This place is famous for the chamber of the blessed Virgin, which, according to the Roman catholic tradition, was brought by angels from Palestine to Dalmatia, and from thence transported over into Italy, and fixed at Loreto.
