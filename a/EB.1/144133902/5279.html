GOBIUS

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOBIUS, in ichthyology, a genus of fishes belonging to the order of thoracici. They have two holes between the eyes, four rays in the membrane of the gills; and the belly-fins are united in an oval form. There are eight species, principally distinguished by the number of rays in their fins.
