COUPER, the name of two towns of Scotland, the one situated about twelve miles north-east of Perth, in the shire of Angus, W. long. 3°, and N. lat. 56° 30'; and the other in the county of Fife, about ten miles west of St Andrews: W. long. 2° 40', and N. lat. 56° 20'.
