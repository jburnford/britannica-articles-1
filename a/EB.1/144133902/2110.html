CORDOUA, CORDOVA, a city of Andalusia, in Spain, situated

situated on the river Guadalquivir, seventy-two miles north-east of Seville, and seventy-five north of Malaga: W. long. 4^{\circ} 45', and N. lat. 37^{\circ} 45'.
