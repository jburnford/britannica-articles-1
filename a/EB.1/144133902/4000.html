EPISODE

EPISODE, in poetry, a separate incident, story, or action, which a poet invents, and connects with his principal action, that his work may abound with a greater diver-

sity of events; though, in a more limited sense, all the particular incidents whereof the action or narration is compounded, are called episodes. See COMPOSITION.
