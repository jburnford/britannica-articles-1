DILEMMA

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: LOGIC

DILEMMA, in logic, an argument consisting of two or more propositions, which divides the whole into all its parts, or members, by a disjunctive proposition, and then infers something concerning each part, which is finally referred to concerning the whole. See LOGIC.
