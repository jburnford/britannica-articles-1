FURR

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FURR, in commerce, signifies the skin of several wild beasts, dressed in alum with the hair on, and used as a part of dress by princes, magistrates, and others. The kinds most in use are, those of the ermine, sable, castor, hare, coney, &c.
