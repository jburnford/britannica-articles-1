White CINNAMON

sub_entry · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

White CINNAMON, called also Winter's bark, is the bark of a tree frequent in the islands of St Domingo, Guadalupe, &c. of a sharp biting taste like pepper. Some use it instead of nutmeg; and in medicine it is esteemed a stomachic and antiscorbutic.
