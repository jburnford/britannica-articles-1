DEVONSHIRE

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEVONSHIRE, a county in the west of England, bounded by the Bristol channel, on the north; by Somersetshire and Dorsetshire, on the east; by the English channel, on the south; and by Cornwall, on the west. From this county the noble family of Cavendish take the title of duke.
