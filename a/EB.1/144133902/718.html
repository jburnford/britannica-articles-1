CATESBÆA

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATESBÆA, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The corolla is long, monopetalous, and shaped like a tunnel; the stamens are within the fauces; and the berry contains but one seed. There is but one species, viz. the spinosa, a native of Providence.
