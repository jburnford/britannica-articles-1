EPITASIS

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPITASIS, in ancient poetry, the second part or division of a dramatic poem, wherein the plot, entered upon in the first part, or protaisis, was carried on, heightened, and worked up, till it arrived at its state, or height, called catastasis.
