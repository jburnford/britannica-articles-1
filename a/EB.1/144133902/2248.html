COTRONA

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COTRONA, a town of the further Calabria, in the kingdom of Naples, situated on the Mediterranean, about fifteen miles south-east of St Severino: E. long. 17° 40', and N. lat. 38° 50'. It is the see of a bishop.
