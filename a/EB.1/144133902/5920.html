HILARIA

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HILARIA, an ancient Roman festival, observed on the eighth of the calends of April, or the twenty-fifth day of March, in honour of the goddess Cybele. It was so called from the various expressions of joy and mirth on this occasion.
