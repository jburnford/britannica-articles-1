GORGONA

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GORGONA, the name of two islands; one in the Pacific Ocean on the coast of Peru, W. long. 79^{\circ}, N. lat. 3^{\circ}; the other in the Mediterranean, twenty-five miles west of Leghorn.
