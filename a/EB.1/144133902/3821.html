EMBLEM

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EMBLEM, a kind of painted enigma, or certain figures painted or cut, metaphorically expressing some action, with reflections underneath, which in some measure explain the sense of the device, and at the same time instruct us in some moral truth, or other matter of knowledge.
