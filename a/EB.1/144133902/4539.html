FIFE-RAILS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FIFE-RAILS, in a ship, are those that are placed on banisters, on each side of the top of the poop, and so along with hances or falls.

They reach down to the quarter-deck, and to the stair of the gang-way.
