HOLOCAUST

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOLOCAUST, a burnt offering, or sacrifice, wholly consumed by fire: of this kind was the daily sacrifice in the Jewish church. This was done by way of acknowledgment, that the person offering and all that belonged to him, were the effects of the divine bounty.
