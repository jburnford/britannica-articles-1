Jear CAPSTAIN

sub_entry · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Jear CAPSTAIN is placed between the main-mast and the mizen, and serves to strain any rope, heave up on the jear rope or upon the viol, or hold off by at the weighing of an anchor.
