CAMALDULIANS, a religious order founded by St Romuald, in a little plain, on the mount Apennine, called Camaldulia, situated in the state of Florence.

The manner of life first enjoined this order was, that they dwelt in separate cells, and met together only at the time of prayer: Some of them, during the two lents of the year, observed an inviolable silence; and others, for the space of an hundred days.
