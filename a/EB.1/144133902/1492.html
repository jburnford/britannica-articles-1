CLEVES, or CLEF

CLEVES, or CLEF, the capital of the duchy of Cleve, in the circle of Westphalia, in Germany, situated near the western shore of the river Rhine: E. long. 5° 36', and N. lat. 51° 40'. It is subject to the king of Prussia.
