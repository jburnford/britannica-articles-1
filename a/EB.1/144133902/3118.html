DIALYSIS

Cross-references: DIAERESIS

DIALYSIS, in grammar, a mark or character, consisting of two points, \ddot{a}, placed over two vowels of a word, in order to separate them, because otherwise they would make a diphthong, as Mosaic, &c. See DIAERESIS.
