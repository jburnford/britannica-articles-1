CALIDUCT

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALIDUCT, in antiquity, a kind of pipes, or canals, disposed along the walls of houses and apartments, used, by the ancients, for conveying heat to several remote parts of the house, from one common furnace.
