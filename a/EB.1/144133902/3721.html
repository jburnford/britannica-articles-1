EFFLUVIUM

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EFFLUVIUM, in physiology, a term much used by philosophers and physicians, to express the minute particles which exhale from most, if not all, terrestrial bodies in form of insensible vapours.
