HALLELUJA, a word signifying, praise the Lord.

The singing halleluja was a sort of invitory, or call to each other, to praise the Lord.

St Austin says, that in some churches, it was sung only on Easter-day, and the fifty days of Pentecost; but that even in those churches where it was most in use, it was never used in the time of lent.
