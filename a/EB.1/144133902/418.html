CAPRAROLA

CAPRAROLA, a town of St Peter's patrimony in Italy, about twenty miles north of the city of Rome, and eight south of Viterbo: E. long. 13^{\circ}, and N. lat. 42^{\circ} 30'.—It is a bishop's see.
