COUSIN

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUSIN, a term of relation between the children of brothers and sisters, who in the first generation are called cousin-germans, in the second generation second cousins, &c. If sprung from the relations of the

father's side, they are denominated paternal cousins; if on the mother's, maternal.
