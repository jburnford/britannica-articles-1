CANDIA, the modern name of Crete, an island situated in

in the Mediterranean sea, between 22^{\circ} and 27^{\circ} E. long. and between 35^{\circ} and 36^{\circ} N. lat.

There is no river of any consequence in the whole island, which is watered by a multitude of rivulets; whereof Lethe is one. Here too is mount Ida, so much celebrated in the writings of the ancients.
