FORMOSA

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORMOSA, an island in the pacific ocean, between 119^{\circ} and 122^{\circ} of E. long. and between 22^{\circ} and 25^{\circ} N. lat. about 100 miles east of Canton in China. It is subject to the Chinese.
