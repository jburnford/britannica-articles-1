DRAINS

Cross-references: AGRICULTURE

DRAINS, a name given, in the fen countries, to certain large cuts or ditches of twenty, thirty, nay sometimes forty foot wide, carried through the marshy ground to some river or other place capable of discharging the water they carry out of the fen-lands. See AGRICULTURE.
