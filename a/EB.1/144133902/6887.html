LEPTURA

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEPTURA, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera, the characters of which are these:—The feelers are bristly; the elytra are attenuated towards the apex; and the thorax is somewhat cylindrical. There are 25 species, principally distinguished by their colour.
