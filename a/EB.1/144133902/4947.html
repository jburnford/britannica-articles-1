FUNEN

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FUNEN, the second island for magnitude belonging to the king of Denmark, situated at the entrance of the Baltic sea, and separated from Jutland by the strait called the lesser Belt, and from the island of Zeland by the strait called the great Belt. Its chief town is Odensee.
