CANOE

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANOE, a small boat, made of the trunk of a tree, bored hollow; and sometimes also of pieces of bark sewed together.

It is used by the natives of America to go a-fishing in the sea, or upon some other expedition, either by sea, or upon the rivers and lakes.
