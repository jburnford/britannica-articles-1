DISPOSITION

Cross-references: CHARTER

DISPOSITION, in Scots law, is that deed or writing which contains the sale or grant of any subject: when applied to heritable subjects, it in some cases gets the name of charter, which differs from a disposition in nothing else than a few immaterial forms. See CHARTER.
