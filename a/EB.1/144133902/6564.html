JULPHA

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JULPHA, or Old Julpha, once the capital of Armenia, but now in ruins, the inhabitants being transplanted to a town within a mile of Isfahan, called New Julpha, and there they carry on a foreign trade with all countries in Asia. The situation of Old Julpha was in E. long. 46°, N. lat. 39°.
