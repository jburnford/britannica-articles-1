CALOPHYLLUM

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALOPHYLLUM, in botany, a genus of the polyandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of five petals; the calix has five teeth; and the drupa is globular. There are but two species, viz. the inophyllum, and calaba, both natives of India.
