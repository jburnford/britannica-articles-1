CAULIFLOWERS, in gardening, a much esteemed species of cabbage.

Cauliflowers have of late years been so much improved in Britain, as to exceed in goodness and magnitude any produced in most parts of Europe; and, by the skill of the gardener, are continued for several months together, but the most common season for them is in May, June, and July.
