CAMBODIA

CAMBODIA, the capital of a kingdom of the same name in India, beyond the Ganges: E. long. 104^{\circ}, N. lat. 12^{\circ} 30'.

The kingdom of Cambodia extends from 9^{\circ} to 15^{\circ} of N. lat. being bounded by the kingdom of Laos on the north, Cochinchina on the east, the Indian ocean on the south, and by the bay of Siam on the west.
