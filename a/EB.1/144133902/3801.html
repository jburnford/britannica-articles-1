ELOQUENCE, the art of speaking well, so as to affect and persuade.

Cicero defines it, the art of speaking with copiousness and embellishment.

Eloquence and rhetoric differ from each other, as the theory from the practice; rhetoric being the art which describes the rules of eloquence, and eloquence that art which uses them to advantage.
