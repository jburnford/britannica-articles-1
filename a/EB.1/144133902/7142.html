LUES

Cross-references: MEDICINE

LUES, among physicians, is, in general, used for a disease of any kind; but, in a more particular sense, is restrained to contagious and pestilential diseases: thus, the lues gallica, or venerea, signifies the venereal disease. See MEDICINE.
