CARELSCROON

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARELSCROON, a port-town of the province of Gothland, in Sweden, situated on the coast of the Baltic: E. long. 15^{\circ}, and N. lat. 56^{\circ} 20'.

It is an excellent harbour, where the Swedes lay up their royal navy.
