CATHOLIC KING

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATHOLIC KING, a title which hath been hereditary to the kings of Spain, ever since Alphonso, who, having gained several victories over the Saracens, and re-established the Christian faith in Spain, was honoured with the title of Catholic. Some say it was in the time of Ferdinand and Isabella.
