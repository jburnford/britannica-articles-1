LICTORS

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LICTORS, in Roman antiquity, the sergeants or beadle who carried the fasces before the supreme magistrates: it was also a part of their office to be the public executioners in beheading, scourging, &c.
