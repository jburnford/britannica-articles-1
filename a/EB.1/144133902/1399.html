CLACKMANNAN, the capital of Clackmannan-shire, in Scotland, situated on the northern shore of the Forth, about twenty five miles north-west of Edinburgh: W. long. 3^{\circ} 40', N. lat. 56^{\circ} 15'.

The county of Clackmannan is joined with that of

Kinros, which each in their turn chuse a member to represent them in parliament.
