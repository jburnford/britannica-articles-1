FORESTER

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORESTER, a sworn officer of the forest, appointed by the king's letters-patent, to walk the forest at all hours, watch over the vert and venison; also to make attachments and true presentments of all trespasses committed within the forest.
