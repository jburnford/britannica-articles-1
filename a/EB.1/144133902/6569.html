JUNK

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUNK, in the sea-language, old cables cut into short pieces, and given to boatswains for making swabs, plats, and nippers; as also to the ship-carpenters, and to poor people, to be picked into oakum, for caulking ships, &c.
