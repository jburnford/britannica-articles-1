HYDROMEL

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYDROMEL, among physicians, water impregnated with honey, either before or after fermentation.

Vinous hydromel, commonly called mead, is said to be good for the gravel. See the article MEAD.
