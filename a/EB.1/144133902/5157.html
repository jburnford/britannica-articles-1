GERMAINS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GERMAINS, or St GERMAINS, a town and royal palace of France, fourteen miles north-west of Paris.

St GERMAINS is also a borough of Cornwall, eight miles west of Plymouth. It sends two members to parliament.
