COSSACKS

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COSSACKS, people inhabiting the banks of the rivers Neiper and Don, near the Black sea and frontiers of Turkey. Their country is commonly called the Ukraine, and is mostly subject to Russia.
