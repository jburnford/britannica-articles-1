ENHARMONIC

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENHARMONIC, in the ancient music, one of their genera or kinds of music, so called from its superior excellence; though wherein it consisted, says Mr Malcolm, is hard to say: it was allowed by all to be so very difficult, that few could ever practise it.
