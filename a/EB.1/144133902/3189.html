DIEPPE

article · 172 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIEPE, a port-town of France, situated on the British channel, about thirty miles north of Rouen, and opposite to port Rye in England: E. long. 1° 15', and N. lat. 49° 55'.
