COLT-EVIL

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLT-EVIL, among farriers, a swelling of the yard and scrotum, incident both to stoned horses and geldings; for which, after washing the part with lukewarm vinegar, it is usual to anoint them with juice of rue, mixed with honey, and boiled in hog's grease, adding bay-leaves and the powder of fenugreek.
