DOMIFYING

article · 428 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOMIFYING, in astrology, the dividing or distributing the heavens into twelve houses, in order to erect a theme, or horoscope, by means of six great circles, called circles of position.

There are various ways of domifying: that of regiomontanus, which is the most common, makes the circles

circles of position pass through the intersections of the meridian and the horizon: others make them pass through the poles of the zodiac.
