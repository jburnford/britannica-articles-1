GUARDIAN

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUARDIAN, in law, a person who has the charge of anything; but more commonly it signifies one who has the custody and education of such persons as have not sufficient discretion to take care of themselves and their own affairs, as children and idiots.
