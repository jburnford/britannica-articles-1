HERMANNIA

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HERMANIA, in botany, a genus of the monadelphia pentandria class. It has but one stylus; the capsule has five cells; and the petals are semitubular at the base. There are nine species, none of them natives of Britain.
