INNATE IDEAS

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INNATE IDEAS, those supposed to be stamped on the mind from the first moment of its existence, and which it constantly brings into the world with it: a doctrine, which Mr. Locke has abundantly refuted.
