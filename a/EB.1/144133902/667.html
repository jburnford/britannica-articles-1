CASTLE-RISING

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTLE-RISING, a borough-town of Norfolk, situated near the sea coast, about thirty miles west of Norwich, and seven north of Lynn: E. long. 40', N. lat. 52^{\circ} 46'. It sends two members to parliament.
