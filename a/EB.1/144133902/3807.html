ELYMUS

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELYMUS, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria digynia class. The involucrum consists of two leaves; and the spiculae are double. There are eight species, only one of which, viz. the arenarius, or sea lyme-grass, is a native of Britain.
