GLORIOSA

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GLORIOSA, SUPERB LILY, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of six undulated and reflected petals; and the style is oblique. There is but one species, a native of Malabar.
