CONCORD

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONCORD, in grammar, that part of construction called syntax, in which the words of a sentence agree; that is, in which nouns are put in the same gender, number, and case; and verbs in the same number and person with nouns and pronouns.
