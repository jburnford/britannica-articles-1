FULMINATION, in chemistry, is used in a synonymous sense with detonation.

Fulmination,

Fulmination in the deputation of the more perfect metals, is, when, upon infusing them with lead, a brighter colour succeeds a kind of sulphureous cloud, before appearing in the metals, during the fusion.
