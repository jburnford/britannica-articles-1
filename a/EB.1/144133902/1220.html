CHRONOGRAM

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHRONOGRAM, a species of false wit, consisting in this, that a certain date or epocha is expressed by numeral letters of one or more verses: such is that which makes the motto of a medal struck by Guiliavus Adolphus, in 1632.
