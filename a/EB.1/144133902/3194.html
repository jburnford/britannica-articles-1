DIET

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MEDICINE

DIET, in medicine, according to some, comprehends the whole regimen, or rule of life, with regard to the six non-naturals, air, meats and drinks, sleep and watching, motion and rest, passions of the mind, retentions and excretions.

The more accurate writers, however, restrain the term of diet to what regards eating and drinking, or solid aliment and drinks. See MEDICINE.
