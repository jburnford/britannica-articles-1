GALLOWAY

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLOWAY is also the capital of a county of the same name, in the province of Connaught, in Ireland: W. long. 9° 12', and N. lat. 53° 12'.

It has a good port, and is advantageously situated for foreign trade.
