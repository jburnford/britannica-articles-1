DIEPHOLT

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIEPHOLT, a city of Westphalia in Germany, situated at the north end of the Dummer lake, thirty-five miles south of Bremen: E. long. 8°, N. lat. 53°.

It is subject to the king of Great Britain, as elector of Hanover.
