DOWNS

DOWNS, a famous road near Deal, in Kent, where both the outward and homeward bound ships frequently make some stay; and squadrons of men of war rendezvous in time of war.

It affords excellent anchorage, and is defended by the castles of Deal, Dover, and Sandwich.
