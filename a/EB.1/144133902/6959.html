LIEGE POUSTIE

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIEGE POUSTIE, in Scots law, is opposed to death bed; and signifies a person's enjoying that state of health, in which only he can dispose of his property at pleasure. See law, Tit. xxvii. 28.
