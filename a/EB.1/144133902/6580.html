JUS PREVENTIONIS

Cross-references: LAW

JUS PREVENTIONIS, in Scots law, the preferable right of jurisdiction acquired by a court, in any cause to which other courts are equally competent, by having exercised the first act of jurisdiction. See LAW, tit. 2.
