CIVIL LAW, is properly the peculiar law of each state, country, or city: but what we usually mean by the civil law, is a body of laws composed out of the best Roman and Grecian laws, compiled from the laws of nature and nations, and, for the most part, received and observed throughout all the Roman dominions for above 1200 years.
