CORFE-CASTLE

CORFE-CASTLE, a borough-town of Dorsetshire, about twelve miles east of Dorchester, near the sea: W. long. 2^{\circ} 10', and N. lat. 50^{\circ} 36'. It sends two members to Parliament.
