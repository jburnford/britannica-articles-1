CHERLERIA

CHERLERIA, in botany, a genus of the decandria trigynia class. The calix consists of five leaves; it has five petals less than the leaves of the calix, and five bifid nectaria; the anthers are alternately barren: and the capsule has three cells and as many valves. There is but one species, viz. the fedoides, a native of Switzerland.
