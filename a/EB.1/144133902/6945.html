LIBERIA

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIBERIA, in Roman antiquity, a festival observed on the sixteenth of the calends of April, at which time the youth laid aside their juvenile habit for the toga virilis, or habit peculiar to grown men.
