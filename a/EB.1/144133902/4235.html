EXCRETORY

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXCRETORY, in anatomy, a term applied to certain little ducts or vessels, destined for the reception of a fluid, secreted in certain glands, and other viscera, for the excretion of it in the appropriated places.
