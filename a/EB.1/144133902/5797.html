HEMEROCALLIS, DAY-LILY

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEMEROCALLIS, DAY-LILY, in botany, a genus of the hexandria-monogynia class. The corolla is bell-shaped, with a cylindrical tube; and the stamens are declinate. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
