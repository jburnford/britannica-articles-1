COUNTER-LIGHT

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-LIGHT, or CONTER-JOUR, a light opposite to any thing, which makes it appear to disadvantage. A single counter-light is sufficient to take away all the beauty of a fine painting.
