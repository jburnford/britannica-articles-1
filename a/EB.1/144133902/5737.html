HEBDOMARY

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEBDOMARY, a solemnity of the ancient Greeks, in honour of Apollo, in which the Athenians sung hymns in honour of that god, and carried in their hands branches of laurel. The word signifies the seventh day, this solemnity being observed on the seventh day of every lunar month.
