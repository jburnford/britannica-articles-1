LITHOMARGA

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LITHOMARGA, Stone-marble, a name given by some authors to a sparry substance highly debased by earth, which is found in great plenty in the caves of the Hart's forest in Germany, and used there.
