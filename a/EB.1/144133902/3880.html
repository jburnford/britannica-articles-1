ENCRATITES

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENCRATITES, in church-history, heretics who appeared towards the end of the second century: they were called Encratites, or Continentes, because they gloried in abstaining from marriage and the use of wine and animal-food.
