CARABUS

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARABUS, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of insecta coleoptera. The feelers are bristly; the breast is shaped like a heart, and margined; and the elytra are likewise margined. There are 43 species of this genus, mostly distinguished by their colour.
