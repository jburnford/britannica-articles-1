HURON

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HURON, a vast lake of North America, situated between 84° and 89° W. long and between 43° and 46° N lat from whence the country contiguous to it is called the country of the Hurons, whose language is spoken over a great extent in the southern parts of north America.
