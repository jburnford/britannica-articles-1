EURYTHMY

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EURYTHMY, in architecture, painting, and sculpture, is a certain majesty, elegance, and easiness, appearing in the composition of divers members, or parts of a body, painting, or sculpture, and resulting from the fine proportion of it.
