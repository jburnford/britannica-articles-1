DRUNKENNESS

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRUNKENNESS, a well known disorder in the brain, occasioned by drinking too freely of spirituous liquors. Drunkenness appears in different shapes, in different constitutions: some it makes gay, some sullen, and some furious.
