CATALONIA

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATALONIA, a province of Spain, bounded by the Pyrenean mountains which divide it from France, on the north; by the Mediterranean, on the east and south; and by the provinces of Aragon and Valencia, on the west.
