ELY

ELY, a city and bishop's see of Cambridgeshire, situated about twelve miles north of Cambridge: E. long. 15^{\circ}, and N. lat. 52^{\circ} 24'.

It is a county of itself, including the territory around, and has a judge who determines all causes civil and criminal within its limits.
