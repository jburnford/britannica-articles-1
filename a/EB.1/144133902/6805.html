LEAK

article · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEAK, among seamen, is a hole in the ship through which the water comes in. To spring a leak, is said of a ship that begins to leak. To stop a leak, is to fill it with a plug wrapped in oakum and well tarred, or putting in a tarpaulin clout to keep the water out; or nailing a-piece of sheet-lead upon the place.
