FORRES

FORRES, a parliament-town of Scotland in the county of Murray, about thirteen miles west of Elgin: W. long. 3^{\circ} 20', and N. lat. 57^{\circ} 40'.

It is classed with Inverness, Fortrose, and Nairn.
