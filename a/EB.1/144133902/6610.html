KENKS

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KENKS, in the sea language, doublings in a rope or cable, when handed in and out, so that it does not run easy; or when any rope makes turns or twists, and does not run free in the block, then it is said to make kenks.
