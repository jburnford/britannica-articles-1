EMUNCTORY

Cross-references: ANATOMY

EMUNCTORY, in anatomy, a general term for all those parts which serve to carry off the excrementitious parts of the blood and other humours of the body. Such more especially are the kidneys, bladder, and most of the glands. See ANATOMY.
