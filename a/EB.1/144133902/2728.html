DAMAN

DAMAN, a port-town of the hither India, in the province of Guzurat or Cambay, situated on the west coast, about eighty miles south of Surat, in 72^{\circ} 20' E. long. and 20^{\circ} N. lat. It is subject to the Portuguese.
