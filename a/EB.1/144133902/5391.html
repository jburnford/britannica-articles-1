GREECE

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GREECE, the present Rumelia, and the ancient Hellas, is situated between 20^{\circ} and 26^{\circ} E. long. and between 36^{\circ} and 44^{\circ} N. lat.

It reaches from the Adriatic sea eastward to the Archipelago, and is generally a healthy and fruitful country.
