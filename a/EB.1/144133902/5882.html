HESSE-CASSEL

article · 323 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HESSE-CASSEL landgraviate, including Wetteravia, is a circle of the Upper Rhine, bounded by Westphalia and Brunswick on the north, by Franconia and Saxony on the east, by the river Maine on the south, and by another part of Westphalia and the electorate of Mentz and Triers on the west: it is subject to the king of Sweden.
