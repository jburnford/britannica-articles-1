Carpenters and Pavizur's LEVEL

sub_entry · 339 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Carpenters and Pavizur's LEVEL, consists of a long ruler, in the middle whereof is fitted, at right angles, another somewhat bigger, at the top of which is fastened a line, which, when it hangs over a fiducial line at right angles with the base, shews that the said base is horizontal. Sometimes this level is all of one board. Ibid. no 8.
