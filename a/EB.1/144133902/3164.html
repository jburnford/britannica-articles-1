DICKER

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DICKER, in old writers, denotes the quantity of ten hides of skins, whereof twenty made a last: also ten pair of gloves, ten bars of iron, and the like, are sometimes expressed by the term dicker.
