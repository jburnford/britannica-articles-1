COSENZA, the capital of the hither Calabria, in the kingdom of Naples: E. long. 16° 35', N. lat. 39° 15'. It is an archbishop's see.

CO-SINE, in trigonometry, the sine of an arch, which is the complement of another to 90°. See GEOMETRY.
