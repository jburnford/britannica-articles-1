CHESTERFIELD

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHESTERFIELD, a market-town of Derbyshire, fifteen miles north of Derby, W. long. 1° 25', N. lat. 53° 20'. It gives the title of earl to a branch of the noble family of Stanhope.
