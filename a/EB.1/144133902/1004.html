CHARLEMONT

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARLEMONT is also the name of a town of Ireland, situated on the river Blackwater, in the county of Armagh, and province of Ulster, about six miles south-east of Dungannon: W. long. 6^{\circ} 50', and N. lat. 50^{\circ} 16'.
