KONIGSBURG

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KONIGSBURG, a city of Poland, the capital of ducal Prussia, and of the king of Prussia's Polish dominions, situated on the river Pregel, near a bay of the Baltic sea, seventy miles north-east of Danzick: E. long. 21°, and N. lat. 54° 40'.
