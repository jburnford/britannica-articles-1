LYRIC

article · 367 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYRIC, in general, signifies something sung or played on the lyre: but it is more particularly applied to the ancient odes and stanzas, answering to our airs and songs, and may be played on instruments. This species of poetry was originally employed in celebrating the praises of gods and heroes, though it was afterwards introduced into feasts and public diversions.
