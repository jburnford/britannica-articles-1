Artificial CAMPHOR

sub_entry · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Artificial CAMPHOR is prepared with gum-sandarach and white vinegar distilled, kept twenty days in horse-dung, and afterwards exposed a month to the sun to dry, at the end of which the camphor is found in form of the crust of a white loaf. This is also called juniper-gum, and mastic.
