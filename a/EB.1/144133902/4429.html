FEE

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

FEE, in Scots law, signifies a complete feudal property. See SCOTS LAW, title 10. Hence, where the bare liferent of any feudal subject is meant to be conveyed to A, and the absolute property to B; that meaning is expressed thus, to A in liferent, and to B in fee.
