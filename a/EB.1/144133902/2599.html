CURCULIO

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CURCULIO, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera. The feelers are subclavated, and rest upon the snout, which is prominent and horny. There are no less than ninety-five species, principally distinguished by their colour.
