CONTOUR

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTOUR, in painting, the out-line, or that which defines a figure. A great part of the skill of the painter lies in managing the contours well. Contour, with the Italian painters, signifies the lineaments of the face.
