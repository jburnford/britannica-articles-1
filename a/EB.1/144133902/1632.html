COLDENIA

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLDENIA, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria trigynia class. The calix has four leaves; the corolla is tunnel-shaped; the fruit consists of four seeds. There is but one species, a native of India.
