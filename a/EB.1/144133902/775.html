CAXAMALCA

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAXAMALCA, the name of a town and district of Peru, in South America, where there was a most sumptuous palace belonging to the Yncas, and a magnificent temple dedicated to the sun. It was at Caxamalca that Pizarro put to death Atualpha, their last king.
