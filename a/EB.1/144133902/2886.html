DEFENDER of the faith

sub_entry · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEFENDER of the faith, a peculiar title, belonging to the king of Great Britain, as Catholic does to the king of Spain, Christian to the king of France, &c. This title was first given by pope Leo X. to king Henry VIII. for writing against Luther.
