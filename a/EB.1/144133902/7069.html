LOCAL

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LOCAL, in law, something fixed to the freehold, or tied to a certain place: thus real actions are local, since they must be brought in the country where they lie; and local customs are those peculiar to certain countries and places.
