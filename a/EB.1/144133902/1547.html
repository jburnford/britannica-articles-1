COBLENTZ

COBLENTZ, a large city of Germany, in the archbishopric of Triers, and circle of the Lower Rhine, situated at the confluence of the Rhine and Moselle, fifty two miles north-east of Triers, and thirty six south of Cologne: E. long. 7^{\circ} 15', N. lat. 50^{\circ} 30'.
