LINTEL

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LINTEL, in architecture, a piece of stone or timber that lies horizontally over door-posts and window-jambs, as well to bear the thickness of the wall over it, as to bind the sides of the wall together.
