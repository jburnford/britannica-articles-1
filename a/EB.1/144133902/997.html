CHARENTON

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARENTON, the name of two towns in France, the one upon the Marmaude, in the Bourbonnois; the other in the isle of France, near the confluence of the Marne with the Seine, about three miles south-east of Paris: E. long. 2° 30', and N. lat. 48° 45'.
