DEAD'S PART

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

DEAD'S PART, in Scots law, that proportion of the funds of a marriage, which, upon the dissolution of it, goes to the executor of the deceased husband or wife, as the defunct or dead's part. See SCOTS LAW, title 28.
