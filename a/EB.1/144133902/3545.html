DRYAS

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRYAS, in botany, a genus of the icofandria polygynia class. The calix consists of eight segments, and the corolla of eight petals; and the seeds are tailed and hairy. There are two species, both natives of Britain, viz. the pentapetala, or cinquefoil avens; and the octopetala, or mountain avens.
