GUADILQUIVIR

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUADILQUIVIR, a river of Spain, which rises in the mountains of Segura in New Castile, runs the whole length of Andalusia, and passing by Cordova and Seville falls into the Atlantic ocean at St. Lucar.
