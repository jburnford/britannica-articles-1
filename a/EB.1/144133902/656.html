CASTELLAN

article · 309 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTELLAN, the name of a dignity or charge in Poland: The castellans are senators of the kingdom, but senators only of the lower class, who, in diets, sit on low seats, behind the palatines, or great senators. They are a kind of lieutenants of provinces, and command a part of the palatine under the palatine.
