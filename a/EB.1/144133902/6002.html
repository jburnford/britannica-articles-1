HORMINUM

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HORMINUM, CLARY, in botany, a genus of the didynamia gymnospermia class. The calix is bell-shaped, with four nearly equal segments, and a fifth larger and emarginated; and the upper labium of the corolla is concave. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
