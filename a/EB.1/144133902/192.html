CALLIGONUM

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLIGONUM, in botany, a genus of the polyandria digynia class. The calix has five leaves; the petals are four; it has two styli; and the capsule is divided into two partitions, each containing two seeds. There is but one species, viz. the polygonoides, a native of mount Ararat.
