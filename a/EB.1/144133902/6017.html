HOSANNA

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOSANNA, a Hebrew word, signifying Save now, or Save, we beseech thee; from the frequent use of which, during the feast of tabernacles, the whole solemnity got the appellation of Hosanna Rabbi.
