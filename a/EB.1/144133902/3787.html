ELLIPSIS

ELLIPSIS, in grammar, a figure of syntax, wherein one or more words are not expressed; and from this deficiency, it has got the name ellipsis.

The ellipsis, properly so called, is when the deficient word or words must be supplied from elsewhere; as Hectoris Andromache, where uxor is understood; that is, Andromache, Hector's wife.
