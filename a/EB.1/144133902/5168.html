GERUND

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GERUND, in grammar, a verbal noun of the neuter gender, partaking of the nature of a participle, declinable only in the singular number, through all the cases except the vocative; as, nom. amandum, gen. amandi, dat. amando, accus. amandum, abl. amando.
