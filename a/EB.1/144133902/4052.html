ERANTHEMUM

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERANTHEMUM, in botany, a genus of the diandra monogynia class. The calix is divided into five segments; the tube is filiform; and the stigma is simple. There is but one species, a native of Ethiopia.
