HISPANIOLA

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HISPANIOLA, an island of America, in the Atlantic ocean, situated between 67^{\circ} and 74^{\circ} of W. long. and between 18^{\circ} and 20^{\circ} N. lat. being about 420 miles long from east to west, and 120 in breadth. It is frequently called St. Domingo, from the capital thereof.
