CLAVUS

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLAVUS, in antiquity, an ornament upon the robes of the Roman senators and knights, which was more or less broad, according to the dignity of the person: hence the distinction of tunica augusti-clavia and lati-clavia.
