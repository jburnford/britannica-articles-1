CÆSALPINIA

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CÆSALPINIA, in botany, a genus of the decandria monogynia class. The calix has five segments, the lowest of which is largest; the corolla consists of five petals; the capsule is of the pod kind. There are four species, all natives of the Indies.
