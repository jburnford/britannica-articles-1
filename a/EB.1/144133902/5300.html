GOMPHRENA

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOMPHRENA, the purple everlasting flower, in botany, a genus of the pentandria digynia class. The calix consists of three coloured leaves; the nectarium is cylindrical, with ten teeth; and the capsule contains one seed. There are seven species, none of them natives of Britain.
