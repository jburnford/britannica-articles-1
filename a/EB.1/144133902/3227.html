DIMENSION

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIMENSION, in geometry, is either length, breadth, or thickness; hence, a line hath one dimension, viz. length; a superficies two, viz. length and breadth; and a body, or solid, has three, viz. length, breadth, and thickness.
