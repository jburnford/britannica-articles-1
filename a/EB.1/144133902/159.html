CALENBURG-CASTLE

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALENBURG-CASTLE, the capital of a duchy of the same name, in Lower Saxony, in Germany, situated upon the river Leine, about fifteen miles south of Hanover: E. long. 9^{\circ} 40', and N. lat. 52^{\circ} 20'.
