Artillery GUARD

sub_entry · 423 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Artillery GUARD, is a detachment from the army, to secure the artillery: their corps de garde is in the front, and their centries round the park. This is a forty-eight hours guard: and upon a march, they go in the front and rear of the artillery, and must be sure to leave nothing behind; if a gun or waggon break down, the captain is to leave a part of his guard to assist the gunners and matrosses in getting it up again.
