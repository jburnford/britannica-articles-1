CANTERBURY

article · 350 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANTERBURY, the capital city of Kent, fifty-five miles east of London, and sixteen north-west of Dover: E. long. 1° 15', N. lat. 51° 16'.

It is a county of itself, and the see of an archbishop, who is primate and metropolitan of all England. It is a large, populous, and trading city; has a good silk manufactory, and sends two members to parliament.
