ST DAVID'S

sub_entry · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ST DAVID'S is also the name of a town and fort situated on the coast of Coromandel, in the hither India, about eighty miles south of Fort St George: E. long. 79^{\circ} 40', and N. lat. 11^{\circ} 45'.
