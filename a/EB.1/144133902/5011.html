GALICIA

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALICIA, or GUADALAJARA, a province of Mexico, bounded by new Mexico on the north, by the gulph of Mexico on the east, by Mexico Proper on the south, and by the Pacific Ocean and gulph of California on the west.
