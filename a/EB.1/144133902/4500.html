FEU-DUTY

article · 156 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

FEU-DUTY, in Scots law, is the annual rent or duty which a vassal, by the tenor of his right, becomes bound to pay to his superior. See SCOTS LAW, title 11.
