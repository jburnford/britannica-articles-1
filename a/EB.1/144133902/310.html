WATER CANDLESTICK

sub_entry · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WATER CANDLESTICK, a kind of fountain, the spout of which is raised upon a pedestal in form of a large balustrade, which carries a small basin like a table or stand, from which the water falls into a larger basin, level with the alleys in a garden.
