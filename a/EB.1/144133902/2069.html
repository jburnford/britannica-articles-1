COPERNICAN system or hypothesis

sub_entry · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COPERNICAN system or hypothesis, that system of the world, wherein the sun is supposed to rest in the centre, and the planets, with the earth, to move in ellipses round him. See Vol. I. p. 434.
