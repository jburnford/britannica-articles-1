FARM

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FARM, or FARM, signifies the chief messuage in a village; or any large messuage, whereto belongs land, meadow, pasture, wood, common, &c. and which has been used to let for term of life or years, under a certain yearly rent payable by the tenant for the same.
