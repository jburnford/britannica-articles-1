DEMI-CULVERIN

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEMI-CULVERIN, of the least size, is 4¼ inches bore,

10 feet long, and 2000 pounds weight. It carries a ball of 4 inches diameter, and of 9 pounds weight, and its level range is 174 paces.
