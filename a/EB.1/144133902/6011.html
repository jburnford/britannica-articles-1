HOROSCOPE

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOROSCOPE, in astrology, is the degree of the ascendent, or the star that rises above the horizon at a certain moment, which is observed in order to predict some future event, as the success of a design, the fortune of a person who was at that instant born, &c.
