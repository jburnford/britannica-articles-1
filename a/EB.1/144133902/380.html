CANZONE

article · 502 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANZONE, in music, signifies, in general, a song where some little figures are introduced: But it is sometimes used for a sort of Italian poem, usually pretty long, to which music may be composed in the style of a cantata. If this term be added to a piece of instrumental music, it signifies much the same as cantata: If placed in any part of a sonata, it implies the same meaning as allegro, and only denotes that

the part to which it is prefixed, is to be played or sung in a brisk and lively manner.
