HOOKER, in naval architecture, a vessel much used by the Dutch, built like a pink, but rigged and masted like a hoy.

Hookers will lie nearer a wind than vessels with cross sails can do. They are from fifty to two hundred tons burden, and with a few hands will sail to the East Indies.
