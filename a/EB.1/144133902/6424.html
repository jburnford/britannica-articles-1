INSTRUMENT

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INSTRUMENT, in general, whatever is subservient to a cause in producing any effect.

A common case of mathematical instruments contains several compasses, a sector, scale, drawing-pen, and protractor.
