DIABOLUS METELLORUM

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIABOLUS METELLORUM, a title given by chemists to jupiter or tin, because, when incorporated with other metals, it renders them incapable of reduction, or at least very difficult to undergo that operation.
