FRET, or FRETTE

sub_entry · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRET, or FRETTE, in architecture, a kind of knot or ornament, consisting of two lifts or small fillets variously interlaced or interwoven, and running at parallel distances equal to their breadth.
