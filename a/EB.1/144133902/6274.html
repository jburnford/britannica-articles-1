IMPERSONAL VERB

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMPERSONAL VERB, in grammar, a verb to which the nominative of any certain person cannot be prefixed; or, as others define it, a verb destitute of the two first and primary persons, as debet, oportet, &c.
