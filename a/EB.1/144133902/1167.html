CHOIR

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHOIR, that part of the church or cathedral where choisters sing divine service: it is separated from the chancel, where the communion is celebrated; and also from the nave of the church, where the people are placed: the patron is said to be obliged to repair the choir of the church.
