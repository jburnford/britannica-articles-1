ENGRAILED, or INGRAILED

sub_entry · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENGRAILED, or INGRAILED, in heraldry, a term derived from the French grefly, hail; and signifying a thing the hail has fallen upon and broke off the edges, leaving them ragged, or with half-rounds, or semicircles, struck out of their edges.
