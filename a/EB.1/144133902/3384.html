DOLE

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOLE, in our ancient customs, signified a part or portion, most commonly of a meadow, where several persons have shares. It also still signifies a distribution or dealing of alms, or a liberal gift made by a great man to the people.
