LORANTHUS

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LORANTHUS, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The margin of the calix is entire; the corolla consists of six segments folded backwards; and the berry contains one seed. There are five species, none of them natives of Britain.
