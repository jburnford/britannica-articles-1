COPAL

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COPAL, in the materia medica, a resin obtained from several sorts of large trees in New Spain. It is brought to us in irregular lumps; but it has never come into use as a medicine, and is rarely to be met with in the shops.
