CLERK of the nichils

CLERK of the nichils, or nihil, an officer of the exchequer, who makes a roll of all such sums as are nichilled by the sheriffs upon their escheats of green wax,

and delivers them in to the remembrancer of the treasury, to have execution done upon them for the king. See Nihil.
