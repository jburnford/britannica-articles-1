GOOSE-WING

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOOSE-WING, in the sea-language. When a ship fails before, or with a quarter wind on a fresh gale, to make the more haste, they launch out a boom, and fail on the lee side; and a fail so fitted, is called a goose-wing.
