CONCUBINAGE

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONCUBINAGE, denotes sometimes a criminal or prohibited commerce between the sexes; in which sense it comprehends adultery, incest, and simple fornication: but, in a more limited sense, it signifies the cohabitation of a man and a woman in the way of marriage, without having passed the ceremony thereof.
