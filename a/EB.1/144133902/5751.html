HEDYOTIS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEDYOTIS, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The calix consists of one funnel shaped petal; and the capsule has two cells, and many seeds. There are three species, all natives of Ceylon.
