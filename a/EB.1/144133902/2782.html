DAUPHINE

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DAUPHINE, or DAUPHINY, a province of France, bounded by Burgundy on the north, by Piedmont on the east, by Provence on the south, and by the river Rhone, which separates it from Languedoc and the Lyonnais, on the west.
