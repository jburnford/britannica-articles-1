EFFERVESCENCE

article · 337 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: POWDER

EFFERVESCENCE, in a general sense, signifies a slight degree of ebullition in liquors exposed to a due degree of heat: but the chemists apply it to that intestine motion excited in various fluids, either by the mixture of fluids with others of a different nature; or by dropping salts or powders of various kinds into fluids.
