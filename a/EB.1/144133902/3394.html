DOMESTIC

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOMESTIC, any man who acts under another, serving to compose his family; in which he lives, or is supposed to live, as a chaplain, secretary, &c. Sometimes domestic is applied to the wife and children, but very seldom to servants, such as footmen, lacquies, porters, &c.
