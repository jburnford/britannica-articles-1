CASTLE-WORK

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTLE-WORK, service or labour done by inferior tenants, for the building and upholding of castles of defence, towards which some gave their personal assistance, and others paid their contributions. This was one of the three necessary charges to which all lands among our Saxon ancestors were expressly subject.
