GOTHA the capital of the duchy of Saxe Gotha, in Upper Saxony; E. long. 10^{\circ} 36', N. lat. 51^{\circ}.

It is subject to the duke of Saxe Gotha, brother of her royal highness the princess dowager of Wales.
