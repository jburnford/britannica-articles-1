CHAMA

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAMA, in zoology, a genus of shell-fish belonging to the order of vermes testacea. The shell is thick, and has two valves; it is an animal of the oyster kind. Limnæus enumerates 14 species, principally distinguished by the figure of their shells.
