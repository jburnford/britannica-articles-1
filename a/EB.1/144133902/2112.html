CORDWAINERS

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORDWAINERS a term whereby shoemakers are denominated in statutes. By a statute of Jac. I. the master and wardens of the cordwainers company, &c. are to appoint searchers and triers of leather; and no leather is to be sold before searched, sealed, &c.
