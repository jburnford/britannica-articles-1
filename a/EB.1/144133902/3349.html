DIVORCE

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

DIVORCE, is the legal dissolution of a marriage which can be obtained at the suit of the injured party, upon the grounds of adultery or wilful desertion proved against the other. See SCOTS LAW, title 6.
