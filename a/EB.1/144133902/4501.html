FEU-HOLDING

article · 159 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

FEU-HOLDING, in Scots law, is that particular tenor by which a vassal is taken bound to pay an annual rent or feu-duty to his superior. See SCOTS LAW, tit. 11.
