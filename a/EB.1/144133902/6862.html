LENÆA

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LENÆA, in antiquity, a festival of Bacchus, surnamed Lenæus from a vine-prefs. Besides the usual ceremonies at feasts sacred to this god, it was remarkable for poetical contentions, and tragedies acted at this time.
