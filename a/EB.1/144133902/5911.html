HIEROGRAMMATISTS

article · 480 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HIEROGRAMMATISTS, i. e. holy registers, were an order of priests among the ancient Egyptians, who presided over learning and religion.

They had the care of the hieroglyphics, and were the expositors of religious doctrines and opinions. They were looked upon as a kind of prophets, and it is pretended that one of them predicted to an Egyptian king, that an Israelite, (meaning Moses) eminent for his

qualifications and achievements, would lessen and depress the Egyptian monarchy.
