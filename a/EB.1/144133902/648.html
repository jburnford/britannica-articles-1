CASSUMUNAR, in the materia medica, a root approaching to that of zedoary.

It is cardiac and sudoric, and famous in nervous cases: It is also an ingredient in many compositions, and is prescribed in powders, bolusses, and infusions. Its dose is from five to fifteen grains.
