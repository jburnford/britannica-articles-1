COLCHESTER

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLCHESTER, a large borough-town of Essex, situated on the river Coln, twenty miles north-east of Chelmsford, on the road to Harwich: E. long. 1°, N. lat. 51° 55'. It sends two members to parliament.
