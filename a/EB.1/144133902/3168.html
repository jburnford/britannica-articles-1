DICTIONARY

DICTIONARY, in its original acceptance, is the arranging all the words of a language according to the order of the alphabet, and annexing a definition or explanation to each word. When arts and sciences began to be improved and extended, the multiplicity of technical terms rendered it necessary to compile dictionaries either of science in general, or of particular sciences, according to the views of the compiler. For further particulars concerning dictionaries of this kind, see the Preface.
