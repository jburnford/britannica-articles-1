HEDYSARUM

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEDYSARUM, in botany, a genus of the diadelphic decandria class. The corolla is transversely carinated; and the pod is jointed, each joint containing one seed. There are 46 species, only one of which is a native of Britain, viz. the St Foin, or cocks-head.
