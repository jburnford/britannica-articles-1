LINIMENT

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LINIMENT, in pharmacy, a composition of a consistence somewhat thinner than an unguent, and thicker than an oil, used for anointing different parts of the body in various intentions.

The materials proper for composing of a liniment, are oils, fats, balsams, and whatever enters the composition of unguents and plasters.
