DINGWEL

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DINGWEL, or DINGWAL, a parliament-town of Scotland, situated at the west end of the Cromarty-bay, in the county of Ross: W. long. 4^{\circ} 15', and N. lat. 57^{\circ} 56'. It classes with Dornock, Wick, and Kirkwall.
