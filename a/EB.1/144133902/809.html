CENTAUR

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CENTAUR, in ancient poetry, denotes a fabulous kind of animal, half man, half horse.

The Thessalians, who first taught the art of breaking horses, appearing on horseback to make only one body with the animal on which they rode, gave rise to the fiction of the hippocentaur.
