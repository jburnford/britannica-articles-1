ELYSIUM

article · 345 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELYSIUM, or ELYSIAN FIELDS, in heathen mythology, certain plains abounding with woods, fountains, verdure, and every delightful object; supposed to be the habitation of heroes and good men after death.

According to some, the fable of Elysium is of Phœnician extraction, or rather founded upon the account of paradise delivered in the Scriptures.
