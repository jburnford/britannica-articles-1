CRASSULA

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRASSULA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria pentagynia class. The calix consists of five leaves, and the corolla of five petals; there are five nectariferous glands at the base of the germen; and it has five capsules. There are 17 species, none of them natives of Britain.
