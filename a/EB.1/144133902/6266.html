IMPATIENS, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia monogynia class. The calix consists of two leaves, and the

the corolla of five irregular petals; and the capsule has five valves. There are seven species, only one of which, viz. the noli-me-tangere, or touch-me-not, is a native of Britain.
