LOXIA

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LOXIA, in zoology, the name of a genus of birds of the order of the passerines; the distinguishing characters of which are, that the tongue is plain, equal and whole; the beak large, thick, and short, and crooked and convex both ways. There are 48 species, principally distinguished by their colour.
