GREEN HOUSE

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GREEN HOUSE, or conservatory, a house in a garden contrived for sheltering and preserving the most tender and curious exotic plants, which, in our climate, will not bear to be exposed to the open air during the winter season. These are generally large and beautiful structures, equally ornamental and useful.
