CHRYSOPRASUS, or CHRYSOPRASUS, the tenth

of the precious stones, mentioned in the Revelations, as forming the foundation of the heavenly Jerusalem.

The chrysoprasus is a species of prasus, of a pale but pure green colour, with an admixture of yellow.
