IMPERATORIA, MASTER-WORT, in botany, a genus of the pentandria digynia class. The fruit is roundish, compressed, and gibbous in the middle. There is but one species, a native of Switzerland. The root of this plant, though an excellent aromatic, has only place in the plague water of the Edinburgh pharmacopœia.
