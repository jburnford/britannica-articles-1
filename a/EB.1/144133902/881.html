CESARE

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CESARE, among logicians, one of the modes of the second figure of syllogisms; the minor proposition of which is an universal affirmative, and the other two universal negatives: Thus,

Ce No immoral books ought to be read:

sa But every obscene book is immoral:

re Therefore no obscene book ought to be read.
