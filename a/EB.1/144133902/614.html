CARYOTA

article · 376 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARYOTA, in botany, a genus of plants ranged under the palmæ bipennatifolix. The calix of the male is common; the corolla is divided into three parts; and the stamens are numerous. The calix and corolla of the male are the same with those of the female; there is but one pistillum; and the berry contains two seeds. There is but one species, viz. the urens, a native of India.
