COUNTER-POTENT

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-POTENT, contre-potencé, in heraldry, is reckoned a sur as well as vair and ermine, but composed of such pieces as represent the tops of crutches, called in French potences, and in old English potents.
