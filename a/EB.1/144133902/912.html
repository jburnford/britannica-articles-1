CHALLONS on the Marne

CHALLONS on the Marne, the capital of the Chalonnois, in the province of Champaign in France, situated eighty-two miles east of Paris, and thirty south-east of Reims: E. long. 4° 35', N. lat. 48° 55'. It is a bishop's see.
