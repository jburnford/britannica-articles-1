FRIDBURG

FRIDBURG, an imperial city of Bavaria, in Germany: E. long. 10^{\circ}, and N. lat. 48^{\circ} 30'.

FRIDBURG is also the name of two other towns in Germany, both situated in the circle of Upper Saxony, the one nine miles south-west of Dresden, and the other thirty miles west of Leipzig.
