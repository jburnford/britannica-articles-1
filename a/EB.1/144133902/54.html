CADENCE

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CADENCE, in the menage, an equal measure or proportion, observed by a horse in all his motions; so that his times have an equal regard to one another, the one does not embrace or take in more ground than the other, and the horse observes his ground regularly.
