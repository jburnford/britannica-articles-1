CADARI

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CADARI, or KADARI, a sect of Mahometans, which attributes the actions of men to men alone, and not to the divine decree determining his will; and denies all absolute decrees, and predestination. Ben Aun calls the cadari, the magi or manichees of the mufslmans.
