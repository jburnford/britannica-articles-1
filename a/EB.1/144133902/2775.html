DAVIDISTS

article · 366 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DAVIDISTS, in church-history, a sect of Christian heretics in the XVIth century; so called from David George, their leader, who began by giving out that he was the Messiah, and was sent into the world in order to people the kingdom of heaven, which was quite empty of inhabitants, for want of virtuous and good men: he rejected marriage, and denied the resurrection.
