CONCAVE GLASSES

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: OPTICS

CONCAVE GLASSES, such as are ground hollow, and are usually of a spherical figure, though they may be of any other, as parabolical, &c. All objects seen through concave glasses, appear erect and diminished. See OPTICS.
