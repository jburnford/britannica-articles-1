CALIXTINS

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALIXTINS, is also a name given to those, among the Lutherans, who follow the sentiments of George Calixtus, a celebrated divine, who opposed the opinion of St. Augustine, on predestination, grace, and free-will.
