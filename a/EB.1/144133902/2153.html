CORNUCOPIÆ

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORNUCOPIÆ, in botany, a genus of the triandria digynia class. The involucrum consists of one tunnel-shaped crenated leaf, containing many flowers; and the calices are double-valved. There is but one species, viz. the cucullatum, a native of Smyrna.
