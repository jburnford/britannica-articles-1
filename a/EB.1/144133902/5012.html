GALILEE

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALILE, or GALILEE, once a province of Judea, now of Turkey in Asia, was bounded by mount Lebanon on the north, by the river Jordan and the sea of Galilee on the east, by the river Chifon on the south, and by the Mediterranean on the west. It was the scene of many of our Saviour's miracles.
