CONTRARY

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTRARY, a positive opposite, which, subsisting by turns in the same subject with its opposite, is as remote from it as possible, expells it, and is mutually expelled by it. Blackness and whiteness, cold and heat, are such contraries.
