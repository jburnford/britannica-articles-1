KING'S COUNTY

KING'S COUNTY, a county of Ireland, in the province of Leinster, bounded by West-meath on the north, by the county of Kildare on the east, by Queen's county and Tipperary on the south, and by the river Shannon, which separates it from Galway, on the west.
