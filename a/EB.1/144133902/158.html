CALEFACTION

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALEFACTION, the production of heat in a body from the action of fire, or that impulse impressed by a hot body upon other bodies about it. This word is used in pharmacy, by way of distinction from coction, which implies boiling; whereas calefaction is only heating a thing.
