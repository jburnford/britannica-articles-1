HAMPTON-COURT

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAMPTON-COURT, a town in Middlesex, situated on the north side of the Thames, twelve miles west of London, and two west of Kingston; in which is the finest palace belonging to the king of Britain.
