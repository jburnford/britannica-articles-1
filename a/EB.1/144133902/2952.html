DEMI-CULVERIN

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEMI-CULVERIN, of the largest sort, is 4\frac{1}{2} inches bore, 10\frac{1}{2} feet long, and weighs 3000 pounds weight. It carries a ball 4\frac{1}{2} inches diameter, weighing 12 pounds 11 ounces, point blank 178 paces.
