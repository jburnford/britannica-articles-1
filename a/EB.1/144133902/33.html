CACALIA

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CACALIA, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygonia æqualis class. The receptacle is naked; the pappus is hairy; and the calix is cylindrical, oblong, and has a kind of small cup at the base. There are 12 species, none of which are natives of Britain.
