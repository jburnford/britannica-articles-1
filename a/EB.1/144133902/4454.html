FEOFFMENT

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FEOFFMENT, in law, is a gift or grant of any manors, messuages, lands, or tenements, to another in fee; that is, to him and his heirs for ever, by delivery of seisin, and possession of the estate granted.
