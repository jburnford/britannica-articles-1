DESPOTICAL

article · 372 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DESPOTICAL, in general, denotes any thing that is uncontrolled and absolute; but is particularly used for an arbitrary government, where the power of the prince is unlimited, and his will a law to his subjects: such are those of Turkey, Persia, and most of the eastern governments; and even those of Europe, if we except the republics, our own, and the Swedish government.
