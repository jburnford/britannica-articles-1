DEMOCRACY

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEMOCRACY, the same with a popular government, wherein the supreme power is lodged in the hands of the people: such were Rome and Athens of old; but as to our modern republics, Basil only excepted, their government comes nearer to aristocracy than democracy.
