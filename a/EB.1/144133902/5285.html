GOLD-WIRE fluted

sub_entry · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: STUFF

GOLD-WIRE fluted, is the former wire flatted between two rollers of polished steel, to fit it to be spun on a flick, or to be used flat, as it is, without spinning, in certain stuffs, laces, embroideries, &c. See STUFF, &c.
