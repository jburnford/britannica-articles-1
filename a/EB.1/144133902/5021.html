GALLEY

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SHIP

GALLEY, in naval affairs, a low-built vessel, using both sails and oars, and commonly carrying only a main-mast and fore-mast, which may be struck or lowered at pleasure. Such vessels are much used in the Mediterranean, especially by the king of France. See SHIP.
