CALVI

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALVI is also the name of a sea-port in the island of Corsica, situated on a bay, on the west side of the island, about forty miles south-west of Balia: E. long. 9^{\circ} 5', and N. lat. 42^{\circ} 16'.
