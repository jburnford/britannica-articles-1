HYPOCHAERIS

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPOCHAERIS, in botany a genus of the syngenesia polygamia æqualis class. The receptacle is paleaceous; the calix is imbricated; and the pappus is plumose. There are four species, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the radicata, or long-rooted hawkweed; and the maculata, or spotted hawkweed.
