COURT-BARON, a court that every lord of a manor has within his own precincts. This court must be held by

by prescription, and is of two kinds, viz. by common law, and by custom: the former is where the barons or freeholders, being suitors, are the judges; the other is that where the lord, or his steward, is the judge.
