LIMIT

article · 483 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ALGEBRA

LIMIT, in a restrained sense, is used by mathematicians for a determinate quantity to which a variable one continually approaches; in which sense, the circle may be said to be the limit of its circumscribed and inscribed polygons. In algebra, the term limits is applied to two quantities, one of which is greater, and the other less, than another quantity: and in this sense it is used, in speaking of the limits of equations, whereby their solution is much facilitated.
