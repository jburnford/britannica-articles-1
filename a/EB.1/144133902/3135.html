DIAPEDESIS

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIAPEDESIS, in medicine, a transfusion of the fluids through the sides of the vessels that contain them, occasioned by the blood's becoming too much attenuated, or the pores becoming too patent.
