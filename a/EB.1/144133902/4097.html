ERZERUM

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERZERUM, the capital of the province of Turcomania, or Armenia: E. long. 41°, N. lat. 40°. It is a great thoroughfare from Persia and India to Constantinople, by the way of Trebisond and the Black-sea.
