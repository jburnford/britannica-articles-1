Letters of EXCUSATION

sub_entry · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Letters of EXCUSATION, in Scots law, a writ or summons issued by authority of the court of judicature, at the instance of a panel, for citing witnesses to prove his defences, or his objections to any of the jury or witnesses cited against him. See tit. 33.
