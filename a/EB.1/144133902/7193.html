LYGEUM

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYGEUM, in botany, a genus of the triandria monogynia class. The spatha consists of one leaf; there are two corollæ above the same germin; and the nut has two cells. There is but one species, a native of Spain.
