DOM

article · 335 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOM, or DON, a title of honour, invented and chiefly used by the Spaniards, signifying, sir, or lord.

This title, it seems, was first given to Pelayo, in the beginning of the VIIIth century. In Portugal no person can assume the title of don, without the permission of the king, since it is looked upon as a mark of honour and nobility.
