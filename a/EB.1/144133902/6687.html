LABRADOR

article · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LABRADOR, also called New-Britain, and Eskimaux, is a country in North America, bounded by Hudson's Straits and the Atlantic Ocean, on the north; by the same ocean, on the east; by the river of St. Lawrence

and Canada, on the south; and by Hudson's bay, on the west: situated between 59^{\circ} and 79^{\circ} of W. long. and between 50^{\circ} and 64^{\circ} of N. lat.
