DESCENT

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DESCENT, in heraldy, is used to express the coming down of any thing from above; as, a lion en descent, is a lion with his head towards the base points, and his heels towards one of the corners of the chief, as if he were leaping down from some high place.
