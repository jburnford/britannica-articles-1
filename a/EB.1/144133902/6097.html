HYDROCOTYLE

article · 309 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYDROCOTYLE, in botany, a genus of the pentandria digynia class. The umbella is simple; the involucrum consists of four leaves; the petals are entire, and the seeds are roundish and compressed. There are five species, only one of them, viz. the vulgaris, marsh pennywort, or white rot, is a native of Britain.
