LIGNICENSIS terra

sub_entry · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIGNICENSIS terra, in the materia medica, the name of a fine yellow hole, dug in many parts of Germany, particularly about Emeric in the circle of Westphalia, and used in cordial and astringent compositions.
