HELIOCENTRIC latitude of a planet

sub_entry · 154 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELIOCENTRIC latitude of a planet, the inclination of a line drawn between the centre of the sun and the center of a planet, to the plane of the ecliptic.
