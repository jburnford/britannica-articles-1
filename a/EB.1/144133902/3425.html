DORMANT

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DORMANT, in heraldry, is used for the posture of a lion, or any other beast, lying along in a sleeping attitude, with the head on the fore-paws; by which it is distinguished from the couchant, where though the beast be lying, yet he holds up his head.
