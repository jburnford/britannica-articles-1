GAUGER

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GAUGER, a king's officer, who is appointed to examine all tuns, pipes, hogheads, and barrels of wine, beer, ale, oil, honey, &c. and give them a mark of allowance, before they are sold in any place within the extent of his office.
