CLEF

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLEF, or CLIFF, in music, a mark set at the beginning of the lines of a song, which shews the tone or key in which the piece is to begin; or it is a letter marked on any line, which explains the rest.
