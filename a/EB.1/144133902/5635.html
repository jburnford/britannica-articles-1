HANDS

article · 359 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HANDS, in heraldry, are borne in coat-armour dexter and sinister, that is, right and left, expanded or open. These are the most necessary parts of the human body, as they serve to express all sorts of actions, and even our very thoughts and designs; thus, joining of hands is an universal token of friendship, and clapping of hands a general mark of applause.
