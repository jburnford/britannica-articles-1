ELOPEMENT

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELOPEMENT, in law, is where a married woman de-

parts from her husband, and cohabits with an adulterer; in which case the husband is not obliged to allow her any alimony out of his estate, nor is he chargeable for necessaries for her of any kind.
