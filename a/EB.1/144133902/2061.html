CONZA

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONZA, a town of the kingdom of Naples in Italy, situated on the further Principate, on the river Offanto, fifty miles south-east of the city of Naples: E. long. 16^{\circ}, N. lat. 41^{\circ}. It is the see of an archbishop.
