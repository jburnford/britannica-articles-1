COPENHAGEN

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COPENHAGEN, the capital of the kingdom of Denmark, situated on the eastern shore of the island of Zealand, upon a fine bay of the Baltic sea, not far from the strait called the Sound: E. long. 13^{\circ}, and N. lat. 55^{\circ} 30'.
