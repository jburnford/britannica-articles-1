FLAGOLET

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLAGOLET, or FLAJOLET, a little flute, used chiefly by shepherds and country people. It is made of box, or other hard wood, and sometimes of ivory, and has six holes besides that at the bottom, the mouth-piece, and that behind the neck.
