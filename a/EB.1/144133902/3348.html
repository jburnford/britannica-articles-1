DIUL

article · 163 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIUL, a port-town of Asia, situated on the Indian ocean, westward of the river Indus, and sixty miles west of the city of Tatta: E. long. 67°, and N. lat. 25° 15'.
