DIASEBESTEN

article · 296 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIASEBESTEN, in pharmacy, a soft purgative electuary, whereof sebestens are the principal ingredients. The other ingredients are prunes, tamarinds, juices of iris, anguria and mercurialis, penidies, simple diaprurum, violet seeds, and diagrydium. It is good in remitting and continued fevers, &c.
