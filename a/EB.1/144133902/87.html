CÆSARIANS

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CÆSARIANS, caesarienses, in Roman antiquity, were officers or ministers of the Roman emperors: They kept the account of the revenues of the emperors, and took possession, in their name, of such things as devolved, or were confiscated to them.
