LANDSKIP

LANDSKIP, or LANDSCAPE, in painting, the view or prospect of a country, extended as far as the eye will reach.

Landskips are esteemed one of the lowest branches of painting, representing some rural scene, as hills, valleys, rivers, country-houses, &c. where human figures are only introduced as accidents.
