CONCENTRATION

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CHEMISTRY

CONCENTRATION, in general, signifies the bringing things nearer a center. Hence the particles of salt, in sea-water, are said to be concentrated; that is, brought nearer each other, by evaporating the watery part. See CHEMISTRY.
