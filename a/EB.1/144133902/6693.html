LACE

article · 336 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LACE, in commerce, a work composed of many threads of gold, silver or silk, interwoven the one with the other, and worked upon a pillow with spindles, according to the pattern designed. The open work being formed with pins, which are placed and displaced as the spindles are moved.

The importation of gold and silver lace is prohibited.
