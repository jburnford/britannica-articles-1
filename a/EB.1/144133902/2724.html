DALMATIA

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DALMATIA, a frontier province of Europe, mostly subject to the Turks, but some towns on the sea-coast to the Venetians: it is bounded by Bosnia on the north, by Servia on the east, by Albania on the south,

and by Morlachia and the gulph of Venice on the west.
