LYNN-REGIS

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYNN-REGIS, a port-town of Norfolk, situated at the mouth of the river Ouse, on a bay of the German sea, thirty-two miles west of Norwich.

It sends two members to parliament.
