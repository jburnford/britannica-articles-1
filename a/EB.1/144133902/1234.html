CRYSOGONUM

article · 332 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRYSOGONUM, or MOTH-MULLEIN, in botany, a genus of plants belonging to the syngenesia polygamia necessaria class. The receptacle is paleaceous; the pappus is monophyllous, and three-toothed; the calix consists of five leaves; and the seeds are caliculated, and invaled in four leaves. There is but one species, a native of Virginia.
