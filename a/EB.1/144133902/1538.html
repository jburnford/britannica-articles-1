COAL, or PIT-COAL, in natural history. See LITHANTHRAX.

Cannel-Coal, in natural history. See AMPELITES.

Small-Coal, a sort of charcoal prepared from the spray and brush-wood stripped off from the branches of coppice-wood, sometimes bound in bavers for that purpose, and sometimes charred without binding, and then it is called coming it together.
