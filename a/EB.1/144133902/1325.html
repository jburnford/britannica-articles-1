CIRCOLO MEZZO

article · 151 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIRCOLO MEZZO, in the Italian music, denotes a diminution of four quavers or semiquavers, which represent a semicircle, proceeding by conjoint degrees.
