HANOVER, a city of Germany, in the circle of Lower Saxony, and dukedom of Brunswick, situated on the river Leina, thirty-six miles west of Brunswick: it is the capital of his Britannic majesty's German dominions, situated in E. long. 9^{\circ} 45', N. lat. 52^{\circ} 32'.
