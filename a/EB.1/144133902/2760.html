DARMSTADT

DARMSTAT, the capital of Hesse-Darmstat, in the circle of the upper Rhine in Germany, situated on a river of the same name, fourteen miles south of Frankfurt, and thirteen south-east of Mentz: E. long. 8° 25', and N. lat. 49° 45'.
