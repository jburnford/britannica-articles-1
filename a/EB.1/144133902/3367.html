DODARTIA

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DODARTIA, in botany, a genus of the didynamia angiospermia class. The calix has five teeth; the inferior labium is much less than the superior; and the capsule is roundish, and has two cells. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
