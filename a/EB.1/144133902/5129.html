GENIUS

Cross-references: DAMON

GENIUS, a good or evil spirit, or demon, whom the ancients supposed set over each person, to direct his birth, accompany him in life, and be his guard. See DAMON.

The rank and office of the genii were inferior to those of the lares; for the latter were the tutelary

gods of a family, whereas the genii had the care or government only of single persons, or places.
