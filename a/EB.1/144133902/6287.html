IMPROPRIATION

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMPROPRIATION, a parsonage or ecclesiastical living, the profits of which are in the hands of a layman; in which sense, it stands distinguished from appropriation, which is where the profits of a benefice are in the hands of a bishop, college, &c. though these terms are now often used promiscuously.
