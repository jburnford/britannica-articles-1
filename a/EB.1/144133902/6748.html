LANGUEDOC

LANGUEDOC, a province of France, bounded by Liois, on the north; by the river Rhone, which divides it from Dauphine and Provence, on the east; by the Mediterranean and the Pyrenees, on the south; and by Guienne and Gascony, on the west.
