GANGES

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GANGES, a large river of the hither India, rises in the mountains which separate India from Tartary; and, running from the north west to the south east near 1500 miles through the Mogul's dominions, discharges itself by several channels into the bay of Bengal.
