LITHOZUGIA

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LITHOZUGIA, in natural history, a genus of fossils, composed of a simple stony matter, making a kind of cement, and holding firmly together small pebbles &c. embodied in it.
