COCCUS

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COCCUS, in zoology, a genus belonging to the order of hemiptera. The rostrum proceeds from the breast; the belly is bristly behind; the wings of the male are erect; and the female has no wings. The species are twenty-two, denominated principally from the plants they frequent.
