LAUNCH

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAUNCH, in the sea language, signifies to put out: as, launch the ship, that is, put her out of the dock: launch ast, or forward, speaking of things that are stowed in the hold, is, put them more forward: launch, ho! is a term used when a yard is hoisted high enough, and signifies, hoist no more.
