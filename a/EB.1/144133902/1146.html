CHIOZZO

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHIOZZO, or CHIOTTO, a town on an island of the same name, in the gulph of Venice, by which there is a passage into the Lagunes, situated about twelve miles south of the city of Venice.
