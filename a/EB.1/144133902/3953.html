EPAULEMENT

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPAULEMENT, in fortification, a work raised to cover sidewall, is either of earth, gabions, or fascines loaded with earth. The epaulments of the places of arms for the cavalry, at the entrance of the trenches, are generally of fascines mixed with earth.
