COMMEMORATION

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMEMORATION, in a general sense, the remembrance of any person or thing; or the doing any thing in honour of a person's memory, or in remembrance of any past event. Thus the eucharist is a commemoration of the sufferings of Jesus Christ.
