CASTILE

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTILE, the name of two inland provinces of Spain, situated almost in the middle of that kingdom: The most southerly one is called New Castile, and the other, towards the north, Old Castile; Madrid being the capital of the former, and Burges of the latter.
