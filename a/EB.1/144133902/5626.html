HAMPSHIRE, an English country, bounded by Berkshire, on the north; by Surrey and Sussex, on the east; by the English channel, on the south; and by Wiltshire and Dorsetshire, on the west. It comprehends the Isle of Wight. Its chief towns are Winchester, Southampton, and Portsmouth.
