ECPIESMUS

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ECPIESMUS, in the ancient writers of medicine, a word used to express a distemperature of the eye, consisting in a very great prominence of the entire globe of the eye, which is, as it were, thrust out of its socket or orbit, by a great flux of humours, or an inflammation.
