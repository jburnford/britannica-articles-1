GRASS

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRASS, in botany, &c. a name given to several distinct plants; as, the agrostis, or couch grass; the briza, or quaking-grass, &c. Under the term grass are also comprehended all manner of herbaceous plants serving for the food of cattle, as clover, ryegrass, &c.
