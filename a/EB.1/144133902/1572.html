COCONATO

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COCONATO, a town of Italy in the province of Piedmont, about twenty miles east of Turin; it is said to be the birth-place of the famous Columbus, who discovered America: E. long. 8°, and N. lat. 44° 50'.
