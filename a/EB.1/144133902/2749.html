DANUBE

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DANUBE, one of the largest rivers in Europe, which, taking its rise in the Black Forest in Swabia, runs eastward through Bavaria, Austria, Hungary, and Turkey in Europe; discharging itself by several channels into the Pontus Euxinus, or Black Sea.
