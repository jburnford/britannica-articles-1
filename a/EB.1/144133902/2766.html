DATA

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DATA, among mathematicians, a term for such things or quantities as are given or known, in order to find other things thereby that are unknown. Euclid uses the word data (of which he hath a particular tract) for

for such spaces, lines, and angles as are given in magnitude, or to which we can assign others equal.
