CONDITION

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONDITION, in the civil law, a clause of obligation stipulated as an article of a treaty or contract; or in a donation of testament, legacy, &c. in which last case a donee does not lose his donative, if it be charged with any dishonest or impossible conditions.
