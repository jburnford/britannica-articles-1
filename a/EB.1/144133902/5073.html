CASTEROSTEUS

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTEROSTEUS, in ichthyology, a genus of fishes belonging to the order of thoracici. There are three

rays in the membrane of the gills; the body is carinated; and there are some distinct prickles before the back-fin. There are eleven species, distinguished by the number of prickles on the back.
