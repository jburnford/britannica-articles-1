COLLEGIATE churches

sub_entry · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLLEGIATE churches, those which though no bishop's see, yet have the retinue of the bishop, the canons and prebends. Such are, among us, Westminster, Windsor, Rippon, Wolverhampton, Southwell, Manchester, &c. governed by deans and chapters.
