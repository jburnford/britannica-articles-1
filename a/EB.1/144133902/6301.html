INCARNATION

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INCARNATION, in theology, the act whereby the second person of the holy Trinity assumed the human nature, viz. a true body and reasonable soul, in order to accomplish the redemption of fallen mankind.
