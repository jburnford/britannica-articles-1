GUERNSEY

article · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUERNSEY, or GARNSEY, an island in the English channel, on the coast of Normandy, fifty-eight miles south of Portland, in Dorsetshire, and twenty two west of cape la Hogue, in Normandy; about ten miles long and as many broad, containing ten parishes. The natives, who speak French, are still governed by the Norman laws, but are subject to England.
