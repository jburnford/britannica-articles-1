GOBE

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOBE, in heraldry, one of the abatement, which, according to Guillim, denotes a coward. It is a figure consisting of two arch lines drawn one from the sinister chief, and the other from the sinister base, both meeting in an acute angle in the middle of the fess point. See Plate XCVII. fig. 4.
