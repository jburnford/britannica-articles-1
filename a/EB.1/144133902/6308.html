INCISIVE

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INCISIVE, an appellation given to whatever cuts or divides: thus, the fore-teeth are called dentes incisivi, or cutters; and medicines of an attenuating nature, incidents, or incisive medicines.
