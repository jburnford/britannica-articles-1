EPIMEDIUM

EPIMEDIUM, BARREN-WORT, in botany, a genus of the tetrandia monogynia class. It has four cap-shaped nectaria lying upon the petals; the corolla consists of four petals; and the calix is caducous. There is but one species, a native of Germany.
