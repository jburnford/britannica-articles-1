CORBAN

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORBAN is also a ceremony which the Mahometans perform at the foot of mount Ararat, in Arabia, near Mecca. It consists in killing a great number of sheep, and distributing them among the poor.
