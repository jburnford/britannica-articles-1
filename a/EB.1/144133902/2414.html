CREMONA

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CREMONA, a city of Italy, and capital of a district of the Milanese, called from it the Cremonese, is situated forty-five miles south-east of Milan, in 10° 30' E. long. and 45° N. lat.
