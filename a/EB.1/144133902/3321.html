DITRIHEDRIA

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: SPAR

DITRIHEDRIA, in mineralogy, a genus of spars with twice three sides, or six planes, being formed of two trigonal pyramids joined base to base, without any intermediate column. See SPAR.

The species of ditrihedria are distinguished by the different figures of these pyramids.
