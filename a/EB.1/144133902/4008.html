EPITAPH

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPITAPH, a monumental inscription in honour or memory of a person deceased, or an inscription engraven or cut on a tomb, to mark the time of a person's decease, his name, family; and, usually, some eulogium of his virtues, or good qualities.
