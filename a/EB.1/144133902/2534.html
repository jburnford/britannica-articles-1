CRUSADO

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRUSADO, in commerce, a Portuguese coin, struck under Alphonse V. about the year 1457, at the time when pope Calixtus sent thither the bull for a crusade against the infidels.

This coin has a cross on one side, and the arms of Portugal on the other.
