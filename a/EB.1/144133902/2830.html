DECENNALIA

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DECENNALIA, ancient Roman festivals celebrated by the emperors, every tenth year of their reign, with sacrifices, games, and largesses for the people. The emperor Augustus first instituted these solemnities, in which he was imitated by his successors.
