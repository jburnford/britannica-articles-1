CANCELLI

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANCELLI, a term used to denote lattice-windows, or those made of cross-bars, disposed lattice-wise; it is also used for rails or ballusters, inclosing the communion-table, a court of justice, and the like, and for the net-work in the inside of hollow bones.
