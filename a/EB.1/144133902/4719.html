FonteVRAUD

FonteVRAUD, or Order of FonteVRAUD, a religious order instituted about the latter end of the XIth century. By the rules of this order the nuns were to keep silence for ever, and their faces to be always covered with their veils; and the monks wore a leathern girdle, at which hung a knife and sheath.
