NEW CARTHAGENA

sub_entry · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEW CARTHAGENA, the capital of a province of the same name, in South America, situated on a kind of peninsula: W. long. 77°, and N. lat. 11°. It is one of the largest and best fortified towns in South America.
