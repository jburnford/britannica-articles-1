CAVILLON

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAVILLON, a town of Provence in France, situated on the river Durance, about fifteen miles south of Avignon: E. long. 5°, and N. lat. 43° 50'. It is a bishop's see, and subject to the pope.
