DUUMVIRI SACRORUM

article · 377 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUUMVIRI SACRORUM were magistrates created by Tarquinius Superbus, for the performance of the sacrifice, and keeping of the sybils books. They were chosen from among the patricians, and held their office for life: they were exempted from serving in the wars, and from the offices imposed on the other citizens, and without them the oracles of the sybils could not be consulted.
