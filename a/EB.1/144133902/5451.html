GUADIANA

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUADIANA, a river of Spain, which rises in the middle of New Castile, and running through Estremadura, enters Portugal; where, passing through the provinces of Alentejo and Algarva, it discharges itself into the Atlantic ocean.
