FORISFAMILIATION

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

FORISFAMILIATION, in law, when a child, upon receiving a portion from his father, or otherwise renounces his legal title to any further share of his father's succession, he is said to be forisfamiliarized. See SCOTS LAW, title 28.
