FELUCCA

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FELUCCA, in sea-affairs, a little vessel with six oars, frequent in the Mediterranean, which has this peculiarity, that its helm may be applied either in the head or stern, as occasion requires.
