JACOBITES

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JACOBITES, a term of reproach bestowed on the persons, who, vindicating the doctrines of passive obedience and non-resistance with respect to the arbitrary proceedings of princes, disallow of the late revolution, and assert the supposed rights and adhere to the interests of the late abdicated king James and his family.
