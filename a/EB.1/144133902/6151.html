JAGO DE LA VEGA

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JAGO DE LA VEGA, or Spanish town, the capital of Jamaica, situated at the south-east part of the island, about seven miles north-west of Port Passage and the bay of Port Royal: E. lon. 76° 30', N. lat. 18° 20'.
