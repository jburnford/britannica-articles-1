CHATHAM

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHATHAM, a port-town of Kent, adjoining to Rochester, situated on the river Medway, thirty miles south-east of London: E. long. 40^{\circ}, N. lat. 51^{\circ} 20'.

It is the principal station of the royal navy, furnished with timber, rope-yards, and all manner of naval stores, sufficient for the building and fitting out the largest fleet.
