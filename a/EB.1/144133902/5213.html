GLARIS

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GLARIS, the capital of one of the cantons of Switzerland, of the same name, the inhabitants of which are both protestant and popish: it is situated thirty-five miles south-east of Zurich, in E. long. 9°, and N. lat. 47°.
