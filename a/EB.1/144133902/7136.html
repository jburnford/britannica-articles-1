LUCIA ISLANDS

LUCIA ISLANDS, one of the Caribbee islands in America, situated seventy miles north-west of Barbadoes, being twenty-two miles long, and eleven broad.

St. LUCIA, one of the Cape Verd islands in Africa, lying in W. long. 25^{\circ}, N. lat. 16^{\circ} 30'.
