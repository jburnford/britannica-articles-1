CRINUM

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRINUM, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The corolla is tunnel-shaped, and consists of one leaf, divided into six segments; and the germ is at the bottom of the corolla. There are four species, none of them natives of Britain.
