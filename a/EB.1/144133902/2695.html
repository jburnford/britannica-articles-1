CZACKATHURN

CZACKATHURN, a town of Germany, in the duchy of Stiria, and circle of Austria, situated near the conflux of the rivers Muer and Save, about fifty miles south-east of Gratz: E. long. 17°, and N. lat. 46° 50'.
