ISLE of France

sub_entry · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISLE of France, a province of that kingdom, in which the capital city of Paris is situated, being bounded by Picardy on the north, by Champain on the east, by Orleans on the south, and by Normandy on the west.
