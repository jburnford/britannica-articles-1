CORSICA

CORSICA, an island in the Mediterranean, between 8^{\circ} and 10^{\circ} E. long. and between 41^{\circ} and 43^{\circ} N. lat. about one hundred miles south of Genoa, and subject to that republic; though the natives for many years disputed their right. This island is now in the hands of the French, after a glorious struggle for liberty under general Paoli.
