FLYING FISH

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: EXOCOETUS

FLYING FISH, a name given by the English writers to several species of fish, which, by means of their long fins, have a method of keeping themselves out of water a long time. See EXOCOETUS.
