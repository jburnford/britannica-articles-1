FRIESLAND

FRIESLAND, one of the most northern provinces of the united Netherlands, bounded by the German ocean on the north, by Groningen and Overyssel on the east, by the Zuider-sea and Overyssel on the south, and by the same ocean on the west: its chief town is Lewarden.
