HEIR

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEIR, in Scots law, a generic term applicable to those who are intitled by law to take the possession of any subject which belonged to a person deceased.—For the different kinds of heirs, &c. see LAW, tit. 27 & 28.
