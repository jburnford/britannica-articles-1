FERROL

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FERROL, a sea-port-town of Spain, in the principality of Galicia, situated on a bay of the Atlantic ocean, twenty miles north-east of the Groyne, and fifty miles north of Compostella, a good harbour, where the Spanish squadrons frequently secured themselves in the late war: W. long. 8° 40', N. lat. 43° 30'.
