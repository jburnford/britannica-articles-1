CARWAR

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARWAR, a town on the coast of Malabar, in the Higher India, sixty miles south of Goa: E. long. 73°, and N. lat. 15°. Here our East-India company have a factory, from whence they import pepper.
