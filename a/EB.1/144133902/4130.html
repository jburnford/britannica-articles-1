ESSEX

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESSEX, a county of England, bounded by Suffolk, on the north; by the German sea, on the east; by the river Thames, which divides it from Kent, on the south; and by Middlesex and Hertfordshire, on the west.
