FLUSHING, or VLISSENGEN

sub_entry · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLUSHING, or VLISSENGEN, a port town of Zealand in Holland, five miles south of Middleburgh: E. long. 3° 25', N. lat. 51° 30'. It is a town of great foreign trade, and has a good secure harbour.
