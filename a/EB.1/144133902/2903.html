DEGRADED CROSS

article · 172 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEGRADED CROSS, in heraldry, a cross divided into steps at each end, diminishing as they ascend towards the centre, called by the French perronée. See Plate LXVIII. fig. 6.
