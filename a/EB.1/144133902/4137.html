ESTOILLE'

sub_entry · 167 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESTOILLE', or CROSS ESTOILLE', in heraldry, a star with only four long rays in form of a cross; and, accordingly, broad in the centre, and terminating in sharp points.
