CHEASPEAK-BAY

CHEASPEAK-BAY, a large frith or arm of the sea, which runs up about three hundred miles into the country between Virginia and Maryland, in North America: It is navigable almost all the way for large ships; being about twenty miles broad at the entrance between Charles-cape and Cape Henry, and between twenty and thirty miles broad afterwards.
