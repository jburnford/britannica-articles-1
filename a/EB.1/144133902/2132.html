CORMANDEL-COAST

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORMANDEL-COAST, comprehends the eastern coast of the hither India, bounded by Golconda on the north, the bay of Bengal on the east, Madura on the south, and Bijnagar on the west: it lies between 10^{\circ} and 20^{\circ} N. lat.
