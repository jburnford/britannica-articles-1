CHRYSOPHYLLUM

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHRYSOPHYLLUM, in botany, a genus of the pentandria monogynia class. The corolla is bell-shaped, and divided into ten segments, which alternately spread wider; and the berry contains ten seeds. There are but two species, both natives of America.
