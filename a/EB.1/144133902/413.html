CAPPACIA

CAPPACIA, a town of the hither principate, in the kingdom of Naples. It is a bishop's see, and situated about fifty-five miles south-east of the city of Naples: E. long. 15^{\circ} 20', and N. lat. 40^{\circ} 40'.
