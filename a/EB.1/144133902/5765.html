HELENIUM

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELENIUM, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygamia superflua class. The receptacle is naked; the calix is simple, and consists of many leaves; and the corollule of the radius are semitrifid. There is but one species, a native of Austria.
