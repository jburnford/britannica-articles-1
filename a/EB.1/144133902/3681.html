ECHINUS

ECHINUS, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of vermes mollusca. The body is roundish, covered with a bony crust, and often beset with moveable prickles; and the mouth is below, and consists of five valves. There are seventeen species, all natives of the sea.
