CORPORAL of a ship

sub_entry · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORPORAL of a ship, an officer who has the charge of setting and relieving the watches and centries, and who sees that the soldiers and sailors keep their arms neat and clean: he teaches them how to use their arms, and has a mate under him.
