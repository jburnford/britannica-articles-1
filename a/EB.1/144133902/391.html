CAP-AGA, or CAPOU-AGASSI, a Turkish officer, who is, as it were, grand-master of the seraglio.

He is the first in dignity and repute of all the white eunuchs, and is always near the Grand Signior's person. It is he who introduces ambassadors to audience; and all great affairs pass through his hands before they come to that of the prince.
