LUXEMBURG

article · 168 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUXEMBURG, the capital of the duchy of the same name, situated an hundred miles south-east of Brussels, is a small but strong fortress: E. long. 6° 8', N. lat. 49° 45'.
