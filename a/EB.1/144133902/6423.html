INSTITUTION

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INSTITUTION, in general, signifies the establishing or founding something.

In the canon and common law, it signifies the investing a clerk with the spiritualities of a rectory, &c. which is done by the bishop, who uses the formula, "I institute you rector of such a church, with cure of souls, and receive your care and mine."
