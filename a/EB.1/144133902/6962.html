LIEUTENANT

LIEUTENANT, an officer who supplies the place and discharges the office of a superior in his absence. Of these, some are civil, as the lords-lieutenants of kingdoms, and the lord-lieutenants of counties; and others are military, as the lieutenant general, lieutenant-general of the artillery, lieutenant colonel, lieutenant of the artillery of the tower, lieutenants of horse, foot, ships of war, &c.
