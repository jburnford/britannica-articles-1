CAPUA

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPUA, a city of the province of Lavoro, in the kingdom of Naples, situated on the river Volturno, about fifteen miles north-west of the city of Naples: E. long. 15°, and N. lat. 41° 20'.

It is the see of an archbishop.
