GANGLION

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GANGLIO, or GANGLION, in surgery, a hard tubercle, generally moveable, in the external or internal part of the carpus, upon the tendons or ligaments in that part, usually without any pain to the patient.
