INGRIA

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INGRIA, a province of Russia, bounded by the lake Ladoga, the river Niewa, and the gulph of Finland on the north, by Novogorod on the east and south, and by Livonia on the west.
