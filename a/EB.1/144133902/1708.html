COLOSSUS, a statue of a gigantic, or enormous size.

The most famous of this kind was the colossus of Rhodes, made, in honour of Apollo, by Chares the disciple of Lysippus. It was eighty six feet high, and its thumb so large, that few people could fathom it. This statue was placed across the mouth of the harbour at Rhodes, and the ships with full sails passed betwixt its legs.
