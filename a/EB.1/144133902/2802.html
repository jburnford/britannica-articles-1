DEAL

DEAL, in geography, a port-town of the county of Kent, between which and the Goodwin-fands, the shipping usually rides in the Downs, in going out or coming home: it is about sixty-seven miles eastward of London: E. long. 1° 30', and N. lat. 51° 16'.
