CHIEFTAIN

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHIEFTAIN, denotes the captain, or chief, of any class, family, or body of men: thus, the chieftains, or chiefs, of the highland clans, were the principal noblemen or gentlemen of their respective clans.
