GOTHLAND

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOTHLAND, the most southern province of Sweden, being a peninsula surrounded on three sides by the Baltic Sea. It is subdivided into East and West Gothland, Smaland, Halland, Bleken, and Schonen.
