GILOLO

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GILOLO, a large island of the pacific ocean, lying between 1° S. lat. and 2° N. lat. and between 125° and 128° E. long.

GILOLO is also the name of the capital of the above island, situated in 40° N. lat.
