HALO

article · 364 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALO, a meteor in the form of a luminous ring or circle, of various colours, appearing round the bodies of the sun, moon, or stars.

Concerning the production of halos, Sir Isaac Newton intimates, that they are formed by the light which

which comes through the drops of rain, by two refractions, without any reflection; but how this may be, is not easy to conceive.
