INDIVISIBLE

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INDIVISIBLE, among metaphysicians. A thing is said to be absolutely indivisible, that is a simple being, and consists of no parts into which it may be divided. Thus God is indivisible in all respects, as is also the human mind, not having extension or other properties of body.
