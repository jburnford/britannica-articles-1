HABAKKUK, or the prophecy of Habakkuk, a canonical book of the Old Testament.

There is no mention made in scripture, either of the time when this prophet lived, or of the parents from whom he was descended; but according to the authors of the lives of the prophets, he was of the tribe of Simeon, and a native of Bethzacar.
