DALECHAMPIA

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DALECHAMPIA, in botany, a genus of the monœcia monodelphia class. It has no corolla either in the male or female; and the seeds are roundish and solitary. There is but one species, viz. the scandens, a native of America.
