LUDWIGIA

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUDWIGIA, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of four petals, and the calix of four segments; the capsule has four sides and four cells, with many seeds. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
