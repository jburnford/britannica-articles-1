DUTY

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUTY, in the military art, is the exercise of those functions that belong to a soldier: with this distinction, that mounting guards and the like, where there is no enemy directly to be engaged, is called duty; but their marching to meet and fight an enemy is called going on service.
