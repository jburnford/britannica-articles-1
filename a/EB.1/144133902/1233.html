CHRYSOCOMA

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHRYSOCOMA, in botany, a genus of plants belonging to the syngenesia polygamia æqualis class. The receptacle is naked; the pappus is simple; the calix is imbricated and hemispherical; and the stylus is hardly longer than the floscules. There are nine species, none of which are natives of Britain.
