ELUL

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELUL, in ancient chronology, the twelfth month of the Jewish civil year, and the sixth of the ecclesiastical: it consisted of only twenty-nine days, and answered pretty nearly to our August.
