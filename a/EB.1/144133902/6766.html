LARCENY

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LARCENY, in law, a felonious carrying away another person's goods; and this, according to the value of the thing stolen, is either grand, or petit larceny; the first being stealing effects above the value of 1 s. and the last such as are either of that value, or under it.
