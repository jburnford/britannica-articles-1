DIASTOLE

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIASTOLE, in grammar, a figure of prosody, whereby a syllable naturally short is made long: such is the first syllable of Priamides, in the following verse of Virgil.

Atque hic Priamides! nihil tibi, amice, relitum.
