DAVIT

article · 318 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DAVIT, in a ship, that short piece of timber with a notch at one end, wherein, by a strap, hangs the fish-block.

The use of this block is to help up the fluke of the
VOL. II. No. 43.

anchor, and to fasten it at the ship's bow or loof. The davit is shiftable from one side of the ship to the other, as there is occasion.
