COCK-PIT

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COCK-PIT, in a man of war, a place on the lower floor, or deck, abast the main-captain, lying between the platform and the steward's room, where are partitions for the purser, surgeon, and his mates.
