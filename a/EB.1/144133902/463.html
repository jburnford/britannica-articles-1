CARACT

CARACT is also a certain weight which goldsmiths and jewelers use wherewith to weigh precious stones and pearls.

This carat weighs four grains, but something lighter than the grains of other weights. Each of these grains is subdivided into \frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{4}, \frac{1}{8}, \frac{1}{16}, &c.
