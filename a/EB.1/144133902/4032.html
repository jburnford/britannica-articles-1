EQUES AURATUS

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EQUES AURATUS, is used for a knight bachelor, called auratus, q. d. gilt, because anciently none but knights were allowed to beautify their armour, or other habiliments for war, with gold.
