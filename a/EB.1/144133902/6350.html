INDORSEMENT

Cross-references: BILL

INDORSEMENT, in law, any thing written on the back of a deed, as a receipt for money received.

There is likewise an indorsement, by way of assignment, on bills of exchange and notes of hand; which is done by writing a person's name on the back thereof. See BILL.
