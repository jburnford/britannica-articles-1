LENS

article · 306 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: OPTICS

LENS, in dioptrics, properly signifies a small roundish glass, of the figure of a lentil; but is extended to any optic glass, not very thick, which either collects the rays of light into a point, in their passage through it,

or disperses them further apart, according to the laws of refraction. See OPTICS.
