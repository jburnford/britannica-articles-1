HAYNAULT, a province of the Netherlands, bounded by Brabant and Elanders, on the north; by Namur and

and Liege, on the east; by the Cambresis, Picardy, and Champagne, on the south; and by Artois, and another part of Flanders, on the west: the north part is subject to the house of Austria, and the south part to France. Its capital is Mons.
