CALIBER-compass

sub_entry · 396 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALIBER-compass, the name of an instrument, made either of wood, iron, steel, or brass: that used for measuring bullets consists of two branches, bending inwards, with a tongue fixed to one of them, and the other graduated in such a manner, that if the bullet be compressed by the ends of the two branches, and the tongue be applied to the graduated branch, it will show the weight of the bullet.
