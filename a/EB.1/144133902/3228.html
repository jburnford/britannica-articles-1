DIMINUTIVE

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIMINUTIVE, in grammar, a word formed from some other, to soften or diminish the force of it, or to signify a thing is little in its kind. Thus, cellule is a diminutive of cell, globule of globe, hilllock of hill.
