GUI TAR, GUITARRA

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUI TAR, GUITARRA, a musical instrument of the string-kind, with five double rows of strings, of which those that are bass are in the middle, unless it be for the burden, an octave lower than the fourth.

This instrument was first used in Spain, and by the Italians.
