DEBITA fructuum

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

DEBITA fructuum, in Scots law. Funds are debita fructuum not fundi; so are not recoverable out of the lands themselves, but out of the fruits of the lands out of which they are payable. See SCOTS LAW, title 17.
