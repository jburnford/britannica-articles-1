LIVONIA

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIVONIA, a province of Russia, 160 miles long, and 120 broad; bounded by the gulph of Finland, on the north; by Ingria and great Novogorod, on the east; by Lithuania and Courland, on the south; and by the Baltic, on the west; its chief towns are Narva, Revel, and Riga.
