DENTALIUM

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DENTALIUM, in natural history, a shell-fish belonging to the order of vermes testacea. The shell consists of one tubulous strait valve, open at both ends. There are eight species, distinguished by the angles, striæ, &c. of their shells.
