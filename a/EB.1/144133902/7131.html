LUCAR, or St. LUCAR, a port-town of Spain, in the province of Andalusia: W. long. 6^{\circ} 38', N. lat. 36^{\circ} 42'. St. LUCAR is also a town of Andalusia, in Spain: W. long. 8^{\circ} 12', N. lat. 37^{\circ} 20'.

St. LUCAR is also the name of another town of Spain, fifteen miles west of Seville.
