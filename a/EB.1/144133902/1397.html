CIVITA VECCHIA

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIVITA VECCHIA, a port-town and fortress of Italy, in St Peter's patrimony, situated on a bay of the Mediterranean, thirty miles north-west of Rome: E. long. 12^{\circ} 30', N. lat. 42^{\circ}.

It is the station of the galleys belonging to the pope, who has lately declared it a free port.
