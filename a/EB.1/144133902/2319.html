COUNTER-TIME

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-TIME, in the menage, is the defence or resistance of a horse that interrupts his cadence, and the measure of his menage, occasioned either by a bad horseman, or by the malice of the horse.
