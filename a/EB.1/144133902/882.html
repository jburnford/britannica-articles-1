CESSIO honorum

Cross-references: SCOTS LAW

CESSIO honorum, in Scots law. The name of that action by which an insolvent debtor may apply for liberation from prison, upon making over his whole real and personal estate to his creditors. See SCOTS LAW, title, Sentences and their execution.
