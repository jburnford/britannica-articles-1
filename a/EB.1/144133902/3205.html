DIGEST

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIGEST, in matters of literature, a collection of the decisions of the Roman lawyers properly digested, or arranged under distinct heads, by order of the emperor Justinian. It constitutes the first part or volume of the civil law.
