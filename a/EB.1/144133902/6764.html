LAQUEUS

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAQUEUS, in surgery, a kind of ligature, so contrived, that when stretched by any weight, or the like, it draws up close. Its use is to extend broken or disjoined bones, to keep them in their places when they are set, and to bind the parts close together.
