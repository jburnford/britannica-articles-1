ETHIOPIA

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ETHIOPIA, or ÆTHIOPIA, a very extensive country of Africa, comprehending Abyssinia, Nubia, and Abex: it is bounded by Egypt, and the desert of Barca, on the north; by the Red sea and Indian ocean, on the east; by Anian, and the unknown parts of Africa, on the south; and by other unknown countries on the west.
