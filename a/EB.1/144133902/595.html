CARTON, or CARTOON

sub_entry · 333 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARTON, or CARTOON, in painting, a design drawn on strong paper, to be afterwards calked through, and transferred on the fresh plaster of a wall to be painted in fresco.

Cartoon is also used for a design coloured, for working in mosaic, tapestry, &c. The cartoons at Hampton-court are designs of Raphael Urbin, intended for tapestry.
