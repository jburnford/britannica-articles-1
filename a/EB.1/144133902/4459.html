FERDEN

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FERDEN, or VERDEN, a city of Germany, subject to Hanover; it is situated in lower Saxony, on the river Aller, twenty six miles south east of Bremen: E. long. 9^{\circ}, and N. lat. 53^{\circ} 24'.
