CAMBRAY

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMBRAY, a city in the French Netherlands, situated on the river Schelde, near its source: E. long. 3^{\circ} 15', and N. lat. 50^{\circ} 15'.

It is a large and well-built city, considerable for its linen manufacture, especially cambricks, which took their name from hence.
