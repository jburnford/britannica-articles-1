TRAGOPOGON

article · 323 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRAGOPOGON, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygamia aequalis class. The receptacle is naked; the calix is simple, and consists of many leaves; and the papus is feathered. There are eleven species, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the pratense, or yellow goat's-beard; and the possifolium, or purple goat's-beard.
