SECRETION, the separation of some fluid from the blood by means of the glands.

In the bodies of animals we observe a great number of juices of different natures, viz. the blood, lymph, saliva, stomach liquor, intestinal juices, pancreatic juice, bile, urine, &c. and the blood is the general source of all. See BLOOD, LYMPH, SALIVA, &c.
