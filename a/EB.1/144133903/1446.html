OSTREA

OSTREA, the OYSTER, in zoology, a genus belonging to the order of vermes testacea. The shell has two unequal valves; the cardo has no teeth, but a small hollow pit, with transverse lateral streaks. There are 31 species, principally distinguished by peculiarities in their shells. The common oyster is used both raw, and variously prepared as food.
