RANUNCULUS

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RANUNCULUS, in botany, a genus of the polyandria polygynia class. The calix consists of five leaves, and the corolla of five petals, with a melliferous pore in the claw of each; and the seeds are naked. There are 38 species, 11 of them natives of Britain.
