TERRELLA

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TERRELLA, an appellation given to a loadstone, when turned into a spherical figure, and is placed so, that its poles and equator, &c. correspond to the poles and equator of the world; as being a just representation of the great magnetical globe which we inhabit.
