VERSAILLES

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERSAILLES, a town of France, in the province of the isle of France, situated eleven miles west of Paris, where stands one of the most elegant and magnificent palæes in the world, built by Lewis XIV.
