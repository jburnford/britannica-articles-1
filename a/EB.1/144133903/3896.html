SHAKLES

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHAKLES, in a ship, are the rings with which the ports are shut fast, by lashing the port-bar to them. There also shakles put upon bilbow-bolts, for confining the men who have deserved corporal punishment.
