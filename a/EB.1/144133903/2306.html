PONTERERIA

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PONTERERIA, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of one bilabiated petal cut into six segments; and the capsule has three cells. There are three species, none of them natives of Britain.
