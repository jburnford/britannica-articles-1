MONTE SANTO, or MOUNT-ATHOS, a mountain of European Turkey, in the province of Macedon: E. long. 23°, and N. lat. 40° 12'.

It is called Monte Santo, or Holy Mountain, from twenty two monasteries situated upon it, in which are four thousand monks or friars, who never suffer a woman to come within sight of their convent.
