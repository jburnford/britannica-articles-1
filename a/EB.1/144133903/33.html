MAGDELENA

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAGDELENA, a large river of South America, which, rising near the equator, runs north through Terra Firma, and, uniting its waters with the river Cance, obtains the name of the river Grande, and falls into the north sea, below the town of Madre de Popa.
