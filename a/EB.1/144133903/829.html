NAZARENES

article · 357 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NAZARENES, in church history, a name originally given to all Christians in general, on account that Jesus Christ was of the city of Nazareth; but afterwards restrained to a sect of heretics, whose religion consisted of a strange jumble of Judaism and Christianity, observing at the same time the Mosaical law and the several rites of the Christian religion.
