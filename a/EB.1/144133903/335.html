MEMECYLON

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEMECYLON, in botany, a genus of the octandria monogynia class. The calix is above the fruit, and has an entire margin; the corolla consists of one petal; and the berry is crowned with a cylindrical calix. There is but one species, a native of Ceylon.
