RHINE upper circle

sub_entry · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHINE upper circle consisted of the Landgraves of Alsatia and Hesse, comprehending the Wetteraw: but only Hesse can be accounted a part of Germany at present, France having united Alsace to that kingdom.
