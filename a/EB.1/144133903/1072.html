NOWED

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOWED, in heraldry, signifies knotted, from the Latin nodatus; being applied to the tails of such creatures as are very long, and sometimes represented in coat-armour, as if tied up in a knot.
