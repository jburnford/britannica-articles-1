NEBULY

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEBULY, or NEBULEE, in heraldry, is when a coat is charged with several little figures, in form of words, running within one another, or when the outline of a bordure, ordinary, &c. is indented or waved, as represented in Plate CXXXIV. fig. 3.
