TRIBULUS

TRIBULUS, in botany, a genus of the decandria monogynia class. The calix consists of five segments, and the corolla of five open petals; it has no stylos; and there are five gibbous, bristly capsules, containing many seeds. There are four species, none of them natives of Britain.
