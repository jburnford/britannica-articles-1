MANICORDON

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MANICORDON, or MANICHORD, a musical instrument in the form of a spinet; the strings of which, like those of the clarichord, are covered with little pieces

of cloth, to deaden as well as to soften their sound; whence it is also called the dumb spinet.
