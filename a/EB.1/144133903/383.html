MESSASIPPI

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MESSASIPPI, or MESCHASIPPI, a country of North America, bounded by Canada on the north, the British plantations on the east, the gulph of Mexico on the south, and the province of New Mexico on the west.
