NAVARRE

article · 378 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NAVARRE, a province of Spain, bounded by French Navarre on the north-east, by Arragon on the south-east, by Old Castile on the south-west, and by Guipuscoa on the west: it is a mountainous country.

French Navarre, separated from Spanish Navarre on the south-west by the Pyrenees: it is only thirty miles long, and fifteen broad; being one of the most barren provinces in France.
