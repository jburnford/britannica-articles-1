NUBIA

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NUBIA, a country of Africa, bounded by the desert of Barca, on the north; by Egypt and Abyssinia, on the east; by the Lower Ethiopia, on the south; and by the deserts of Africa, on the west.
