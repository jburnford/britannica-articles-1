REGISTERS

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REGISTERS, in chemistry, are holes, or chinks with stop-plates, contrived in the sides of furnaces, to regulate the fire; that is, to make the heat more intense, or remiss, by opening them to let in the air, or keeping them close to exclude it.
