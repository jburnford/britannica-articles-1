TURBO

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TURBO, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of vermes testacea. This is an animal of the snail kind: the shell consists of one spiral solid valve; and the aperture is orbicular. There are 49 species, distinguished by peculiarities in their shells.
