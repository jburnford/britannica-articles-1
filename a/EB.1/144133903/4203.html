SOUTHWARK

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOUTHWARK, a borough of Surrey, and a suburb to London with which it has a communication by a magnificent bridge. It is situated on the south side of the Thames, and sends two members to parliament.
