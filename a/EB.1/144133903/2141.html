PIPE-OFFICE

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PIPE-OFFICE, is an office, wherein a person, called the clerk of the pipe, makes out leases of crown-lands, by warrant from the lord-treasurer, or commissioners of the treasury, or chancellor of the exchequer.
