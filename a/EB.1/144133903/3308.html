ROWEL

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FARRISKY

ROWEL, among farriers, a kind of issue, made by drawing a skain of silk, thread, hair, or the like, through the nape of the neck, or other part of a horse; answering to what in surgery is called a feron. See FARRISKY.
