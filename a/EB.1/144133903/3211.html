RING BONE

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FARRIERY

RING BONE, in farriery, a hard callous substance, growing in the pattern of a horse, above the coronet: it is thus called from its growing quite round like a ring. See FARRIERY.
