NORWICH

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NORWICH, a large city of great trade in Norfolk, situated 20 miles west of Yarmouth and the German ocean: E. long. 1^{\circ} 26', and N. lat. 52^{\circ} 40'. It sends two members to parliament.
