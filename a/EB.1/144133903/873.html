NEPHRITICS, in pharmacy, medicines proper for diseases of the kidneys, especially the stone.

Such particularly are the roots of althæa, dog's grass, asparagus, fago, pellitory of the wall, mallows, pimpinella, red chick pease, peach kernels, turpentine, the nephritic stone, the nephritic wood, &c. and diuretics.
