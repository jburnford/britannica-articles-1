PAREMBOLE

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAREMBOLE, in rhetoric, a figure wherein something relating to the subject is inserted in the middle of a period. All the differences between the parembole and parenthesis, according to Vollius, is, that the former relates to the subject in hand, whereas the latter is foreign to it.
