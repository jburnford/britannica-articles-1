STOEBE

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STOEBE, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygamia segregata class. The receptacle is paleaceous; the papus is feathered; and every floscule has a five-leaved calix. There is but one species, a native of Ethiopia.
