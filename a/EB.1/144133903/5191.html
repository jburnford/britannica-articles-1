TRADITION

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRADITION, among ecclesiastical writers, denotes certain regulations regarding the rites, ceremonies, &c. of religion, which we suppose to have been handed down from the days of the apostles to the present time.
