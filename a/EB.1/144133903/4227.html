SPARUS, in ichthyology, a genus belonging to the order of thoracici. The fore teeth and dog-teeth are very strong; the grinders are obtuse, and thick set; the lips are folded over; there are five rays in the gill-membrane, and the opercula are scaly; the body is compressed; the lateral line is crooked behind; and the pectoral fins are roundish. There are 26 species.
