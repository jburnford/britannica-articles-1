THEURGY

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THEURGY, a name given to that part of magic called white magic, or the white art. Those who have written of magic have divided it into three kinds: the first is theurgy, as operating by divine means: the second, natural magic, performed by the powers of nature: and the third, necromancy, which they imagined proceeded from invoking demons.
