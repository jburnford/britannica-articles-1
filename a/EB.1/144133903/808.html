NATOLIA

article · 395 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NATOLIA, the modern name of the Lesser Asia, being the most westerly part of Turkey in Asia, and consisting of a large peninsula, which extends from the river Euphrates, as far as the Archipelago, the seas of Marmora, the straits of Galipoli and of Constantinople, which separate it from Europe on the west. It is bounded on the north by the Black Sea, and on the south by the Mediterranean sea.
