PHLOMIS

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHLOMIS, in botany, a genus of the didynamia gymnospermia class. The calix is angular; and the superior lip of the corolla is compressed, incumbent, and hairy. There are twelve species, none of them natives of Britain.
