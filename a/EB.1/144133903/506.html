MNIUM

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MNIUM, in botany, a genus of the cryptogamia musci class. The anthera is operculated; and the capitulum of the female is naked, and dusty. There are 18 species, 7 of which are natives of Britain.
