PORTUGAL

article · 407 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORTUGAL, the most westerly kingdom in Europe: it is about three hundred miles long, and one hundred broad; and is situated between 7° and 10° of W. long. and between 37° and 42° of N. lat. being bounded by Spain on the north and east, and by the Atlantic ocean on the south and west. This country is neither so hot nor so fruitful as Spain; it however produces plenty of grapes, olives, oranges and lemons.
