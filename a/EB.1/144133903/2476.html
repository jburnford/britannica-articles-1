PRINCE

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRINCE, in polity, a person invested with the supreme command of a state, independent of any other.

Prince also denotes a person who is a sovereign in his own territories, yet holds of some other as his superior; such are the princes of Germany.
