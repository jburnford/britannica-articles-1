MUNTINGIA

article · 446 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MUNTINGIA, in botany, a genus of the polyandria monogynia class. The calix consists of five segments, and the corolla of five petals; and the berry has one cell containing many seeds. There is but one species, a native of America.

distinguished by their fins, tail, &c.
