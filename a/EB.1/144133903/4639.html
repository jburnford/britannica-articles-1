SUEZ

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUEZ, a port-town of Egypt, situated at the bottom of the Red-sea, seventy miles east of Cairo: it is from this town that the isthmus of Suez, which joins Africa to Asia, takes its name.
