OROBUS

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OROBUS, in botany, a genus of the diadelphia decandria class. The stylus is linear; the calix is blunt at the base, the superior segments of it being shorter. There are nine species, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the tuberosus, or wood-pease; and the sylvaticus, or bitter vetch.
