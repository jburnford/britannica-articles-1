TANGIER

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TANGIER, a port-town of Africa, in the empire of Morocco and kingdom of Fez, situated at the entrance of the straits of Gibraltar, in W. long. 7°, N. lat. 35° 40'. It was the capital of the ancient Mauritania Tingitana, and was once in the possession of the English.
