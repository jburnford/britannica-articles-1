SPOILS

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPOILS, whatever is taken from the enemy in time of war. Among the ancient Greeks, the spoils were divided in common among the whole army; only the general's share was largest: but among the Romans, the spoils belonged to the republic.
