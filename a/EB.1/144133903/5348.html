TUBIPORA

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TUBIPORA, a genus of submarine plants, belonging to the cryptogamia class, of the hardness of coral, and consisting of cylindrical tubes rising from a thin crust of the same sort of matter with themselves.
