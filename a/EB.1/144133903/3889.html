SHADOW

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHADOW, in optics, a privation or diminution of light, by the interposition of any opaque body; or it is a plane where the light is either altogether obstructed, or great-

ly weakened, by the interposition of some opaque body between it and the luminary.
