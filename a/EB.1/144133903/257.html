MAXIMUM

article · 382 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAXIMUM, in mathematics, denotes the greatest quantity attainable in any given case.

If a quantity conceived to be generated by motion, increases, or decreases, till it arrives at a certain magnitude or position, and then on the contrary grows lesser or greater, and it be required to determine the said magnitude or position, the question is called a problem de maximis et minimis.
