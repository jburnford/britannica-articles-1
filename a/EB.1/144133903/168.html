MARQUE

article · 387 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARQUE, or letters of MARQUE, in military affairs, are letters of reprisal, granting the subjects of one prince or state liberty to make reprisals on those of another.

Letters of marque among us, are extraordinary commissions granted by authority for reparation to merchants, taken and disposed by strangers at sea; and reprisals is only the retaking, or taking of one thing for another.
