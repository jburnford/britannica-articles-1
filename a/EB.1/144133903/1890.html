PENULTIMA

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PENULTIMA, or PENULTIMATE SYLLABLE, in grammar, denotes the last syllable but one of a word, and hence the anti-penultimate syllable is the last but two, or that immediately before the penultima.
