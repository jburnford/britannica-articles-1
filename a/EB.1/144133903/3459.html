SALTIER

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SALTIER, in heraldry, an ordinary in form of a St. Andrew's cross; which may be said to be composed of a bend dexter and sinister, crossing each other in the centre of the escutcheon. See Plate CXLVII fig. 15.
