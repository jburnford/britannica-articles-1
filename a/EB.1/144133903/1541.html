PALAMBOANG

PALAMBOANG, or PALAMBANG, the capital of a kingdom at the east end of the island of Java, in the East-Indies, situated on the straits of Bally, in E. long. 114°, S. lat. 7° 30', and separated from the island of Bally by a narrow strait.
