SIDON

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SIDON, or SAID, a port-town of Palestine, in Asiatic Turkey, seventy miles north of Jerusalem. It is still a place of some consideration, being the residence of a Turkish bashaw.
