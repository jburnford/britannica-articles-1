NOTHUS

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOTHUS, signifies spurious or bastard; whence it is fig-uratively applied by physicians, &c. to such diseases as though in respect of a similitude of symptoms, &c. they have the same denomination as some others, yet are of a different origin, feat, or the like, from the same.
