PANARO

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANARO, a river of Italy, which rising in the Apennine mountains, on the confines of Tuscany, divides Modena from Romagna, and then running through the Ferrarese falls into the Gulf of Venice.
