PTERIS

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PTERIS, in botany, a genus of the cryptogamia filicum class. The fructification is situate in lines near the margin. There are 19 species, only one of them, viz. the aquilina, or female fern, is a native of Britain.
