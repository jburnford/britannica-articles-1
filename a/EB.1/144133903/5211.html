TRANSFORMATION

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRANSFORMATION, in general, denotes a change of form, or the assuming a new form different from a former one. The chemists have been for a long time seeking the transformation of metals; that is, their transmutation, or the manner of changing them into gold.
