TITANS

article · 395 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TITANS, in the heathen mythology, the offspring of Titan, the elder brother of Saturn; upon whom, and his son Jupiter, they made war, in order to recover the sovereignty of which Titan had been deprived. The poets represent them as a race of giants, sprung from the earth, and invading heaven; and tell us, that Jupiter overcame them with thunder, and drove them down to the very bottom of hell.
