MOMORDICA

article · 323 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOMORDICA, in botany, a genus of the monocotyledon class. The calix of the male and female consists of five segments; the corolla of the male has six segments; and the filaments are three. The corolla of the female consists of five segments; and it has three styli. There are eight species, none of them natives of Britain.
