VOICE

article · 353 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOICE, in matters of election, denotes a vote or suffrage. VOIDED, in heraldry, is understood of an ordinary whose inner or middle part is cut out, leaving nothing but its edges to shew its form, so that the field appears through it. Hence it is needless to express the colour or metal of the voided part, because it must of course be that of the field.
