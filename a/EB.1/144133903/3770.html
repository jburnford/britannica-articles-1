SEMI-COLON

Cross-references: PUNCTUATION

SEMI-COLON, in grammar, one of the points or stops used to distinguish the several members of sentences from each other. See PUNCTUATION.

The mark or character of the semi-colon is (;) and has its name as being somewhat of less effect than a colon, or as demanding a shorter pause.
