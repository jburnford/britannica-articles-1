PEDILUVIUM

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PEDILUVIUM, a bathing of the feet. This bath may be prepared of the same ingredients with other baths. It may either consist of light pure water alone; or, to correct the qualities of heavy and hard water, a lixivium or bran of wheat or chamomile-flowers may be added.
