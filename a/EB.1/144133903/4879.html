TEINTS

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TEINTS and SEMI-TEINTS, in painting, denotes the several colours used in a picture, considered as more or less high, bright, deep, thin, or weakened, and diminished, &c. to give the proper relief, softness, or distance, &c. of the several objects.
