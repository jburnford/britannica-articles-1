VILLENAGE

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VILLENAGE, a kind of ancient tenure, whereby the tenant was bound to do such services as the lord commanded, or such as were fit for villains or bondmen to perform.

VILLI, among botanists, a kind of down, like coarse hair, with which some trees abound.
