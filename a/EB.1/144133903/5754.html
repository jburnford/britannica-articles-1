UPSAL

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

UPSAL, a city once the capital of the province of Upland, and of all Sweden, being the only archbishop's see in Scandinavia, and an university; situated in E. long. 17° 20', N. lat. 60°.
