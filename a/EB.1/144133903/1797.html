PAULINIA

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAULINIA, in botany, a genus of the octandria trigynia class. The calix consists of four leaves, the corolla of four petals, and the nectarium of four unequal leaves; it has three compressed, membranaceous capsules. There are seven species, none of them natives of Britain.
