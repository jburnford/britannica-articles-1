MICHELIA

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MICHELIA, in botany, a genus of the polyandria polygynia class. The calix consists of eight segments, and the corolla of eight petals; and the berries contain many seeds. There is but one species, a native of India.
