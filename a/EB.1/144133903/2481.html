PRINOS

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRINOS, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The calix consists of six segments, and the corolla of one rotated petal; and the berry contains six seeds. There are two species, both natives of America.
