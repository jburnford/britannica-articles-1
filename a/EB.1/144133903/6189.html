ZEUS

ZEUS, in ichthyology, a genus belonging to the order of thoracici. The head is compressed, and declines, the upper lip being vaulted over by a transverse membrane; the tongue is subulated; there are seven rays in the gill-membrane; and the body is compressed. The species are four, distinguished by the shape of their tails.
