SCOLOPENDRA

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCOLOPENDRA, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of aptera. The feet are very numerous, being as many on each side as there are joints in the body; the antennæ are setaceous; there are two jointed pappi; and the body is depressed. There are eleven species.
