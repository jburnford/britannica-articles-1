PHILOLOGY

PHILOLOGY, a science, or rather assemblage of several sciences, consisting of grammar, rhetoric, poetry, antiquities, history, and criticism.

Phylogeny is a kind of universal literature, conversant about all the sciences, their rise, progress, authors, &c. It makes what the French call the belles lettres.
