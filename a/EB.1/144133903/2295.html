POLYPREMUM

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLYPREMUM, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The calix consists of four leaves, and the corolla, which is rotated, of four segments; the capsule is compressed, and bilocular.
