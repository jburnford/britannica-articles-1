PARAPHRASE

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARAPHRASE, an explanation of some text, in clearer and more ample terms, whereby is supplied what the author might have said or thought on the subject; such are esteemed Erasmus's paraphrase on the New Testament, the Chaldee paraphrase on the Pentateuch, &c.
