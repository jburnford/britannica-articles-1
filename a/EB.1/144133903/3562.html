SAUCISSON

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAUCISSON, in fortification, a mass of large branches of trees bound together; and differing only from a fascine, as this is composed of small branches of twigs.

Saucissons are employed to cover the men, and to make epaullements.
