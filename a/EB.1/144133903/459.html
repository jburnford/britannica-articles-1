MINERVALIA

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MINERALIA, in Roman antiquity, festivals celebrated in honour of Minerva, in the month of March; at which time the scholars had a vacation, and usually made a present to their masters, called, from this festival, minerval.
