TROPAEOLUM

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TROPAEOLUM, in botany, a genus of the ostandria monogynia class. The calix consists of one calcarated leaf; and the corolla of five unequal petals; and there are three dry berries. The species are three, all natives of Peru.
