STREWS

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STREWS, were also places anciently permitted in England to women of professed incontinency, for the proffer of their bodies to all comers. These were under particular rules and laws of discipline, appointed by the lord of the manor.
