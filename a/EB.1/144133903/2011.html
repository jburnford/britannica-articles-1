PHALLUS

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHALLUS, in botany, a genus of the cryptogamia fungi class. It is woven like a net above, and smooth below. There are two species, both natives of Britain, viz. the esculentus, or esculent morel; and the impudicus, or flinking morel.
