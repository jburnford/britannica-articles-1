SOMERSETSHIRE

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOMERSETSHIRE, a county of England, situated on the Bristol channel, and bounded by Wiltshire on the east, by Dorsetshire on the south, and by Devonshire on the west: it is famous for the cloth manufacture.
