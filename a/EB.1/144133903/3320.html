RUBIGALIA

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RUBIGALIA, in antiquity, a feast celebrated by the Romans, in honour of the god Rubigus, or the goddess Rubigo, to engage these deities to preserve the corn from blights and mildews.
