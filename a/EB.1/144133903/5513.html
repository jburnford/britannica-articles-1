VERBENA

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERBENA, in botany, a genus of the diandria monogynia class. The corolla is funnel-shaped, and crooked; one tooth of the calix is truncated; and it has two or four.

four naked seeds. There are 16 species, only one of them, viz. the officinalis, or vervain, a native of Britain.
