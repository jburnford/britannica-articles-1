SAY

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAY, or SAVE, in commerce, a kind of serge or woollen stuff, much used abroad for linings, and by the religious for shirts: with us it is used for aprons by several sorts of artificers, being usually dyed green.
