REFECTION

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REFECTION, among ecclesiastics, a spare meal or repast, just sufficing for the support of life: hence the hall in convents, and other communities, where the monks, nuns, &c. take their refectioins or meals in common, is called the refectory.
