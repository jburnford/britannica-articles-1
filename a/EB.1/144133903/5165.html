TORREFACTION

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TORREFACTION, in chemistry, is the roasting or scorching of a body by the fire, in order to discharge a part either unnecessary or hurtful in another operation; as sulphur is thus discharged from an ore, before the metal can be obtained to advantage.
