PETECHIE

article · 164 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MEDICINE

PETECHIE, in medicine, denotes spots in the skin like flea-bites, which come out in malignant fevers, hence called petechial or spotted fevers. See MEDICINE, p. 67.
