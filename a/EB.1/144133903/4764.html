SYRINGE, a well known instrument, serving to imbibe or suck in a quantity of fluid, and to squirt or expel the same with violence.

A syringe is only a single pump, and the water ascends

in it on the same principle as in the common sucking pump. See HYDROSTATICS, p. 808.
