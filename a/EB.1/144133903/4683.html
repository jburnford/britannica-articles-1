SUPPOSITORY, a kind of medicated cone, or ball, which is introduced to the anus for opening the belly. Suppositories

itories are usually made of soap, sugar, alum, or a piece of tallow-candle, about the length of a man's thumb and the breadth of a finger, though they may be made smaller for children, and sometimes a little thicker for adults.
