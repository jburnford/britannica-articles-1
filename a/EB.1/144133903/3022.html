REMONSTRANCE

article · 367 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REMONSTRANCE, an expostulation or humble supplication, addressed to a king, or other superior, beseeching him to reflect on the inconveniences, or ill consequences of some order, or the like. This word is also used for an expostulatory counsel, or advice; or a gentle and handsome reproach, made either in general, or particular, to apprise or correct some fault, &c.
