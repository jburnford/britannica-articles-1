Liquid STORAX

sub_entry · 543 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Liquid STORAX, in pharmacy, is a drug very different from the resin above described; being a resinous juice, of the consistence of Venice-turpentine, or thicker: it is, when clean, pellucid, of a brownish colour, with a cast sometimes of reddish, and sometimes of greyish in it. Its smell is somewhat like that of common storax, only much stronger, and even disagreeable: its taste is acrid, aromatic, and somewhat bitterish; and it is oily, or unctuous. It should be chosen thin, pellucid, of a clean brown colour, and of a very strong smell.
