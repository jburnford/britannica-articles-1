SMYRNA, a city and port-town of Asiatic Turkey, situated on a bay of the Archipelago, in the province of Ionia, in Lesser Asia, 100 miles north of Rhodes, and 200 miles nearly south of Constantinople: E. long. 27°, N. lat. 37° 30'.
