MARSHAL

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARSHAL, in its primary signification, means an officer who has the command or care of horses; but it is now applied to officers who have very different employments, as earl marshal, knight marshal, or marshal of the king's house, &c.
