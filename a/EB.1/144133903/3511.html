SANTOLINA

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SANTOLINA, a genus of the synangenia polygamia æqualis class. The receptacle is paleaceous; the pappus is very short; and the calix is hemispherical and imbricated. The species are four, none of them natives of Britain.
