STACHELINA

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STACHELINA, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygamia æqualis class. The receptacle is naked; the pappus is feathered; and the calix is imbricated with scales, membranaceous, coloured, and reflected at the points. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
