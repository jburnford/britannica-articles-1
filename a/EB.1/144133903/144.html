MARGARETTA

MARGARETTA, one of the largest of the Leeward islands; it is about fifty miles long, and twenty-four broad, and situated sixty miles north of the continent of Paria, or New Andalusia, in South America: W. long. 64°, and N. lat. 11° 30'.
