WILDERNESS

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WILDERNESS, in gardening, a kind of grove of large trees, in a spacious garden, in which the walks are commonly made either to intersect each other in angles, or have the appearance of meanders and labyrinths.
