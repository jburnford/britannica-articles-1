MONTPELIER

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTPELIER, a city of France, in the province of Languedoc and county of Nismes, situated on the little river Lez, fifty miles north-east of Narbonne, and forty-five miles south-west of Avignon; a place famous for its delightful situation, and its healthy serene air.
