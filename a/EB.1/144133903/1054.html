NOTONECTA, the BOAT-FLY a genus of insects be-longing to the order hemiptera. The beak is inflected; the antennæ are shorter than the thorax; the four wings are plaited cross-ways; and the feet are hairy, and fitted for swimming. There are three species, distinguished by their colour.
