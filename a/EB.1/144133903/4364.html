STAMINA

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STAMINA, in the animal body, are defined to be these simple original parts, which existed first in the embryo, or even in the seed; and by whose distinction, augmentation, and accretion, by additional juices, the animal body, at its utmost bulk, is supposed to be formed.
