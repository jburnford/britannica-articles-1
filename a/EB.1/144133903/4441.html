STERCULIA

article · 315 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STERCULIA, in botany, a genus of the Monoclea monadelphia class. The calix both of male and female consists of five segments; neither of them have any corolla; the male has 15 filaments; the germen rests upon a column; and there are five capsules containing many seeds. There are two species, both natives of India.
