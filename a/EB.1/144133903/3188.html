RIBES

RIBES, in botany, a genus of the pentandria monogynia class. It has five stamens, and five petals, both inserted into the calix; the stylus is bifid, and the berry contains many seeds. There are eight species, three of them

natives of Britain, viz. the rubrum, or currants; the alpinum, or sweet mountain currants; and the nigrum, or black currants.
