ZANTE

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZANTE, an island in the Mediterranean sea, situated E. long. 21^{\circ} 30', N. lat. 37^{\circ} 50', being about twenty four miles long, and twelve broad. The chief town is Zant, and is situated on the east side of the island, being well fortified and defended by a castle.
