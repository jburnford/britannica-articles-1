MOTTO

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOTTO, in armoury, a short sentence or phrase carried in a scroll, generally under, but sometimes over the arms; sometimes alluding to the bearing, sometimes to the name of the bearer, and sometimes containing whatever pleases the fancy of the deviser.
