OVOLO

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OVOLO, or Ovust, in architecture, a round moulding, whose profile, or sweep, in the ionic and composite capitals, is usually a quadrant of a circle: whence it is also commonly called the quarter-round.

It is usually cut with the representation of eggs and anchors or arrows heads placed alternately.
