TEISSE

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TEISSE, or TAYS, a river of Hungary, which rises in the Carpathian mountains; and running from east to west, passes from Tokay; then turning south, passes by Zolnok and Segedin; and having joined the river Merith, falls into the Danube, opposite to Salankasen.
