PLINIA

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLINIA, in botany, a genus of the polyandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of five petals, and the calix of five segments; the berry is furrowed, and contains one seed. There is but one species, a native of America.
