PERISTALTIC

article · 315 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERISTALTIC, a vermicular spontaneous motion of the intestines, performed by the contraction of the circular and longitudinal fibres, of which the fleshy coats of the intestines is composed; by means whereof the chyle is driven into the orifices of the lacteal veins, and the faeces are protracted towards the anus.
