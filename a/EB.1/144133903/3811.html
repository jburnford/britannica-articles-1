SEPTIZON

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEPTIZON, in Roman antiquity, a celebrated mausoleum, built by Septimus Severus, in the tenth region of the city of Rome: it was so called from septem and zona, by reason it consisted of seven stories, each of which was surrounded by a row of columns.
