VEIL

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VEIL, a piece of stuff, sewn to cover or hide any thing. In the Romish churches, in time of Lent, they have veils or curtains over the altar, crucifix, images of saints, &c.

A veil or crape is worn on the head by nuns, as a badge of their profession: the novices wear white veils; but those who have made the vows black ones.
