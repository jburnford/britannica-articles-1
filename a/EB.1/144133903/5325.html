TRONAGE

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRONAGE, an ancient customary toll, paid for weighing of wool. This word is particularly mentioned in a charter granted to the mayor and citizens of London; in which city there is an officer called tronator, whose business it is to weigh the wool that is brought thither.
