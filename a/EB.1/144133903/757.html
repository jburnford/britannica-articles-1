MYRSINE

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MYRSINE, in botany, a genus of the pentandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of five segments; and the berry has five cells and five seeds. There is but one species, a native of Ethiopia.
