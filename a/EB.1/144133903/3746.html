SEGOVIA

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEGOVIA, a city of Manila, the largest of the Philippine islands, situated in E. long. 119^{\circ}, N. lat. 13^{\circ} 30'. This is also a name of a city of Spain, in the province of Old Castile, situated W. long. 4^{\circ} 35', N. lat. 41^{\circ}.
