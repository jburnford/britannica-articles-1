ROPE

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROPE, hemp, hair, &c. spun out into a thick yarn, and then several strings of this yarn twisted together by means of a wheel. When made very small, it is called a cord; and when very thick, a cable.
