NEGROLAND

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEGROLAND, or NIGRITIA, a country of Africa, which lies between 18° west and 15° east longitude, and between 10° and 20° of north latitude, the great river Niger running through it. It is bounded by Zaara, or the desert, on the north, by unknown countries on the east, by Guinea on the south, and by the Atlantic ocean on the west.
