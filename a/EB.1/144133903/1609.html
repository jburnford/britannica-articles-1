PANTHEON

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANTHEON, in Roman antiquity, a temple of a circular form, dedicated to all the gods: It was built by Agrippa, son-in-law to Augustus; but is now converted into a church, and dedicated to the Virgin and all the martyrs.
