NIESTER

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIESTER, a river which rises in Poland, and running south east divides Podolia in Poland from Moldavia in Turkey; and afterwards, dividing Beffarabia from Budziaz Tartary, falls into the Black Sea near Belgorod.
