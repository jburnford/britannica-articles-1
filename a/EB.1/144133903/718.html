MUREX, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the
order of vermes testacea. This animal is of the snail
kind; the shell consists of one spiral valve, rough with
membranaceous furrows; and the aperture terminates
in an entire canal either straight or somewhat ascend-
ing. There are 60 species, particularly distinguished
by peculiarities in their shells, &c.
