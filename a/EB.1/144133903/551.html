MOMENTUM

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOMENTUM, in mechanics, signifies the same with impetus, or the quantity of motion in a moving body;

which is always equal to the quantity of matter multiplied into the velocity; or, which is the same thing, it may be considered as a rectangle under the quantity of matter and velocity.
