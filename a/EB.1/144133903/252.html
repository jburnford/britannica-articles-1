MAURITANIA

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAURITANIA, the ancient name of the coast of Barbary, from the city of Tangier to that of Algiers: the west part of it, in which Tangier stands, was called Mauritania Tingitana; and that farther east, Mauritania Caesariensis.
