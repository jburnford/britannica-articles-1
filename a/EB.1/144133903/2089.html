PHYSIOLOGY

article · 345 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHYSIOLOGY, properly denotes a discourse of nature, and natural bodies; or, it is that part of natural philosophy which treats of the various phenomena of nature in a scientific and speculative way.

Among physicians, the term physiology denotes the history of the human body and its several constituent parts, with their relations and functions.
