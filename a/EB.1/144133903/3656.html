SCOLYMUS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCOLYMUS, in botany, a genus of the syngeneia polygamia æqualis class. The receptacle is paleaceous; the calix is imbricated and prickly; and it has no pappus. There are two species, both natives of Italy.
