RADICAL

article · 338 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RADICAL, in general, something that serves as a basis or foundation.

Hence physicians talk much of a radical moisture. In grammar, we give the appellation radical to primitive words, in contradistinction to compounds and derivatives.

Algebraists also speak of the radical sign of quantities, which is the character expressing their roots.
