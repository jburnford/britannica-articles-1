NOTE

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOTE is likewise used for a mark made in a book or writing where there occurs something remarkable and worthy of particular notice: as also for an observa-tion or explication of some passage in an author added in the margin, at the bottom of the page, or elsewhere, in which sense it stands contradistinguished to text.
