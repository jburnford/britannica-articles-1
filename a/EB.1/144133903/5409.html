TYPHA

TYPHA, in botany, a genus of the monoecia triandria class. The amentum of both male and female is cylindrical; the calix of the male consists of three leaves; and neither of them have any corolla. There are two species, both of them natives of Britain, viz. the latifolia, or great cat's-tail; and angustifolia, or narrow-leaved cat's-tail.
