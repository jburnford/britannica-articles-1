MALPIGHIA

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MALPIGHIA, in botany, a genus of the decandria trigynia class. The calix consists of five leaves, and the corolla of five roundish petals; and the berry has one cell and three seeds. There are nine species, none of them natives of Britain.
