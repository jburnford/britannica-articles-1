QUOIN, or COIN

sub_entry · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUOIN, or COIN, on board a ship, a wedge fastened on the deck close to the breech of the carriage of a gun, to keep it firm up to the ship-side.

Cantile quoins are short three-legged quoins put between casks to keep them steady.
