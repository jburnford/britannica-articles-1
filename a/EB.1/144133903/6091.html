WREATH

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WREATH, in heraldry, a roll of fine linen or silk (like that of a Turkish turbant) consisting of the colours borne in the escutcheon, placed in an achievement between the helmet and the crest, and immediately supporting the crest.
