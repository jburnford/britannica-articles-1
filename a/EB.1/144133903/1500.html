OXYMEL, in pharmacy, a composition of vinegar and honey.

There are several sorts of oxymel, whereof the simple kind is made by boiling, in a glazed earthen vessel, and with a gentle fire, two pounds of clarified honey, in a pint of vinegar, to the consistence of a syrup.
