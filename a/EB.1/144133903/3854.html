SESELI

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SESELI, in botany, a genus of the pentandria digynia class. The umbellæ are globular: the involucrum consists of many leaves; and the fruit is oval and striated. There are 13 species, only one of them, viz. the caruifolia, or meadow-fax, a native of Britain.
