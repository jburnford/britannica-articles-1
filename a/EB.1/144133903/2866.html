RATISBON

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RATISBON, a city of Germany, in the circle of Bavaria, situated at the confluence of the rivers Danube and Regen, in E. long. 12° 5', N. lat. 49°. This is a free imperial city, and here the assembly or diet of the states of the empire meets.
