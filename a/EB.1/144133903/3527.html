SARCASM

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SARCASM, in rhetoric, a keen bitter expression which has the true point of satire, by which the orator scoffs and insults his enemy: such was that of the Jews to our Saviour, "He saved others, himself he cannot save."
