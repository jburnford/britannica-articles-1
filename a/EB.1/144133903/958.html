NIGELLA

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIGELLA, in botany, a genus of the polyandria pentagynia class. The calix is wanting; the corolla consists of five petals; there are five trifid nektaria within the corolla; and there are five connected capsules. The species are five, none of them natives of Britain.
