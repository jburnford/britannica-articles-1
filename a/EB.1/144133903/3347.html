RUNG-heads

sub_entry · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RUNG-heads, in a ship, are made a little bending, to direct the sweep or mold of the futtocks and navel timbers: for here the lines, which make the compass and bearing of a ship, do begin.
