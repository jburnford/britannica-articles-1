MASSACHUSET-COLONY

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASSACHUSET-COLONY, the principal sub-division of New-England, is bounded by New Hampshire, on the north; by the Atlantic ocean, on the east and south; and by Connecticut and New York, on the west. It is about 100 miles long, and 40 broad.
