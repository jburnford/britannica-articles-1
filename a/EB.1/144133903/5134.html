TOLLENON

article · 407 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TOLLENON, among the Romans, a warlike machine, formed in this manner: One beam was fixed very deep in the earth, and on the top of it another more than twice as long, and moveable upon a centre; on one end of this cross-beam were placed a covering of hurdles or planks, within which a few soldiers were put, and by pulling down the other end with ropes, these were raised above the walls of a besieged town.
