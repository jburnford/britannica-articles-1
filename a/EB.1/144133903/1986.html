PETITVERIA

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PETITVERIA, in botany, a genus of the hexandria tetragynia class. The calix consists of four segments; it has no corolla; and but one seed with reflected prickles at the point. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
