PAPILIO

PAPILIO, the BUTTERFLY, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of lepidoptera. It has four wings, imbricated with a kind of downy scales; the tongue is convoluted in a spiral form; and the body is hairy. There are 273 species, principally distinguished by the colour of their wings.
