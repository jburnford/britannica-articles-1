PARAPHIMOSIS

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MEDICINE

PARAPHIMOSIS, in medicine, a disorder in the penis, wherein the prepuce is shrunk, and withdrawn behind the glans, so as not to be capable of being brought to cover the same; which generally happens in venereal disorders. See MEDICINE.
