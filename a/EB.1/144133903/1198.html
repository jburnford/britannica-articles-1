ODOROUS

article · 167 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ODOROUS, or ODORIFEROUS, appellations given to whatever smells strongly, whether they be fetid or agreeable; but chiefly to things whose smell is brisk, and agreeable.
