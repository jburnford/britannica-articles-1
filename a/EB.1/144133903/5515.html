VERBESINA

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERBESINA, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygamia superflua class. The receptacle is palæaceous; the pappus is furnished with an awn; the calix is double; and it has about five floscules in the radius. There are 13 species, none of them natives of Britain.
