SEGURA

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEGURA, a town of Portugal, in the province of Beira, ten miles north-west of Alcantara. This is also the name of a town in Spain, in the province of New Castile, and territory of La Mancha, situated among the mountains of Segura; W. long. 2^{\circ} 50', N. lat. 38^{\circ} 25'.
