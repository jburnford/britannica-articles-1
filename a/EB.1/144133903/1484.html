OUSTIACH

article · 336 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OUSTIACH, or OUSTIACH Country, is a part of Asiatic Russia, extending along the river Irtysh to its confluence with the river Oby and from thence northward along the banks of the Oby and Jenisa, into the gulph of the Mangasca, or the frozen ocean; and extending also along

the banks of several rivers which fall into the Oby and Jenisa.
