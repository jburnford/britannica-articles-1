TUBE

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TUBE, in general, pipe, conduit, or canal; a cylinder hollow withinside, either of lead, iron, wood, glass, or other matter, for the air, or some other fluid, to have a free passage or conveyance through.
