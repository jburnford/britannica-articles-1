TAY

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TAY, a river of Scotland, rising from the loch or lake of Tay, in Braidalbin, and running east through Athol: it afterwards turns south-east, and dividing the counties of Perth and Angus from Strathern and Fife, falls into the frith of Tay.
