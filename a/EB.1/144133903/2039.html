PHILIPPINE ISLANDS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHILIPPINE ISLANDS, are situated in the Pacific ocean, in Asia, between 114° and 131° east longitude, and between 5° and 19° north latitude: there are a great number of them, and some very large.
