NOMENCLATORES

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOMENCLATORES, among the botanical authors, are those who have employed their labours about settling and adjusting the right names, synonyms, and etymologies of names, in regard to the whole vegetable world.
