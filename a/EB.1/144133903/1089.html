NUMIDIA

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NUMIDIA, in ornithology, a genus belonging to the order of gallinæ. On each side of the head there is a kind of coloured fleshy horn; and the beak is furnished with cere near the nostrils. There is but one species, a native of Africa.
