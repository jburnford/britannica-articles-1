PHLEGM

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHLEGM, in the animal economy, one of the four humours whereof the ancients supposed the blood to be composed. The chemists make phlegm, or water, an elementary body, the characters of which are fluidity, insipidity, and volatility.
