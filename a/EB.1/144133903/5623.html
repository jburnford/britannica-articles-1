VIRGINIA, one of the British American colonies, situated between seventy-four and eighty degrees east long. and between thirty-six and thirty-nine degrees of north lat.

lat. bounded by the river Patowmack, which separates it from Maryland, on the north; by the Atlantic-ocean, on the east; by Carolina, on the south; and may be extended as far westward as we think fit.
