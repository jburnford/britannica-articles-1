VESICATORIUM

VESICATORIUM, an external medicine, serving to raise a blister; whence also it is itself, though improperly, called a blister.

We have vesicatories made of cantharides, euphorbium, figs, sublimate of mercury, lapis infernalis, mustard, anacardium, squills, briony, vinegar, pepper, leaven, &c.
