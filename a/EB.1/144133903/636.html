MOTACILLA

article · 332 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOTACILLA, in ornithology, a genus of birds, of the order of the passerines, distinguished by a straight beak, of a subulated figure, and a lacerated tongue. There are 49 species belonging to this genus, most of which feed upon insects, and migrate from the north to the southern countries in order to procure subsistence in winter.
