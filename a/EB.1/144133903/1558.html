PALISSE

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALISSE, in heraldry, a bearing like a range of palisades before a fortification, represented on a fesse, rising up a considerable height, and pointed a-top, with the field appearing between them. See Plate CXXXIV. fig. 8.
