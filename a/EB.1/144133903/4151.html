SOLOTHURN

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOLOTHURN, one of the cantons of Switzerland, lying between those of Basil and Bern, the former on the north, and the latter on the south.

The city of Solothurn, capital of the said canton, is situated in E. long. 7° 15', and N. lat. 47° 18'.
