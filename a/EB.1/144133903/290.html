MEDITERRANEAN SEA

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEDITERRANEAN SEA, extends from the straits of Gibraltar to the coasts of Syria and Palestine, being upwards of 2000 miles in length, but of a very unequal breadth: the west-part of it separates Europe from Africa; and the Levant or east-part of it divides Asia from Africa.
