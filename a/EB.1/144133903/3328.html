RUDBECKIA

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RUDBECKIA, in botany, a genus of the syngenesia polygamia frustanea class. The receptacle is conical and palleaceous: the pappus has four teeth on its edge; and the calix consists of a double row of scales. There are six species, none of them natives of Britain.
