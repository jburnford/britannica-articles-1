MAES

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAES, a river which arises in Burgundy, and runs through Lorraine and Champagne into the Netherlands, and at last, after passing by many considerable towns, discharges itself into the German sea, a little below the Briel.
