OTIS

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OTIS, in ornithology, a genus of birds belonging to the order of grallæ. The superior mandible of the bill is arched; the nostrils are oval; the tongue is bifid; the feet are made for running, being furnished with four toes. There are four species, principally distinguished by their colour.
