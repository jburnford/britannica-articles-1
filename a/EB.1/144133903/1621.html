PAPOUS

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAPOUS, or NEW GUINEA, a large continent in the Pacific ocean, a little south of the equator; situated east of the Spice islands, in 120° east long. but how far it extends farther to the eastward or southward, is uncertain.
