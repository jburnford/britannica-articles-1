QUICK

article · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUICK, or QUICKSET HEDGE, among gardeners, denotes all live hedges of whatever sort of plants they are composed, to distinguish them from dead hedges: but in a

more strict sense of the word, it is restrained to those planted with the hawthorn, under which name these young plants, or sets, are sold by the nursery-gardeners who raise them for sale.
