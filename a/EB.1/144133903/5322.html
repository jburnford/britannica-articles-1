TROGLODYTES

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TROGLODYTES, in the ancient geography, a people of Æthiopia, said to have lived in caves under ground. Pom. Mela gives a strange account of the Troglodytes: he says, they did not so properly speak as shrick, and that they lived on serpents.
