TRUSS

article · 161 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRUSS, a bundle, or certain quantity of hay, straw, &c. A truss of hay is to contain fifty-six pounds, or half an hundred weight; thirty-six trusses make a load.
