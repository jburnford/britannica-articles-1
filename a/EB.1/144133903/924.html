NEWSTAT, the name of several towns; one eight miles north of Landau; another fifteen miles south-west of Ratibon; a third in Silesia, fifteen miles south

of Breslaw; a fourth in Hungary, sixty-five miles east of Tockay; and a fifth in Moravia, ten miles north of Olmutz.
