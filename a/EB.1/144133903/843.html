NECYDALIS

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NECYDALIS, in zoology, a genus of insects belonging to the order of coleoptera. The feelers are setaceous; the elytra are shorter and narrower than the wings; and the

the tail is simple. There are eleven species, chiefly distinguished by the size and figure of the elytra.
