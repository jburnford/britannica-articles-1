PENTAE DROSTYLA, in natural history, the name of a genus of spars. See SPAR.

The bodies of this genus are spars, in form of pentagonal columns, terminated by pentagonal pyramids at one end, and irregularly affixed at the other to some solid body. PENTAGON, in geometry, a figure of five sides and five angles. See GEOMETRY.
