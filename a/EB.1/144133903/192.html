MARTINICO

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARTINICO, the chief of the French Caribbee-islands, situated in 61^{\circ} of west long. and between 14^{\circ} and 5^{\circ} N. lat. It is sixty miles long, but is scarce twenty broad in any part.
