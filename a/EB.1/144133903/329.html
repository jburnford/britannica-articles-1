MELOTHRIA

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELOTHRIA, in botany, a genus of the triandria monogynia class. The calix consists of five segments; the corolla is monopetalous and bell shaped; and the berry has one cell, containing many seeds.

In Canada, Virginia, and Jamaica, where this fruit commonly grows, it is pickled for the table.
