PAUSANIA

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAUSANIA, in Grecian antiquity, a festival, in which were solemn games, wherein nobody contended but free-born Spartans; in honour of Pausanias, the Spartan general, under whose conduct the Greeks overcame Marathon, in the famous battle at Plateæ.
