MATERAN

article · 175 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MATERAN, the capital of a kingdom of the same name, situated on the south coast of the island of Java. This city is said to lie in E. long. 110^{\circ}, S. lat. 7^{\circ} 45'.
