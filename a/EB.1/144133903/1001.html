NODUS

Cross-references: DIALLING

NODUS, or NODE, in dialling, a certain point or pole in the gnomon of a dial, by the shadow or light whereof either the hour of the day in dials without furniture, or the parallels of the sun's declination, and his place in the ecliptic, &c. in dials with furniture, are shewn. See DIALLING.
