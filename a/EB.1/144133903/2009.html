PHALARIS

article · 315 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHALARIS, in botany, a genus of the triandria digynia class. It has two carinated valves, of equal lengths, and including the corolla. There are 10 species, three of them natives of Britain, viz. the canariensis, or manured canary-grass; the arenaria, or sea canary-grass; and the arundinacea, or reed canary-grass.
