NEGROPONT

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEGROPONT, or EGIPPOS, the capital of the island of Negropont, anciently called Euboea, situated in the Archipelago, on the west side of the island; where the strait is so narrow, that it is joined to the continent by a bridge: E. lon. 24° 30', N. lat. 38° 30'.
