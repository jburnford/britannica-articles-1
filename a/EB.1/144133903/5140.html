TONE

Cross-references: MUSIC

TONE, or TUNE, in music, a property of sound, whereby it comes under the relation of grave and acute; or it is the degree of elevation a sound has, from the degree of swiftness of the vibrations of the part of sonorous bodies. See MUSIC.
