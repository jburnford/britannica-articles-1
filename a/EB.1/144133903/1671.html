PAIREIRA BRAVA

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAIREIRA BRAVA, in the materia medica, a kind of oblong and large root, brought from the Brasils.

It is certainly a diuretic of no mean character, and has done great service in nephritic cases: and in pleurisy and quinsy it has been attended with more success than almost any medicine we know of singly.
