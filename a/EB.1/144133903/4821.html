TANGENT

Cross-references: GEOMETRY

TANGENT of an arch is a right-line drawn perpendicularly from the end of a diameter, passing to one extremity of the arch, and terminated by a right-line drawn from the centre through the other end of the arch, and called the secant. See GEOMETRY.
