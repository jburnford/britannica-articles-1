TENTHREDO, a genus of insects belonging to the order of hymenoptera. The mouth is furnished with jaws, but has no proboscis; the wings are plain and tumid; the sting consists of two serrated laminae, and the scutellum of two grains placed at a distance. There are 55 species, principally distinguished by their colour, and the figure of the antennæ.
