XYSTARCHA

XYSTARCHA, in antiquity, the master or director of the xystus. In the Greek gymnasium, the xystarcha was the second

second officer, and the gymnasiarcha the first; the former was his lieutenant, and presided over the two xylis, and all exercises of the athletes therein.
