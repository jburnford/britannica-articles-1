SCUTELLARIA

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCUTELLARIA, in botany, a genus of the didynamia gymnosperma class. The calix is entire on the edge, is shut after the flowering, and operculated. The species are 13, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the galericulata, or hooded willow-herb; and the minor, or lesser hooded willow-herb.
