PARTHENIUM

article · 352 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARTHENIUM, in botany, a genus of the monocœcia pentandria class. The common calix of the male consists of five leaves; and the corolla of the disk are monopetalous. The corolla of the radius in the female are five; two of each side are males, and the intermediate one female, above a naked seed. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
