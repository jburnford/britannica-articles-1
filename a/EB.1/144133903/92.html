MAMMEA

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAMMEA, in botany, a genus of the polyandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of four petals, and the calix of two leaves; and the berry is large, and contains four seeds. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
