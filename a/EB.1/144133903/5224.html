TRANSMUTATION

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRANSMUTATION, in alchemy, denotes the art of changing or exalting imperfect metals into gold or silver. This is also called the grand operation, and, they say, is to be effected with the philosopher's stone.
