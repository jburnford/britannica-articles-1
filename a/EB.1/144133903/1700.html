PARODY

PARODY, a popular maxim, adage, or proverb. Parody is also a poetical pleasantry, consisting in applying the verses written on one subject, by way of ridicule to another; or in turning a serious work into a

burlesque, by affecting to observe, as nearly as possible, the same rhymes, words, and cadences.
