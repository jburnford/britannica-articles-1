RHINEFIELD

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHINEFIELD, the name of two towns of Germany, one whereof is situated in the circle of Suabia, on the Rhine, eight miles east of Basil; the other is the capital of the county of Rhinefield, situated in the circle of the Upper Rhine, sixteen miles north-west of Mentz.
