PILE

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PILE, in heraldry, an ordinary in form of a wedge, contracting from the chief, and terminating in a point towards the bottom of the shield. See Plate CXXXIV. fig. 16.

The pile, like other ordinaries, is borne inverted, ingrailed, &c. and issues indifferently from any point of the verge of an escutcheon.
