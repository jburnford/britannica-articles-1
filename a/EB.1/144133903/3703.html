SEALING

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEALING, in architecture, the fixing a piece of wood or iron in a wall with plaster, mortar, cement, lead, and other solid binding. For staples, hinges and joints, plaster is very proper.
