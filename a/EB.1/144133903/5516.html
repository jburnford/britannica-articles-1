VERD

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERD, or CAPE-VERD, a promontory of Africa, forty miles north-west of the mouth of the river Gambia: W. long. 18°, N. lat. 15°.

There are a number of islands in the Atlantic ocean, called Cape-Verd islands, from their being situated off this cape.
