THUYA

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THUYA, in botany, a genus of the monoclea monadelphia class. The calix of the male is an amentum, that of the female a strobilus; neither of them have any corolla; there is one pistillum, and one nut surrounded with an emarginated wing. The species are three, all natives of warm countries.
