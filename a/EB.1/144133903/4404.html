STATICE

article · 327 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STATICE, in botany, a genus of the pentandria trigynia class. The calix is one entire plaited leaf; the corolla consists of five petals; it has but one seed. There are 14 species, three of them natives of Britain, viz. the armeria, or sea gilly-flower; the limonium, or sea-lavender; and the reticulata, or matted sea-lavender.
