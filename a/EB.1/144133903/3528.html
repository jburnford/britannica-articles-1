SARCOCELE

SARCOCELE, in surgery, a spurious rupture, or hernia, wherein the testicle is considerably tumid or indurated, like a scirrhus, or much enlarged by a fleshy excrescence, which is frequently attended with acute pains and sometimes ulceration, so as to degenerate at last into a cancerous disposition. See SURGERY.
