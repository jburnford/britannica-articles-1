TRANSILVANIA

TRANSILVANIA, a principality bounded by the Carpathian mountains, which divide it from Poland on the north; by Moldavia on the east; by Walachia, and part of Hungary, on the south; and by another part of Hungary on the north; being about 120 miles long, and almost as many broad; and lying between 22 and 25 degrees east long. and between 45 and 48 of north lat.
