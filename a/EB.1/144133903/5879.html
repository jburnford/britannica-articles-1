WATERFORD

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WATERFORD, a port-town of Ireland, capital of the county of Waterford, situated on the river Sure, eight miles north of the sea: W. long. 7°, N. lat. 52° 12'.

It is one of the largest cities in Ireland, and has a good foreign trade.
