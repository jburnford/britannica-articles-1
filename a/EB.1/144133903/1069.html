NOVOGOROD

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOVOGOROD, the capital of a province of the same name in Muscovy, situated on the river Wolcoss, 130 miles

miles south east of Petersburg; E. long. 34^{\circ}, N. lat. 58^{\circ}.

It is an archbishop's see, and has 180 churches and monasteries.
