SORBUS

SORBUS, in botany, a genus of the icofandria trigynia class. The calix consists of five leaves, and the corolla of five petals; and the berry contains three seeds. There are three species, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the domestica, or the true service or sorb; and the aucuparia, quicken-tree, or mountain-ash.
