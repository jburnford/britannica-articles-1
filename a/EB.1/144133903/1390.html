ORLE

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORLE, ORLET, or ORLO, in architecture, a fillet under the ovolo or quarter round of a capital. When it is at the top or bottom of the shaft, it is called cincture.

Palladio uses the word orlo, for the piast of the bases of the columns.
