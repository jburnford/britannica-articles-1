MUZZLE

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MUZZLE of a gun or mortar, the extremity at which the powder and ball is put in; and hence, the muzzling is the metalline circle, or moulding, that surrounds the mouth of the piece.
