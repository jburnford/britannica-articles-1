VERBERATION

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERBERATION, in physics, a term used to express the cause of sound, which arises from a verberation of the air, when struck in divers manners by the several parts of the sonorous body first put into a vibratory motion.
