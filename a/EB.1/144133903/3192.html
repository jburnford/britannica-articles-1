RICHARDIA

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RICHARDIA, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The calix has six segments, and the corolla one subcylindrical petal; and the seeds are three. There is but one species, a native of Vera Cruz.
