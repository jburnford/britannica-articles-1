PAVETTA

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAVETTA, in botany, a genus of the tetrandria monogynia class. The corolla consists of one funnel-shaped petal; the stylus is crooked; and the berry contains one seed. There is but one species, a native of India.
