UNITED NETHERLANDS

article · 530 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

UNITED NETHERLANDS consist of the provinces of Holland, Zealand, Friesland, Groningen, Overijssel, Gelderland, with Zutphen and Utrecht; these are bounded by the German sea on the north and west; by the circle of Westphalia, on the east; and by Flanders, Brabant, and the duchy of Cleves, on the south: lying between 3° 20' and 7° 30' east longitude, and between 51° 35' and 52° 40' north latitude; being about fifty miles long, and as many broad including the Zuyder-sea, which takes up a considerable space between these limits.
