VINCA

VINCA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria monogynia class. It has two erect follicles; the seeds are plumose; and the tube of the corolla terminates in a lacerated corona. There are four species, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the minor, or periwinkle; and major, or greater periwinkle.
