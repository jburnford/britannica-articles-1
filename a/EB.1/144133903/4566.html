STUCCO

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STUCCO, in building, a composition of white marble, pulverized and mixed with plaster of lime; and the whole being sifted and wrought up with water, is to be used like common plaster: this is what Pliny means by marmoratum opus, and albarium opus.
