QUADRATRIX

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUADRATRIX, in geometry, a mechanical line, by means whereof we can find right lines equal to the circumference of circles, or other curves, and their several parts.

QUADRATRIX of Dinostrates, so called from its inventor

Dinostrates, is a curve, whereby the quadrature of the circle is effected mechanically.
