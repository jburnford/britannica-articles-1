POLYGONUM

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLYGONUM, in botany, a genus of the oestandria trigynia class. It has no calix; the corolla has five segments; and there is but one angular seed. The species are 27, eleven of them natives of Britain.
