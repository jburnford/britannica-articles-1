PHLEBOTOMY

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHLEBOTOMY, the opening a vein with a proper sharp-edged and pointed instrument of steel, in order to let out a proper quantity of blood, either for the preservation or recovery of a person's health.
