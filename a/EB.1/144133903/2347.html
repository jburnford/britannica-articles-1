PORTO RICO

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORTO RICO, an island in the American ocean, one hundred and twenty miles long, and sixty broad, which produces sugar, rum, and ginger: situated between 64° and 68° of W. long. and in 8° of N. lat. It is subject to Spain. The capital is also called Porto Rico, and St John's city.
