VIENNA

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VIENNA, the capital city of the circle of Austria and of the German empire, is situated on the Danube, in E. long. 16^{\circ} 21', and N. lat. 48^{\circ} 20'.
Vienna is an archbishop's see, and has a celebrated university.
