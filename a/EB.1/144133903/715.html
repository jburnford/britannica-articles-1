MURDER, or MURTHER

Cross-references: LAW

MURDER, or MURTHER, in law, is the wilful and
felonious killing a person from premeditated malice;
provided the party wounded, or otherwise hurt, die
within a year and a day after the fact was committed.
See LAW, Tit. xxxiii.
