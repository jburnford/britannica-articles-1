SALSETTE

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SALSETTE, an island on the western coast of the hither India, separated from that of Bombay by a narrow channel: it belongs to the Portuguese, and is twenty miles long, and seventeen broad.
