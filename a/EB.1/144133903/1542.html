PALAMEDIA

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALAMEDIA, in ornithology, a genus belonging to the order of grallæ. The bill is conical, the superior mandible being crooked; and the feet have three divided toes. There are two species, both natives of Brazil.
