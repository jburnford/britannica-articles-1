SCALPRADENTALIA

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCALPRADENTALIA, instruments used by the surgeons to take off those black, livid, or yellow crusts, which infest the teeth, and not only loosen and destroy them, but taint the breath.
