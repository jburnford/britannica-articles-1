STAPHYLEA

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STAPHYLÆA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria trigynia class. The calix consists of five segments, and the corolla of five petals; the capsules are inflorescent and connate; and there are two globular seeds. There are two species, both natives of the Cape of Good Hope.
