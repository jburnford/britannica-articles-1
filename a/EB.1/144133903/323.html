MELITIS

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELITIS, in botany, a genus of the didynamia gymnosperma class. The calix is larger than the tube of the corolla; the upper lip of the corolla is plain; the under lip is crenated; and the antheræ are in the form of a cross. There is but one species, viz. the melisophyllum, or bastard baum.
