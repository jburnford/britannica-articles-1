NATIVITY

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NATIVITY, or NATAL DAY, the day of a person's birth: The word nativity is chiefly used in speaking of the saints, as the nativity of St. John the Baptist, &c. But when we say the Nativity, it is understood of that of Jesus Christ, or the feast of Christmas.
