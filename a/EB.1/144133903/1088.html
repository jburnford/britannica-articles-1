NUMERICAL

article · 357 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NUMERICAL, NUMEROUS, or NUMERAL, something belonging to numbers; as numerical algebra is that which makes use of numbers, instead of letters of the alphabet. Also, numerical difference, is the difference whereby one individual is distinguished from another. Hence a thing is said to be numerically the same, when it is so in the strictest sense of the word.
