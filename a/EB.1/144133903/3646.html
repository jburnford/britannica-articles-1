SCIRPUS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCIRPUS, in botany, a genus of the triandria monogynia class. The glume are paleaceous, and imbricated; it has no corolla, and but one bearded seed. The species are 27, ten of which are natives of Britain.
