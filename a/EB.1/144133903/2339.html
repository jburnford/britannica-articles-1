PORT-ROYAL

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORT-ROYAL, an island on the coast of South-Carolina, which, with the neighbouring continent, forms one of the most commodious harbours in the British plantations: W. long. 80^{\circ}, N. lat. 31^{\circ} 45'.
