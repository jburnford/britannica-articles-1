MACEDONIA

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MACEDONIA, a province of European Turkey, bounded by Servia and Romania, on the north and east; by the gulphs of Salonichi, Contessa and Theffaly, on the south; and by Albania and Epirus, on the west.
