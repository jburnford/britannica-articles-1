QUATUOR VIR

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUATUOR VIR, in antiquity, formerly written III. VIR, a Roman magistrate who had three colleges joined with him in the same administration, and had the care of conducting and settling the colonies sent into the provinces,

There were also quatuor-viri appointed to inspect and take care of repairs, &c.
