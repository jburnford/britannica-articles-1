WEXFORD

WEXFORD, a county of Ireland, in the province of Munster, bounded by the county of Wicklow on the north, by the ocean on the east and south, and by Kilkenny and Waterford on the west.

Wexford, the capital of this county, is situated at the mouth of the river Slaney, sixty five miles south of Dublin.
