SEELING, in the manege. A horse is said to feel when he begins to have white eye brows, that is, when there grows on that part about the breadth of a farthing of white hairs, mixed with those of his natural colour, which is a mark of old age. It is said, that a horse never feels till he is fourteen years old, and always does before he is sixteen years.
