ZIRANIA

article · 332 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZIRANIA, in botany, a genus of the monoecia hexandria class. The male has no calix; the corolla consists of a double-valved glume without any awn. The female has no calix; the corolla consists of a single-valved glume with an awn; the filius is bifid; and there is but one seed. The species are two, none of them natives of Britain.
