SHELF

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHELF, among miners, the same with what they otherwise call salt ground, or salt country; being that part of the internal structure of the earth, which they find lying even; and in an orderly manner, and evidently having retained its primitive form and situation.
