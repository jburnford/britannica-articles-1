RETIRADE

article · 341 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RETIRADE, in fortification, a kind of retrenchment made in the body of a bastion, or other work, which is to be disputed, inch by inch, after the defences are dismantled. It usually consists of two faces, which making a re-entering angle, when a breach is made in a bastion, the enemy may also make a retirade or new fortification behind it.
