STAR-BOARD

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STAR-BOARD, in the sea-language, denotes the right-hand side of a ship: thus they say, star-board the helm, or helm a star-board, when he that conds would have the men at the helm, or steering-wheel, put the helm to the right-side of the ship.
