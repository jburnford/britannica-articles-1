SPHERANTHUS

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPHERANTHUS, in botany, a genus of the syngenia polygamia segregata class. The receptacle is naked; it has no pappus; the calix is imbricated. There are two species, none of them natives of Britain.
