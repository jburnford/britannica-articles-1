MONTFERRAT

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTFERRAT, a duchy in Italy, bounded by the lordship of Vercell on the north, by the Alexandrin on the east, by the territory of Genoa on the south, and by the county of Asti on the west.
