OROBANCHE

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OROBANCHE, in botany, a genus of the didynamia angiospermi class. The calix is bifid, and the corolla ringent; the capsule has two valves, and contains many seeds. There are seven species, two of them natives of Britain, viz. the major, or broom-rape; and the ramosa, or branched broom-rape.
