PALM-SUNDAY

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALM-SUNDAY, in the Christian church, the Sunday next before Easter; being so called in memory of our Saviour's triumphal entry into Jerusalem when the multitude that attended him strewed palm-branches in his way.
