TENEBRÆ

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TENEBRÆ, an office in the Romish church, performed on Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday, in Passion-week, at which time neither flowers nor images are allowed to be set upon the altars, but they must be covered with purple.
