MOLLOSSSES

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOLLOSSSES, in commerce, the thick fluid matter remaining after the sugar is made, resembling syrup. In Holland molasses are much used in the manufacture of tobacco, and by the poor people for sugar. A brandy is also distilled from them, but it is said to be unwholesome.
