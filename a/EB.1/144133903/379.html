MESOLOGARITHMS

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MESOLOGARITHMS, according to Kepler, are the logarithms of the co-sines and co-tangents, the former of which were called by lord Napier antilogarithms, and the latter differentials.
