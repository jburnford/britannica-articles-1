OENOTHERA

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OENOTHERA, in botany, a genus of the octandria monogynia class. The calix consists of four segments, and the corolla of four petals; the capsule is cylindrical, and the seeds are naked. There are seven species, none of them natives of Britain.
