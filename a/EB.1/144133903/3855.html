SESQUI

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SESQUI, a Latin particle, signifying a whole and a half, which joined with altera, terna, quarta, &c. is much used in the Italian music to express a kind of ratios, particularly several pieces of triples.
