POLAND

article · 345 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLAND, a large kingdom of Europe, situated between 16° and 34° east longitude, and between 46° and 57° north latitude; bounded by Russia on the north and east; by Bessarabia, Moldavia, Transylvania, and Hungary, on the south; and by Pomerania, Brandenburg, and Silesia, on the west; being almost square, and seven hundred miles over either way.
