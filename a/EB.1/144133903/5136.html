TOLUIFERA

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TOLUIFERA, in botany, a genus of the decandria monogynia class. The calix is bell-shaped, and has five segments; the corolla has five petals, the lowest being longest, and somewhat cordated; it has no stylus. There is but one species, a native of America.
