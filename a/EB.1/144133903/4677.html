SUPERVISOR

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUPERVISOR, a surveyor or overseer. It was formerly, and still remains, a custom among some persons, to appoint a supervisor of a will, to see that the executors thereof do punctually observe and perform the same.
