TELEPHIUM

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TELEPHIUM, in botany, a genus of the pentandria trigynia class. The calix consists of five leaves; and the corolla of five petals, inserted into the receptacle; and the capsule has one cell and three valves. The species are two, none of them natives of Britain.
