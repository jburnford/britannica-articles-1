MENSTRUUM

Cross-references: CHEMISTRY

MENSTRUUM, in chemistry, any body which in a fluid or subtilised state is capable of interposing its small parts betwixt the small parts of other bodies, so as to divide them subtilly, and form a new uniform compound of the two. See CHEMISTRY.
