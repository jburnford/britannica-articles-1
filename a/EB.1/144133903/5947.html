WESTPHALIA

WESTPHALIA, the north-west circle of the empire of Germany; bounded by the German ocean, on the north; by the circle of Lower Saxony, on the east; by the Landgraviate of Hesse, the Palatinate of the Rhine, and the electorate of Trier, on the south; and by the Netherlands, on the west: being 200 miles in length, and from 150 to 200 in breadth.
