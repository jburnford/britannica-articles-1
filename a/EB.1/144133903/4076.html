SMILAX

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SMILAX, in botany, a genus of the dicæia hexandria class. The calix both of male and female consists of six leaves; none of them have any corolla; the stylus of the female is trifid; and the berry has three cells, containing two seeds. There are 13 species, none of them natives of Britain.
