TO WIND

sub_entry · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TO WIND, or WEND a ship, signifies to bring her head about. How winds or wends the ship? is a question asked by mariners, concerning a ship under sail; signifying as much as, upon what point of the compass does she lie with her head?
