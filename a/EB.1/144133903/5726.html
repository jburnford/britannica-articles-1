VOLKAMERIA

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOLKAMERIA, in botany, a genus of the didynamian-giospermae class. The calix consists of five segments; and the berry contains two bilocular seeds. There are two species, none of them native of Britain.
