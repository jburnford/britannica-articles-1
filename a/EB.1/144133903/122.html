MANTUA

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MANTUA, the capital of a duchy of the same name in Italy, is situated in the middle of a lake, formed by the river Mincio, but has a communication with the continent by three causeways: E. long. 11° 15', N. lat. 45° 20'.
