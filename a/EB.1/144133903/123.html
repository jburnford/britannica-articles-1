MANUMISSION

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MANUMISSION, in Roman antiquity, the act of setting a slave at liberty; which was usually performed before the prætor, who laid his hand called vindicta, on the slave's head, and declared him free.
