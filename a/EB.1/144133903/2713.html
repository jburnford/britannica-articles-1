QUADRIGA

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUADRIGA, in antiquity, a car or chariot drawn by four horses.

On the reverses of medals, we frequently see the emperor or victory in a quadriga, holding the reins of the horses; whence these coins are, among the curious, called nummi quadrigati, and victoriati.
