TRITON

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRITON, in zoology, a genus belonging to the order of vermes mollusca. The body is oblong; the tongue is spiral; it has 12 tentacula, six on each side, the hind-most ones having claws like a crab. There is but one species, found in holes of rocks about the sea-shore.
