PROPONTIS

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PROPONTIS, or Sea of MARMORA, divides Europe from Asia; having the Bosphorus on the north-east, by which it has a communication with the Euxine sea; and the Hellespont on the south-west, by which it communicates with the Archipelago. It is one hundred and twenty miles long, and in some places upwards of forty broad.
