PETITIO PRINCIPII

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PETITIO PRINCIPII, in logic, the taking a thing for true, and drawing conclusions from it as such; when it

is really false, or at least wants to be proved, before any inferences can be deduced from it.
