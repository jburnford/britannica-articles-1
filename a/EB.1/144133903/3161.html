RHEXIA

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHEXIA, in botany, a genus of the ostandria monogynia class. The calix consists of four segments, and the corolla of four petals, inserted into the calix; the anthers are declined; and the capsule has four cells. There are three species, none of them natives of Britain.
