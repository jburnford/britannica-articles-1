STIGMATIZING

article · 351 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STIGMATIZING, among the ancients, was inflicted upon slaves, as a punishment, but more frequently as a mark to know them by, in which case it was done by applying a red-hot iron marked with certain letters to their foreheads, till a fair impression was made, and then pouring ink into their furrows, that the inscription might be the more conspicuous.
