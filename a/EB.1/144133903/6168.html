ZANONIA

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZANONIA, in botany, a genus of the diccia pentandria class. The calix of both male and female consists of three leaves, and the corolla of five segments; the female has three styli; and the berry contains two seeds. There is but one species, a native of Malabar.
