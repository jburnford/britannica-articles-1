TETRAPYRAMIDIA

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TETRAPYRAMIDIA, in natural history, the name of a genus of spars, influenced in their shape by an admixture of particles of tin, and found in form of broad-bottomed pyramids of four sides.
