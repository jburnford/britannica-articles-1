VERONICA

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VERONICA, in botany, a genus of the diandria monogynia class. The corolla is rotated, and consists of four segments, the lowest one being less than all the rest; and the capsule has two cells. There are 34 species, 13 of them natives of Britain.
