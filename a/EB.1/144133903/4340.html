SQUARE

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SQUARE, an instrument consisting of two rules, or branches, fastened perpendicularly at one end of their extremities, so as to form a right angle: it is of great use in the description and mensuration of right angles, and laying down perpendiculars.
