SHOE

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHOE, a covering for the foot, usually made of leather, by the company of cordwainers.

SHOE for an anchor, in a ship, the place for the anchor to rest, and fitted to receive the stock, &c. so as to prevent the sheets, tacks, and other running rigging, from galling, or being entangled with the flocks.
