SEYNE

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEYNE, a river of France, which rising near Dijon, in Burgundy, runs north-west through Champagne and the isle of France, through Paris, &c. and crossing Normandy falls into the Bristol channel between Havre-de-grace and Honfleur.
