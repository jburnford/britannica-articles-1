VIRTUE

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VIRTUE, a term used in various significations. In the general it denotes power, or perfection of any thing, whether natural or supernatural, animate or inanimate, essential or accessory. But in its more proper or restrained sense, virtue signifies an habit, which improves and perfects the possessor and his actions.
