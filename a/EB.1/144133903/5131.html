TOLEDO

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TOLEDO, a city of New Castile, in Spain; the archbishop of which is primate of Spain, &c. and possesses the largest revenue of any archbishop of Europe: it is situated in W. long. 4° 12', and N. lat. 39° 45'.
