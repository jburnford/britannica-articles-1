ORONTIUM

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORONTIUM, in botany, a genus of the hexandria monogynia class. The spadix is cylindrical, and covered with floccules; the corolla consists of six petals; it has no stylus; and the capsule has three cells. There is but one species, a native of Virginia.
