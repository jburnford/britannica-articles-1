REFRACTION

Cross-references: OPTICS

REFRACTION, in general, is the deviation of a moving body from its direct course, occasioned by the different density of the medium it moves in; or, it is a change of direction, occasioned by a body's falling obliquely out of one medium into another of a different density. See OPTICS.
