WORCUM

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WORCUM, a town of Holland, situated on the river Waal, twenty-three miles east of Rotterdam. This is also the name of a port-town of the United Netherlands, situated on the province of Friesland, on the Zuyder-sea, twenty miles south-west of Leewarden.
