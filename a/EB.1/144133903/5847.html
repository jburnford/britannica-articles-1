WARD-HOOK

article · 176 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: WADD

WARD-HOOK, or WADD-HOOK, in gunnery, a rod or staff with an iron end turned serpent-wise, or like a scraw, to draw the wadding out of a gun when it is to be unloaded. See WADD.
