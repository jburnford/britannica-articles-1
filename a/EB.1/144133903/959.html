NIGER

article · 349 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIGER, a great river of Africa, which runs from east to west through the middle of Negroland, and discharges itself into the Atlantic ocean by three channels, called Rio Grande, Gambia, and the river Senega. It is 300 miles between the northern and southern channels; and all the country between them is annually overflowed, as Egypt is by the Nile.
