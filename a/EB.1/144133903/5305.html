TRISMEGISTUS

article · 152 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TRISMEGISTUS, an epithet or surname given to one of the two Hermeses, or Mercuries, kings of Thebes in Egypt, who is said to be contemporary with Moses.
