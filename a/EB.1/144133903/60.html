MAIRE

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAIRE, or streights of Le MAIRE, is a passage to Cape Horn, situated between Terra del Fuego in South America, and Staten island; which being discovered by Le Maire, obtained his name.
