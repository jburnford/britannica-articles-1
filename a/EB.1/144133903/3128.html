REVE, REVE, or GREVE

sub_entry · 149 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REVE, REVE, or GREVE, the bailiff of a franchise, or manor, thus called, especially in the west of England. Hence shire-reve, sheriff port-greve, &c.
