SOUND

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOUND, in geography, denotes in general any straight, or inlet, of the sea, between two head-lands. However, the name found is given, by way of eminence, to the straight between Sweden and Denmark, joining the German ocean to the Baltic, being about four miles over.
