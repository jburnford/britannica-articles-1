SWITZERLAND

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SWITZERLAND, or SWISSLAND, called Helvetia by the Romans, is surrounded by the territories of Germany, France, and Italy, being about two hundred and sixteen miles long, and upwards of one hundred miles broad.
