POPAYAN, a province of South America, bounded by Terra-Firma, on the north; by New Granada, on the east; by Peru, on the south; and by the pacific ocean, on the west; situated between 75 and 80 degrees west longitude, and between the equator and 5 degrees of north latitude, being four hundred miles long, and about three hundred broad.
