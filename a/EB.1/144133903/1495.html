OXUS

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OXUS, a river which rises in the mountains on the north of India; and running no th-west, through Ufbee Tartary, afterwards separates Persia from Ufbee Tartary, and falls into the Caspian sea, in 44^{\circ} N. lat.
