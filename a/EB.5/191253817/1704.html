WHEELS

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WHEELS, in a ship, the seaman's term for those brackets which are set up on the captain's deck under the masts; they give the sweep to it, and are so contrived that the cable winding about them may not surge so much as it might otherwise do if the body of the captain's deck were quite round and smooth.
