WEAR

Cross-references: RIVER

WEAR, or WEAR, a great flank or dam in a river, fitted for the taking of fish, or for conveying the stream to a mill. New wears are not to be made, or others altered, to the nuisance of the public, under a certain penalty. See RIVER.
