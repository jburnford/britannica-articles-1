SWIVELS

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SWIVELS, a kind of ring made to turn round in a staple, or other ring. These are used when a ship lies at her moorings; also in tethers for cattle, that they may turn round without unwarping the tether.
