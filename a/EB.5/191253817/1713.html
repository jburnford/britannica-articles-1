WHIP

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WHIP, or Water-Spout, in a ship, a piece of timber, in form of a strong staff, fastened into the helm, for the steer-man, in small ships, to hold in his hand, in order to move the rudder, and direct the ship.
