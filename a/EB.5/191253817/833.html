TOLU, a town of South America in Terra Firma, and in the government of Carthagena; famous for the fine balsam of Tolu, brought into Europe from thence, and produced from a tree like a pine. It is seated on a bay of the North Sea, 60 miles south of Carthagena. W. Long. 72. 55. N. Lat. 9. 40.
