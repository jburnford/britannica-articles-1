VOCAL

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOCAL, something that relates to the voice or speech ; thus vocal music is that set to words, especially verses, and

to be performed by the voice; in contradistinction to instrumental music, composed only for instruments, without singing.
