TUMBRILL, TUMBRILLUM, or Turbichetum

sub_entry · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TUMBRILL, TUMBRILLUM, or Turbichetum, is an engine of punishment, which ought to be in every liberty that hath the view of frank-pledge, for the correction of scolds and unquiet women.
