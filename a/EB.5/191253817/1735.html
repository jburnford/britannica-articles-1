WICKLOW, the capital of a county of the same name, in Ireland; seated on the sea-side, with a narrow harbour, at the mouth of the river Leirrim, over which stands a rock,

instead of a castle, surrounded by a strong wall, 24 miles south of Dublin. W. Long. 6. 7. N. Lat. 52. 55.
