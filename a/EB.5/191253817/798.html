TINNING

Cross-references: FOLIATING

TINNING, the covering or lining any thing with melted tin, or tin reduced to a very fine leaf. Looking-glasses are foliated or tinned with thin plates of beaten tin, the whole bigness of the glass, applied or fastened thereto by means of quicksilver. See FOLIATING of Looking Glasses.
