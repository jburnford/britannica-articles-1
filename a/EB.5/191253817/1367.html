VIOLONCELLO

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VIOLOCELLO, of the Italians, is properly our fifth violin; which is a little bass-violin half the size of the common bass violin, and the strings bigger and longer in proportion; consequently its sound is an octave lower than our bass violin; which has a noble effect in concerts.
