WHITBY

WHITBY, a sea-port town in the North Riding of Yorkshire, seated on the river Esk, near the place where it falls into the sea. The houses are neat, strong, and convenient; the number of inhabitants about 9000. Ship-building is their principal manufacture. W. Long. 0. 24. N. Lat. 54. 30.
