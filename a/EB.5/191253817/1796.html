WOLFSBERG

article · 354 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WOLFSBERG, a town of Germany, in Lower Carinthia, with a castle, on which the district about it depends, which is 20 miles in length, and 10 in breadth. It is seated on the river Lavand, at the foot of a mountain covered with wood, and full of wolves, from whence the town took its name. It is 36 miles east of Clagenfurt. E. Long. 15. o. N. Lat. 46. 16.
