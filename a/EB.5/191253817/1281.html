VERMILION

Cross-references: CHEMISTRY

VERMILION, a very bright and beautiful red colour, composed of quicksilver and sulphur, in great esteem among the ancients under the name of minium; but what goes by the name of minium amongst us, is a preparation of lead, known also by the name of red-lead. See CHEMISTRY, no 1404.
