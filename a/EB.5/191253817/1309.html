VESICATORIUM

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VESICATORIUM, a BLISTER; an application of an acrid nature made to any part of the body, in order to draw a flux of humours to that part, and thus elevate the scarf-skin into a blister.
