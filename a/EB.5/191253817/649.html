THALIA, in botany: A genus of plants belonging to the class of monandria, and order of monogynia; and in the natural system ranging under the 8th order, Scitaminee. The corolla is pentapetalous and undulated; and the drupe has a

bilocular kernels. There is only one species, the geniculata.
