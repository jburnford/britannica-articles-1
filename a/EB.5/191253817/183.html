SUPERPARTIENT PROPORTION

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUPERPARTIENT PROPORTION, or Ratio, is when the greater term contains the less term once, and leaves some number greater than 1 remaining. As the ratio

of 3 to 5, which is equal to that of 1 to 1\frac{2}{3};
of 7 to 10, which is equal to that of 1 to 1\frac{1}{3}, &c.
