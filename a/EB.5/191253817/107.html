SUCCORY, in botany. See CICHOZUM.
SUCCOTH (anc. geog.), a town which lay between the brook Jabbok and the river Jordan, where Jacob fixed his tents. There was another Succoth where the Israelites first encamped after their departure from Rameses towards the Red Sea. Succoth signifies tents.
