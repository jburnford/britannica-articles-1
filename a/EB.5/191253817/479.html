TAVIRA

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TAVIRA, or TAVILA, a considerable town of Portugal, and capital of the province of Algarve, with a handsome castle, and one of the best harbours in the kingdom, defended by a fort. It is seated in a pleasant fertile country, at the mouth of the river Gilão, between Cape Vincent and the Strait of Gibraltar, 100 miles west by north of Cadiz. W. Long. 7. 46. N. Lat. 37. 18.
