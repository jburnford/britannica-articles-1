TANAIS, or DON. See DON.

TANGENT of an ARCH, is a right line drawn perpendicularly from the end of a diameter, passing to one extremity of the arch, and terminated by a right line drawn from the centre through the other end of that arch, and called the front. See GEOMETRY.
