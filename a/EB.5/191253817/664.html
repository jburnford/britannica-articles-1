TURB-Bate

sub_entry · 282 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TURB-Bate (from the Saxon theof, i. e. fur, and bate, compensatis), is the receiving of a man's goods again from a thief, after stolen, or other amends not to prosecute the felon, and to the intent the thief may escape; which is an offence punishable with fine and imprisonment, &c.
