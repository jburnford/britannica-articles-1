SYSTEM, in music, an assemblage of the rules for harmony, deduced from some common principle by which they are reunited; by which their connection one with another is formed; from whence, as from their genuine source, they naturally flow; and to which, if we would account for them, we must have recourse. System, TONIC, ENHARMONIC, HARMONY, INTERVAL, and MUSIC.
