VOLUME

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOLUME, in matters of literature, a book or writing of a just bulk to be bound by itself. The name is derived from the Latin volvere, "to roll up;" the ancient manner of making up books being in rolls of bark or parchment. See Book.
