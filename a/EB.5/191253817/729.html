THRINAX, SMALL JAMAICA FAN-PALM, in botany; a genus of plants belonging to the natural class of palmas, and order of stabilifolia. The calyx is sixdentate; there is no corolla; there are six stamens; the stigma is emarginate, and the berry monospermous. This plant was brought from Jamaica to Kew garden by Dr. William Wright.
