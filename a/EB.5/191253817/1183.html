VALET

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VALET, a French term, used as a common name for all domestic men servants employed in the more servile offices, as grooms, footmen, coachmen, &c. But with us it is only used in the phrase valet de chambre, which is a servant whose office is to dress and undress his master, &c.
