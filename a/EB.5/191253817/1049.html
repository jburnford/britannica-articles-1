TROGLODYTES

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TROGLODYTES, in the ancient geography, a people of Ethiopia, said to have lived in caves under ground. Pomponius Mela gives a strange account of the Troglodytes: he says, they did not so properly speak as shrick; and that they lived on serpents.
