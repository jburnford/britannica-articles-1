ZEALAND

article · 383 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZEALAND, is also a province of the United Netherlands, consisting of eight islands, which lie in the mouth of the river Scheld, bounded by the province of Holland, from which they are separated by a narrow channel on the north; by Brabant on the east; by Flanders, from which they are separated by one of the branches of the Scheld, on the south; and by the German Ocean on the west.
