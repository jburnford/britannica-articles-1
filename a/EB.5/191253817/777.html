TIGRIS

article · 376 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TIGRIS, a river of Asia, which has its source near that of the Euphrates in the mountain Tchildir in Turkomania: afterwards it separates Diarbeck from Erzerum, and Khustan from Iraq-Arabia; and uniting with the Euphrates at Gorno, it falls into the gulf of Bafforah, under the name of Schat el-Arab. This river passes by Diarbekar, Gezira, Mouful, Bagdad, Gorno, and Bafforah.
