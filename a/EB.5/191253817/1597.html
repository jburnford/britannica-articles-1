WARRANTY

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WARRANTY, WARRANTIA, in law, a promise, or covenant, by deed, made by the bargainer for himself and his heirs, to warrant and secure the bargainee and his heirs, against all men, for enjoying the thing agreed on or granted between them.
