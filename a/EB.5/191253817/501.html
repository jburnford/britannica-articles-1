TECTONA, in botany; a genus of plants belonging to the class of pentandria, and order of monogynia. The stigma is dentate; the fruit a dry spongy plum within an inflated calyx; and the nucleus is trilocular. There is only one species, the grandis, Indian oak, or teak wood, which is a native of India.
