WORT

article · 379 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WORT, the infusion of malt, of which beer is made. The uses of this infusion in common affairs are well known. By Dr. McBride it has lately been found to have a strong antiseptic virtue, and to be useful in preventing the scurvy and other diseases to which sailors are liable; which was confirmed by captain Cook in his late voyages. See Means of Preserving the Health of Seamen.
