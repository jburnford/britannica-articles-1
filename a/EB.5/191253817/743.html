THUMB-CAP

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THUMB-CAP, an island in the South Sea, lies about seven leagues north-west of Lagoon island; it is a low, woody island, of a circular form, and not much above a mile in compass. There was no appearance of inhabitants; the land was covered with verdure of many hues.
