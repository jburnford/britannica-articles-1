VINCULUM

article · 329 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VINCULUM, in algebra, a character in form of a line, or stroke drawn over a factor, divisor, or dividend, when compounded of several letters or quantities to connect them, and shows that they are to be multiplied or divided, &c. together by the other term.

Thus d \times a + b - c shows that d is to be multiplied into a + b - c.
