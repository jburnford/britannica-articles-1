TETHYS

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TETHYS, a genus of insects belonging to the class of termes, and order of mollusca. The body is oblong, fleshy, and without feet; the mouth consists of a cylindrical proboscis under the duplicature of a lip; and there are two foramina at the left side of the neck. The species are two, both inhabitants of the ocean.
