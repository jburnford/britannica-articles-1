USUFRUIT

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

USUFRUIT, in the civil law, the use or enjoyment of any lands or tenements; or the right of receiving the fruits and profits of an inheritance, or other thing, without a power of alienating or changing the property thereof.
