ZYMOSIMETER

Cross-references: MEDICINE · PERCH · MUR

ZYMOSIMETER (formed from Zymosis fermentation,
and metre measure), an instrument proposed by Swammer-
dam in his book De Respiratione, wherewith to measure the
degree of fermentation occasioned by the mixture of diffe-
rent matters, and the degree of heat which those matters
acquire in fermenting, as also the heat or temperament of
the blood of animals.
