WARD-Hook

sub_entry · 166 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WARD-Hook, or Wadd-book, in gunnery, a rod or staff, with an iron end turned serpentwise, or like a screw, to draw the wadding out of a gun when it is to be unloaded.
