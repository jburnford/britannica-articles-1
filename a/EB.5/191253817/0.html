STRENGTHENERS, or CORROBORANTS

STRENGTHENERS, or CORROBORANTS, such medicines as add to the bulk and firmness of the solids; and such are all agglutinant and astringent medicines. See MATERIA MEDICA, p. 649. art. 6.
