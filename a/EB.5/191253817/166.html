SUNDA-ISLANDS

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUNDA-ISLANDS, a general name for a cluster of islands in the India Ocean, between 93° and 120° of east longitude, and between 8° north and 8° south latitude. The particular names of the islands are Borneo, Sumatra, Java, Bally, Banca, &c.
