WAIVE

WAIVE, in law, a woman that is put out of the protection of the law. She is called waive, as being forsaken of the law; and not outlaw as a man is; by reason women cannot be of the decenna, and are not sworn in leets to the king, nor to the law, as men are; who are therefore within the law; whereas women are not, and so cannot be outlawed, since they never were within it.
