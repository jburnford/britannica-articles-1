Passion-WEEK

Passion-WEEK, or the Holy WEEK, is the last week in Lent, wherein the church celebrates the mystery of our Saviour's death and passion.

WEEK or WYCK, in geography, a parliament and port-town of Scotland, in the shire of Caithness. W. Long. 3. 2. N. Lat. 58. 30.
