ZABULON

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZABULON (anc. geog.), one of the twelve tribes; bounded on the north by the tribes of Asher and Naphthali; on the east by the sea of Galilee; on the south by the tribe of Issachar or the brook Cifon, which ran between both; on the west by the Mediterranean; so that it touched two seas, or was bimarous.
