THRASYMENUS LACUS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THRASYMENUS LACUS (anc. geog.), a lake of Etruria, n° 199 ris., near Perugia, and not far from the Tiber, fatal to the Romans in the Punic war. Now Il Lago de Perugia on the Ecclesiastical State.
