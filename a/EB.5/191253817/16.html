STRUMPFIA

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STRUMPFIA, in botany; a genus of plants belonging to the class of syngensia, and to the order of monogamia. The calyx is quinquedentate and superior; the corolla is pentapetalous; and the berry monospermous. There is only one species, the maritima.
