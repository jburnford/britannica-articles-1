SUBTENSE

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUBTENSE, formed from sub "under," and tendo "I stretch," in geometry, a right line which is opposite to an angle, and drawn between the two extremities of the arch which measures that angle.
