WOLGAST

article · 318 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WOLGAST, a pretty considerable town of Germany, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and in Pomerania, capital of a territory of the same name, with a castle, and one of the best and largest harbours on the Baltic Sea. It is a well-built place, subject to Sweden, and seated on the river Pfin. E. Long. 14. 4. N. Lat. 54. 1.
