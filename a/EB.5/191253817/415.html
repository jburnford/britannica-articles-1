TAMBOUR

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TAMBOUR is also used for a little box of timber work, covered with a ceiling, within side the porch of certain churches; both to prevent the view of persons passing by, and to keep off the wind, &c. by means of folding doors, &c.
