TICKERA

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TICKERA, a considerable article of merchandise in Fezzan in Africa; it is valued by travellers as a portable and highly salubrious food. It is a preparation of pounded dates, and the meal of Indian corn, formed into a paste, and highly dried in an oven.
