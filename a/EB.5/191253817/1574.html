WALTHERIA, in botany; a genus of plants in the class monodelphia, and order triandria; and in the natural system arranged under the 37th order, Columniferæ.

There is only one pistillum, and the capsule is unilocular, bivalved, and monospermous. There are three species, none of which are natives of Britain.
