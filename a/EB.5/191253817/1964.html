ZOEGEA

ZOEGEA, in botany; a genus of plants of the class syngensia, and order polycardia frustrans. The receptacle is bristly; the pappus setaceous; the corollae of the radius ligulated; the calyx imbricated. There are two species, the capensis and the leptaurea, neither of which are natives of Britain.
