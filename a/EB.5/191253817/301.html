SYMONDSBOROUGH

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SYMONDSBOROUGH, a remarkable large barrow of Flint, near Wellington in Devonshire, in the northern extremity of Hemyock. The common people have a notion that a king called Symon was buried here. The tradition of the country plainly shows that it was the burial-place of some person or persons of eminence.
