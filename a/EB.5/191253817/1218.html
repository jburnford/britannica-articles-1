VAULT

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VAULT, in architecture, an arched roof, so contrived that the stones which form it sustain each other.

Vaults are on many occasions to be preferred to soffits or flat ceilings, as they give a greater height and elevation, and are besides more firm and durable.
