SUBAH

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUBAH, the general name of the viceroyships, or greater governments, into which the Mogul empire was divided, consisting of several provinces. The jurisdiction of a subahdar, the same as subahship, subadarree, or nizamut.
