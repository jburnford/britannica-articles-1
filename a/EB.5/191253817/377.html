TACCA, in botany: A genus of plants belonging to the class of dodecandria, and order of trigynia. The flower is above. The corolla has six petals, and is vaulted. The calyx is hexaphyllous; the fruit a dry, angular, three-celled berry. There is only one species known, the pinna-tifida.
