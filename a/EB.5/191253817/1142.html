TUSCAN Earth

sub_entry · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TUSCAN Earth, a yellowish kind of bole dug in many parts of Italy, particularly about Florence, where there is a stratum of it eight or ten feet thick, at the depth of five or six feet from the surface. It is supposed to have an asfringent property.
