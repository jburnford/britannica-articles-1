TOPOGRAPHY

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TOPOGRAPHY, a description or draught of some particular place, or small tract of land, as that of a city or town, manor or tenement, field, garden, house, cattle, or the like; such as surveyors set out in their plots, or make draughts of, for the information and satisfaction of the proprietors.
