TRICOSANTHES

TRICOSANTHES, in botany: A genus of plants belonging to the class of monocotylia, and order of syngenesia; and in the natural system ranging under the 34th order, Cucurbitaceae. There are four species; only one of which is cultivated in the British gardens, the anguina or snake-gourd, which is a native of China, an annual, and of the cucumber tribe.
