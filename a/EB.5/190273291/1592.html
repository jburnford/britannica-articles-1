ALMACARRON

ALMACARRON, a sea-port town of Spain, in the province of Murcia, at the mouth of the river Guadalantin. It is about twenty miles west of Carthagena, and is remarkable for the prodigious quantity of alum found in its territory. W. Long. 1. 15. N. Lat. 37. 40.
