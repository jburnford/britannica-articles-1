AMPLITUDE

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPLITUDE, in astronomy, an arch of the horizon intercepted between the east or west point and the centre of the sun, or a planet, at its rising or setting; and so is either north and south, or east and west.
