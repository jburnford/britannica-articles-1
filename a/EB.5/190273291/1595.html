ALMADIE

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALMADIE is also the name of a kind of long-boats, fitted out at Calicut, which are eighty feet in length,

and six or seven in breadth. They are exceedingly swift, and are otherwise called cathuri. Almagest
