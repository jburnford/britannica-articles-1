AFFRONTEE

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AFFRONTÉE, in heraldry, an appellation given to animals facing one another on an escutcheon; a kind of bearing which is otherwise called confrontee, and stands opposed to adverse.
