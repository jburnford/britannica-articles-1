ÆQUANA JUGA

ÆQUANA JUGA, (anc. geog.); mountains of Picenum, in the kingdom of Naples, now called Montagna di Sorrento, denominated from the town Æqua, which being destroyed, was replaced by Vicus, now Vico di Sorrento; called also Æquana, Sil. Italicus.
