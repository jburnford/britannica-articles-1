AMPHIPOLIS, a town of Syria, on the Euphrates, built by Seleucus, called by the Syrians Turmeda, (Stephanus): the same with Thapsacus, (Pliny); and supposed to have been only renewed and adorned by Seleucus, because long famous before his time, (Xenophon.)
