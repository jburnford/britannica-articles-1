ALCALA DE GUADIBRA

ALCALA DE GUADIBRA, a small town of Spain, in Andalusia, upon the river Guadeira. Here are an abundance of springs, from whence they convey water to Seville by an aqueduct. W. long. 6. 16. N. lat. 37. 15.
