AFFINITY

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AFFINITY, or ELECTIVE ATTRACTION, are terms used by modern chemists to express that peculiar propensity which different species of matter have to unite and combine with certain other bodies exclusively, or in preference to any other connection.
