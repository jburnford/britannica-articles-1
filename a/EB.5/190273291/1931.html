AMPELUSIA

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPELUSIA, (anc. geog.) a promontory of Mauritania Tingitana, called Cottes by the natives, which is of the same signification with a town of the same name not far from the river Lixus, near the straits of Gibraltar: now Cape Spartel. W. Long. 6. 30. Lat. 36. o.
