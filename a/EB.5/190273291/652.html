ADAMI POMUM

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADAMI POMUM, in anatomy, a protuberance in the fore-part of the throat, formed by the os hyoides. It is thought to be so called upon a strange conceit, that a piece of the forbidden apple, which Adam eat, stuck by the way, and occasioned it.
