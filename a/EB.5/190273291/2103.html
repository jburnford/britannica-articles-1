ANASUS, or ANISUS (anc. geog.), now the Ens, a river of Germany; which, rising on the borders of the

Anathema the territory of Salzburg, then separating Upper Styria from Upper Austria, and washing the town of Ens, falls, at the distance of a mile below it, into the Danube, in a course from south to north.
