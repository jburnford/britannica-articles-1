AMMON, or BEN-AMMI, the son of Lot, begot by this patriarch upon his youngest daughter (Gen. xix. 38.) He was the father of the Ammonites, and dwelt to the east of the Dead Sea, in the mountains of Gilead. See AMMONITES and AMMONITES.
