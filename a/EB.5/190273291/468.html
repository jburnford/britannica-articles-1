ACHMET

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHMET, son of Seerim, has left a book concerning the interpretation of dreams according to the doctrine of the Indians, Persians, and Egyptians, which was translated out of Greek into Latin by Leo Tuscus in 1160. He lived in the 9th century.
