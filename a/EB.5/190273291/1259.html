ALAMODALITY

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALAMODALITY of writing, is defined the accommodation of mental productions, both as to the choice of subject and the manner of treating it, to the genius or taste of the times, in order to render them more acceptable to the readers.
