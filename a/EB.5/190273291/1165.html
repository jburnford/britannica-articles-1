AHAZIAH

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AHAZIAH, king of Judah, the son of Jehoram and Athaliah, succeeded his father in the kingdom of Judah in the year of the world 3119. He walked in the ways of Ahab's house, to which he was allied, his mother being of that family. He reigned only one year, being slain by Jehu the son of Nimshi.
