AGGLUTINANTS

article · 167 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGGLUTINANTS, in pharmacy, a general name for all medicines of a glutinous or viscid nature; which, by adhering to the solids, contribute greatly to repair their loss.
