ABYSS

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABYSS, in a general sense, denotes something profound, and, as it were, bottomless. The word is originally Greek, abysos; compounded of the primitive ab, and byssos, q. d. without a bottom.
