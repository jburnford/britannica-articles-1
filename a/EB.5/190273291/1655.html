ALPISTE

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALPISTE, or ALPIA, a sort of feed used to feed birds with, especially when they are to be nourished for breeding. The alpiste feed is of an oval figure, of a pale yellow, inclining to an isabel colour, bright and glossy. It is an article of the corn-clandlers and feed-mens trade.
