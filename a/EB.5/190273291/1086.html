AGISTOR

AGISTOR, or AGISTATOR, an officer belonging to forests, who has the care of cattle taken in to be grazed, and levies the moneys due on that account. They are generally called quest-takers or gift-takers.
No 6.

and are created by letters-patent. Each royal forest has four agistors.
