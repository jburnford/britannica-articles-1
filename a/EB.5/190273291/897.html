ÆLIPONS

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ÆLIPONS (anc. geog.), one of the fortresses near the wall or rampart, or, in the words of the Notitia, through the line of the hither wall; built, as is thought, by Adrian*. Now Porteland, (Camden), in Northumberland, between Newcastle and Morpeth. *See Adrian (emperor.)
