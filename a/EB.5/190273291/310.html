ACARON

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACARON, or ACCARON, a town of Palestine, called Ekron in scripture. It was the boundary of the Philistines to the north; stood at some distance from the sea, near Bethshemeth; and was famous for the idol of Baalzebub.
