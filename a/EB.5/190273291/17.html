ABACÆNUM

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABACÆNUM (anc. geog.), a town of Sicily, whose ruins are supposed to be those lying near Trippi, a citadel on an high and steep mountain not far from Messina. The inhabitants were called Abacenini.
