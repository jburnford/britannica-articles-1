AMPHILOCHIA

article · 329 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPHILOCHIA (anc. geog.), the territory of the city of Argos in Acarnania; Amphilocheum, (Thucydides); called Amphilochei (from the people,) in the lower age, (Stephanus.) A town also of Spain, in Galicia, built by Teucer, and denominated from Amphilochus one of his companions, (Strabo): now Orense. W. Long. 8. 20. Lat. 42. 36.
