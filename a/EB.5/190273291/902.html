ÆMILIUS

ÆMILIUS (Paulus), the son of Lucius Paulus, who was killed at the battle of Cannæ, was twice consul. In his first consulate he triumphed over the Ligurians; and in the second subdued Perseus king of Macedonia, and reduced that country to a Roman province, on which he obtained the surname of Macedonicus. He returned to Rome loaded with glory, and triumphed for three days. He died 168 years before Christ.
