AMETHYST

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMETHYST, in heraldry, a term for the purple colour in the coat of a nobleman, in use with those who blazon with precious stones, instead of metals and colours. This, in a gentleman's escutcheon, is called Purple; and in those of sovereign princes, Mercury.
