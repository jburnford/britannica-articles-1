ALLOBROGES

ALLOBROGES (Inscriptions, Livy, Velleius, Flavius); from Allobrox (Horace): a people of Gallia Narbonensis, situated between the rivers Isara and Rhodanus, and the Lacus Lemanus; commanded by Cicero for their fidelity, discommended by Horace on account of their fondness for novelty.
