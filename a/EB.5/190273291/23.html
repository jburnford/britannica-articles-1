ABACTORS

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABACTORS, or ABACTORES, a name given to those who drive away, or rather steal, cattle by herds, or great numbers at once; and are therefore very properly distinguished from surers, or thieves.
