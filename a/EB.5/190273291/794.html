ADRAMMELECH

article · 434 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADRAMMELECH, one of the gods of the inhabitants of Sepharvaim, who were settled in the country of Samaria, in the room of those Israelites who were carried beyond the Euphrates. The Sepharvaimites made their children pass through the fire, in honour of this idol and another called Anamelech. It is supposed, that Adrammelech meant the sun, and Anamelech the moon: the first signifies the magnificent king; the second the gentle king.
