ADIAPHOROUS

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADIAPHOROUS, ADIAPHOROUS, a name given by Mr Boyle to a kind of spirit distilled from tartar and some other vegetable bodies; and which is neither acid, vinous, nor urinous; but in many respects different from any other sort of spirit.
