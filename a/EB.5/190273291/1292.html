ALBANA

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALBANA (anc. geog.), a sea-port town of Albania, on the Caspian sea, between the rivers Casius and Albanus; now called Bachu, or Bachy, giving name to the Caspian sea, viz. Mar de Babu. E. Long. 49. 0. Lat. 40. 0.
