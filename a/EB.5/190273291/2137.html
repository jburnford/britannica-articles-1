ANCIENTY

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANCIENTY, in some ancient statutes, is used for eldership or seniority. The elder sister can demand no more than her other sisters, beside the chief mesne, by reason of her ancienty. This word is used in the statute of Ireland, 14 Hen. III.
