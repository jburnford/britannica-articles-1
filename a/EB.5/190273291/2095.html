ANASSUS

article · 371 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANASSUS, or ANAXUS (anc. geog.), a river in the territory of Venice, (Pliny); now the Piave, which rising from the mountains of Tyrol, not far from the borders of Carinthia, runs from north to south, through the territories of Cadorina, Belluno, Feltre, and, after running from west to east, through Trevisi, falls into the Adriatic, 13 miles to the south-east of Venice.
