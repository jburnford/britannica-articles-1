ABCOURT

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABCOURT, a town near St Germain, four leagues from Paris. Here is a brisk chalybeate water, impregnated with fixed air and the fossil alkali; and resembling the waters of Spa and Ilmington.
