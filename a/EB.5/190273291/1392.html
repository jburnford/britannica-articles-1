ALCOVE

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCOVE, among builders, a recess, or part of a chamber separated by an estrade, or partition of columns, and other corresponding ornaments, in which is placed a bed of slate, and sometimes seats to entertain company. These alcoves are frequent in Spain; and the bed is raised two or three ascents, with a rail at the foot.
