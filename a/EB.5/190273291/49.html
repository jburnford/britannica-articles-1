ABARTICULATION

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABARTICULATION, in anatomy, a species of articulation admitting of a manifest motion; called also Diarthrosis, and Dearticulation, to distinguish it from that sort of articulation which admits of a very obscure motion, and is called Synarthrosis.
