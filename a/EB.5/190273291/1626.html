ALMUCANTARI-STAFF

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALMUCANTARI-STAFF, is an instrument usually made of pear-tree or box, having an arch of 15 degrees; used to take observations of the sun, about the time of its rising and setting; in order to find the amplitude, and consequently the variation of the compass.
