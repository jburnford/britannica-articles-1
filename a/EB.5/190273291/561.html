ACROCERAUNIA

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACROCERAUNIA, or MONTES CERAUNII (anc. geog.), mountains running out into the sea (so called from their being often thunderstruck), separating the Ionian sea from the Adriatic; where Illyria ends and Epirus begins; now called Monti della Ghibera.
