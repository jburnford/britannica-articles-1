ACETARY

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACETARY, Nehemiah Grew, in his anatomy of plants, applies this term to a pulpy substance in certain fruits, e. g. the pear, which is inclosed in a congeries of small calculeous bodies towards the base of the fruit, and is always of an acid taste.
