ADVENTUM INSPECIENDUM

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADVENTUM INSPECIENDUM, in law, a writ by which a woman is to be searched whether she be with child by a former husband, on her withholding of lands from the next, failing issue of her own body.
