ALTERCATION

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALTERCATION, a debate or contest between two friends or acquaintance. The word comes from altercari, which anciently signified to converse or hold discourse together.—Thus, we say, They never come to an open quarrel, but there is continually some little altercation or reel.
