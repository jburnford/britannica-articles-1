AHOUAI

article · 368 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AHOUAL, in botany, a synonyme and also the trivial name of a species of CEREARIA.

A-HULI, in the sea-language, the situation of a ship when all her sails are furled on account of the violence of the storm, and when having lashed her helm on the lee-side, she lies nearly with her side to the wind and sea, her head being somewhat inclined to the direction of the wind.
