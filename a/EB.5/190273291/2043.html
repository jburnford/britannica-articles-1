ANAGOGY

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANAGOGY, or ANAGOGIE, among ecclesiastical writers, the elevation of the mind to things celestial and eternal.—It is particularly used, where words, in their natural or primary meaning, denote something sensible, but have a further view to something spiritual or invisible.
