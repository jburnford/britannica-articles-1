LÆ

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LÆ, (Strabo); AMANI PORTÆ, (Pliny): straits or defiles in mount Amanus, through which Darius entered Cilicia; at a greater distance from the sea than the Pylæ Ciliciæ or Syriæ, through which Alexander passed.
