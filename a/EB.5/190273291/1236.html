AKOND

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AKOND, an officer of justice in Persia, who takes cognizance of the causes of orphans and widows; of contracts, and other civil concerns. He is the head of

the school of law, and gives lectures to all the subaltern officers; he has his deputies in all the courts of the kingdom, who, with the second sadra, make all contracts.
