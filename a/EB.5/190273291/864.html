EDILITIUM

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EDILITIUM edictum, among the Romans, was that whereby a remedy was given a buyer, in case a vicious or unsound beast, or slave, was sold him. It was called edilitium, because the preventing of frauds in sales and contracts belonged especially to the curule ediles.
