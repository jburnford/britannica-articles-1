ANCYLOSIS

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANCYLOSIS, in surgery, implies a distortion or stiffness of the joints, caused by a settlement of the humours, or a distension of the nerves, and therefore remedies of a mollifying and relaxing nature are required.
