ABISHAI

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABISHAI, son of Zeruah, and brother to Joab, was one of the celebrated warriors who flourished in the reign of David: he killed with his own hand 300 men, with no other weapon but his lance; and slew a Philistine giant, the iron of whose spear weighed 300 shekels. 1 Sam. xxvi. 2 Sam. xxiii.
