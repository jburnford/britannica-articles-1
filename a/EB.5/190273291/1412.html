ALDROVANDA

ALDROVANDA, in botany, a genus of the pentandria order, belonging to the pentagynia class of plants; of which there is but one species. The calyx is divided into five parts; the petals are five; and the capsule has five valves, with ten seeds. It is a native of Italy and the Indies; and has no English name.
