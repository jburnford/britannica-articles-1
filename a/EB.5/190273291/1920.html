AMORÆANS

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMORÆANS, a sect or order of gemaric doctors, or commentators on the Jerusalem Talmud. The Amoræans succeeded the Mithenic doctors. They subsisted 250 years; and were succeeded by the Schureans.
