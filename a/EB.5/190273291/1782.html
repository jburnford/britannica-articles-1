AMATORII MUSCULI

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMATORII MUSCULI, in anatomy, a term sometimes used for the obliquus superior and obliquus inferior muscles of the eye, as these muscles assist in ogling or drawing the eye sideways.
