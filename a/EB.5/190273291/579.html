Acrothymion ACROTHYMION

sub_entry · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Acrothymion ACROTHYMION, from akro, extreme, and thyme, thyme. A sort of wart described by Celsus as hard, rough, with a narrow basis and broad top; the top is of the colour of thyme, it easily splits and bleeds. This tumour is also called thymus.
