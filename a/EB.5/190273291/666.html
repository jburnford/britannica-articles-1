ADCRESCENTES

article · 165 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ACCENSI

ADCRESCENTES, among the Romans, denoted a kind of soldiery, entered in the army, but not yet put on duty; from these the standing forces were recruited. See ACCENSI.
