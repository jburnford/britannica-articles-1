ANECDOTES

article · 323 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANECDOTES is also an appellation given to such works of the ancients as have not yet been published. In which sense, M. Muratori gives the name Anecdota Graeca to several writings of the Greek fathers, found in the libraries, and first published by him.—F. Martene has given a Theſaurus Anecdotarum Novus, in folio, 5 vols.
