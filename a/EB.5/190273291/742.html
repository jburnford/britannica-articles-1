ADJUNCT is also used to signify a colleague, or some person associated with another as an assistant.

Adjunct Gods, or Adjuncts of the Gods, among the Romans, were a kind of inferior deities, added as assistants to the principal ones, to ease them in their functions. Thus, to Mars was adjoined Bellona and Nemesis; to Neptune, Salacia; to Vulcan, the Cabiri; to the Good Genius, the Lares; to the Evil, the Lemures, &c.
