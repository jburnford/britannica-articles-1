ADATAIS, ADATSI, or ADATYS

sub_entry · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADATAIS, ADATSI, or ADATYS, in commerce, a mullin or cotton-cloth, very fine and clear, of which the piece is ten French cils long, and three quarters broad. It comes from the East-Indies; and the finest is made at Bengal.
