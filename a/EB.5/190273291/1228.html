AJUTAGE

Cross-references: FOUNTAIN

AJUTAGE, or ADJUTAGE, a kind of tube fitted to the mouth of the vessel through which the water of a fountain is to be played. To the different form and structure of ajutages, is owing to the great variety of fountains. See FOUNTAIN and HYDROSTATICS.
