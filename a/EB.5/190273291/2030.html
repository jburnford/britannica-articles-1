ANACROSIS

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANACROSIS, in antiquity, denotes a part of the Pythian song, wherein the combat of Apollo and Python are described.—The anacrosis was the first part, and contained the preparation to the fight.
