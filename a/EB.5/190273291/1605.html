ALMEDIA

ALMEDIA, a frontier-town of Portugal, in the province of Tralos Montes, on the confines of Leon, where there was a very brisk action between the French and Portuguese in 1663; 17 miles N. W. of Ciudad Rodrigo. W. Long. 7. 10. N. Lat. 40. 41.
