ENUS (anc. geog.), now the Inn, a river of Germany, which, rising in the country of the Grisons, out of the Alps, in the district called Gottes-haus-punt, runs through the Grisons, the county of Tyrol, the duchy of Bavaria, and through Passau into the Danube.
