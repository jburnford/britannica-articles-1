ALEIUS CAMPUS

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALEIUS CAMPUS (anc. geog.), a plain in Cilicia, on this side the river Pyramus, near the mountain Chimera, famous for Bellerophon's wandering and perishing there, after being thrown off Pegasus; which is the reason of the appellation.
