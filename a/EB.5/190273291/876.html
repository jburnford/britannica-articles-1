EGINA

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EGINA, in fabulous history, the daughter of Æsopos, king of Boeotia, was beloved by Jupiter, who debauched her in the similitude of a lambent flame, and then carried her from Epidaurus to a desert island called Oenopia, which afterwards obtained her own name.
