ANAMIM

ANAMIM, the second son of Mizraim (Gen. x. 13). Anamim, if we may credit the paraphrase Jonathan the son of Uzziel, peopled the Marcotis; or the Pentapolis of Cyrene, according to the paraphrase of Jerusalem. Bochart is of opinion that these Anamims were the people that dwelt in the parts adjacent to the temple of Jupiter Ammon, and in the Nasamonitis. Calmet thinks the Amnians and Garamantes to be descended from Anamim.
