AGER PICENUS

article · 390 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGER PICENUS, or Picenum, (anc. geog.) a territory of Italy to the south-east of Umbria, reaching from the Apennine to the Adriatic. The people are called Picentes (Cicero, Livy), distinct from the Picentini on the Tuscan sea, though called by Greek writers Πικεντι. This name is said to be from the bird Picus, under whose conduct they removed from the Sabines, of whom they were a colony.
