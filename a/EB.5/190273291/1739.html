ALWAIDII

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALWAIDII, a sect of Mahometans who believe all great crimes to be unpardonable.—The Alwaidii stand in opposition to the Miorgii. They attribute less efficacy to the true belief in the salvation of men than the rest of the Musselmans.
