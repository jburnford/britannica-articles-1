AMENTUM

article · 309 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMENTUM, in Roman antiquity, a thong tied about the middle of a javelin or dart, and fastened to the forefinger, in order to recover the weapon as soon as it was discharged. The ancients made great use of the amentum, thinking it helped to enforce the blow. It also denotes a latchet that bound their sandals.
