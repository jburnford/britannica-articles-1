AETIUS

article · 377 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AETIUS, one of the most zealous defenders of Arianism, was born in Syria, and flourished about the year 336. After being servant to a grammarian, of

whom he learned grammar and logic, he was ordained deacon, and at length bishop, by Eudoxus patriarch of Constantinople. St. Epiphanius has preserved 47 of his propositions against the Trinity. His followers were called AETIANS.
