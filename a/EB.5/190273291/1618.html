LORD ALMONER

sub_entry · 385 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LORD ALMONER, or LORD HIGH ALMONER, of England, is an ecclesiastical officer, generally a bishop, who has the forfeiture of all deadlands, and the goods of feods de se, which he is to distribute among the poor. He has also, by virtue of an ancient custom, the power of giving the first dish from the king's table to whatever poor person he pleases, or, instead of it, an alms in money.
