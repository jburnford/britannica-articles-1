ALCMEENA

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCMEENA, the daughter of Electryo king of Mycænæ, and wife of Amphitryon. Jupiter putting on the shape of her husband while he was abroad in the wars, begot Hercules upon her: he made that night as long as three ordinary ones.
