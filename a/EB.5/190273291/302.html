ACANTHOPTERYGIOUS FISHES

article · 125 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACANTHOPTERYGIOUS FISHES, a term used by Linnaeus and others for those fishes whose back-fins are hard, osseous, and prickly.
