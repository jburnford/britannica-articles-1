ABOMASUS

Cross-references: COMPARATIVE ANATOMY

ABOMASUS, ABOMASUM, or ABOMASIUS, names of the fourth stomach of ruminating animals. It is in the abomasus of calves and lambs that the runnet or curdling is formed wherewith milk is curdled. See COMPARATIVE ANATOMY.
