ALCAZAR LEGUER

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCAZAR LEGUER, a town of Africa, in the kingdom of Fez, and in the province of Ilabat. It was taken by Alphonso, king of Portugal, in 1468; but soon after that, it was abandoned to the Moors. It is seated on the coast of the Straits of Gibraltar. W. Long. 5. 30. N. Lat. 38. 0.
