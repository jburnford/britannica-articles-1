ADOSCOLATION

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADOSCOLATION, a term used by Dr. Grew, to imply a kind of impregnation, without intromission; and in this manner he supposes the impregnation of plants is effected by the falling of the farina fecundans on the pistil.
