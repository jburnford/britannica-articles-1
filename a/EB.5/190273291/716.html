ADESSENARIANS, ADESSENARI, in church-history, a sect of Christians who hold the real presence of Christ's body in the eucharist, though not by way of transubstantiation. They differ considerably as to this presence; some holding that the body of Christ is in the bread; others that it is about the bread; and others that it is under the bread.
