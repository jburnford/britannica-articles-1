ALTENBURG

ALTENBURG, or OWAR, a small but strong town of Hungary, seated in a marsh, with wide streets. It is near the river Danube, and is surrounded with deep ditches. It is 15 miles south of Presburg, 40 south-east of Vienna, and 65 south-west of Buda. E. Long. 17. 56. N. Lat. 44. 0.
