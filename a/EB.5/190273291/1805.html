AMBIGENÆ OVES

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMBIGENÆ OVES, in the heathen sacrifices, an appellation given to such ewes as, having brought forth twins, were sacrificed together with their two lambs, one on each side. We find them mentioned among other sacrifices to Juno.
