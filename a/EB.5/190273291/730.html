ADJAZZO, ADRAZZO, or AJACCIO, in geography,

a handsome town and castle of Corsica in the Mediterranean, with a bishop's see, and a good harbour. It is populous, and fertile in wine. It is 27 miles S. W. of Corte. E. long. 41. 54 lat. 38. 5.
