ABEL KERAMIM

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL KERAMIM, or VINCARUM, beyond Jordan, in the county of the Ammonites, where Jephthah defeated them, seven miles distant from Philadelphia, abounding in vines, and hence the name. It was also called Abela.
