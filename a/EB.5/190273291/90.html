ABCEDARY

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABCEDARY, or ABCEDARIAN, an epithet given to compositions, the parts of which are disposed in the order of the letters of the alphabet: thus we say, Abcedarian psalms, lamentations, hymns, &c.
