ABASSI

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABASSI, or ABASSIS, a silver coin current in Persia, equivalent in value to a French livre, or tenpence halfpenny Sterling. It took its name from Schah Abas II. king of Persia, under whom it was struck.
