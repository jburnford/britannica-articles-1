AARON HARISCHON

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AARON HARISCHON, a learned rabbi and CARAITE in the 15th century, wrote an Hebrew grammar, printed at Constantinople in 1581; probably the same with Aaron the carait, who wrote a commentary on the five books of Moses, which is in MS. in the French king's library.
