ACASTUS

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACASTUS, in classic history, the son of Pelias king of Thessaly, and one of the most famous hunters of his time, married Hippolyta, who falling desperately in love with Peleus her son-in-law, and he refusing to gratify her wishes, she accused him to her husband of a rape; on which he slew them both.
