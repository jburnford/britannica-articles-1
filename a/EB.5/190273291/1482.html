ALGAGIOLA

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALGAGIOLA, a small sea-port town in the island of Corfica, fortified with walls and bastions. It was almost destroyed by the malcontents in 1731, but has since been repaired. E. Long. 9. 45. N. Lat. 42. 20.
