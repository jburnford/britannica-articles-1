AGNEL

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGNEL, an ancient French gold coin, first struck under the reign of St. Louis, worth about twelve sols six deniers. The agnel is also called sometimes monnaie d'or, and agnel d'or. The denomination is supposed to have arisen from the figure of a lamb, agnus, or sheep, struck on one side.
