AHUN

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AHUN, a town in France, in the Upper Marche and generality of Moulins, and is a royal jurisdiction. It is seated on the river Creuse, near a Benedictine abbey of the same name, eight miles south-east of Guerret, 30 north-east of Lomages, and 55 south-east of Moulins. E. Long. 2. 8. N. Lat. 49. 5.
