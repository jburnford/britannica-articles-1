ADUACA

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADUACA, or ATUACA, anciently a large and famous city of the Tungri; now a small and inconsiderable village, called Tongeren, in the bishopric of Liege, to the north-west of the city of Liege, in the territory of Haspengow, on the rivulet Jecker, that soon after falls into the Mese. E. Long. 5. 52. Lat. 50. 54.
