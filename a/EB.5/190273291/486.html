ACIDALUS

article · 162 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACIDALUS, a fountain in Orchomenus, a city of Boeotia, in which the Graces, who are sacred to Venus, bathed. Hence the epithet Acidalia, given to Venus, (Virgil.)
