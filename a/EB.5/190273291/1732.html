ALVEARIUM

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALVEARIUM also signifies a bee-hive. The word is formed of alveus, a "channel or cavity;" in allusion to the alveoli, or cells in bee-hives.

Some of the ancients use also the word alvearium for a bee-house, more usually called among us apiary.
