ALSTON-MORE

ALSTON-MORE, a town in Cumberland, seated on a hill, at the bottom of which runs the river Tyne, with a stone bridge over it. Near this place is plenty of lead-ore. W. Long, 2. 4. N. Lat. 54. 45.
