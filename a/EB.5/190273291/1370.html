ALCAZER

ALCAZER, a town of Spain, in New Castile, seated on the river Guardamana, which has a fortress on a high hill for its defence, and lies in a very fruitful country. It is 100 miles north-west of Carthagenia. W. Long. 2. 10. N. Lat. 38. 15.
