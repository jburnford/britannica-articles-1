ACAMANTIS

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACAMANTIS (the ancient name of the island of Cyprus), taken from one of its promontories situated to the west, and called Acamas. Teos in Ionia was also called thus from Acamus the founder.
