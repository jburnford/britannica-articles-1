ALBINTEMELIUM, ALBINTIMILIUM, (Tacitus;) or at full length, ALBIUM INTEMELIUM, (Pliny, Strabo;) now Vintimiglia, situated in the south-west of the territory of Genoa, near the borders of the county of Nice, with a port on the Mediterranean, at the mouth of the rivulet Rotta, almost about half-way between Monaco and S.
