ANDES, a hamlet of Mantua in Italy, the birth-place of Virgil. Hence the epithet Andinus (Silius Italicus). Now called Pietola, two miles to the west of Mantua.

ANDETRIUM; ANDRETIUM (Strabo); ANDRECIUM, or ANDRECIUM (Ptolemy): An inland town of Dalmatia. The genuine name is Andetrium (Inscription). It is described as situated near Salone, on a naturally strong and inaccessible rock, surrounded with deep valleys, with rapid torrents; from which it appears to be the citadel now called Clissa. E. Long. 17. 46. Lat. 43. 20.
