ABU SAID

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABU SAID, (Ebn Aljaptu), sultan of the Moguls, succeeded his father anno 717 of the hegira. He was the last monarch of the race of Jenghizkhan; and after his death, which happened the same year that Tamerlane was born, the empire was made a scene of blood and desolation.
