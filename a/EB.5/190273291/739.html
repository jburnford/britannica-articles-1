ADJUNCT

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADJUNCT, among philosophers, signifies something added to another, without being any necessary part of it. Thus water absorbed by cloth or a sponge, is an adjunct, but no necessary part of either of these substances.
