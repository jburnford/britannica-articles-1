ALETUM, or ALETA

sub_entry · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALETUM, or ALETA, (anc. geog.) a town of Celtic Gaul, now extinct. From its ruins arose St Malo, in Brittany, at the distance of a mile. Its ruins are called Guich Aleth in the British.
