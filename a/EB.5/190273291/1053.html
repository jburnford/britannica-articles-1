AGENDA

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGENDA, among ecclesiastical writers, denotes the service or office of the church. We meet with agenda matutina & vespertina, "morning and evening prayers;" agenda diei, "the office of the day," whether feast or fast day; agenda mortuorum, called also simply agenda, "the service of the dead."
