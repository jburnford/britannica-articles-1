AGONISTARCHA

article · 367 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGONISTARCHA, from agon "combat," and arche "chief," in antiquity, seems to have been much the same with agonthetes; though some suggest a difference, making it the office of the former to preside at and direct the private exercises of the athlete, which they went through by way of practice, before they made their appearance on the public theatres or amphitheatres.
