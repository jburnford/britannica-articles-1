ALICATA, a town of Sicily, remarkable for corn and good wine. It was plundered by the Turks in 1543; and is seated on a sort of peninsula near the sea, twenty-two miles S. E. of Girgenti. E. Long. 15. 20. N. Lat. 37. 11.
