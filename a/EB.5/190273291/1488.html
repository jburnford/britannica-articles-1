ALGOL

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALGOL, a fixed star of the third magnitude, called Medusa's Head, in the constellation Perseus; its longitude is 21^{\circ} 50' 42'' of Taurus, and its latitude 23^{\circ} 23' 47'' north; according to Flamsteed's catalogue. For an account of its changes, period, and other circumstances, see ASTRONOMY (Index).
