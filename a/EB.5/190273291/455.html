ACHERON

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHERON, or ACHEROS, a river of the Brutii in Italy, running from east to west; where Alexander king of Epirus was slain by the Lucani, being deceived by the oracle of Dodona, which bid him beware of Acheron.
