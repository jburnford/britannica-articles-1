AGGREGATION

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGGREGATION, in physics, a species of union whereby several things which have no natural dependence or connection with one another are collected together, so as in some sense to constitute one. Thus, a heap of sand, or a mass of ruins, are bodies by aggregation.
