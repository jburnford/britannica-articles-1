AMBETTUWAY

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMBETTUWAY, in botany, a barbarous name of a tree, the leaves of which, when boiled in wine, are said to create an appetite, and is used by the people in Guinea with that intention.
