ABBEYBOYLE, a town of Ireland, in the county of Roscommon, and province of Connaught. W. Long. 8. 32. Lat. 56. 54. It is remarkable for an old abbey.

Abbeyholm ABBAYHOLM, a town in Cumberland; so called from an abbey built there by David king of Scots. It stands on an arm of the sea. W. Long. 2. 38. Lat. 54. 45.
