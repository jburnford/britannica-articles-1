AMASIA

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMASIA, the name of the northern division of Lesser Asia, lying on the south shore of the Euxine sea in Natolia. It takes its name from Amasia the capital, mentioned in the preceding article.
