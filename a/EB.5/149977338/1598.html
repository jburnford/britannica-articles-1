AVERSION

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVERSION, according to Lord Kames, is opposed to affection, and not to desire, as it commonly is. We
VOL. II. PART II.

have an affection to one person; we have an aversion to another: the former disposes us to do good to its object, the latter to do ill.
