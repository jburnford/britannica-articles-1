ASTRANTIA

ASTRANTIA, MASTERWORT: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 45th order, Umbellatæ. The involucrum is lanceolated, open, equal, and coloured. The species are two,

the major and minor, both natives of the Alps, and possessing no remarkable properties.
