ANTITACTE

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTITACTE, in church history, a branch of Gnostics, who held, that God was good and just, but that a creature had created evil; and consequently that it is our duty to oppose this author of evil, in order to avenge God of his adversary.
