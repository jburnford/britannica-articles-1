BALK

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALK, among builders, is sometimes used for the summer-beam of a house; sometimes for the poles and rafters which support the roofs of barns, &c.; and sometimes for the beams used in making sea-holds.
