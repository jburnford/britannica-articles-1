BALTIMORA, in botany: A genus of the polygamia necessaria order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants. The receptaculum is chaffy; there is no pappus; the calyx is cylindrical and polyphyllous; and the ray of the corolla is quinqueflorous. There is but one species, the recta, a native of Maryland.
