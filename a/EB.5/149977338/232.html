ANTHROPOMORPHITES

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTHROPOMORPHITES, in church-history, a sect of ancient heretics, who, taking every thing spoken of God in Scripture in a literal sense, particularly that passage of Genesis in which it is said God made man after his own image, maintained that God had a human shape. They are likewise called Audens, from Audeus their leader.
