ANTENCELEMA

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTENCELEMA, in oratory, is where the whole defence of the person accused turns on criminalising the accuser. Such is the defence of Orestes, or the oration for Milo: Occisus est, sed latro. Exsectus, sed raptor.
