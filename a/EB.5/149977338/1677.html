AVOWEE

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVOWEE, one who has a right to present to a benefice. He is thus called in contradistinction to those who only have the lands to which the advowson belongs for a term of years, or by virtue of intrusion or disseisin.
