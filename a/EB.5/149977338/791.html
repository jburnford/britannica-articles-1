ARCOS

article · 419 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCOS, a strong city of Andalusia, in Spain, seated on a high craggy rock, at the bottom of which runs the Gaudeloto. Its strength lies not only in its situation, but in the works erected for its defence, and it is inaccessible on every side but one. The governor resides in an old castle, from whence there is a delightful prospect, which extends very far into the neighbouring country. W. Long. 2. 10. N. Lat. 36. 40.
