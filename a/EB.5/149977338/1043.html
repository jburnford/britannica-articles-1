ARPENT

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARPENT, signifies an acre or furlong of ground; and according to the old French account in domestic book, 100 perches make an arpent. The most ordinary acre, called P arpent de France, is 100 perches square: but some account is but half an acre.
