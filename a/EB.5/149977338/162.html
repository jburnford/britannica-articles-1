ANTARES

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTARES, in astronomy, the name of a star of the first magnitude, called also the scorpion's heart. Its longitude is 60^{\circ} 13' 14'' of Sagittarius; and its latitude 4^{\circ} 31' 26'' S.
