BALAGATE

article · 567 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALAGATE, a province of the Mogul empire, and the largest of the three that compose the kingdom of Dekkan. It has Kandish and Barar to the north, Telinga to the east, Baglana with part of Guzerat to the west, and Visiapur to the south. It is a fruitful and pleasant country, abounding with cotton and sugar. Here they have sheep without horns; but so strong, that when bridled and saddled they will carry boys of ten years of age. Its present capital is Aurangabad, but formerly was Dowlet Abad; and from the latter the whole province is sometimes called Dowlet Abad.
