ARCANUM (anc. geog.), a villa of Q. Cicero, Tullius's brother, in Latium, (Cicero). Now Arce, in the Terra di Lavoro, in the kingdom of Naples, on the borders of the Campagnia di Roma, on the river Melis, between Arpinum and Aquinum.
