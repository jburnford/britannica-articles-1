BACHELOR

article · 369 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BACHELOR is also a name given in the six companies of merchants at Paris to the elders, and such as having served the offices, have a right to be called by the masters and wardens to be present with them, and assist them in some of the functions, particularly in what relates to the chef-d'oeuvres or master pieces, of such as are candidates for being admitted masters.
