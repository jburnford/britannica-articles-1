ANTISAGOGUE

Cross-references: CONCESSION

ANTISAGOGUE, in rhetoric, a figure differing little from that called concession. The following passage from Cicero is an instance of it: Difficilis ratio belli gerendi; at plena fidei, plena pietatis; et si dicas, magnus labor, multa pericula proponuntur; at gloria ex his immortalis est consecutura. See CONCESSION.
