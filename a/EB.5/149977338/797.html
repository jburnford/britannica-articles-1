ARCTURUS

article · 374 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCTURUS, in astronomy, a fixed star, of the first magnitude, in the constellation of Arctophylax, or Bootes. The word is formed of αρκτος, bear, and ουρα, tail, q. d. bear's tail, as being very near it. This star

Arcuatio. star was known to the ancients, as in the following verse of Virgil:

Arcturum, pluviasque Hyades, geminosque Triones,

See also Job ix. 9. xxxviii. 32.
