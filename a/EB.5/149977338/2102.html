BAMIER

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAMIER, the name of a plant common in Egypt. It produces a pyramidal husk, with several compartments of the colour of a lemon, and filled with musky seeds. This husk dressed with meat is a wholesome food, and has a very agreeable flavour. The Egyptians make great use of it in their ragouts.
