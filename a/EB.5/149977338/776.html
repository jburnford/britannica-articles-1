ARCHPRESBYTER

Cross-references: PRESBYTER

ARCHPRESBYTER, or ARCH-PRIEST, a priest established in some dioceses with a superiority over the rest. He was anciently chosen out of the college of presbyters at the pleasure of the bishop. These arch-presbyters were much of the same nature with deans in the cathedral churches, as the college of presbyters answers to the chapter. See PRESBYTER.
