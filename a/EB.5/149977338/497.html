APONOGETON

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APONOGETON: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the heptandria class of plants, which has no English name. The calyx is an oblong omentum; there is no corolla; and the capsule is three-seeded. There are two species, natives of the Indies.
