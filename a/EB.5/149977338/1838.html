BABYLONIAN, BABYLONIUS

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BABYLONIAN, BABYLONIUS, is used in some ancient writers for an astrologer, or any thing related to astrology. Hence Babylonica cura, the art of casting nativities; and numeri Babylonii, the computation of astrologers.
