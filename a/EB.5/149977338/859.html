ARGENTON

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGENTON, a town and county of France, in the duchy of Berry, divided into two by the river Creuse. Here was formerly a castle; but it was demolished by Lewis XIV. E. Long. 1. 38. N. Lat. 49. 30.
