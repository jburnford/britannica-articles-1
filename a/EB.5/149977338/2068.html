BALLOON, BALLON, OR BALLOT

article · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALLOON, BALLON, OR BALLOT, in the French glass-trade, signifies a certain quantity of glass-plates, smaller or greater according to their quality. The balloon of white-glass contains 25 bundles, of six plates per bundle; but the balloon of coloured glass is only of 12½ bundles, and of three plates to a bundle.
