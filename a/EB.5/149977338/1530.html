AUBAGNE

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUBAGNE, a town of Provence in France, situated on the river Veaune, on the road from Marseilles to Toulon. The flates sometimes hold their sessions at this place. E. Long. 5. 52. N. Lat. 43. 17.
