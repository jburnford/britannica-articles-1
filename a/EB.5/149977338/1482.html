ATTACK, in the military art, is an effort made to force a post, break a body of troops, &c.

Attack of a Siege, is a furious assault made by the besiegers with trenches, covers, mines, &c. in order to make themselves masters of a fortress, by storming one of its sides. If there are two or three attacks made at the same time, there should be a communication betwixt them. See WAR.
