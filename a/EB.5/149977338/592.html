APPROVER

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APPROVER is particularly used in ancient law writers, for a bailiff or land-steward, appointed to have the care of a manor, franchise, or the like, and improve and make the most of it for the benefit of his master. In this sense, the word is also written appruare.
