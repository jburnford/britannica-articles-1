ATHELSTAN

article · 503 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATHELSTAN, a Saxon king of England, natural son of Edward the elder, and grandson of the great Alfred. He succeeded to the crown in 925, and reigned 16 years. There was a remarkable law passed by this prince, which shows his just sentiments of the advantages of commerce, as well as the early attention to it in this country: it declared, that any merchant who made three voyages on his own account beyond the British channel or narrow seas, should be entitled to the privilege of a thane, or gentleman.
