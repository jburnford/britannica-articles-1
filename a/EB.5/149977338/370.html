ANTRUM

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTRUM, among anatomists, a term used to denote several cavities of the body: as the antrum high-morianum, or that in the maxillary or jaw bone; antrum pylori, or that at the bottom of the pylorus, &c.
