BANTAM

article · 324 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BANTAM, a large town of the island of Java, in the East Indies, situated in E. Long. 105. 16. S. Lat. 6. 20. It is the capital of a kingdom of the same name, with a good harbour and fortified castle. It is divided into two towns, separated by a river, and one of them inhabited by the Chinese. For its history, &c. see JAVA.
