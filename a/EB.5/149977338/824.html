ARENA

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARENA, in Roman antiquity, a place where the gladiators fought; so called from its being always strewed with sand, to conceal from the view of the people the blood spilt in the combat. Nero is said to have strewed the arena with gold dust.
