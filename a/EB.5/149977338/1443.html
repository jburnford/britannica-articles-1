ATIBAR

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATIBAR, the name by which the inhabitants of the kingdom of Gago in Africa call gold-dust; from which word Europeans, and especially the French, have composed the word tibir, which also signifies gold-dust among those who trade in that commodity.
