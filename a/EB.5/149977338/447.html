APINA

APINA, or Apina, a town of Apulia, built by Diomedes, as was also Trice, (Pliny). Apina and Trice is a proverbial saying for things trifling and of no value, (Martial); and Apinari was the appellation for triflers or buffoons, (Trebellius Pollio.)
