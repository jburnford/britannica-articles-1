BALLOTADE

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALLOTADE, in the menage, the leap of a horse between two pillars, or upon a straight line, made with justness of time, with the aid of the hand and the calves of the legs: and in such a manner, that when his forefeet are in the air, he shows nothing but the shoes of his hinder-feet without jerking out.
