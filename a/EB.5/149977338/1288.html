ASSAULT

article · 309 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSAULT, in the military art, a furious effort made to carry a fortified post, camp, or fortress, wherein the assailants do not screen themselves by any works: while the assault continues, the batteries cease, for fear of killing their own men. The ensans perdus march first to the assault. See Ensans Perdus.
