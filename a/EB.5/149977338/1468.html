ATRAGENE

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATRAGENE, in botany: A genus of the polynandria order belonging to the polygamia class of plants. The calyx has four leaves; the petals are 12; and the seeds are candated. There are three species, all natives of the east.
