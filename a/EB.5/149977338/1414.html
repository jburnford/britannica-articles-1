ATHANATI

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATHANATI, in Persian antiquity, a body of ca-
valry, consisting of 10,000 men, always complete.
They were called athanati (a word originally Greek,
and signifying immortal), because, when one of them
happened to die, another was immediately appointed to
succeed him.
