ARRETUM, (Cicero Cæsar); Arretium, (Ptolemy); Urbs Arretinorum, (Polybius 3) one of the twelve ancient towns of Tuscany, near the Arnis and Clanis, situated in a pleasant valley. Now Arezzo, 42 miles east of Florence. E. Long. 13. 18. Lat. 43. 15.
