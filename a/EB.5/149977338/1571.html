AUDRAN

article · 515 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUDRAN (Louis), the last son of Germain Audran, was born at Lyons in 1670; from whence he went to Paris, after the example of his brothers, to complete his studies in the school of his uncle Girard. He died suddenly at Paris in 1712, before he had produced any great number of prints by his own hand. He assisted, it is presumed, his brothers in their more extensive works. Among the most esteemed prints by this artist are the seven acts of mercy, on seven middle-sized plates, length-ways, from Sebastian Bourdon.
