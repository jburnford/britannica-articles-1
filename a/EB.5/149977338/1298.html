ASSENS

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSENS, a sea-port town of Denmark, situated upon the Little Belt, a strait of the Baltic, which separates the isle of Funen from the continent. It is the common passage from the duchy of Sleswick to Copenhagen. E. Long. 10. 30. N. Lat. 55. 15.
