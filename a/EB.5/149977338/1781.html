AXINOMANCY

article · 506 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AXINOMANCY, AXINOMANTIA, from axin, securis and manus, divinatio; an ancient species of divination, or a method of foretelling future events by means of an ax or hatchet.—This art was in considerable repute among the ancients; and was performed, according to some, by laying an agate-stone on a red-hot hatchet; and also by fixing a hatchet on a round

flake so as to be exactly poised; then the names of those that were suspected were repeated, and he at whose name the hatchet moved was pronounced guilty.
