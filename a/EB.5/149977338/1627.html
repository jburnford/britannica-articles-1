AUGUSTA Historia

sub_entry · 281 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUGUSTA Historia, is the history of the Roman emperors from the time of Adrian to Carinus, that is, from the year of our Lord 157 to 285, composed by six Latin writers, Æl. Spartanus, Julius Capitolinus, Æl. Lampridius, Vulcatus Gallicanus, Trebellius Pollio, and Flavius Vopiscus.
