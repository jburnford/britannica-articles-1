ARROBAS

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARROBAS, or AROBAS, a weight used in Spain, Portugal, and the foreign dominions of both. The Arrobas of Portugal is also called Arata, and contains thirty-two Lisbon pounds; that of Spain contains

twenty-five Spanish pounds. In Peru it is called Arroba.
