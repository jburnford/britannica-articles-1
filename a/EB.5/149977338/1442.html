ATHY, a town of Ireland in the county of Kildare, not far from the borders of Queen's county.

W. Long. 7. o. N. Lat. 53. o. It is situated on the river Barrow; is governed by a sovereign, two bailiffs, and a recorder; and is, alternately with Naas, the affizes town.
