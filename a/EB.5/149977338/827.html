ARENARI

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARENARI, in antiquity, gladiators who combatted with beasts in the arena, or amphitheatre. The arenarii were slaves of the lowest rank; so that, tho' manumitted, they were not capable of being Roman citizens. They were the same with what were otherwise called Bellarii.
