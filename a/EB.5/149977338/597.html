APRIES, son of Plamnis, king of Egypt; the

same with Pharaoh Hophrah in Jeremiah and Ezekiel. He ruined Sidon, and some say he put Jeremiah to death. He thought neither God nor man could de-throne him; which yet was easily done by Amasis, and he himself was strangled by the Egyptians.
