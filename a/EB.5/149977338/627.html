AQUÆ Tauri

AQUÆ Tauri, hot waters or baths in Tuscany, at the distance of three miles from the sea, said to be discovered by a bull, hence the appellation. There are still to be seen the ruins of these baths. Now Aquæpendente, in Orvieto. E. Long. 12. 40. Lat. 42. 40.
