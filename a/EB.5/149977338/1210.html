ASCRIVUM

ASCRIVUM (anc. geog.), a town of Dalmatia, on the Sinus Rhizicus (Pliny, Ptolemy): Now Cattaro (Harduin); the capital of the territory of Cattaro, in Venetian Dalmatia. E. Long. 19. 20. Lat. 45. 25.
