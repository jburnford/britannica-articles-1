ASSANCALA

article · 342 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSANCALA, a strong town in Armenia, near the river Arras, in the road between Erzerum and Erivan, and noted for its hot baths. It stands on a high hill; the walls are built in a spiral line all round the rock, and strengthened with square towers. The ditches are about two fathoms over, cut out of hard rock. E. Long. 41. 30. N. Lat. 39. 46.
