ARMOURY

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMOURY, a store-house of arms, or a place wherein military habiliments are kept, to be ready for use. There are armouries in the Tower, and in all arsenals, citadels, castles, &c.

ARMOURY is also used for a branch of heraldry; being the knowledge of coat-armours, as to their blazons, and various intendments.
