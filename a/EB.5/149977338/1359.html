ASTIGI

ASTIGI (anc. geog.), a colony, and conventus juridicus, of Bætica, furnished Augusta Firma, situated on the Siogulus, which falls into the Bætic; called also Colonia Astigitana (Pliny): Now Erya, midway between Seville and Corduba. W. Long. 5°. Lat. 37. 20.
