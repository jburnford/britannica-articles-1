AUTRICUM, the capital of the Carnutas, a people of Gallia Celtica; afterwards called Carnotena, Carnotenus, and Civitas Carnotenum: Now Chartres, in the Orleanois on the Eure. E. Long. 1. 32. N. Lat. 48. 47.
