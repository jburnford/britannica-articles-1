BACCHINI

BACCHINI (Benedict), a Benedictine monk, and one of the most learned men in his time, was born at Borgo San Domino in 1651; and wrote a great number of books in Latin and Italian, the most considerable of which is a Literary Journal. He died at Bologna in 1721, aged 70.
