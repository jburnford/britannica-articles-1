ATRIP

article · 285 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATRIP, in nautical language, is applied either to the anchor or sails. The anchor is atrip, when it is drawn out of the ground in a perpendicular direction, either by the cable or buoy-rope. The top-sails are atrip, when they are hoisted up to the mast-head, or to their utmost extent.
