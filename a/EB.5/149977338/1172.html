ASBAMEA

ASBAMEA, a fountain of Cappadocia, near Tyana, sacred to Jupiter, and to an oath. Though this fountain bubbled up, as in a state of boiling, yet its water was cold; and never ran over, but fell back again: (Philostratus, Ammian).
