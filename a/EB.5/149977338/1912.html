BADIANE

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADIANE, or BANDIAN, the feed of a tree which grows in China, and smells like anise-feed. The Chinese, and the Dutch in imitation of them, sometimes use the badiane to give their tea an aromatic taste.
