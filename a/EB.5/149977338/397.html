APARITHMESIS

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APARITHMESIS, in rhetoric, denotes the answer to the protasis or proposition itself. Thus, if the protasis be, Apellandi tempus non erat,—the aparithmesis is, At tecum anno plus vixi.
