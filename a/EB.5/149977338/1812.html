AZOGA ships

sub_entry · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AZOGA ships, are those Spanish ships commonly called the quicksilver ships, from their carrying quicksilver to the Spanish West Indies, in order to extract the silver out of the mines of Mexico and Peru. These ships, strictly speaking, are not to carry any goods unless for the king of Spain's account.
