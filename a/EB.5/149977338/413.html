APERIENTS

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APERIENTS, in the materia medica, an appellation given to such medicines as facilitate the circulation of the humours by removing obstructions.—The five aperient roots of the shops are smallage, fennel, asparagus, parsley, and butcher's broom.
