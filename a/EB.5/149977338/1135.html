ARTOBRIGA, a town of Vindelicia (Ptolemy): No. 30.

Now Altzburg, in Bavaria, on the Danube, below Ingolstadt (Aventinus): but Cluverius supposes it to be Lebenau, on the Salzach, below Laufen, in the arch-bishopric of Salzburg.
