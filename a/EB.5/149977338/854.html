ARGENTATI MILITES

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGENTATI MILITES, in Antiquity. Livy, lib. vi. speaks of argentatimilites, as distinguished from aurati. Aquinas supposes these to have been similar to the argyrapides and chrysaspides; but the descriptions do not quadrature. Livy only represents the argentati as clothed in white linen coats.
