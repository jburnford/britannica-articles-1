BAGNOLAS, a town of Lower Languedoc in France. It has a very handsome square, and two fountains which rise in the middle of the town; the waters of which, being received in a basin, are conveyed by a canal out of town, and from thence to the lands about it. E. Long. 4. 43. N. Lat. 44. 10.
