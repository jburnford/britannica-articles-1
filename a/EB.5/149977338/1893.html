BACULE

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BACULE, in fortification, a kind of portcullis, or gate, made like a pit-fall with a counterpoise, and supported by two great flukes. It is usually made before the corpade-guard, not far from the gate of a place.
