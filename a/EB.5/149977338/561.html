APPETITE

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APPETITE, in medicine, a certain painful or uneasy sensation, always accompanied with a desire to eat or drink.—An excessive appetite is called by physicians bulimy or fanus canina; a defect or loss of it, anorexy; and that after things improper for food, pica.
