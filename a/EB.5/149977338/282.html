ANTIMERIA

article · 285 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTIMERIA, in a more restrained sense, is a figure where the noun is repeated instead of the pronoun. The antimeria is frequent in the Hebrew, and is sometimes retained in our version of the Old Testament accordingly: e. g. Hear my voice, ye wives of Lamech, for my wives, Gen. iv. 23.
