ANTEROS

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTEROS, in mythology, one of the two Cupids
who were the chief of the number. They are placed
at the foot of the Venus of Medici; this is represented
with a heavy and full look, agreeably to the poetical
description of him, as the cause of love's ceasing.
The other was called Eros.
