ANIMATED, or ANIMATE

sub_entry · 155 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANIMATED, or ANIMATE, in a general sense,
denotes something endowed with animal-life. It also
imports a thing to be impregnated with vermin or ani-
malcules.
