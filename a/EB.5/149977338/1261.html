ASPER

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASPER, in grammar, an accent peculiar to the Greek language, marked thus (\acute{a}); and importing, that the letters over which it is placed ought to be strongly aspirated, or pronounced as if an b were joined with them.
