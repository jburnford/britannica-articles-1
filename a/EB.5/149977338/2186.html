BAPTISMAL PRESENTS

article · 389 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAPTISMAL PRESENTS are in use in Germany, made by the sponsors to the infant, consisting of money, plate, or even sometimes fiefs of lands; which by the laws of the country are to be kept for the child till of age, the parents having only the trust, not the right, of disposing of them. An anonymous author has published a discourse express on this occasion, intitled, De pecunia lustrica.
