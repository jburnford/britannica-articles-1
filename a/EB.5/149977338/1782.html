AXIOM

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AXIOM, AXIOMA (from axia, I am worthy); a self-evident truth, or a proposition whose truth every person receives at first sight. Thus, that the whole is greater than a part; that a thing cannot be and not be at the same time; and that from nothing, nothing can arise; are axioms.
