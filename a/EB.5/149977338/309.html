ANTIPELARGIA

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTIPELARGIA, among the ancients, a law, whereby children are obliged to furnish necessaries to their aged parents. The eiconia, or stork, is a bird famous for the care it takes of its parents when grown old. Hence, in some Latin writers, this is rendered lex eiconiaria, or the stork's law.
