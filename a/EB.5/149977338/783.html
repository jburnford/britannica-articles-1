ARCHON

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCHON is also applied by some authors to divers officers, both civil and religious, under the eastern or Greek empire. Thus bishops are sometimes called archbishops; and the same may be said of the lords of the emperor's court. We also read of the archon of the antimenæa, archon of archons, grand archon, archon of churches, archon of the gospel, archon of the walls, &c.
