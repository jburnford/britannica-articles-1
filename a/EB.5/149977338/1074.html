ARRIERE Bon

sub_entry · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARRIERE Bon, in the French customs, is a general proclamation, whereby the king summonses to the war all that hold of him, both his vassals, i. e. the noblesse, and the vassals of his vassals.
