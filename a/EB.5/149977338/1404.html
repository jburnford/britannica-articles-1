ATEGUA, or ATTEGUA

ATEGUA, or ATTEGUA, an ancient town of Spain, placed by some in the road from Antiquara, now Antequera, to Hispalis, or Seville; by others near Alcala Real; which last is the more probable situation, because the Flumen Salsum, now the Salado, was in its neighbourhood. Now Tebala Visja, or Teivela.
