ASSIENTO, a Spanish word signifying a farm, in commerce, is used for a bargain between the king of Spain

Assign
Assise. Spain and other powers, for importing negroes into the Spanish dominions in America, and particularly to Buenos Ayres. The first assiento was made with the French Guinea-company; and, by the treaty of Utrecht, transferred to the English, who were to furnish 4800 negroes annually.
