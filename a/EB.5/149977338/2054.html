BALLAD, a kind of song, adapted to the capacity of the lower class of people; who, being mightily taken with this species of poetry, are thereby not a little influenced in the conduct of their lives. Hence

No 40.

we find, that sedition and designing men never fail to spread ballads among the people, with a view to gain them over to their side.
