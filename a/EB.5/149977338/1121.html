ARTICULATION

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARTICULATION, in botany, is the connection of parts that consist of joints or knees, such as the pods of French honey-suckles, which when ripe divide into so many parts as there are knees or joints; also those parts of plants which swell into nodes or joints, and which usually send forth branches.
