AUSPEX, a name originally given to those who were afterwards denominated augurs. In which sense the word is supposed to be formed from avis, "bird," and inspicere, "to inspect;" auspices, q. d. avispices. Some will therefore have auspices properly to denote those who foretold future events from the flight of birds.
