BARANWAHR, a town of Lower Hungary, in a county of the same name, taken by the emperor of Germany from the Turks in 1684. It is seated between

Barathrum, tween Buda and Belgrade, in E. Long. 20. 5. N. Lat.
Baratiere. 46. o.
