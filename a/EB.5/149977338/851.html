ARGENTAN, a town of France, in Lower Normandy, and in the diocese of the Seez, with the title No. 27.

of a marquise. It is seated on an eminence, in the middle of a fertile plain, on the banks of the river Orne, and carries on a considerable trade. E. Long. 0. 5. N. Lat. 48. 54.
