ASCANIUS

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASCANIUS, the son of Aeneas and Creusa, succeeded his father in the kingdom of the Latins, and defeated Mezentius king of the Tuscans, who had refused to conclude a peace with him. At length he founded Alba Longa; and died about 1139 years before the Christian era, after a reign of 38 years.
