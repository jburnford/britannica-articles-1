ARIMASPI

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARIMASPI, (Pliny), a people of Sarmatia Europea, to the south of the Montes Riphæi, said by Meila to have but one eye; a fable broached by Aristeas Proconnesius, according to Herodotus.
