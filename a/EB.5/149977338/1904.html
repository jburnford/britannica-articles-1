BADENOCH

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADENOCH, the most easterly part of Inverness-shire in Scotland, extending about 33 miles in length from east to west, and 27 from north-east to south-west where broadest. It has no considerable town, and is very barren and hilly, but abounds with deer, and other kinds of game.
