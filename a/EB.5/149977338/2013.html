BALBOA

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALBOA, (Vasco Nugnes de), a Castilian; a celebrated navigator, and one of the first discoverers of South America. He was beheaded by the Spanish governor of St Mary, through jealousy of his growing reputation, in 1517, aged 42.
