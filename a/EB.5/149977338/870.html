ARGO Navis

sub_entry · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGO Navis, the Ship Argo, in astronomy, is a constellation in the southern hemisphere, whose stars, in Ptolemy's catalogue, are 45; in Tycho's 11; in the Britannic catalogue, and Sharp's appendix, 64.
