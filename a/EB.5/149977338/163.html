ANTAVARE

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTAVARE, a province of the island of Madagascar, lying about 21^{\circ} 30' S. Lat. and bounded by the province and cape of Manoufi. The greatest part of it is watered by the river Mananzari, whose source is in the red mountains of Ambohitse.
