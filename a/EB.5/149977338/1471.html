ATREUS

article · 463 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATREUS, in fabulous history, the son of Pelops and Hippodamia, and the father of Agamemnon and Menelaus, is supposed to have been king of Mycenæ and Argos about 1228 years before the Christian æra. He drove his brother Thyeestes from court, for having a criminal commerce with Ærope his wife: but understanding that he had had two children by her, he sent for him again, and made him eat them; at which

which horrid action the sun, it is said, withdrew his light.
