BANQUET

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BANQUET, or Banquette, in fortification, a little foot-bank, or elevation of earth, forming a path which runs along the inside of a parapet, upon which the musketeers get up, in order to discover the counterscarp, or to fire on the enemy, in the moat or in the covert-way.
