ARMOISIN

article · 176 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMOISIN, a silk stuff, or kind of taffety, manufactured in the East Indies, at Lyons in France, and at Lucca in Italy. That of the Indies is lighter than those made in Europe.
