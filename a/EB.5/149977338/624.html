AQUÆ Quintianæ

AQUÆ Quintianæ, put by Ptolemy in room of the Aquæ Cilinæ of Antonine. Now supposed to be Sarria, a town of Gallicia, on a rivulet of the same name, three leagues to the south of Lugo.
