APANOMIA

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APANOMIA, a town of Santorin, an island in the Mediterranean sea, called in this part, by some, the Sea of Candia: it has a spacious harbour, in the form of a half-moon; but the bottom is so deep, that ships cannot anchor there, E. Long. 25. 59. N. Lat. 36. 18.
