ARMORUM concessio

sub_entry · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CLASSICUM

ARMORUM concessio, the clashing of armour practised by the Roman armies previous to an engagement, and intended to strike a panic into their enemies: It always followed the Classicum and the Barritus. See CLASSICUM and BARRITUS.
