AROPH

AROPH, a contraction of aroma philosophorum; a name given to saffron.

АРОМ Paracelsi, a name given to a kind of chemical

cal flowers, probably of the same nature with the Ens Veneris, elegantly prepared by sublimation from equal quantities of lapis haematitis and sal ammoniac.

AROPH is also a term used frequently by Paracelsus in a sense synonymous with lithontripic.
