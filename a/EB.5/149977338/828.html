ARENARIUM

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARENARIUM, in ecclesiastical writers, denotes a cemetery or burying-ground. The arenaria were properly a kind of pits, or holes, under ground wherein in the ancient Christians not only buried their dead, but held their religious assemblies in times of persecution.
