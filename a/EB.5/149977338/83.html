ANNANO

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANNANO, a strong fort of Italy, in the duchy of Milan. It has been twice taken by the French; but was restored to the duke of Savoy in 1706. It is seated on the river Tanaro, in E. Long. 8. 30. N. Lat. 44. 40.
