ARMISTICE

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARMISTICE, in military affairs, a temporary truce or cessation of arms for a very short space of time. The word is Latin, armistitium; and compounded of arma, "arms," and sto "to stand, or stop."
