ASTANDA

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASTANDA, in antiquity, a royal courier or messenger, the same with ANGARUS.—King Darius of Persia is said by Plutarch, in his book on the fortune of Alexander, to have formerly been an astanda.
