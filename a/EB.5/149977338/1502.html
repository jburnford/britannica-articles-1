ATTIUM

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATTIUM (anc. geog.), a promontory on the north-west of Corsica (Ptolemy). It still retains some traces of its ancient name, being now called Punta di Acciolo (Cluverius).
