AUXERRE

AUXERRE, an ancient town of France in the duchy of Burgundy, and capital of the Auxerrois, with a bishop's see. The episcopal palace is one of the finest in France, and the churches are also very beautiful. This town is advantageously situated for trade with Paris, on the river Yone. E. Long. 3. 35. N. Lat. 47. 54.
