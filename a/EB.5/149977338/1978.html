BAJULUS

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAJULUS was also the name of a conventional officer in the ancient monasteries, to whom belonged the charge of gathering and distributing the money and legacies left for masses and obits; whence he was also denominated bajulus obituum novorum.
