BALKERS

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALKERS, in the fishery, persons placed on rocks, and eminences at sea, to spy the herring droves, and give notice to the fishermen, by waving boughs, what way they go and where they may be found.
