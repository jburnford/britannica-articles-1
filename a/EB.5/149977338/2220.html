BARBARA, among logicians, the first mode of

the first figure of syllogisms. A syllogism in barbara is one whereof all the prepositions are universal and affirmative; the middle term being the subject of the first proposition, and attribute in the second.

Examp. BAR. Every wicked man is miserable;
