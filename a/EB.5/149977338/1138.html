ARTOLICA

ARTOLICA (anc. geog.), a town of the Salassi, in Gallia Cispadana, (Antonine); at the foot of the Alps: now called la Tuile by the inhabitants, a hamlet of Savoy, in the duchy of Aosta, at the foot of mount St Bernard the Leis.
