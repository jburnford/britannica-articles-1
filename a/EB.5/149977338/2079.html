BALNEARIUS SERVI

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALNEARIUS SERVI, in antiquity, servants or attendants belonging to the baths. Some were appointed to heat them, called fornicatores; others were denominated capsarii, who kept the cloaths of those that went into them; others alptae, whose care it was to pull off the hair; others unfuarii, who anointed and perfumed the body.
