BAGOI

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAGOI, among the ancient Persians, were the same with those called by the Latins spadones, viz. a species of eunuchs, in whom the canal of the penis was so contorted by a tight vinculum, that they could not emit the semen.
