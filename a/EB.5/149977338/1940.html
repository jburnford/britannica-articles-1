BAGNIO

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAGNIO, an Italian word signifying a bath. We use it for a house with conveniences for bathing, cupping, sweating, and otherwise cleansing the body; and sometimes for worse purposes. In Turkey it is become

come a general name for the prisons where the slaves are inclosed, it being usual in these prisons to have baths.
