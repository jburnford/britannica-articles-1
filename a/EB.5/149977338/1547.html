AUDEUS

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUDEUS, the chief of the Audens, obtained the name of an heretic, and the punishment of banishment, for celebrating Easter in the manner of the Jews, and attributing an human form to the Deity. He died in the country of the Goths, about the year 370.
