BADGE

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADGE, in naval architecture, signifies a sort of ornament placed on the outside of small ships, very near the stern, containing either a window for the convenience of the cabin, or a representation of it. It is commonly decorated with marine figures, martial instruments, or such like emblems.
