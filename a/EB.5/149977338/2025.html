BALK-Goods

sub_entry · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALK-Goods, among the English merchants, are all such as are imported or exported in bales; but the French give that name to certain hardware, and other sorts of merchandize, which come to Paris, and are commonly made by bad workmen of indifferent materials.
