MYRIOPHYLLUM

article · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MYRIOPHYLLUM, in botany: A genus of the polyandria order, belonging to the monocasia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 15th order, inundata. The male calyx is tetraphyllous; there is no corolla; the stamens are eight in number. The female calyx is tetraphyllous; the pistils four; there is no style; and four naked seeds.
