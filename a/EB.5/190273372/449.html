Frederic MOREL

sub_entry · 488 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Frederic MOREL, who was interpreter in the Greek and Latin tongues, as well as printer to the king, was heir to Vascoian, whose daughter he had married.— He was born in Champagne, and he died in an advanced age at Paris 1583. His sons and grandsons trode in his steps; they distinguished themselves in literature, and maintained also the reputation which he had acquired by printing. The edition of St Gregory of Nyssa, by his son Claude Morel, is held in great estimation by the learned.
