MORINORUM CASTELLUM

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MORINORUM CASTELLUM (anc. geog.), simply Castellum (Antonine); situated on an eminence, with a spring of water on its top, in the territory of the Morini. Now Mont Cappel, in Flanders.
