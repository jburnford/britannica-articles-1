MONSONIA, in botany: A genus of the dodecandria order, belonging to the polyadelphia class of plants. The calyx is pentaphyllous; the corolla pentapetalous and irregular; the stamens are 15 in number, and coalited into five filaments; the style bifid; the capsule pentacoccos.
