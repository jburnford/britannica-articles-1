MURENGERS

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MURENGERS, two officers of great antiquity in the city of Chester, annually chosen out of the aldermen, to see that the walls are kept in repair, and to receive a certain toll and custom for the maintenance thereof.
