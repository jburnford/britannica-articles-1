MOLUCCA ISLANDS

article · 811 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOLUCCA ISLANDS, lie in the East Indian sea under the line; of which there are five principal, namely, Ternate, Tybor, Machian, Motyr, and Bachian. The largest of them is hardly 30 miles in circumference. They produce neither corn, rice, nor cattle, except

except goats: but they have oranges, lemons, and other fruits; and are most remarkable for spices, especially cloves. They have large snakes, which are not venomous, and very dangerous to crocodiles. At present they have three kings: and the Dutch, who are very strong there, keep out all other European nations, being jealous of their spice-trade. The religion is idolatry; but there are many Mahometans. They were discovered by the Portuguese in 1511, who settled upon the coast; but the Dutch drove them away, and are now masters of all these islands.
