MYRON

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MYRON, an excellent Grecian statuary, flourished 442 B. C. The cow he represented in brass was an admirable piece of workmanship, and was the occasion of many fine epigrams in Greek.
