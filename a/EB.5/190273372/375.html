MONTE-SANTO

MONTE-SANTO, formerly called Mount-Abba, a mountain of Turkey in Europe, on the gulph of Contessa. It is called Monte-Sancto, or the Holy Mount, because there are 22 monasteries thereon, in which are 4000 monks, who never suffer a woman to come near them. It is 17 miles south of Salonichi. E. Long. 24. 30. N. Lat. 40. 27.
