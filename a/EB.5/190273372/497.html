MORPHEUS

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MORPHEUS, in fabulous history, the god of sleep, or, according to others, one of the ministers of Somnus. He caused sleepiness, and represented the forms of dreams. Ovid styles him the kindest of the deities; and he is usually described in a recumbent posture, and crowned with poppies.
