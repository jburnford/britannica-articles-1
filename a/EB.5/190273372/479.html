MORINA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the diandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 48th order, Aggregate. The corolla is unequal; the calyx of the flower is monophyllous and dented; the calyx of the flower bifid; there is one seed under the calyx of the flower.
