MONTEGO-BAY

article · 474 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTEGO-BAY, a town of Jamaica, and, next to Kingston, the most flourishing in the island, contains above 350 houses; and carries on a very considerable commerce with Great Britain and our remain, ing colonies in North America. The harbour is capacious; but rather exposed to the north winds, which at certain times in the year blow with great violence. It is the capital of the parish of St James; in which are 70 sugar-plantations, 70 other settlements, and 27,000 slaves.
