MITRA

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MITRA, was a cap or covering for the head, worn by the Roman ladies, and sometimes by the men; but it was looked upon as a mark of effeminacy in the last, especially when it was tied upon their heads.
