NARCOTICS

NARCOTICS, in medicine, soporiferous drugs, which bring on a stupefaction. Among narcotics the most eminent are those usually prepared for medicinal uses from the poppy, especially opium; as also all those prepared from mandragoras, hyoscyamus, stramonium, and datura.
