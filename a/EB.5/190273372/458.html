MORETON

MORETON, a town of Devonshire, with a market on Saturdays, seated on a hill, near Dartmore, and is a pretty large place, with a noted market for yarn. It is 14 miles south-west of Exeter, and 185 west by south of London. W. Long. 3.46. N. Lat. 50. 39.
