MOUNT-EDGEUMBER

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOUNT-EDGEUMBER, a prodigious high peak, at the entrance of Cook's strait, in New Zealand, on the west side. Its height is supposed not to be much inferior to that of the peak of Teneriffe.
