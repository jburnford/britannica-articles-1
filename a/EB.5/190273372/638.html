MULLER

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MULLER, or Mullar, denotes a stone flat and even at bottom, but round atop; used for grinding of matters on a marble. The apothecaries use mullers to prepare many of their teflaceous powders; and painters for their colours, either dry or in oil.
