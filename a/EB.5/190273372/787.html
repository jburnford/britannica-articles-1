MYOSURUS, in botany: A genus of the polygynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 26th order Multiflora. The calyx is pentaphyllous, the leaves cohering at the base; there are five subulated nectaria resembling petals: the seeds are numerous.
