NAGERA

NAGERA, or NAGARA, a town of Spain, in Old Castile, and the territory of Rioja, with the title of a duchy and fortress; famous for a battle fought in its neighbourhood in 1369. It is situated in a fertile country, on a brook called Nasrilla. W. Long. 2. 20. N. Lat. 42. 25.
