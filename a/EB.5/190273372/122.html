MISANTHROPY

article · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MISANTHROPY (formed misos, hatred, and anthros, a man); a general dislike or aversion to man, and mankind. In which sense it stands opposed to philanthropy, or the love of mankind.
