MONTMEDI

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTMEDI, a small but strong town of France, in Luxemburg, seated on the river Chire, which divides it into the upper and lower towns. It is 22 miles south-east of Sedan, 27 south-west of Luxemburg, and 135 north-east of Paris. E. Long. 5. 23. N. Lat. 49. 32.
