NAMA

NAMA, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and, in the natural method, ranking under the 13th order, Succulente. The calyx is pentaphyllous, the corolla quinquepartite, the capsule unilocular and bivalved.
