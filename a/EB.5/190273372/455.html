MORESBY

MORESBY, a harbour a little above Whitehaven, in Cumberland; in and about which many remains of antiquity have been dug up, such as altars and stones, with inscriptions on them; and several caverns have been found called Pitt's Holes. Here is supposed to have been a Roman fortification.
