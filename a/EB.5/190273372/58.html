MILTHORP

article · 363 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MILTHORP, a port-town of Westmoreland, at the mouth of the Can, five miles from Kendal. It is the only sea-port in the county; and goods are brought hither in small vessels from Grange in Lancashire. Here are two paper-mills. It has a market on Friday, and a fair on Old May-day; and there is a good stone-bridge over the river Betha, which runs through the town.
