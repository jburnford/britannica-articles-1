MYRSINE

MYRSINE, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 18th order, Bicornes. The corolla is semiquinquefid and connivent; the germin filling the corolla; the berry quinquelocular and pentaspermous.
