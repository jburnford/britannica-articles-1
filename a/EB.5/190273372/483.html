MORISONIA, in botany: A genus of the polyandria order, belonging to the monadelphia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 25th order, Putaminea. The calyx is single and bifid; the corolla tetrapetalous; there is one pistil; the berry has a hard bark, is unilocular, polyspermous, and pedicellated.
