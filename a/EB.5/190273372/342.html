MONT-DIDER

MONT-DIDER, an ancient town of France in Picardy, where the kings of France formerly had a palace and kept their court. It is seated on a mountain, 17 miles from Amiens and Compeigne, and 38 north of Paris. W. Long. 2. 34. N. Lat. 49. 39.
