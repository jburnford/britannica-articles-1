MILLO

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MILLO, a part of mount Zion at its extremity; and therefore called Millo of the city of David (2 Chron. xxxii.), taken in with the wall that encompassed mount Zion. Uncertain whether Beth-Millo, (Judges ix. 20.) denotes a place; if it did, it lay near Sechem.
