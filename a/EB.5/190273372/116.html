MIREVELT (Peter), son of Michael, was born at

Delft in 1596, and died in 1632. In his manner of design, in his style of colouring, and in the delicacy of his pencil, he exactly resembled his father; and by the best judges of that time he was accounted to be in no degree inferior to him.
