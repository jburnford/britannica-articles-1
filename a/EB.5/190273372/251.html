MOLO, a philosopher of Rhodes, called also Apollonius. Some are of opinion that Apollonius and Molo are two different persons, who were both natives of Alabanda, and disciples of Menecles of the same place. They both visited Rhodes, and there opened a school; but Molo came some time after Apollonius. Molo had Cicero and J. Cæsar among his pupils.
