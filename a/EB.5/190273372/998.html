NEEDHAM

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEEDHAM, in Suffolk, 72 miles from London, stands on the Orwell, 9 miles from Ipswich, in the road to Huntingdonshire. Its market is on Wednesday, and fair in October 28.
