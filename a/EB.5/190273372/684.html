MURALT

article · 384 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MURALT (N— de), a native of Switzerland, travelled through a great part of Europe with the views of a philosopher. He published a collection of Lettres sur les Français et sur les Anglais, 12mo, 2 vols. 1726, which met with great success, though they are written in a vague and superficial manner. Some other works which he published are below mediocrity. He died about the year 1750.
