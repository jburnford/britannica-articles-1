MYRTIS

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MYRTIS, a Greek woman who distinguished herself by her poetical talents. She flourished about 500 years before the Christian era, and instructed the celebrated Corinna in the several rules of versification. Pindar himself, as some report, was also one of her pupils.
