NATURAL Note

Cross-references: NOTE · ARTIFICIAL

NATURAL Note, in music, is used in opposition to flat and sharp notes, which are called artificial notes. See NOTE, SCALE, &c.

NATURAL is also used for something coming immediately out of the hands of nature: in which sense it stands opposed to artificial or artificial, which signifies something wrought by art. See ARTIFICIAL.

Bishop Wilkins observes, that there appears a world
VOL. XII. Part II.

of difference between natural and artificial things, when viewed with microscopes. The first ever appear adorned with all imaginable elegance and beauty; the latter, though the most curious in their kind, infinitely rude and unhewn: the finest needle appears a rough bar of iron; and the most accurate engraving or embossment, as if done with a mattock or a trowel.
