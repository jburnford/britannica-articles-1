MIZRAIM

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MIZRAIM, or MISRAIM, the dual name of Egypt, used in scripture to denote the Higher and Lower Egypt, which see. It sometimes occurs singular, Mazor: 2 Kings xix. Isaiah xix. Micah vii.
