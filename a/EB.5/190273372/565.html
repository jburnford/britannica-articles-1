Cross-references: LETTER-FOUNDRY

MOULDS of letter-founders are partly of steel and partly wood. The wood, properly speaking, serves only to cover the real mould which is within, and to prevent the workman, who holds it in his hand, from being incommoded by the heat of the melted metal. Only one letter or type can be formed at once in each mould. See LETTER-FOUNDRY.
