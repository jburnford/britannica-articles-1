MNEMOSYNE

MNEMOSYNE (fab. hist.), a daughter of Cœlus and Terra. She married Jupiter, by whom she had the nine muses. The word mnemosyne signifies "memory;" and therefore the poets have rightly called Memory the mother of the muses, because it is to that mental endowment that mankind are indebted for their progress in science.
