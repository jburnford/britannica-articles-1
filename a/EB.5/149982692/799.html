MAGNUS PORTUS

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAGNUS PORTUS, (anc. geog.), a port of the Belgæ, in Britain, on the Channel. Now thought to be Portsmouth, in Hampshire.—Another Portus Magnus of Batica in Spain; a fort to the east of Abdera.
