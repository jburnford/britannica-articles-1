LUPERCI, a name given to the priests of the god Pan. See LUPERCALIA.

The Luperci were the most ancient order of priests in Rome; they were divided into two colleges or companies, the one called Fabii and the other Quintilii. To these Cæsar added a third, which he called Julii.
