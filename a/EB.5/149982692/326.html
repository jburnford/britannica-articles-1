LIXA

LIXA, or LIXUS (anc. geog.) a town on the Atlantic near the river Lixus; made a Roman colony by Claudius Cæsar; famous in mythology for the palace of Anteus and his encounter with Hercules, (Pliny). Now Larache, sixty-five leagues to the south of the straits of Gibraltar.
