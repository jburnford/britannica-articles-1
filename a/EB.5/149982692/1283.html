MASTER of the Household

sub_entry · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASTER of the Household, is an officer under the treasurer of the household, in the king's gift: his business is to survey the accounts of the household.—He has L. 66 : 13 : 4 a-year wages, and L. 433 : 6 : 8 board-wages.
