MARIANUS SCOTUS

article · 154 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARIANUS SCOTUS, an Irish monk, was related to the Venerable Bede, and wrote a chronicle which is esteemed. He died in the abbey of Fuld in 1036, aged 58.
