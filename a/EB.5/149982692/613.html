LUTZEN

LUTZEN, a town of Upper Saxony in Germany; famous for a battle fought here in 1632, when Gustavus Adolphus king of Sweden was killed. It is situated on the river Elster, in E. Long. 12. 37. N. Lat. 51. 20.
