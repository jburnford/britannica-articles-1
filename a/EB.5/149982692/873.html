MALIGNANT

MALIGNANT, among physicians, a term applied to diseases of a very dangerous nature, and generally infectious; such are the dysentery, hospital-fever, &c. in their worst stages.

Malignity among physicians signifies much the same with contagion. See CONTAGION.
