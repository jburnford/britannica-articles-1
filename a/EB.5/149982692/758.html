MAGAZINE

article · 364 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAGAZINE, on ship-board, a close room or store-house, built in the fore or after part of the hold, to contain the gunpowder used in battle. This apartment is strongly secured against fire, and no person is allowed to enter it with a lamp or candle: it is therefore lighted, as occasion requires, by means of the candles or lamps in the light-room contiguous to it.
