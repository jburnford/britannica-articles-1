LYSANDRIA

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYSANDRIA, a Samian festival, celebrated with games and sacrifices in honour of the Lacedemonian general Lysander. It was anciently called hera; but this name the Samians abolished by a public decree.
