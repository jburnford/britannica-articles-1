MACERATA

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MACERATA, a handsome and populous town of Italy, in the territory of the church, and in the Marche of Ancona, with a bishop's see, and an university. It is seated near the mountain Chiento, in E. Long. 13. 37. N. Lat. 43. 15.
