MANSFELD (Henry-Francis, count of), was of the same house with the former, and signalized himself in the wars for the Spanish succession. He died at Vienna on the 8th of June 1715, at the age of 74, after being a prince of the Holy Empire, a grandee of Spain, field-marshall general of the emperor's armies, general of artillery, ambassador to France and Spain, president of the Aulic council, and great chamberlain to the emperor.
