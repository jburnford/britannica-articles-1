MARCGRAVE, or MARGRAVE, a kind of dignity.

March. nity in Germany, answering to our marquis; (see MARQUIS.) The word is derived from the German Marche, or Mark, which signifies "a frontier;" and Graf, "count, governor;" Maregraves being originally governors of cities lying on the frontiers of a country or state.
