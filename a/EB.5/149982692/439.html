LORICATION

Cross-references: CHEMISTRY

LORICATION, or COATING, in chemistry, is the covering a glass or earthen vessel with a coat or crust of a matter able to resist the fire, to prevent its breaking in the performing an operation that requires great violence of fire. See CHEMISTRY.
