LIBELLA, a piece of money amongst the Romans, being the tenth part of the denarius, and equal in value to the as. It was called libella, as being a little pound, because equal to a pound of brass. Its value in our money is 1 ob. 1 qu. or a half-penny farthing. See MONEY.
