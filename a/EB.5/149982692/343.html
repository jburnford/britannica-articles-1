LOBETUM

LOBETUM, anciently a town of the Hither Spain: said to have been built by the Libyan Hercules, (Pliny.) Now Albarazin, a town of Arragon on the confines of New Castile, on the river Guadalavir. E. Long. 2. N. Lat. 40. 40.
