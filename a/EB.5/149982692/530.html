LUDWIGIA

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUDWIDGIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 17th order, Calycanthemæ. The corolla is tetrapetalous; the calyx quadripartite, superior; the capsule tetragonal, quadrilocular, inferior, and polypermous.
