MASTER of the Horse

sub_entry · 582 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASTER of the Horse is reckoned the third great officer of the court, and is an office of great honour and antiquity, and always (when not put in commission) filled by noblemen of the highest rank and abilities. He has the management and disposal of all the king's stables and bred horses. He has authority over the equestrians and pages, coachmen, footmen, grooms, riders of the great horse, farriers, and smiths.
