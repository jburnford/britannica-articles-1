LUNETTE, in fortification, an enveloped counter-guard, or elevation of earth, made beyond the second ditch, opposite to the places of arms; differing from the ravelins only in their situation. Lunettes are usually made in ditches full of water, and serve to the same purpose as faussebrays, to dispute the passage of the ditch. See FORTIFICATION.
