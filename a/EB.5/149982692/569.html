LUNATIC

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUNATIC, a person affected with that species of madness termed lunacy. The word is indeed properly applied to one that hath lucid intervals; sometimes enjoying his senses, and sometimes not; and that frequently supposed to depend on the influence of the moon.
