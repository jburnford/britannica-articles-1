LEUK

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEUK, a town of Switzerland, almost in the middle of the Valais; remarkable for its natural strength, for the assembly of the states that often meet there, and for its baths, whose water is so hot that they will boil eggs.
