LUCRINUS LACUS

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUCRINUS LACUS (anc. geog.), a lake of Campania, between Baiae and Puteoli, famous for its oysters (Horace, Martial, Juvenal); Lucrinenses (Cicero), the people dwelling on it. Now a perfect bay since the earthquake in 1538.
