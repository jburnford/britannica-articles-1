MARTLETS

article · 347 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: HERALDRY

MARTLETS, in heraldry, little birds represented without feet; and used as a difference or mark of distinction for younger brothers, to put them in mind that they are to trust to the wings of virtue and merit, in order to raise themselves, and not to their feet, they having little land to set their foot on. See HERALDRY, Plate CCXXVII. fig. 1. A.
