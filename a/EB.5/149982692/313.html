LITUUS

LITUUS, was also an instrument of music in use in the Roman army. It was straight, excepting that it had a little bending at the upper end like a lituus or sacred staff of the augurs; and from the similitude it derived its name. The lituus, as an instrument of martial music, was of a middle kind, betwixt the cornu and the tuba.
