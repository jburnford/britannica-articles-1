LUNDEN

LUNDEN, or LUND, a considerable town of Sweden, in Gothland; and capital of the territory of Schonen, with an archbishop's see and an university. It was ceded to the Swedes by the Danes in 1658. E. Long. 13. 25. N. Lat. 55. 40.
