MACROCOSM

article · 127 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MACROCOSM, a word denoting the great world or universe. It is compounded of the Greek words ΜΑΚΡΟΣ "great," and ΚΟΣΜΟΣ "world."
