LIPPA

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIPPA, a town of Hungary, with a castle. It was taken by the Turks in 1552; by the Imperialists in 1688; and by the Turks again in 1691; who abandoned it in 1695, after having demolished the fortifications. It is seated on a mountain, in E. Long. 21. 55. N. Lat. 36. 5.
