LOTION

article · 422 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LOTION, is, strictly speaking, such washing as concerns beautifying the skin, by cleansing it of those deformities which a disordered blood throws upon it. Medicines of this kind, however, are for the most part insignificant, and sometimes very dangerous; the only proper method of treating these disorders is, by administering such medicines as tend to correct the morbid state of the constitution from whence they arise.
