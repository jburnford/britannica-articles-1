Free MARTIN

Cross-references: HERMAPHRODITE

Free MARTIN, in zoology, is a name given in this country to a cow-calf cast at the same time with a bull-calf, which is a kind of hermaphrodite that is never known to breed nor to discover the least inclination for the bull, nor does the bull ever take the least notice of it. See HERMAPHRODITE.
