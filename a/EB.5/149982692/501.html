LUCERIA (anc. geog.), a town of Apulia in Italy; which in Strabo's time still exhibited marks of Diomed's

Diomed's sovereignty in those parts. Ptolemy has Lucernia; whether from mistake, or the custom of his time, uncertain. Now Nocera de Pagani, in the kingdom of Naples. E. Long. 15. o. N. Lat. 40. 40.
