MARK

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARK is also used among us for a money of account, and in some other countries for a coin. See Money-Table.

The English mark is two thirds of a pound Sterling, or 13 s. 4 d. and the Scotch mark is of equal value in Scots money of account, viz. 13 1/2 d.
