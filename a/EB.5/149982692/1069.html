MARCHERS

article · 375 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MARCHERS, or LORDS-MARCHERS, were those noblemen that lived on the marches of Wales or Scotland; who, in times past, according to Camden, had their laws, and potestatem vias, &c. like petty kings, which are abolished by the stat. 27 H. 8. c. 26. and 1 Edw. 6. c. 10. In old records the lords marches of Wales were styled Marchiones de Marchia Wallie. See 1 & 2 P. & M. c. 15.
