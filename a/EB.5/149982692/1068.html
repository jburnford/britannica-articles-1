MARCHENA, an handsome, ancient, and considerable town of Spain, in Andalusia, with the title of a duchy, and a suburb as large as the town, seated in the middle of a plain, particularly fertile in olives, though very destitute of water. W. Long. 5. 20. N. Lat. 37. 20.
