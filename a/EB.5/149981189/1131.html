FRACTION

Cross-references: ALGEBRA

FRACTION, in arithmetic and algebra, a part or division of an unit or integer; or a number which stands to an unit in the relation of a part to its whole. The word literally imports a broken number.

Fractions are usually divided into decimal, sexagesimal, and vulgar. See ALGEBRA and ARITHMETIC.
