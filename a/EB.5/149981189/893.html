FOUNDER, FLUX, or But, in ichthyology. See PLEURONECTES.

Founders may be fished for all day long, either in a swift stream, or in the still deep water; but best in the stream, in the months of April, May, June, and July: the most proper baits are all sorts of worms, wasps, and gentles.
