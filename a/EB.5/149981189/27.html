EUCRASY

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUCRASYS, (of well, and spastic temperature), in medicine, an agreeable well proportioned mixture of qualities, whereby a body is said to be in good order and disposed for a good state of health.
