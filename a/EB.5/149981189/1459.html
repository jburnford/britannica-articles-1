GALEGA

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALEGA, in botany: A genus of the decandria order, belonging to the diadelphia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 32d order, Papilionaceae. The calyx is composed of subulated nearly equal denticles or segments; the legumen has oblique suture, and seeds lying between them.
