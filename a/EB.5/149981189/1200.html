FREDERICA, a town of North America, in Georgia, seated at the mouth of the river Alatamaha, lately built and fortified by general Oglethorpe. The island it stands upon is called St Simon's; and is about 13 miles in length, and 4 in breadth. W. Long. 81. 35. N. Lat. 31. 0.
