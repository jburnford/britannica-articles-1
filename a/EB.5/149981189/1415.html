GAD

GAD (anc. geog.), a district of the Transjordan Palestine, situated between Gilead and the kingdom of Bashan to the north, and the kingdom of the Amorites to the south; having the Jordan to the west, and bounded by various people on the east; so called from a tribe of that name.
