FRÆNUM Penis

sub_entry · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRÆNUM Penis, a slender ligament, whereby the prepuce is tied to the lower part of the glans of the penis. Nature varies in the make of this part; it being so short in some, that unless divided it would not admit of perfect erection. There is also a kind of little frænus, fastened to the lower part of the clitoris.
