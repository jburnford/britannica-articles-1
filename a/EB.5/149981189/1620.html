GAULANITIS, or GAULONITIS, (Josephus); (anc. geog.) according to the different manner of writing the capital, Golan or Gaulon; the extreme part of Bactrian to the south, and bordering on the tribe of Gad. It was divided into the Superior, which to the east extended to Arabia; and into the Inferior, which lay on the lake of Genesareth. (Josephus).
