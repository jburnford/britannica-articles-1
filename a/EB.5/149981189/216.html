EXOTIC

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXOTIC, a term properly signifying foreign or extraneous, i. e. brought from a remote or strange country. In which sense we sometimes say exotic or barbarous terms or swords, &c. The word is derived from the Greek ex, extra, "without, on the outside."
