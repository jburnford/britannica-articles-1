FATHEMITES

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FATHEMITES, FATEMITES, or FATHIMITES, the descendants of Mahomet by Fathema, or Fatima, his daughter. They never enjoyed the khalifat of Mecca or Bagdad, but reigned in Barbary and Egypt. See the history of these countries.
