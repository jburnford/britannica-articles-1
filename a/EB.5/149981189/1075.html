FORSTERA

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORSTERA, in botany: A genus of the triandria order, belonging to the gynandria class of plants. The perianthium is double; the exterior one beneath three-leaved; the interior one above, and six-cleft; the corolla tubular.
