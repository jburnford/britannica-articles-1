FLUMMERY, a wholesome sort of jelly made of oat-meal.

The manner of preparing it is as follows. Put three large handfuls of finely ground oat-meal to sleep, for 24 hours, in two quarts of fair water: then pour off the clear water, and put two quarts of fresh water to it.
