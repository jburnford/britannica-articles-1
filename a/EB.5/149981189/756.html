FIRING-IRON

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FIRING-IRON, in farriery, an instrument not unlike the blade of a knife; which being made red hot, is applied to a horse's hams, or other places standing in need of it, as in preternatural swellings, farcy, knots, &c. in order to discuss them.
