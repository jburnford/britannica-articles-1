GALLINACEUS LAPIS

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLINACEUS LAPIS, a glossy substance produced by volcanic fires; the same with the lapis obsidianus of the ancients. A kind of it is brought from Paris, of a beautiful black, resembling the colour of a large crow in that country named gallinaceo.
