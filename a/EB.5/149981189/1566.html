GARLAND also denotes ornaments of flowers, fruits, and leaves, intermixed; anciently much used at the gates of temples, where feasts and solemn rejoicings

were held; or at any other place where marks of public joy or gaiety were required, as at triumphal arches, tournaments, &c.
