FORLORN-HOPE

article · 344 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORLORN-HORE, in the military art, signifies men detached from several regiments, or otherwise appointed, to make the first attack in day of battle; or, at a siege, to storm the counterscarp, mount the breach, or the like. They are so called from the great danger they are unavoidably exposed to; but the word is old, and begins to be obsolete.
