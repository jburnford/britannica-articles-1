GMELINA, in botany: A genus of the angiospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 40th order, Perforata. The calyx is nearly quadridentate; the corolla campanulated or bell-shaped; there are two bipartite and two simple anthers; the fruit is a plum with a bilocular kernel.
