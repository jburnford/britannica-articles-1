GALENA

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALENA, a name given by mineralists to a species of poor lead-ore. It was also the original name given by Andromachus to the theriaca, from its effect in bringing on a pleasing calm over the blood and spirits on taking it.
