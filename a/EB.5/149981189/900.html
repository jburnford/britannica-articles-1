FLOWERS

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLOWERS, in the animal economy, denote women's monthly purgations or menses.—Nicod derives the word in this sense from fluere, q. d. fluous. Others will have the name occasioned hence, that women do not conceive till they have had their flowers; so that these are a sort of forerunners of their fruit.
