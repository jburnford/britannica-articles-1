GLUCKSTADT

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GLUCKSTADT, a strong and considerable town of Germany, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and duchy of Holstein, with a strong castle, and subject to Denmark. It is seated on the river Elbe, near its mouth; E. Long. 9. 15. N. Lat. 52. 53.
