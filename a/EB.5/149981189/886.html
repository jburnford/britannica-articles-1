FLORIST

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLORIST, a person curious or skilled in flowers; their kinds, names, characters, culture, &c. It is also applied to an author who writes what is called the flora of any particular place, that is, a catalogue of the plants and trees which are found spontaneously growing there.
