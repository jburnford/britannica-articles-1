GENIPPA

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GENIPPA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 30th order, Consorta. The corolla is wheel-shaped; the stigma club-shaped; the berry bilocular; the seeds nestling in a carnosous heart-shaped substance.
