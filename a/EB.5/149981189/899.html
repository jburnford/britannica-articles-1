FLOWERS

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLOWERS, in chemistry, are the finest and most subtle parts of dry bodies, raised by fire, into the vessel's head and aludel; and adhering to them in form of a fine powder or dust. Such are the flowers of sulphur, benjamin, &c.
