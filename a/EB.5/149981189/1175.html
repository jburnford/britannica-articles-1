FRANKENDAL

FRANKENDAL, a strong town of Germany, in the dominions of the Elector Palatine. It was taken by the Spaniards in 1623, by the Swedes in 1632, and burnt by the French in 1688. E. Long. 8. 29. N. Lat. 49. 28.
