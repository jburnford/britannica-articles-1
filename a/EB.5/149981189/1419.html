GADARENORUM AGER

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GADARENORUM AGER (anc. geog.), the country of the Gadarenes, called by Matthew the country of the Gergesens, because it was a district that lay between Gadara and Gergesa, otherwise called Gerasa, both which lay within the Decapolis on the other side Jordan.
