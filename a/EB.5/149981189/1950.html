GNIDIA

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GNIDIA, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the octandria class of plants. The calyx is funnel-shaped and quadrifid, with four petals inserted into it; there is one seed somewhat resembling a berry.
