FORDWICH

article · 386 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORDWICH, a town of Kent, called in the Doomsday-Book "the little Borough of Fordwich," is a member of the port of Sandwich, and was anciently incorporated by the style of the Barons of the town of Fordwich, but more lately by the name of the mayor, jurats, and commonalty, who enjoy the same privileges as the cinque-ports. This place is famous for excellent trouts in its river Stour.
