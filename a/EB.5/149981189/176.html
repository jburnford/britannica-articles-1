EXECUTORY

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXECUTORY, in law, is where an estate in fee, that is made by deed or fine, is to be executed afterwards by entry, livery, or writ. Leases for years, annuities, conditions, &c. are termed inheritances executory.
