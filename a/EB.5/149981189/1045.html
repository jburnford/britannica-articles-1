FORLI, an ancient and considerable town of Italy, and capital of a territory of the same name, in Romagna, with a bishop's see. The public structures are very handsome; and it is seated in a fertile, healthy, and pleasant country, 10 miles south-east of Faenza, and 45 north-east of Florence. E. Long. 12. 1. N. Lat. 44. 28.
