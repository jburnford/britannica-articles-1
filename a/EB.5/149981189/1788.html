GESTURE

Cross-references: DECLAMATION

GESTURE, a motion of the body, intended to signify some idea or passion of the mind. It consists principally in the action of the hands and face; and may be defined, a suitable conformity of the motions of the countenance, and of several parts of the body, in speaking to the subject matter of the discourse. See DECLAMATION, and ORATORY, n° 130, 131.
