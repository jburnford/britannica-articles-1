And lire-money is reduced to exchange-money by dividing it by 5½.

In Milan, 1 crown = 80
In Naples, 1 ducat = 86
In Leghorn, 1 piastra = 20
In Sicily, 1 crown = 127½
12 Denari make 1 foldi s. d.
20 Soldi make 1 piastra = 4 6 Ster.

Books and accounts are kept in piastres, foldi, and denari. The piastra here consists of 6 lires, and the lire contains 20 foldi, and the foldi 12 denari, and consequently exchange money is 6 times better than lire money. The course of exchange is from 47 d. to 58 d. Sterling per piastra.
