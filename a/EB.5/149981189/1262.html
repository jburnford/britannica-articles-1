FRIGATOON

article · 166 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRIGATOON, a Venetian vessel, commonly used in the Adriatic, built with a square stern, and without any fore-mast, having only a main-mast, mizen-mast, and bow-sprit.
