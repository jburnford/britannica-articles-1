EXIGENTERS

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXIGENTERS, four officers in the court of common-pleas, who make all exigents and proclamations, in all actions where process of outlawry lies. Writs of superedeas, as well as the prothonotaries, upon exigents, were likewise drawn up in their office.
