FLORENCE, an ancient piece of English gold-coin. Florentis Every pound-weight of standard-gold was to be coined into 50 Florences to be current at six shillings each; all which made in tale 15 pounds; or into a proportionate number of half-Florences or quarter-pieces, by indenture of the mint: 18 Edw. III.
