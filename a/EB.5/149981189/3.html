ETHNOPHRONES

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ETHNOPYRONES, in antiquity, a sect of heretics in the seventh century, who made a profession of Christianity, but joined thereto all the ceremonies and follies of paganism, as judicial astrology, fortileges, auguries, and other divinations.
