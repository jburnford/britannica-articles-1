FORISFAMILIATION

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORISFAMILIATION, in law. When a child, upon receiving a portion from his father, or otherwise, renounces his legal title to any further share of his father's succession, he is said to be forisfamiliarized.
