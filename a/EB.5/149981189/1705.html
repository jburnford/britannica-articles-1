GENET

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GENET, GENNET, or Jennet, in the manège, denotes a small-sized well-proportioned Spanish horse.

To ride a la genette, is to ride after the Spanish fashion, so short, that the spurs bear upon the horse's flank.
