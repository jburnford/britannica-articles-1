FUSIL

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FUSIL, in heraldry, a bearing of a chomboidal figure, longer than the lozenge, and having its upper and lower angles more acute and sharp than the other two in the middle. It is called in Latin fusus, "a spindle," from its shape.
