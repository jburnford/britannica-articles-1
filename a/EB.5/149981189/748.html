FIRE-LOCK

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FIRE-LOCK, or Fusil, a small gun which fires with a flint. It is distinguished from an old musket, or match-lock, which was fired with a match. The fire-lock is now in common use in the European armies.
