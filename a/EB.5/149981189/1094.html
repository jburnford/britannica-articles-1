FOTHER

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FOTHER, or FODDER, is a weight of lead, containing eight pigs, and every pig one and twenty stone and a half; so that it is about a ton or common cartload. Among the plumbers in London, it is nineteen hundred and a half; and at the mines it is two and twenty hundred and a half. The word is of Teutonic origin, from fuder.
