FRIGID

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRIGID (frigidus), in a general sense, denotes the quality of being cold. It is frequently applied to a jejunie fly, that is unanimated by any ornaments, and consequently without any force or vigour.
