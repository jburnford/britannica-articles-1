EUBAGES

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUBAGES, an order of priests or philosophers among the ancient Celts or Gauls: some will have the cubages to be the same with the druids and farinids of Diodorus; and others, that they were the same with what Strabo calls EVATES.
