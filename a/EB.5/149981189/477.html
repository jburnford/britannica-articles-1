FAT

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FAT, or VAT, is used also for several utensils: as, 1. A great wooden vessel, employed for the measuring of malt, and containing a quarter or eight bushels. 2. A large brewing vessel, used by brewers to run their wort in.

3. A leaden pan or vessel for the making of salt at Droitwich.
