PHYSICAL

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHYSICAL, something belonging to, or really existing in, nature. In this sense we say a physical point, in opposition to a mathematical one, which only exists in the imagination; a physical substance or body, in opposition to spirit, or metaphysical substance, &c.
