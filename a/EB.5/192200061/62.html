PATOMACK

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PATOMACK, a large river of North America, in Virginia, which rises in the Alleghany mountains, separates Virginia from Maryland, and falls into Chesapeake bay. It is about seven miles broad, and is navigable for near 200 miles.
