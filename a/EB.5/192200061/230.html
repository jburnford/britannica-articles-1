PELTARIA

PELTARIA, in botany: A genus of the filiculosa order, belonging to the tetradynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 39th order, Siliquosa. The filicula is entire, and nearly orbiculate, compressed-plane, and not opening.
