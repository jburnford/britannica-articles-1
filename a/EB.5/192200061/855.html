PINNACLE

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PINNACLE, in architecture, the top or roof of an house, terminating in a point. This kind of roof among the ancients was appropriated to temples; their ordinary roofs were all flat, or made in the platform way.
