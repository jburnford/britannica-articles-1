PELOPS

article · 367 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PELOPS, in fabulous history, the son of Tantalus king of Phrygia, went into Elis, where he married Hippodamia the daughter of Enomau king of that country; and became so powerful, that all the territory which lies beyond the Isthmus, and comprises a considerable part of Greece, was called Peloponnesus, that is, the island of Pelops, from his name and the word Nesos.
