PERONNE

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERONNE, a strong town of France, in Picardy, capital of Santerre. It is said never to have been taken, though often besieged. It is seated on the river Somme, in E. Long. 3. 1. N. Lat. 44. 50.
