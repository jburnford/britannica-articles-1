PHARMACOPOLA, or PHARMACOPOLUS, an
apothecary; or a person who prepares and sells medi-

cines. (See APOTHECARY). The word is seldom used
but by way of ridicule. It is formed from pharmakon and
polon, to sell. See Horace, Satire 2. lib. i. ver. 1.
