PINDUS

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PINDUS (anc. geog.), not a single mountain, but a chain of mountains, inhabited by different people of Epirus and Thessaly; separating Macedonia, Thessaly, and Epirus: An extensive chain, having Macedonia to the north, the Perrhæbi to the west, the Dolopes to the south, and the mountain itself of Thessaly (Strabo).
