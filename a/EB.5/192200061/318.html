PEOR

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PEOR, was a city of the tribe of Judah, which is not read in the Hebrew, nor in the Vulgate, but only in the Greek of the Septuagint (Josh. xv. 60.) Eusebius says it was near Bethlehem, and Jerom adds, that in his time it was called Paora.
