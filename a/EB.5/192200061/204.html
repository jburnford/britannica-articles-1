PELASGIA (Pliny); the ancient name of Lesbos; so called from the Pelasgi, its first inhabitants (Diodorus Siculus.) Also the ancient name of Peloponnesus, from Pelasgius, a native of the country (Nicolau Damascenus, Ephorus).
