PENTHORUM

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PENTHORUM, in botany; a genus of the pentagynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. The calyx is quinquefid; there are either five petals or none; the capsule is five-pointed and quinquelocular.
