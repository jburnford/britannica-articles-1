PETAGUEL

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PETAGUEL, a territory of South America, in Brazil, bounded on the north by Dele; on the east by the sea; on the south by the captainship of Rio-Grande; and on the west by Tupuys. It contains mines of silver.
