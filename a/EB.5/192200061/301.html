PENTANDRIA

Cross-references: BOTANY

PENTANDRIA (from πέντε five, and άνδρ a man or husband); the name of the fifth class in LINNÆUS'S sexual method, consisting of plants which have hermaphrodite flowers, with five stamens or male organs. See BOTANY, p. 430.
