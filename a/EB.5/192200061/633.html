PHILIPPISTS, a sect or party among the Lutherans; the followers of Philip Melancthon. He had strenuously opposed the Ubiquists, who arose in his time; and the dispute growing still hotter after his death, the university of Wittemberg, who espoused Melancthon's opinion, were called by the Flaccians, who attacked it, Philippists.
