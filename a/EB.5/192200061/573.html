PHARMACOCHÉMIA

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHARMACOCHÉMIA, means that part of the chemical art which treats of the preparation of medicines. It is so named by way of distinction from that chemistry which is wholly employed about the transmutation of metals by means of the philosopher's stone; this being called spagirico-chemia.
