PICKETS

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PICKETS, in fortification, stakes sharp at one end, and sometimes shod with iron, used in laying out the ground, of about three feet long; but, when used for pining the fascines of a battery, they are from three to five feet long.
