PENNY-WEIGHT, a Troy weight, containing 24 grains; each grain weighing a grain of wheat gathered out of the middle of the ear, well dried. The name took its rise hence, that this was anciently the weight of one of our ancient silver pennies. See PENNY.

Twenty of these penny-weights make an ounce Troy.
