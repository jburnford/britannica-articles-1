PWAT

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PWAT, or Pivot, a foot or shoe of iron or other metal, usually conical or terminating in a point, whereby a body, intended to turn round, bears on another fixed at rest, and performs its revolutions. The pivot usually bears or turns round in a sole, or piece of iron or brass hollowed to receive it.
