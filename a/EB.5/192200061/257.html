PENETRALE

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PENETRALE, a sacred room or chapel in private houses, which was set apart for the worship of the household gods among the ancient Romans. In temples also there were penstralia, or apartments of distinguished sanctity, where the images of the gods were kept, and certain solemn ceremonies performed.
