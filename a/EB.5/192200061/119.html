PAURÆDASTYLÆ

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAURÆDASTYLÆ, in natural history, the name of a genus of perfect crystals with double pyramids, and no intermediate column, composed of 12 planes, or two hexangular pyramids joined base to base.
