PHRENETIC

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHRENETIC, a term used to denote those who,
without being absolutely mad, are subject to such strong
fallacies of imagination as in some measure pervert their
judgment, and cause them to act in a way different
from the more rational part of mankind.
