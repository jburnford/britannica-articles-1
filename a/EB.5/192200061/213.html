PELETHRONII

article · 352 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PELETHRONII, a name or epithet given to the Lapithæ, either because they inhabited the town of Pelethronium at the foot of mount Pelion in Thessaly, or because one of their number bore the name of Pelethronius. It is to them, we are told, that mankind are indebted for the invention of the bit with which they tamed their horses with so much dexterity.
