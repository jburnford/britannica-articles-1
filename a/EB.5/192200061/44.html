PATEX NOSTER

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PATEX NOSTER, islands of Asia, in the East Indian sea, so called because of the great number of rocks, which sailors have likened to the heads with which the Papists tell their pater-noster. They abound in corn and fruits, and are very populous.
