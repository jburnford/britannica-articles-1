PIERRE

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PIERRE (St), is a large river in North America, scarcely inferior to the Rhine or the Danube, and navigable almost to its source. Together with many other large streams, it falls into the great river Mississippi.
