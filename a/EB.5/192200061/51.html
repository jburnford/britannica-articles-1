PATHOGNOMONIC

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PATHOGNOMONIC, among physicians, an appellation for a symptom, or concurrence of symptoms, that are inseparable from a distemper, and are found in that only, and in no other.
