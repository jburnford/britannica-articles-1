PETROSA OSSA

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PETROSA OSSA, in anatomy, a name given to the fourth and fifth bones of the cranium, called also ossa temporum and ossa squamosa; the substance whereof, as their first and last names express, is squamous and very hard.
