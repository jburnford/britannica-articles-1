CONGESTION

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONGESTION, in medicine, a mass or collection of humours, crowded together and hardened in any part of the body, and there forming a preternatural tumor.

Congestion is effected by little and little: in which it differs from diffusion, which is more sudden.
