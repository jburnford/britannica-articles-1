CONSIGNMENT of Goods

sub_entry · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONSIGNMENT of Goods, in commerce, is the delivering or making them over to another: thus, goods are said to be consigned to a factor, when they are sent to him to be sold, &c.; or when a factor sends back goods to his principal, they are said to be consigned to him.
