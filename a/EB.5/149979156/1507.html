COUCHANT

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUCHANT, in heraldry, is understood of a lion, or other beast, when lying down, but with his head raised; which distinguishes the posture of couchant from dormant, wherein he is supposed quite stretched out and asleep.
