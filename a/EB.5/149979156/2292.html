DEAD-MENS-EYES

article · 351 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: PLATE CLXV

DEAD-MENT-EYES, in the sea-language, a kind of blocks with many holes in them, but no shevers, whereby the shrowds are fastened to the chains; the crow-feet receive also through these holes: and, in some ships, the main-stays are set tight in them; but then they have only one hole, through which the lanyards are passed several times.
