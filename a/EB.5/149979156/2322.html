DECAN

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DECAN, a kingdom of Asia, in the peninsula on this side the Ganges, bounded on the south by the kingdom of Binagar, on the west by the ocean, on the north by Mogulistan, and on the east by the mountains which separate it from Golconda.
