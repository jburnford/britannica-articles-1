CRANK

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRANK, a contrivance in machines, in manner of an elbow, only of a square form, projecting out from an axis or spindle; and serving, by its rotation, to raise and fall the pistons of engines for raising water or the like.
