CULEUS

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CULEUS, in Roman antiquity, the largest measure of capacity for things liquid, containing 20 amphoræ, or 40 urnæ. It contained 143 gallons 3 pints, English wine-measure; and was 11.095 solid inches.
