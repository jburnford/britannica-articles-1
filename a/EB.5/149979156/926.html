CONDITION

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONDITION, in the civil law, a clause of obligation stipulated as an article of a treaty or a contract; or in a donation of a testament, legacy, &c. in which last case a donee does not lose his donative if it be charged with any dishonest or impossible conditions.
