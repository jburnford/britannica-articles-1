COELIOBRIGA

COELIOBRIGA (anc. geog.), a town of the Bracari in the Hither Spain, to the south of Bracara Augusta, the north of the Durius, and not far from the Atlantic; a municipium (Coin). Now thought to be Barcelos, a town of Entre Minho y Duero. W. Long. 9. 15. Lat. 41. 20.
