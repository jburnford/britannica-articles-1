CRUCITA

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRUCITA, in botany, a genus of the digynia order, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants, and in the natural method ranking with those the order of which is doubtful. The interior calyx is tetraphyllous, the exterior calyx triphyllous; there is no corolla, and only one seed.
