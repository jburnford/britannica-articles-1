DAHOMY

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DAHOMÉ, a kingdom of Africa, on the coast of Guinea, to the north of Whidah, or Fida. The king of this country conquered Whidah, and very much disturbed the slave-trade of the Europeans.
