CONDOM

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONDOM, a town of Gascony in France, capital of the Condomois, with a bishop's see. It is but a poor place, and the trade is very small. It is seated on the river Gélise, in E. Long. 0. 22. N. Lat. 44.
