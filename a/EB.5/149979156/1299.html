CORISPERMUM

CORISPERMUM, TICKSEED: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the monandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 12th order, Holocarces. There is no calyx; two petals, and one oval naked seed. There are two species; but none of them are remarkable for their beauty or any other quality.
