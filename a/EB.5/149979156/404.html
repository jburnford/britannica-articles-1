COATING of Vials, Panes of Glass

sub_entry · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ELECTRICITY

COATING of Vials, Panes of Glass, &c. among electricians, is usually performed by covering the outside of the vial with tin-foil, brass or gold-leaf, &c. and filling its inside with loose pieces of brass-leaf, by which means it becomes capable of being charged. See ELECTRICITY.
