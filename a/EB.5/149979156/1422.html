CORRUNNA

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORRUNNA, or GROVNE, a port-town of Galicia in Spain, situated on a fine bay of the Atlantic ocean, about 32 miles north of Compostella: W. Long. 9. o. and N. Lat. 43. o.
