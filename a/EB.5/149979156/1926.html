CUMA

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CUMA, or CUMÆ (anc. geog.), a town of Æolia in Asia Minor. The inhabitants have been accused of stupidity for not laying a tax upon all the goods which entered their harbour during 300 years. They were called Cumani.
