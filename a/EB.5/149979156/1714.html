CREPITATION

CREPITATION, that noise which some salts make over the fire in calcination, called also detonation.

CREPITATION is also used in surgery, for the noise made by the ends or pieces of bones, when the sur-

geon moves a limb to assure himself by his ear of the Crepundia existence of a fracture.
