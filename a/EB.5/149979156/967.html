CONFORMITY

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONFORMITY, in the schools, is the congruency, or relation of agreement between one thing and another; as between the measure and the thing measured, the object and the understanding, the thing and the division thereof, &c.
