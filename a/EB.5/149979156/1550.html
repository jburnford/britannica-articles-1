COUNTER-Point

Cross-references: COMPOSITION

COUNTER-Point, in music: a term derived from the Latin preposition contra and the verb pungere: because the musical characters by which the notes in each part are signified are placed in such a manner each with respect to each as to show how the parts answer one another. See COMPOSITION.
