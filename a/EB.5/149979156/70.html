CIRCLES of perpetual Occultation

sub_entry · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIRCLES of perpetual Occultation, is another circle at a like distance from the equator, and contains all those stars which never appear in our hemisphere. The stars situated between these circles alternately rise and set at certain times.
