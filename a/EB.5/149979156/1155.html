CONVENTUAL, something belonging to a convent or monastery. See MONASTERY, and COENOBIOTE.

CONVENTUAL is particularly used for a religious who actually resides in a convent; in contradistinction to those who are only guests, or are entertained there,

or in possession of benefices depending on the house. See MONK.
