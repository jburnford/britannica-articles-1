DECUMARIA

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DECUMARIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the dodecandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under those of which the order is doubtful. The calyx is decaphyllous, superior; the petals ten; the fruit unknown.
