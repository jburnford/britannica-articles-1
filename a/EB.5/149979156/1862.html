CRYPTOGAMIA

CRYPTOGAMIA, (from κρυπτο, occultus, "concealed," and γαμω, nuptia, "nuptials"), the 24th class in the Linnean system, comprehending those plants whose fructification is concealed, either through minuteness, or within the fruit. See BOTANY, the Scheme and Explanation, Vol. III. p. 430.
