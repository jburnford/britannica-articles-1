COGNI

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COGNI, an ancient and strong town of Carmanian in Turkey in Asia, and the residence of a beglerbeg. It is seated in a pleasant country, abounding in corn, fruits, pulse, and cattle. Here are sheep whose tails weigh 30 pounds. E. Long. 32. 56. N. Lat. 37. 56.
