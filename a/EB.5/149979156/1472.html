COSTA-RICCA

article · 333 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COSTA-RICCA, a province of North America in New Spain, and in the audience of Guatemala, bounded on the north-east by the northern ocean, on the south-west by the south sea, on the north-west by Nicaragua, and on the south-east by Veragua. The soil is not very fertile, though there is plenty of cattle. Carthage is the capital town.
