COMMODITY

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMODITY, in a general sense, denotes all
sorts of wares and merchandizes whatsoever that a per-
son deals or trades in.

Staple Commodities, such wares and merchandizes
as are commonly and readily sold in a market or ex-
ported abroad; being for the most part the proper
produce or manufacture of the country.
