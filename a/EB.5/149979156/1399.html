CORRIGIOLA

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORRIGIOLA, in botany: A genus of the trigynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 54th order, Miscellanea. The calyx is pentaphyllous; the petals five; and one three-cornered seed.
