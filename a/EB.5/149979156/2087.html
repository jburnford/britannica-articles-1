CYNOSSEMA

CYNOSSEMA, the tomb of Hecuba, on the promontory Massilia, over against Sigeum, in the south of the Chersonesus Thracia; named either from the figure of a dog, to which she was changed, or from her sad reverse of fortune (Pliny, Mela).
