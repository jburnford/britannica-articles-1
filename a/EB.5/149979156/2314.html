DEBRUIZED

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEBRUIZED, in heraldry, a term peculiar to the English, by which is intimated the grievous restraint of any animal, debarred of its natural freedom, by any of the ordinaries being laid over it.
