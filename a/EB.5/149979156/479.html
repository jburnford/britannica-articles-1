COESFELDT

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COESFELDT, a town of Germany, in Westphalia, and in the territories of the bishop of Munster, where he often resides. It is near the river Burkel, E. Long. 64. 2. N. Lat. 51. 58.
