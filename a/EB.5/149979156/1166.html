CONVEX

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONVEX, an appellation given to the exterior surface of gibbous or globular bodies; in opposition to the hollow inner surface of such bodies, which is called concave: thus we say, a convex frieze, lens, mirror, superlicies, &c.
