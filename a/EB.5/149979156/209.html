CLAUDIA

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLAUDIA, or Clodia Via (Ovid), was that road which, beginning at the Pons Milvius, joined the Flaminia, passing through Etruria, on the south side of the Lacus Sabatinus, and striking off from the Cassia, and leading to Luca (Antoine): large remains of it are to be seen above Bracciano (Holtenius).
