COOKERY, the art of preparing and dressing victuals for the table: An art, in its simplest and ordinary modes, sufficiently familiar to every housekeeper; and, in its luxurious refinements, too copiously detailed in manuals and directories published for the purpose to require any enlargement here, were it even a topic that at all deserved consideration in a work of this nature.
