COUNTER-Proof

sub_entry · 369 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-Proof, in rolling-press printing, a print taken off from another fresh printed; which by being passed through the press, gives the figure of the former, but inverted. To counter-prove, is also to pass a design in black lead, or red chalk, through the press, after having moistened with a sponge both that and the paper on which the counter-proof is to be taken.
