CRATER, Cup, in astronomy, a constellation.

of the southern hemisphere; whose stars, in Ptolemy's Catalogue, are seven; in Tycho's, eight; in Hevelius's, ten; in the Britannic Catalogue, thirty-one.

CRATER is also used to signify the mouth or opening of a volcano or burning mountain, from whence the fire is discharged. See VOLCANO.
