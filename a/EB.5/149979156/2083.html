CYNOPHONTIS

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYNOPHONTIS, in antiquity, a festival observed in the dog-days at Argos, and so called ἡμέρᾱ τοῦ κυνοφόντου, i.e. from killing dogs; because it was usual on this day to kill all the dogs they met with.
