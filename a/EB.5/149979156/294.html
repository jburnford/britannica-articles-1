CLERK of the Treasury

sub_entry · 354 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLERK of the Treasury, an officer belonging to the court of common pleas, who has the charge of keeping the records of the court, makes out all records of justi pium, and likewise all exemplifications of records being in the treasury. He has the fees due for all searches; and has under him an under keeper, who always keeps one key of the treasury-door.
