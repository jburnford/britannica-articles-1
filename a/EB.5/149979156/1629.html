CRAMERIA

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRAMERIA stalks divide into fine branchy heads of flowers. It is propagated by seeds sown in any common light earth in autumn or spring, where the plants are to remain, which, when two years old, will produce shoots fit for use, will multiply exceedingly by the roots, and continue for many years.
