CRATCHES

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRATCHES, in the manège, a swelling on the pastern, under the fetlock, and sometimes under the hoof; for which reason it is distinguished into the sinew cratches, which affect the sinew, and those upon the coronet, called quitter-bones.
