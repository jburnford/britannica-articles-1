CRUISE

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRUISE, from the German kruist, "across," signifies to cross to and fro, to sail up and down within a certain space of the sea, called the cruising latitude, in quest of vessels, or fleets of an enemy, &c.
