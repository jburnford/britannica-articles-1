CRITICAL DAYS and SYMPTOMS

Cross-references: MEDICINE-I

CRITICAL DAYS and SYMPTOMS, among physicians, are certain days and symptoms in the course of acute diseases, which indicate the patient's state, and determine him either to recover or grow worse. See MEDICINE-Index.
