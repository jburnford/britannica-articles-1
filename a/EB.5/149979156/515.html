COLAPIS

COLAPIS, COLOPS (anc. geog.), a river of Liburnia, which after a winding north-east course, falls into the Savus, at the Insula Segestica. Now the Cwps, the boundary of the Alps, running through Croatia into the Save. Colapiani, the people living on it (Pliny).
