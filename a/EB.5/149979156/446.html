COCYTUS

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COCYTUS, one of the rivers of hell, according to the theology of the poets. It has its name κωκύτης, from groaning and lamenting. Hence Milton,

Cocytus nam'd of lamentation loud,
Heard on the rucful stream.

It was a branch of the river Styx; and flowed, according to Horace, with a dull and languid stream.
