DIALYSIS

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIALYSIS, in grammar, a mark or character, consisting of two points, ··, placed over two vowels of a word, in order to separate them, because otherwise they would make them a diphthong, as Mosaic, &c.
