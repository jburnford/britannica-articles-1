CLEOBULUS

article · 158 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLEOBULUS, son of Evagoras, and one of the Grecian sages; he was valiant, a lover of learning, and an enemy to vice. Flourished about 560 years before Christ.
