CRURÆUS, or CRUREUS, Musculus

Cross-references: ANATOMY

CRURÆUS, or CRUREUS, Musculus, in anatomy, a fleshy mass, covering almost all the foreside of the os femoris, between the two vasti, which likewise cover the edges of this muscle on each side. See ANATOMY, Table of the Muscles.
