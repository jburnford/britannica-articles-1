COMPREHENSION

COMPREHENSION, in metaphysics, is that act of the mind whereby it apprehends or knows any object that is presented to it, on all the sides whereon it is capable of being apprehended or known. To comprehend a thing is defined by the schoolmen, rem aliquam totam et totaliter cognoscere.
