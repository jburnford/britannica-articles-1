IV. YELLOWS. 1. King's-yellow is the most useful and the most brilliant, levigated with spirits of wine, and compose the different teints as before directed. Yellow ochre and Naples yellow ground with spirits will make useful crayons. 2. Orange is produced with king's-yellow and vermilion ground together with spirits, and the teints formed as in other cases, but no great quantity of them is required.
