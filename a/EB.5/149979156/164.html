CLAMPETIA

CLAMPETIA (anc. geog.), a town of the Brutii, one of those which revolted from Hannibal, (Livy); called Lampetia by Polybius. Now Avantia, or Mantia, a town of Calabria Ultra, near the bay of Euphemia. E. Long. 16. 20. N. Lat. 39. 15.
