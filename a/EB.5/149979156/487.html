COGNAC

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COGNAC, a town of France in Angoumois, with a castle, where Francis I. was born. It is seated on the river Charante, in a very pleasant country, abounding in wine, and remarkable for excellent brandy. W. Long. 6. 10. N. Lat. 45. 42.
