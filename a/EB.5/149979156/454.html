CODIA, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the octandria class of plants. The calyx is tetraphyllous, with small oblong horizontal leaves; the corolla consists of four very small linear petals; the stamens are eight filaments twice as long as the calyx; the anthers are roundish.
