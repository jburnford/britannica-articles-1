DENUNCIATION, a solemn publication or pro-
mulgation of any thing.

All vessels of enemies are lawful prizes, after de-
nunciation or proclamation of war. The design of the
denunciation of excommunicated persons is, that the
sentence may be the more fully executed by the per-
son's being more known.
