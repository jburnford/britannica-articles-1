DEBIR

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEBIR (anc. geog.), a sacerdotal city of Palestine, near Hebron; but neither distance, nor point of the compass on which it lies, can be determined. It was anciently called Kariath-sepher or Kirjath-sepher, and Kirjath-jann (Joshua).—Another Debir in the tribe of Gad, beyond Jordan.
