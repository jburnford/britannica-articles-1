COUNTER-Scarp

sub_entry · 337 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-Scarp, in fortification, is properly the exterior talus or slop of the ditch; but it is often taken for the covered way and the glacis. In this sense we say, the enemy have lodged themselves on the counter-scarp. Angle of the counter-scarp, is that made by two sides of the counter-scarp meeting before the middle of the curtain.
