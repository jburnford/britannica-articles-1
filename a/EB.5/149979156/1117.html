CONTORTÆ, the name of the 30th order in Linnaeus's

næus's Fragments of a natural method, consisting of plants which have a single petal that is twisted or bent to one side. This order contains the following genera, viz. echites, cerbera, gardenia, genipa, microcnemum, nerium, periploca, rawolfia, tabernemontana, vinca, apocynum, asclepias, comeraria, ceropegia, cynanchum, plumeria, stapelia.
