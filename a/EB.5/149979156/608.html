COLMAR

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLMAR, a considerable town of France, in Upper Alsace, of which it is the capital. It has great privileges, and the Protestants have liberty of conscience. It is seated near the river Ill, in E. Long. 7. 16. N. Lat. 48. 5.
