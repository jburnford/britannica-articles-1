CLERK of the Effoins

sub_entry · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLERK of the Effoins, in the court of common pleas, keeps the effoin-roll, or enters effoins: he also provides parchment, cuts it into rolls, marks the number on them, delivers out all the rolls to every officer, and receives them again when written. See Essoin.
