CRUX, or ST CROIX, one of the Caribbee islands, situated about 60 miles south-east of Porto-Rico, and subject to Denmark. From being a perfect desert, it has begun to flourish exceedingly, being made a free port, and receiving great encouragement from government. W. Long. 64. 0. N. Lat. 17. 30.
