DEMI-Attici

DEMI-Attici, boroughs or larger villages of Attica. The Athenian tribes were distributed into Demi. Homer, in his catalogue, distinguishes the Athenians by the appellation Demos. And when Theseus prevailed on them to quit the country and settle at Athens, they still continued to frequent the Demi, and to perform their several religious ceremonies there (Pausanias, Livy).
