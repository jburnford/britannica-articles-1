COUNTRY-Dance

sub_entry · 415 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTRY-Dance is of English origin, though now transplanted into almost all the countries and courts of

County. Europe. There is no established rule for the composition of tunes to this dance, because there is in music no kind of time whatever which may not be measured by the motions common in dancing; and there are few song tunes of any note within the last century, that have not been applied to country-dances.
