CONSERVATOR of Seals Privileges

sub_entry · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONSERVATOR of Seals Privileges, at Campvere, is an officer belonging to the royal boroughs of Scotland, who takes care of the mercantile affairs of Scotland, agreeable to the staple contract between them and the States-General.
