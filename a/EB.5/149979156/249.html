CLEMENT VII. (Julius de Medicis), pope, memorable for his refusing to divorce Catharine of Arragon from Henry VIII.; and for the bull he published upon

upon the king's marriage with Anne Bolcyn; which, according to the Romish authors, lost him England. He died in 1534.
