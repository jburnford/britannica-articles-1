CYNTHUS

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYNTHUS (anc. geog.), a mountain of the island Delos, so high as to overshadow the whole island. On this mountain Latona brought forth Apollo and Diana: hence the epithet Cynthius (Virgil), and Cynthia (Lucan, Statius).
