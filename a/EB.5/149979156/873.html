CONCAVE GLASSES

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONCAVE GLASSES, such as are ground hollow, and are usually of a spherical figure, though they may be of any other, as parabolic, &c. All objects seen through concave glasses appear erect and diminished.
