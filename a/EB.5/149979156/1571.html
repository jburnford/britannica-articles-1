COUPAR

COUPAR, the name of a town in Scotland, capital of the county of Fife, situated about 10 miles west of St Andrew's: W. Long. 2. 40. N. Lat. 56. 20.—Coupar is also the name of a village in the shire of Angus, inhabited chiefly by weavers in the linen trade.
