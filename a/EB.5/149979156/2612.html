DESPOUILLE

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DESPOUILLE, in heraldry, the whole case, skin, or flough of a beast, with the head, feet, tail, and all appurtenances, so that being filled and fluffed it looks like the entire creature.
