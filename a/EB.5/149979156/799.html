COMPEIGNE

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMPEIGNE, a handsome town of the isle of France, in the county of Senlis, with a palace, or castle, where the king often resides. The maid of Orleans was taken prisoner here in 1430. It is seated on the river Oise, near a large forest. E. Long. 3. 12. N. Lat. 49. 25.
