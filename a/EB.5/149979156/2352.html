DECOLLATION, REREADING

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DECOLLATION, REREADING, a term seldom used but in the phrase decollation of St John Baptist; which denotes a painting, wherein is represented the Baptist's head struck off from his trunk; or the feast held in honour of that martyr.
