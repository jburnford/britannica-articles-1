CKICKLADE

article · 172 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CKICKLADE, a borough-town of Wiltshire, situated on the river Isis, about 26 miles south-west of Oxford. It sends two members to parliament. W. Long. 1. 55. N. Lat. 51. 35.
