COPHTIC Liturgies

sub_entry · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COPHTIC Liturgies are three; one attributed to Basil, another to St Gregory, and the third to Cyril: they are translated into Arabic for the use of the priests and people.
