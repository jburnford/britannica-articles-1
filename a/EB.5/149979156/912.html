CONCRETION

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONCRETION is also the act whereby soft bodies are

rendered hard; or an insensible motion of the particles of a fluid or soft body, whereby they come to a consistence. It is indifferently used for induration, condensation, congelation, and coagulation.
