CUPANIA

article · 434 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CUPANIA, in botany: A genus of the adelphia order, belonging to the monœcia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 38th order, Tricocca. The calyx of the male is triphyllous; the corolla pentapetalous; the staminal five. The calyx of the female triphyllous; the corolla tripetalous; the style trifid; and a pair of seeds. There is but one species, a native of America, and which possesses no remarkable property.
