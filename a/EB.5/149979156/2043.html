CYAXARES

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYAXARES II. is supposed by some to be the same as Darius the Mede. He was son of Astyages king of Media. He added seven provinces to his father's dominions, and made war against the Assyrians, whom Cyrus favoured.
