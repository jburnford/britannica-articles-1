COUCHER, or COUCHER, in our statutes, is used for a factor, or one that continues in some place or country for traffic; as formerly in Gascoigne, for the buying of wines. Anno 37. Edw. III. c. 16.

COUCHER is also used for the general book in which any religious house or corporation register their particular acts. Anno 3 and 4. Edw. VI. c. 10.

COUCHING of a CATARACT, in surgery. See SURGERY-Index.
