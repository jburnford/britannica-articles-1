CRITHOMANCY

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRITHOMANCY, a kind of divination, performed by considering the dough or matter of the cakes offered in sacrifice, and the meal strewed over the victims to be killed. Hence, in regard they ordinarily used barley-meal in these ceremonies, this kind of divination was called critthomaney, from critis barley, and divination.
