CORNUCOPIÆ

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORNUCOPIÆ, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the triandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 4th order, Gramina. The involucrum is monophyllous, funnel-shaped, crenated, and multiflorous; the calyx-bivalved; corolla one valved.
