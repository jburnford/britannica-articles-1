DENEB

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DENEB, an Arabic term signifying tail, used by astronomers to denote several fixed stars. Thus, deneb elect, signifies the bright star in the lion's tail. Deneb adigege, that in the iwan's tail, &c.
