CLERK of the Hamper

sub_entry · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLERK of the Hamper, or Hansper, an officer in chancery, whose business is to receive all money due to the king for the feals of charters, letters patent, commissions, and writs; also the fees due to the officers for enrolling and examining t. m.
