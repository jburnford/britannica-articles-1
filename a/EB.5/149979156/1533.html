COUNSELLOR, in general, a person who advises another: thus we say, a counsellor at law, a privy counsellor, &c.

COUNSELLOR at Law, a person retained by a client to plead his cause in a public court of judicature. See ADVOCATE, BARRISTER, COUNSEL, and SERJEANT.
