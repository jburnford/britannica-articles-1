DENNIS, or ST DENNIS

DENNIS, or ST DENNIS, a famous town of the Isle of France, with a Benedictine abbey, wherein are the tombs of the kings of France, with a considerable treasure. E. Long. 2. 26. N. Lat. 48. 56.
