To CROWD

sub_entry · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

To CROWD, in the sea-language, is to carry an extraordinary force of sail upon a ship, in order to ac-

celerate her course on some important occasion; as in Crowland, pursuit of, or flight from, an enemy; to escape any Crowland, immediate danger, &c.
