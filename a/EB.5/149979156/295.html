CLERK of the Warrants

sub_entry · 391 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLERK of the Warrants, an officer of the common pleas, whose business is to enter all warrants of attorney for plaintiffs and defendants in suit; and to enrol deeds of bargain and sale, that are acknowledged in court, or before a judge. His office is likewise to effreat into the exchequer all issues, fines, effreats, and amercements,

amercements, which grow due to the crown in that court.
