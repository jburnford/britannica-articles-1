COOTWICH

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COOTWICH (John), doctor of laws, was born at Utrecht, and spent great part of his life in travelling. He published in Latin, in 1619, an account of his journey from Jerusalem and from Syria; which is very scarce and in high esteem. Time of his death uncertain.
