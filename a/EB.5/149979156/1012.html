CONNOISSEUR

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONNOISSEUR, a French term, of late used in English: it literally denotes a person well versed in any thing; being formed of the verb connoître, "to know, understand." Hence it comes to be used in our language for a critic, or person who is a thorough judge or master in any way, particularly in matters of painting and sculpture.
