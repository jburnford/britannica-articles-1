PSEUDONYMUS

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PSEUDONYMUS, among critics, an author who publishes a book under a false or feigned name; as crypto-nymus is given to him who publishes one under a disguised name, and anonymous to him who publishes without any name at all.
