QUADI

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUADI, (Tacitus); a people of Germany, situated to the south-east of the mountains of Bohemia, on the banks of the Danube, and extending as far as the river Marus, or March, running by Moravia, which country they occupied.
