PROVEND

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PROVEND, or PROVENDER, originally signified a kind of vessel containing the measure of corn daily given to a horse, or other beast of labour, for his subsistence; but is now generally used to signify the food for cattle, whatever it is.
