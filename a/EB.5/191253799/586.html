PRETORIAN GUARDS

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRETORIAN GUARDS, in Roman antiquity, were the emperor's guards, who at length were increased to 10,000: they had this denomination, according to some, from their being stationed at a place called Pretorium: their commander was styled praefectus praetorii.
