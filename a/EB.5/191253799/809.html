PRUNELLA

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRUNELLA, in botany: A genus of the gymnospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 12th order, holocarum. The filaments are bifurcated, with an anthera only on one point; the stigma is bifid.
