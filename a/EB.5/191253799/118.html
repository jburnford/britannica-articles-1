PLUSH

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLUSH, in commerce, &c. a kind of stuff, having a sort of velvet knap or flag on one side, composed regularly of a woof of a single woollen thread and a double warp; the one wool, of two threads twisted; the other goats or camels hair; though there are some plushes entirely of worsted, and others composed wholly of hair.
