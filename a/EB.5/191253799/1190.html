RAMMER

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RAMMER, an instrument used for driving down stones or piles into the ground; or for beating the earth, in order to render it more solid for a foundation.

RAMMER of a Gun, the Gun-stick; a rod used in charging of a gun, to drive home the powder, as also the shot, and the wad which keeps the shot from rolling out.
