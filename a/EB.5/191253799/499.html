PRECIE, (precis, "early,") the name of the 21st order in Linnaeus's fragments of a natural method; consisting of primrose, an early flowering plant, and a few genera which agree with it in habit and structure, though not always in the character or circumstance expressed in the title. See BOTANY, p. 461. col. 2.
