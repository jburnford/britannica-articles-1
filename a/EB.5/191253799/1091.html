QUINTEN

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUINTEN, a town of France, in Bretagne, with the title of a duchy, and a handsome castle. It is seated in a valley near the river Guy, and near a large forest of the same name, eight miles south of St Brieux, and 200 west of Paris. W. Long. 2. 40. N. Lat. 48. 26.
