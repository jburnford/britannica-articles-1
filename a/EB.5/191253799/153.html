POINTING

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POINTING, in grammar, the art of dividing a discourse, by points, into periods and members of periods, in order to show the proper pauses to be made in reading, and to facilitate the pronunciation and understanding thereof. See the article PUNCTUATION.
