PORTÆ-ROMANÆ

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORTÆ-ROMANÆ (anc. geog.), according to Pliny, Romulus left but three, or at most four, gates of Rome: afterwards, on enlarging the Pomæria, or compass of the city, they amounted to 37.
