PROVOST

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PROVOST, or Prevost, in the king's stables; his office is to attend at court, and hold the king's stirrup when he mounts his horse. There are four provosts of this kind, each of whom attends in his turn, monthly.
