PORTA-ANGUSTA

PORTA-ANGUSTA (anc. geog.), mentioned only by Ptolemy; a town of the Vaccæi in the Hither Spain; thought by some to be Torre Quemada, in Old Castile; by others Los Valvases, a village between Burgos and Torre Quemada.
