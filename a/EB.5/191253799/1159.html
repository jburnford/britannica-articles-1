RAGUSA, a city of Dalmatia, and capital of Ragusen. It is about two miles in circumference, is pretty well built, and strong by situation, having an inaccessible mountain on the land-side, and on the side of the

sea a strong fort. It has an archbishop's see and a republic, and has a doge like that of Venice, but he continues a month only in his office. It carries on a considerable trade with the Turks, and is 60 miles northwest of Scutari, and 110 north of Brindisi. E. Long. 18.10. N. Lat. 42.50.
