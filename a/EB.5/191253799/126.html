PLAYING

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLAYING, in the sea language, the act of making, or endeavouring to make, a progress against the direction of the wind. Hence a ship that advances well in her course in this manner of sailing, is said to be a good pleyer.

Plymouth. plyer. See the articles BEATING, PITCHING, and TACKING.
