QUOIN

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUOIN, or COIN, on board a ship, a wedge fastened on the deck close to the breach of the carriage

of a gun, to keep it firm up to the ship's side. Cantic quoina are short three-legged quoina put between casks to keep them steady.
