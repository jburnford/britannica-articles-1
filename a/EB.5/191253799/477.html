PRATIQUE

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRATIQUE, or PRATTIC, in commerce, a negotiation or communication of commerce which a merchant vessel obtains in the port it arrives in and the countries it discovers: hence to obtain a pratique, is to obtain liberty to frequent a port, to go ashore, to buy and sell, &c.
