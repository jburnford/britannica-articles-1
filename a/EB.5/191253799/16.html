PLANTATION

Cross-references: COLONY

PLANTATION, in the West Indies, denotes a spot of ground which a planter, or person arrived in a new colony, pitches on to cultivate for his own use, or is assigned for that purpose. However, the term plantation is often used in a term synonymous with colony. See COLONY.
