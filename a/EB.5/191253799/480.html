PRAXAGORAS

article · 323 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRAXAGORAS, a native of Athens, at 92 years of age composed the History of the Kings of Athens, in two books; and at 22 the Life of Constantine the Great, in which, though a pagan, he speaks very advantageously of that prince. He also wrote the History of Alexander the Great. He lived under Constantine about the year 345.
