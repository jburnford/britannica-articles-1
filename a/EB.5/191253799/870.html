PUERILITY

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PUERILITY, in discourse, is defined by Longinus to be a thought which, by being too far fetched, becomes flat and insipid. Puerility, he adds, is the common fault of those who affect to say nothing but what is brilliant and extraordinary.
