POTHOS

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POTHOS, in botany; a genus of the polyandria order, belonging to the gynandria class of plants. The spathe, or sheath is a simple spadix covered; there is no calyx, but four petals, and as many stamens; the berries dispermous.
