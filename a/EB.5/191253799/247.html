POLYMNIA

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLYMNIA, in botany: A genus of the polygamia necessaria order, belonging to the syngencia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 45th order, Compositæ. The receptacle is paleaceous; there is no pappus; the exterior calyx is tetraphyllous, or pentaphyllous; the interior decaphyllous, and composed of concave leaflets.
