PROCOPIUS

article · 328 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PROCOPIUS, a famous Greek historian, born in Cæsaria, acquired great reputation by his works in the reign of Julian, and was secretary to Belisarius during all the wars carried on by that general in Persia, Africa, and Italy. He at length became senator, obtained the title of illustrious, and was made pretor of Constantinople.
