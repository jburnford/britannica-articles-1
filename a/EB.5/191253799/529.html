PREMNA

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PREMNA, in botany; a genus of the angiosperma order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants. The calyx is bilobed; the corolla quadrifid; the berry quadriocular; the seeds foliary.
