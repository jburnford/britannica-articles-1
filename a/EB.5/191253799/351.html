PORT-Royal

sub_entry · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORT-Royal, an island in North America, on the coast of South Carolina, which, with the neighbouring continent, forms one of the most commodious harbours in the British plantations. It is 15 miles in length; and the town on the north shore is called Beaufort. W. Long. 80. 10. N. Lat. 31. 40.
