PYROLA

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PYROLA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the decandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 18th order, Bicornes. The calyx is quinquepartite; there are five petals; the capsule is quinquelocular, opening at the angles.
