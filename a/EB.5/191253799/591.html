PRIAPISMUS

article · 437 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MEDICINE

PRIAPISMUS, or PRIAPISM, is an erection of the penis without any concomitant pain, or the consent of other parts. It is thus called, because the person in this state resembles the lewd god Priapus. Cælius Aurelianus says it is a palsy of the seminal vessels, and other nerves distributed to the parts about the penis, by the distension of which this disorder is produced. It is of the same nature as the fattyrisis. See MEDICINE, n° 372.
