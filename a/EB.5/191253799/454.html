POWDIKE, in the fens of Norfolk and Ely. By stat. 22 Hen. VIII. c. 11. perversely to cut down and destroy the powdike in the fens of Norfolk and Ely is felony. See Blackstone's Commentaries, vol. iv. p. 243.
