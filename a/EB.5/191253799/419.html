POTENTIAL Causa

sub_entry · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POTENTIAL Causa, in medicine, denotes the consuming, or reducing to an eschar, any part of the human body by a caustic alkaline or metallic salt, &c. instead of a red-hot iron, which last is called the actual causa.
