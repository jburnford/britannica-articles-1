PORANA

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORANA, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. The corolla is campanulated; the calyx is quinquefid, and larger than the fruit; the style femibifid, long, and permanent; the stigma globular; the perianthium bivalved.
