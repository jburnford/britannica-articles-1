PTERONIA

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PTERONIA, in botany: A genus of the polygamia equalis order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Compositeæ. The receptacle is full of multipartite bristles; the pappus a little plume; the calyx imbricated.
