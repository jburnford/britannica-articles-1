PLOCAMA

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLOCAMÁ, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. The calyx is quinquetentate; the fruit a berry and trilocular, with solitary seeds. Of this there is only one species, viz. the pendula, a native of the Canaries.
