PRAM

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PRAM, or PRAME, a kind of lighter used in Holland and the ports of the Baltic Sea, to carry the cargo of a merchant-ship along-side, in order to load or to bring it to shore to be lodged in the storehouses after being discharged out of the vessel.
