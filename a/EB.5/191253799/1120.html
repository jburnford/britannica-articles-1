RABBETTING

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RABBETTING, in carpentry, the planning or cutting of channels or grooves in boards, &c.

In ship-carpentry, it signifies the letting in of the planks of the ship into the keel, which, in the rake and

run of a ship, is hollowed away, that the planks may join the clover.
