PLEIADES

article · 611 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLEIADES, in fabulous history, the seven daughters of Athas king of Mauritania and Picione, were thus called from their mother. They were Maia, Electra, Taygete, Alceste, Merope, Halcyone, and Celeno; and were also called Atlantides, from their father Atlas. These princesses were carried off by Buisiris king of Egypt; but Hercules having conquered him, delivered them to their father: yet they afterwards suffered a new persecution from Orion, who pursued them five years,

till Jove, being prevailed on by their prayers, took them up into the heavens, where they form the constellation which bears their name.
