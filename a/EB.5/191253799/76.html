PLIMPTON

PLIMPTON, a town of Devonshire, in England, with a market on Saturdays. It is seated on a branch of the river Plime, and had once a castle, now in ruins. It sends two members to parliament; is seven miles E. of Plymouth, and 218 W. by S. of London. W. Long. 4. 0. N. Lat. 50. 22.
