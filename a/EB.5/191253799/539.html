PREPOSITION

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PREPOSITION, in grammar, one of the parts of speech, being an indeclinable particle which yet serves to govern the nouns that follow it; such as per, pro, propter; and through, for, with, &c.

F. Buffier allows it to be only a modificative of a part of speech, serving to circumscribe a noun.
