POLYCHREST

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLYCHREST, in pharmacy, signifies a medicine that serves for many uses, or that cures many diseases.

Sul Polychrest, a compound salt made of equal parts of saltpetre and sulphur, deflagrated in a red-hot crucible.
