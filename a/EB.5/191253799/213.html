POLOCSKI

POLOCSKI, a palatinate in the duchy of Lithuania, bounded on the north by the palatinate of Weytepiski, on the south by the Dwina, on the north by Muscovy, and on the west by Livonia. It is a desert country full of wood, and had formerly its own dukes.
