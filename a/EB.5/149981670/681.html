HAQUE

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAQUE, in our old writers, a little hand gun, prohibited to be used for destruction of game, &c. by statute 33 Hen. VIII. cap. 6. and 2 & 3 Ed. VI. cap. 14. There is also the half-haque, or demi-haque, within the said acts.
