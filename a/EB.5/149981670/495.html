GYPSOPHILA

GYPSOPHILA, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the decandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 22d order, Caryophyllæ. The calyx is monophyllous, campanulated, and angulated; the petals are five in number, ovate, and sessile; the capsule globose and unilocular.
