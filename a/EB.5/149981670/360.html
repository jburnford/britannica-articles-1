GUADALAVIAR

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUADALAVIAR, a river of Spain, which rises on the confines of Aragon and New Castile, and, running by Turvel in Aragon, crosses the kingdom of Valencia, passes by the town of the same name, and soon after falls into the Mediterranean sea, a little below Valencia.
