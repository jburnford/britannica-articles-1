HADERSLEBEN

HADERSLEBEN, a sea-port town of Denmark, in the duchy of Schleswick, with a strong citadel, built upon a small island. It is seated on a bay of the Baltic Sea, and has a well-frequented harbour. E. Long. 9. 35. N. Lat. 55. 24.
