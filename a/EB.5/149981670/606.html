HALORAGUS

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALORAGUS, in botany: A genus of the tetragynia order, belonging to the octandria class of plants. The calyx is quadrifid above; there are four petals; a dry plum, and a quadrilocular nut.
