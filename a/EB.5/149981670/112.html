GOTTINGEN

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOTTINGEN, a considerable town of Lower Saxony in Germany, and in the duchy of Brunswick; formerly free and imperial, but now subject to the elector of Hanover. Here his late Majesty George II. founded an university. It is seated on the river Leine, in E. Long. 10. 5. N. Lat. 51. 32.
