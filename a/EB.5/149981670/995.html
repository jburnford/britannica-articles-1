HEMEROTROPHIS

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEMEROTROPHIS, in antiquity, a measure of capacity, the same with the chœnix. It was so called from its holding one day's food. The word is compounded of hemera a day, and trope food.
