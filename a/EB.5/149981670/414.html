GUIARA

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUIARA, a sea-port town of South America, and on the Caracca coast. The English attempted to take it in 1739 and 1743; but they were repulsed both times. W. Long. 66. 5. N. Lat. 10. 35.
