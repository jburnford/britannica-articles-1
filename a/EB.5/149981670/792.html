HAVELBERG

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAVELBERG, a town of Germany, in the circle of Lower Saxony, and in the electorate of Brandenburg, with a bishop's see, secularized in favour of the house of Brandenburg. It is seated on the river Havel, in E. Long. 12. 43. N. Lat. 53. 4.
