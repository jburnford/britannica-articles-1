GUAMANGA

GUAMANGA, a considerable town of South America, and capital of a province of the same name in Peru, and in the audience of Lima, with a bishop's see. It is remarkable for its sweetmeats, manufactures, and mines of gold, silver, loadstone, and quicksilver. W. Long. 7. 50. S. Lat. 13. 0.
