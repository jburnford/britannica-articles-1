Horse-Grenadier GUARDS

sub_entry · 425 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Horse-Grenadier GUARDS, are divided into two troops, called the 1st and 2d troops of horse-grenadier guards. The first troop was raised in 1693, and the command given to lieutenant-general Cholmondeley; the second in 1702, and the command given to lord Forbes. Each troop has one colonel, lieutenant-colonel, one guidon or major, three exempts and captains, three lieutenants, one adjutant, three cornets, and 60 private men.
