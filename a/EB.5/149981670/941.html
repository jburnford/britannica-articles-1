HELIOPHILA

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELIOPHILA, in botany: A genus of the filiquosa order, belonging to the tetradynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 39th order, Siliquosa. There are two nectaria recurved towards the velicular base of the calyx.
