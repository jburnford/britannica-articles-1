HAMELIN

HAMELIN, a strong town of Germany, in the duchy of Calenberg in Lower Saxony. It is situated at the extremity of the duchy of Brunswick, to which it is the key, near the confluence of the rivers Hamel and Wefer, in E. Long. 9. 55. N. Lat. 52. 13.
