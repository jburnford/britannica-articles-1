GROUND

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GROUND, in etching, denotes a gummos composition smeared over the surface of the metal to be etched, to prevent the aquafortis from eating, except in such places where this ground is cut through with the point of a needle. See the article ETCHING.
