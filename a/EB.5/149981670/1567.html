HUGONIA

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HUGONIA, in botany: A genus of the decandria order, belonging to the monadelphous class of plants; and in the natural method ranking with those of which the order is doubtful. The corolla is pentapetalous; the fruit is a plum with a striated kernel.
