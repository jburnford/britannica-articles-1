GRIELUM

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRIELUM, in botany: A genus of the pentagynia order, belonging to the decandria class of plants. The calyx is quinquefid; there are five petals; the filaments perisilicifolius; and there are five monospermous seed-cases.
