HALLERIA, in botany: A genus of the angiospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 40th order, Perfonata. The calyx is trifid; the corolla quadrifid; the filaments longer than the corolla; the berry inferior and bilocular, (the fruit not yet fully described.)
