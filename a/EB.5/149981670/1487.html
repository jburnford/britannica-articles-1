HORREA

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HORREA, in Roman antiquity, were public magazines of corn and salt meat, out of which the soldiers were furnished on their march in the military roads of the empire. Horrea was also the name which they gave to their granaries.
