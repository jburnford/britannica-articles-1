HECATOMPOLIS

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HECATOMPOLIS (anc. geog.), a surname of the island of Crete, from its 100 cities. The territory of Laconia also had anciently this name for the same reason; and the custom of these 100 cities was to sacrifice a hecatomb annually.
