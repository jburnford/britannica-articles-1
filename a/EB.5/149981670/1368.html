HOLOSTEUM

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOLOSTEUM, in botany: A genus of the trigynia order, belonging the triandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 2nd order, Caryophylli. The calyx is pentaphyllous; the petals five, the capsule unilocular, and nearly cylindrical, opening at top.
