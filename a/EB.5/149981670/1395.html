HOMOGENEOUS

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOMOGENEOUS, or HOMOGENEAL (composed of the Greek ὁμοίως like, and γένος kind), is a term applied to various subjects, to denote, that they consist of similar parts, or of parts of the same nature and kind: in contradistinction to heterogeneous, where the parts are of different natures, &c.
