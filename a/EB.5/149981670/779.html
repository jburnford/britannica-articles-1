HATCHET

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HATCHET, a small light sort of an axe, with a basil edge on its left side, and a short handle, as being to be used with one hand.—Hatchets are used by various artificers, and more particularly in hewing of wood.
