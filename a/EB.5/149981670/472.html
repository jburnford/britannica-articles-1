GUTTA, a Latin term for what in English we call drop.

Gutta Rosacea, in medicine, denotes a red or pimpled face; a distemper which, though not always owing its original to hard drinking, is nevertheless most incident to tipplers of strong beer, wines, spirits, &c.

Gutta Serena, a disease in which the patient, without any apparent fault in the eye, is deprived of sight. See (Index subjoined to) MEDICINE.
