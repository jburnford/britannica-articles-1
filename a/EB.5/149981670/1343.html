HO-KIEN-rou

sub_entry · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HO-KIEN-rou, a city of China, and one of the principal in the province of Pe-tcheli. It has two cities of the second, and fifteen of the third, class in its district, but is remarkable for nothing but the neatness of its streets.
