HALESIA

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALESIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the dodecandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 18th order, Bicornes. The calyx is quadridentated, superior; the corolla quadrifid; the nut quadrangular and dispermous.
