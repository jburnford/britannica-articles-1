HADES

Cross-references: HELL

HADES, in the scriptures, is used in various senses. Sometimes it signifies the invisible regions of the dead, sometimes the place of the damned, and sometimes the grave. In Greek authors it is used to signify in general the regions of the dead. See HELL.
