HYDROPHYLLACIA

article · 341 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYDROPHYLLACIA, a word used by Kircher and some others who have written in the same system, to express those great reservoirs of water which he places in the Alps and other mountains for the supply of rivers which run through the several lower countries. This he makes to be one of the great uses of mountains in the economy of the universe.
