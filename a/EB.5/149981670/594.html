HALLE

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALLE, a handsome and considerable town of Germany, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and in the duchy of Magdeburg, with a famous university and salt-works. It belongs to the king of Prussia; and is seated in a pleasant plain on the river Sale, in E. Long. 12. 33. N. Lat. 51. 36.
