GUISE

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUISE, a small town of France in Picardy, and in Tierache, with a very strong castle, and the title of a duchy. It is seated on the river Ouse, in E. Long. 3. 42. N. Lat. 49. 54.
