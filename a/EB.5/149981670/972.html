HELONIAS

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELONIAS, in botany: A genus of the trigynia order, belonging to the hexandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 10th order, Coronarie. The corolla is hexapetalous; there is no calyx; and the capsule is trilocular.
