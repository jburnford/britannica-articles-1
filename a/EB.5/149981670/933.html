HELICONIA

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELICONIA, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. The spathe is universal and partial; there is no calyx; the corolla has three petals, and the nectarium two leaves; the capsule is three-grained.
