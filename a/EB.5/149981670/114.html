GOUANIA

article · 393 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOUANIA, in botany: A genus of the monocæcia order, belonging to the polygamia class of plants. The calyx of the hermaphrodite is quinquefid; there is no corolla; there are five anthers covered with an elastic calyptra or hood; the style trifid; the fruit, inferior to the receptacle of the flower, divisible into three seeds. The male is like the hermaphrodite, but wanting stigma and germin.
