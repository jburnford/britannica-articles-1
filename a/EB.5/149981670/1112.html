HERMIANI

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HERMIANI, or HERMIATITÆ, a sect of heretics in the second century, thus called from their leader Hermias. They were also denominated Seleuciani.

One of their distinguishing tenets was, that God is corporeal. Another, that Jesus Christ did not ascend into heaven with his body, but left it in the sun.
