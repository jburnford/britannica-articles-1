HURON

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HURON, a vast lake of North-America, situated between 84° and 89° W. Long. and between 43° and 46° of N. Lat. from whence the country contiguous to it is called the country of the Hurons, whose language is spoken over a great extent in the southern parts of America.
