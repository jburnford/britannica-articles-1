HORTAGILERS

HORTAGILERS, in the grand signior's court, upholsterers, or tapestry-hangers. The grand signior has constantly 400 in his retinue when he is in the camp: these go always a day's journey before him, to fix upon a proper place for his tent, which they prepare first; and afterwards those of the officers, according to their rank.
