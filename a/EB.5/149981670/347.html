GRUME

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRUME, in medicine, denotes a concreted clot of blood, milk, or other substance. Hence grumous blood is that which approaches to the nature of grume, and by its viscosity and stagnating in the capillary vessels produces several disorders.
