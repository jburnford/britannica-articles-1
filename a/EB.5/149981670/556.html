HAIR-CLOTHS

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAIR-CLOTHS, in military affairs, are large pieces of cloth made with half hair. They are used for covering the powder in waggons, or upon batteries; as also for covering charged bombs or hand-grenades, and many other uses in magazines.
