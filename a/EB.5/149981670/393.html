GUASTALLA

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUASTALLA, a strong town of Italy, in the duchy of Mantua, with the title of a duchy, remarkable for a battle between the French and Imperialists in 1734. It was ceded to the duke of Parma in 1748, by the treaty of Aix la Chapelle. It is seated near the river Po, in E. Long. 10. 33. N. Lat. 44. 55.
