HOARSENESS

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOARSENESS, in medicine, a diminution of the voice, commonly attended with a preternatural asperity and roughness thereof. The parts affected are the aspera, arteria and larynx. For its causes and cure, see (the Index subjoined to) MEDICINE.
