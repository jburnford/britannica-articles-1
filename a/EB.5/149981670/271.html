GRENA DINES, or GRENA DILLOS. See the preceding article.—It is there mentioned that the Grenadines do not afford a single drop of fresh water. A small spring however has been lately discovered in the principal island Cariacou, by digging; but being of great value, it is kept locked by the proprietor Mr Mayes. The capital of that island is called Hillsborough, in which there is a small church.
