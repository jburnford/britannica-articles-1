BATTORY

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BATTORY, a name given by the Hans Towns to their magazines or factories abroad. The chief of these battories are those at Archangel, Novogrod, Berghmen, Lisbon, Venice, and Antwerp.
