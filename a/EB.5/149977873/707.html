BERCHEROIT,

Bercheroit || Berengarianism. BERCHEROIT, or BERKOUTS, a weight used at Archangel, and in all the Russian dominions, to weigh such merchandizes as are heavy and bulky. It weighs about 164 lib. English avoirdupois weight.
