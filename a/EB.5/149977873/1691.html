BOURG

article · 531 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOURG, the capital of the island of Cayenne, a French colony on the coast of Guiana, in South America; in W. Long. 52. 0. N. Lat. 5. 0.

Bourges-en-Bresse, a town of France, and capital of Bresse, in the province of Burgundy. It is seated on the river Resoncle, almost in the centre of Bresse, in E. Long. 4. 19. N. Lat. 46. 13.

Bourges-sur-Mer, a sea-port town of France in Guienne, and in the Bourdelois, with a tolerable good harbour: seated at the confluence of the rivers Dordogne and Garonne, in W. Long. 3. 35. N. Lat. 45. 0.
