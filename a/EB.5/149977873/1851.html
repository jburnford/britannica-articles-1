BRASSICAVIT

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRASSICAVIT, or BRACHICAVIT, in the manège, is a horse whose fore-legs are naturally bended arch-wise: being so called by way of distinction from an arched horse, whose legs are bowed by hard labour.
