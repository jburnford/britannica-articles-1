WIRE-BRUSHES

article · 360 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WIRE-BRUSHES, are used by silver smiths and gilders, for scrubbing silver, copper, or brass pieces, in order to the gilding of them. There is a method of dying or colouring leather, performed by only rubbing the colour on the skin with a brush. This the French leather-gilders call broussure; being the lowest of all the sorts of dye allowed by their statutes.
