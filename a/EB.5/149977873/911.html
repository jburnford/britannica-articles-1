BIDET

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIDET, a nag or little horse, formerly allowed each trooper and dragoon, for his baggage and other occasions. Bidets are grown into dilute, on account of the expences thereof, and the disorders frequently arising from those who attended on them, &c.
