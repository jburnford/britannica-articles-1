BOVIUM, (Itinerary); a town of the Silures, in Britain, fifteen miles to the south of Itea Silurum, or

Caer-leon, in Monmouthshire: Now called Cowbridge; Boulainvilliers, according to Baudrand, Banger in Carnarvonshire.
