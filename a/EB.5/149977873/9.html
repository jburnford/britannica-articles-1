BARBED and Grested

sub_entry · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBED and Grested, in heraldry, an appellation given to the combs and gills of a cock, when particularized for being of a different tincture from the body.

A barbed cross, is a cross the extremities whereof are like the barbed irons used for striking of fish.
