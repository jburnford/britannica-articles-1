BIGGLESWADE

BIGGLESWADE, a town of Bedfordshire in England, seated on the river Ivel, over which there is a handsome bridge. The town is much more considerable now than formerly, on account of its commodious inns for passengers, it lying on the principal road from London to York. W. Long. o. 15. N. Lat. 52. 5.
