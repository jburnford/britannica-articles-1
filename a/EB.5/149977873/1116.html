BLACK-FOREST

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLACK-FOREST, a forest of Germany, in Suabia, running from north to south between Ortnau, Briggau, part of the duchy of Wirtemberg, the principality of Fulstemberg towards the source of the Danube, as far as the Rhine above Basil. It is part of the ancient Hyrcanian forest.
