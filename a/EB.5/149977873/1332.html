BOCCALE

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOCCALE, or BOCAL, a liquid measure used at Rome, answering to what among us is called a bottle, being equivalent to about an English quart. Seven boccales and an half make the rubbia.
