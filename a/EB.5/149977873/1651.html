BOVINA APPETITIO

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOVINA APPETITIO, a distemper of black cattle, caused by a worm lodged between the skin and the flesh, and perforating the same. This distemper is not mentioned by the ancient Greeks, and is but little known in Europe.
