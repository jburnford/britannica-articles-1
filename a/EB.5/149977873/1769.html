BRACKTEARIA

article · 332 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRACKTEARIA, in natural history, a genus of scales, composed of small plates in form of spangles, each plate either being very thin, or fissile into very thin ones.

Of this genus there are a great many species, called, from their different colours, mica aurea, or gold-glimmer; and mica argentea, silver-glimmer, or cats-silver, &c.
