BOUND

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOUND, in dancing, a spring from one foot to the other; by which it differs from a hop, where the spring is from one foot to the same. It also differs from a half coupee, as in the latter the body always bears on the floor, either on one foot or the other; whereas, in the bound, it is thrown quite from the floor.
