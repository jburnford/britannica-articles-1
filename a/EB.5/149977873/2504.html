BUYING the small-pox

sub_entry · 363 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUYING the small-pox, is an appellation given to a method of procuring that disease by an operation similar to inoculation; frequent in South Wales, where it has obtained time out of mind. It is performed either by rubbing some of the pox taken out of a pustule of a variolous person on the skin, or by making a puncture in the skin with a pin dipped in such pus.
