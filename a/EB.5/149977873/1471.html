BONARELLI

BONARELLI (Gui Ubaldo), an Italian count. He was entrusted with several important negotiations, and was esteemed an able politician and learned philosopher. He was the author of a fine Italian pastoral, intitled, Filli di Sciro. He died at Fano, in 1608, aged 45.
