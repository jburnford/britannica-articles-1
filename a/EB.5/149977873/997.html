BIOTHANATI

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIOTHANATI (supposed by some to be derived from bio, life, and thanos, death, and alluding to the belief of a future life after death), was also a name of reproach given by the Heathens to the primitive Christians, for their constancy and forwardness to lay down their lives in martyrdom.
