BEG, or BEY, in the Turkish affairs. See BEY.

Beg is more particularly applied to the lord of a banner, called also in the same language fangiak-beg. A beg has the command of a certain number of the spahis, or horse, maintained by the province under the denomination of timoriots. All the begs of a province obey one governor-general called begler-beg, or begler-beg, q. d. lord of lords, or of the beys of the province.
