BONFRERIUS

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONFRERIUS (James), a learned Jesuit, born at Dinant, in 1573. He wrote a commentary on the Pentateuch, and learned notes on the Onomasticon of the places and towns mentioned in the Scripture. He died at Tournay in 1643, aged 70.
