BRENTFORD

article · 322 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRENTFORD, a town of Middlesex, in the great London road to the west. It is divided into old and new Brentford, in which last are the church and market-house, and where the county elections are held. It is a long place, well stocked with public houses, and is seated on the river Thames, in W. Long. 0. 10. N. Lat. 51. 26.
