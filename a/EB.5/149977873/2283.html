BUFONIA, ROAD-GRASS

article · 309 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUFONIA, ROAD-GRASS: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the diandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 22d order, Caryophyllae. The calyx is quinquedentate; there is no corolla; the berry is monospermous. There is but one species, viz. the tenuifolia, a native of Britain.
