BRENNUS, a celebrated captain among the Gauls, who, about 388 years before the Christian era, entered

Brent
Bresciano.

Italy with a powerful army; made great conquests there; defeated the Romans; and sacked Rome. The capital alone was defended; and Camillus coming to its relief, drove the Gauls not only out of Rome, but out of all Italy. See (History of) ROME.
