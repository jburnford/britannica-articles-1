BURRE

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURRE, BOUREE, or Borre, a kind of dance composed of three steps joined together in two motions, begun with a crotchet rising. The first couplet contains twice four measures, the second twice eight. It consists of a balance and couplet.
