BERITH

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BERITH, a simple mentioned in Scripture, used for cleansing or taking out spots (Jer. ii. 22). Some will have it to be the kali or salt-wort, from the ashes of which soap is made; and in our version it is rendered soap: others, after Rudbeck, made it to be the dye of the purple-fish.
