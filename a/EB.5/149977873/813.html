BETHAVEN

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETHAVEN (anc. geog.), a town in the tribe of Ephraim, and a name given Bethel by Hosia, after the establishment of the idolatry of Jeroboam there; meaning it to have become the house of iniquity, from being the house of God: but Bethaven was a distinct town (Joshua vii. 2.) to the south-east of Bethel.
