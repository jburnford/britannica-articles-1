Fungus of BIRCH

sub_entry · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Fungus of BIRCH, an excrecence growing on its trunk. It is astringent, and good against hemorrhagies. When boiled, beaten, and dried in an oven, it makes excellent spunk or touchwood.
