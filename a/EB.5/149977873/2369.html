BURGAU

BURGAU, in natural history, the name of a large species of sea-snail, of the lunar or round-mouthed kind. It is very beautifully lined with a coat, of the nature of the mother of pearl; and the artificers take this out, to use under the name of mother of pearl, though some call it after the name of the shell they take it from, burgaudine.
