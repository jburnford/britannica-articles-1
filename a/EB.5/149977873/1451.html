BOMBAX

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOMBAX, in zoology, a synonyme of a species of CONUS.—Bombax is also used sometimes for silk or cotton; but the true botanic name of cotton is GOSYRUM. It is likewise applied by Linnaeus to lignify such insects as have incumbent wings, and feelers resembling a comb.
