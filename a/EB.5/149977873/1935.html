BRESCIANO

article · 457 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRESCIANO, a province of Italy in the territory of Venice; bounded on the north, by the Grisons and the bishopric of Trent; on the east, by the lake Garda, the Veronese, and the duchy of Mantua; on the south, by the duchy of Mantua and the Cremonese; and on the west, by the Cresmasco, the Burgomasco, and the Valtelina. It is watered by several small rivers, which

tender it very fertile; and is full of towns and villages, of which Brescia is the capital.
