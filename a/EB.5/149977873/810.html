BETHANY

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETHANY (anc. geog.), a village at the foot of mount Olivet, on the east side, about two miles to the east of Jerusalem, (John, Jerome); where Lazarus dwelt and was raised from the dead; and where happened the ascension of our Saviour.
