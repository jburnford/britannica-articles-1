BREVE Perquirere

sub_entry · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BREVE Perquirere, the purchasing of a writ or licence for trial in the king's courts; whence comes the present use of paying 6s. 8d. fine to the king in suit, for money due on bond, where the debt is L. 40, and of 10 s. where it is L. 100, &c.
