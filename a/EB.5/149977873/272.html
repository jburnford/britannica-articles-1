BATAVORUM Oppidum

sub_entry · 384 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BATAVORUM Oppidum (anc. geog.), a town in the island of the Batavi, mentioned by Tacitus, without any particular name; which has given rise to several surmises about it, some supposing it to be Nimeguen, but Cluverius, Batavadurum or Batemburg, both without the island; which situation renders both these places inadmissible, since Tacitus places this nameless town within the island.
