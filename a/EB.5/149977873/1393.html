BOIORUM DESERTA

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOIORUM DESERTA, (anc. geog.) a district of Pannonia, so called from the excision of the Boii by the Getae. Now the Weinerwald, of Lower Austria, towards Styria, to the east of mount Cetius, or the Hahenberg, and south of Vindobona or Vienna.
