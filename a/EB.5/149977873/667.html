BENEFICIARI

article · 361 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BENEFICIARI was also used for those raised to a higher rank by the favour of the tribunes or other magistrates. The word beneficiarius frequently occurs in the Roman inscriptions found in Britain, where consulis is always joined with it; but besides beneficiarius consulis, we find in Grutar beneficiarius tribuni, praetorii, legati, praefecti, proconsulis, &c.
