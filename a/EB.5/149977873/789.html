BESANT

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BESANT, or BEZANT, a coin of pure gold, of an uncertain value, struck at Byzantium, in the time of the Christian emperors; from hence the gold offered by the king at the altar is called besant or bisant.
