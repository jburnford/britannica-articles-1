BLATTARIE

Cross-references: VERBASCUM

BLATTARIE (from Blatta, a moth or little worm), the title of Scopoli's 12th natural class, in his Flora Caroliolica. It is taken from the Blattaria, which was Tournefort's generic name for the verbasum of Linnaeus. See VERBASCUM.
