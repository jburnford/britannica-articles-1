BREVET

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BREVET, more particularly denotes the commission of a subaltern officer, being only written on parchment, and without seal. A brevet officer is one whose rank in the army is above his pay; for instance, a brevet major serves only as a captain, and receives pay as such.
