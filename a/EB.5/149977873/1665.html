BOULOGNOIS

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOULOGNOIS, a territory of France, in the north part of Picardy, about 30 miles in length and 20 in breadth. The chief town is Boulogne, and the chief trade is in pit-coal and butter.
