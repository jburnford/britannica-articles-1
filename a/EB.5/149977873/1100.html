BITURIGES (Cæsar); Bituriges Cubi (Strabo, Pliny, Ptolemy): a people in that part of Gallia Cætica afterwards assigned to Aquitania. Now called Berry.

Bituriges Vibisci (Ptolemy), a people of Aquitain.
