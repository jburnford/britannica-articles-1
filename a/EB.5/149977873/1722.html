BOXING

article · 378 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOXING, among sailors, is used to denote the rehearsing the several points of the compass in their proper order.

BOXING is also used for the tapping of a tree to make

Boxtchude make it yield its juice. The boxing of maple is performed by making an hole with an ax or chisel into the side of the tree about a foot from the ground; out of it flows a liquor of which sugar is made.
