Order of the BEAR

sub_entry · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Order of the BEAR was a military order in Switzerland, erected by the Emperor Frederick II. in 1213, by way of acknowledgment for the service the Swiss had done him, and in favour of the abbey of St. Gaul. To the collar of the order hung a medal, on which was represented a bear raised on an eminence of earth.
