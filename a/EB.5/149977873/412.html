BEAR'S-SKIN

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEAR'S-SKIN makes a fur in great esteem, and on which depends a considerable article of commerce, being used in housings, on coach-boxes, &c. In some countries, clothes are made of it, more especially bags wherein to keep the feet warm in severe colds. Of the skins of bears cubs are made gloves, muffs, and the like.
