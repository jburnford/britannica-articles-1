BELLONIA

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BELLONIA (so named from the famous Petrus Bellonius, who left many valuable tracts on natural history, &c.), a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. Of this genus there is only one species known, viz. the aspera, with a rough balm leaf. This is very common in the warm islands of America.
