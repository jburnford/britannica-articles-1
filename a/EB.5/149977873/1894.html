Cross-references: ANATOMY

BREAST, in anatomy, denotes the fore-parts of the thorax. See ANATOMY, no 36, 111.

Smiting the breast is one of the expressions of penitence. In the Romish church, the priest beats his breast in rehearsing the general confession at the beginning of the mass.
