BLANC-Manger

sub_entry · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLANC-Manger, Fr. q. d. white food, is a preparation of dissolved singlats, milk, sugar, cinnamon, &c. VOL. III. Part I.

boiled into a thick consistence, and garnished for the table with blanched almonds. It is cooling and strengthening. Blancards Blanching.
