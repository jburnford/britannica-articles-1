BULLINGER

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BULLINGER (Henry), born at Bremgarten in Switzerland in 1504, was an eminent Zwinglian minister, a great supporter of the reformation, and employed in many ecclesiastical negotiations. He composed many books, one against Luther in particular. He died in 1575.
