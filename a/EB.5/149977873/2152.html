BROWNA

BROWNA, in botany; a genus of the endecandra order, belonging to the monadelphia class of plants. The calyx is bifid, the corolla double, the exterior quinquefid, and the interior pentapetalous. There is but one species, the coccinea, a native of the West Indies.
