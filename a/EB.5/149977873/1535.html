BOQUINIANS

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOQUINIANS, in church-history, a sect of heretics, so called from Boquinus their founder, who taught that Christ did not die for all mankind, but only for the faithful, and consequently was only a particular Saviour.
