BLOCZIL

BLOCZIL, a fortress of Ower-yssel in the United Provinces, seated on the river Aa, at the place where it falls into the Zuider Zee. It has a port sufficient to contain 200 vessels, and serves to defend those ships that cross the sea. It has six good bastions, and several other regular fortifications. E. Long. 6. o. N. Lat. 52. 44.
