BRIGANTES

BRIGANTES, (Tacitus), a people of Britain, reaching from sea to sea, the whole breadth of the island, (Ptolemy). Now Yorkshire, Lancashire, Durham, Westmoreland, and Cumberland, (Camden). Also a people of Ireland, of uncertain position.
