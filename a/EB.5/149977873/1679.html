BOURBON-LANCY

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOURBON-LANCY, a town of France, in the duchy of Burgundy, and in the Autunois. It is remarkable for its castle and baths; and there is a large marble pavement, called the great bath, which is a work of the Romans. It is seated near the river Loire, in E. Long. 3. 46. N. Lat. 46. 37.
