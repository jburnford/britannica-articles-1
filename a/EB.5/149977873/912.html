BIDIS

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIDIS, (anc. geog.), a small city of Sicily, not far from Syracuse, whose ruins are still to be seen in the territory of Syracuse, about 15 miles to the south-west, with a church called S. Giovanni di Bidini.
