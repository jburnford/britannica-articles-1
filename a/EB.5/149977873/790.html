BESANTED

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BESANTED, or BEZANTED. This word means full of besants; and is used to denote a field, ordinary, or charge, covered with above eight besants: for if there be but eight or fewer, their number must be particularly mentioned.
