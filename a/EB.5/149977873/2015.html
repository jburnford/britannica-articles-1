BRIGANTIA

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRIGANTIA, or BRIGANTIUM, (anc. geog.), a town of Vindelicia; now Bregenz, in Tyrol, at the east end of the lake of Constance.—Another Brigantium in the Alpes Cottiae; which last is probably Briancourt, a town on the borders of Dauphin.
