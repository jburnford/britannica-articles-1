BEECH-MAST

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEECH-MALT, the fruit of the beech-tree, said to be good for fattening hogs, deer, &c.—It has sometimes, even to men, proved an useful substitute for bread. Chios is said to have endured a memorable siege by means of it.
