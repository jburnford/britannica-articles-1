BRIESCIA

article · 567 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRIESCIA, a palatinate in the duchy of Lithuania, in Poland. The name given to it by some is Polisia. It is bounded on the north, by Novogrod and Troki; on the west, by those of Bielisko and Lublin; on the south, by that of Chelm and upper Volhnia; and on the east, by the territory of Rzecica. This province is of considerable extent from east to west, and is watered by the rivers Bug and Pripefe: it is full of woods and marshes; and there are lakes that yield large quantities of fish, that are fished by the inhabitants, and sent into the neighbouring provinces.
