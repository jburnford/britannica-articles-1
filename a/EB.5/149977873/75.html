BARLETTA

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARLETTA, a handsome and strong town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in the Terra di Bari, with a bishop's see. It is situated on the gulph of Venice, in E. Long. 16. 32. N. Lat. 41. 30.
