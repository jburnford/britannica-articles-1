BARBUDA

article · 413 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBUDA, one of the British Caribbean islands, about 20 miles long and 12 broad. It is low land, but fruitful and pretty populous. The inhabitants addict themselves to husbandry, and find always a ready market for their corn and cattle in the sugar islands. Barbuda is the property of the Codrington family, who have great numbers of negroes here as well as in Barbadoes. It lies in W. Long. 61. 3. N. Lat. 18. 5.
