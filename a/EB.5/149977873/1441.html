BOMB-VEGETI

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: KETCH

BOMB-VEGETI, which are small ships formed for throwing bombs into a fortress, are said to be the invention of M. Reyneau, and to have been first used at the bombardment of Algiers. Till then it had been judged impracticable to bombard a place from the sea. See KETCH.
