BIRETUM

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIRETUM, or BIRKETUM, a sort of black bonnet, or covering of the head, in form of a pyramid, much used in Italy and France, about 500 or 600 years ago, as a badge of victory, honour, or sacerdotal preferment.
