BOMBUS

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOMBUS, in music, an artificial motion with the hands, imitating in cadence and harmony the buzzing of bees. The word is originally Greek, and signifies the buzz or noise of bees, gnats, and the like. In this sense, bombus made one of the species of applause used by the ancient auditories.
