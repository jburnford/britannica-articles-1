BEAUCÉ

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEAUCÉ, a province of France, lying between the isle of France, Blaisois, and Orléannois. It is so very fertile in wheat, that it is called the Granary of Paris. Chartres is the principal town.
