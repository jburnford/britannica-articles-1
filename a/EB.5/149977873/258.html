BASTON, Baton, or Batune. This word is French, and signifies a staff or cudgel: it should be spelt Bâton; but is, by most English writers, corruptly spelt as above. It is only borne in English coats of arms, as a badge of illegitimacy; but French heralds introduce it in arms as a difference, or mark of consanguinity.
