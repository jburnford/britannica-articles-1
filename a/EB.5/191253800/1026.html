STAFFORD, the county town of Staffordshire, in W. Long. 2. o. N. Lat. 53. o. It stands on the river Sowe, has two parish-churches, a fine square market-place, and a flourishing cloth-manufacture. It sends two members to parliament, and is 135 miles from London.
