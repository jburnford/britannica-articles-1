SELAGO

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SELAGO, in botany: A genus of the angiosperma order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 48th order, Aggregate. The calyx is quinquefid: the tube of the corolla capillary, with the limb nearly equal, and a single feed. There are 22 species.
