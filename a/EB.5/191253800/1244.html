STRATAGEM

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STRATAGEM, in the art of war, any device for deceiving and surprising an enemy. The ancients dealt very much in stratagems; the moderns wage war more openly, and on the square. Frontinus has made a collection of the ancient stratagems of war.
