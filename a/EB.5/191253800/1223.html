STRAIKS

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STRAIKS, in the military art, are square plates of iron, six in number, fixed with large nails called streak-nails, on the circumference of a cannon-wheel, over the joints of the fellows; both to strengthen the wheel, and to save the fellows from wearing on hard ways or streets.
