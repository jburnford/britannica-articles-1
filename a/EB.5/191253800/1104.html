STEMPHYLA

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STEMPHYLA, a word used by the ancients to express the husks of grapes, or the remains of the pressings of wine. The same word is also used by some to express the remaining mass of the olives, after the oil is pressed out.
