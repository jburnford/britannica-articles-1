SEMENDRIA

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEMENDRIA, a town of Turkey in Europe, in the province of Servia, with a good citadel. It is the capital of a sanguineate, was taken by the Turks in 1690, and is seated on the Danube, in E. Long. 21. 45. N. Lat. 45. 0.
