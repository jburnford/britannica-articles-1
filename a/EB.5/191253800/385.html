SHEADING

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHEADING, a riding, tything, or division, in the Isle of Man; the whole island being divided into six sheadings; in every one of which is a coroner or chief constable.

Shearbill constable, appointed by the delivery of a rod at the annual convention.
