STRANGER

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STRANGER, in law, denotes a person who is not privy or party to an act. Thus a stranger to a judgment is he to whom a judgment does not belong; in which sense the word stands directly opposed to party or privy.
