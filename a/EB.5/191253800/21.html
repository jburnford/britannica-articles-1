SCRUPLE, SCRUPULUS, or Scrutulum, the least of the weights used by the ancients, which amongst the Romans was the 24th part of an ounce, or the 3d part of a dram. The scruple is still a weight among us,

Scruple, Scrutiny. us, containing the 3d part of a dram, or 20 grains. Among goldsmiths it is 24 grains.
