STERNOCOSTALES

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STERNOCOSTALES, commonly called the musculi triangulares sterni, in anatomy, are five pairs of fleshy planes, disposed more or less obliquely on each side the sternum, on the insides of the cartilages of the second, third, fourth, fifth, and sixth true ribs.
