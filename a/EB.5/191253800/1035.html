STALE

article · 341 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STALE, among sportsmen, a living fowl put in a place to allure and bring others where they may be taken. For want of these, a bird shot, his entrails taken out, and dried in an oven in his feathers, with a flick thrust through to keep it in a convenient posture, may serve as well as a live one.

STALE is also a name for the urine of cattle.
