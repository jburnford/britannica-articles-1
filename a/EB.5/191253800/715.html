SODOM, formerly a town of Palestine in Asia, famous in Scripture for the wickedness of its inhabitants, and their destruction by fire from heaven on account of that

Sodomy, that wickedness. The place where it flood is now covered by the waters of the Dead Sea, or the Lake Asphaltites. See ASPHALTITES.
