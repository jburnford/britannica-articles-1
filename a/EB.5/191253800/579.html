SIREX

SIREX, in zoology, a genus of animals belonging to the class of insecta, and to the order of hymenoptera. The mouth has two strong jaws; there are two truncated palpi or feelers, filiform antennæ, an exserted, stiff, serrated sting, a sessile, mucronated abdomen, and lanceolated wings. There are seven species.
