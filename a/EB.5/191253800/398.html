SHEFFIELDIA, in botany; a genus of plants

belonging to the class of pentandria, and to the order of monogynia. The corolla is bell-shaped; the filaments are 10, of which every second is barren. The capsule consists of one cell, which has four valves. There is only one species, the repens.
