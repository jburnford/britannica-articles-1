SERULARIA

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SERULARIA, in natural history, a genus belonging to the class of vermes, and to the order of zoophyta. The stem is radicated, fibrous, naked, and jointed; the florets are hydrous, and there is one at each joint. This genus comprehends 42 species of corallines.
