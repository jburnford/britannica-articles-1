SODA

Cross-references: ALKALI · MEDICINE

SODA, the name given by the French chemists to the mineral alkali, which is found native in many parts of the world: it is obtained also from common salt, and from the ashes of the kali, a species of salsola. See ALKALI, n° 7, and CHEMISTRY-Index.

SODA is also a name for a heat in the stomach or heart-burn. See MEDICINE, n° 275.
