SOPHISTICATION, the mixing of any thing

with what is not genuine; a practice too common in Sophocles. the making up of medicines for sale; as also among vintners, distillers, and others, who are accused of sophisticating their wines, spirits, oils, &c. by mixing with them cheaper and coarser materials; and in many cases the cheat is carried on so artfully as to deceive the best judges.
