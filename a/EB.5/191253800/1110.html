ROBERT STEPHENS

sub_entry · 368 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROBERT STEPHENS, the son of Robert the first of that name, did not accompany his father to Geneva, but continued to profess the Catholic religion, and to

reside at Paris. His letter was remarkably beautiful. — Stephens. He was made king's printer, and died about 1589.

His brother FRANCIS was also a printer. He embraced the Protestant religion, and resided at Geneva.
