SHADOW

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHADOW, in painting, an imitation of a real shadow, effected by gradually heightening and darkening the colours of such figures as by their dispositions cannot receive any direct rays from the luminary that is supposed to enlighten the piece.
