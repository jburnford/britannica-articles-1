SPARMANNIA, in botany; a genus of plants belonging to the class of polyandria, and to the order of monogynia. The corolla consists of four petals, and is bent back; the nectaria are numerous, and swell a little; the calyx is quadriphyllous; the capsule is angulated, quinquelocular and echinated. There is only one species, the Africana.
