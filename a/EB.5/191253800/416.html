SHERBET

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHERBET, or SHERBIT, a compound drink, first brought into England from Turkey and Persia, consisting of water, lemon-juice, and sugar, in which are dif-

folved perfumed cakes made of excellent Damascus fruit, containing an infusion of some drops of rose water. Another kind of it is made of violets, honey, juice of raisins, &c.
