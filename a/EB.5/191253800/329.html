SEVERUS (Cornelius), an ancient Latin poet of the Augustan age; whose Atina, together with a fragment De morte Ciceronis, were published, with notes and a prose interpretation, by Le Clerc, 12mo, Amsterdam, 1703. They were before inserted among the Catalesta Virgilio published by Scaliger; whose notes, with others, Le Clerc has received among his own.
