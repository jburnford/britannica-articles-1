STERN

article · 406 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STERN, the posterior face of a ship; or that part which is represented to the view of a spectator, placed on the continuation of the keel behind. The stern is terminated above by the taffard, and below by the counters; it is limited on the sides by the quarter-pieces, and the intermediate space comprehends the galleries and windows of the different cabins. See Quarter of a Ship, SHIP, and SHIP-BUILDING.
