SLOUGH

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SLOUGH, a deep muddy place. The cast skin of a snake, the damp of a coal pit, and the fear of a wound, are also called by the same appellation. The slough of a wild boar is the bed, foil, or mire, wherein he wallows, or in which he lies in the day-time.
