SIRLET

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SIRLET (Flavius), an eminent Roman engraver on precious stones: his Læcon, and representations in miniature of antique statues at Rome, are very valuable and scarce. He died in 1737.
