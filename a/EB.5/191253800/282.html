SERVETISTS'S

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SERVETIST'S, a name given to the modern Antitrinitarians, from their being supposed to be the followers of Michael Servetus; who, in the year 1553, was burnt at Geneva, together with his books.
