SETTING

article · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SETTING, in the sea-language. To set the land or the sun by the compass, is to observe how the land bears on any point of the compass, or on what point of the compass the sun is. Also when two ships sail in sight of one another, to mark on what point the chafed bears, is termed setting the chafe by the compass.
