STERCORARIANS

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STERCORARIANS, or STERCORARIANUS, formed from stercus "dung," a name which those of the Romish church anciently gave to such as held that the host was liable to digestion, and all its consequences, like other food.
