SERIPHUM

SERIPHUM, in botany; a genus of plants belonging to the order of monogamia, and to the class of syngenesia. The calyx is imbricated; the corolla is monopetalous and regular, with one oblong seed under it. There is only one species, the cinerum, which is a native of the Cape of Good Hope.
