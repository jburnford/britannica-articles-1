STIGMA

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STIGMA, in botany, the summit or top of the style, accounted by the sexualists the female organ of generation in plants, which receives the fecundating dust of the tops of the itamina, and transmits its vapour or effluvia through the style into the heart of the feed-bud, for the purpose of impregnating the seeds.
