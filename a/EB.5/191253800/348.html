SEXUALISTÆ

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEXUALISTÆ, among botanical writers, those who have established the classes of plants upon the differences of the sexes and parts of fructification in plants, according to the modern method; as Linnaeus, &c.
