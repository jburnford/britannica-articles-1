STOICS

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STOICS, the name given to a sect of Grecian philosophers, from stoa, "the porch in Athens," which the founder of the sect chose for his school. For the peculiar tenets of this sect, see METAPHYSICS, Chap. IV. Part 3. MORAL PHILOSOPHY, no 8. and ZENO.
