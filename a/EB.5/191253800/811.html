SORRENTO

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SORRENTO, a sea-port town of the kingdom of Naples, with an archbishop's see. It is seated in a peninsula, on the bay of Naples, at the foot of a mountain of the same name, 17 miles south-east of Naples. It is the birth-place of Torquato Tasso. E. Long. 14. 24. N. Lat. 40. 36.
