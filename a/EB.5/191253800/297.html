SESQUI

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SESQUI, a Latin particle, signifying a whole and a half; which, joined with aliera, terza, quarta, &c. is much used in the Italian music to express a kind of ratios, particularly several species of triples.
