SIMON MACCABEUS

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SIMON MACCABEUS, a celebrated leader and high-priest of the Jews, who, after rendering the most important services to his country, was at last treacherously slain by his son-in-law. See the History of the Jews, n. 15.
