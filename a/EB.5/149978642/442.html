CAMPIDUCTORES

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMPIDUCTORES, or CAMPIDUCTORES, in the Roman army, were officers who instructed the soldiery in the discipline and exercises of war, and the art of handling their weapons to advantage. These are also sometimes called campigeni, and armiductores.
