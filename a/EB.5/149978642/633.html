CANZONETTA, a diminutive of canzone, denoting a little short song. The canzonette neapolitane has two strains, each whereof is sung twice over, as the vaudevilles of the French: The canzonette siciliane is a species of jig, the measure whereof is usually twelve eighths, and six eighths, and sometimes both, as rondeaus.
