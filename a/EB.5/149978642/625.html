CANTRED

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANTRED, or CANTREE, signifies an hundred villages. It is a British word, compounded of the adjective cant, i. e. hundred; and tree, a town or village. In Wales some of the counties are divided into cantreds, as in England into hundreds.
