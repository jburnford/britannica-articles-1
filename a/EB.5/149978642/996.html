CARYOCAR

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARYOCAR, in botany: A genus of the tetragynia order, belonging to the polyandria class of plants. The calyx is quinquepartite, the petals five, the styles more frequently four. The fruit is a plum, with nucleuses, and four furrows netted.
