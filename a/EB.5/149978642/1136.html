CASU CONSIMILI

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASU CONSIMILI, in law, a writ of entry granted where a tenant, by courtesy or, for life, aliens either in fee, in tail, or for the term of another's life. It is brought by him in reversion against the person to whom such tenant does so alien to the prejudice of the reversioner in the tenant's life-time.
