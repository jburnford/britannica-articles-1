CHARM

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARM, a term derived from the Latin carmen, a
"verse;" and used to denote a magic power, or spell,
by which, with the assistance of the devil, sorcerers
and witches were supposed to do wonderful things,
far surpassing the power of nature.
