CANTHI

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANTHI, in anatomy, cavities at the extremities of the eye-lids, commonly called the corners of the eye: the greater of them, or the greater canthus, is next the nose; the lesser of them, or the little canthus, lies towards the temple.
