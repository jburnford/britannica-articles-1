CAPILLAMENT

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPILLAMENT, in a general sense, signifies a hair: whence the word is applied to several things,

which on account of their length or their fineness resemble hairs: as,

CAPILLAMENTS of the Nerves, in anatomy, the fine fibres or filaments whereof the nerves are composed.
