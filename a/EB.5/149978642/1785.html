CHERLERIA, in botany: A genus of the trigynia-order, belonging to the decandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 22d order, Caryophyllae. The calyx is pentaphyllous; there are five nectaria, biud, and petal-like; the anthers alternately barren; the capsule is trilocular and three-valved.
