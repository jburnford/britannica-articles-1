CADORINO

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CADORINO, a province of Italy, in the territory of Venice; bounded on the east by Friuli Proper, on the south and west by the Bellunese, and by the bishopric of Brixen on the north. It is a very mountainous country, but pretty populous. The only town is Pieve de Cadore.
