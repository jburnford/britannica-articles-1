Antecedent CAUSE

sub_entry · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Antecedent CAUSE, πρὸ τὰς προηγούμεναι, a latent disposition of the body, from whence some disease may arise; such as a plethora in respect of a fever, a cacochymia in respect of a scurvy.
