CHRYSOLITE-PASTE

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHRYSOLITE-PASTE, a kind of glass made in imitation of natural chrysolite, by mixing two ounces of prepared crystal with ten ounces of red-lead, adding 12 grains of crocus martis made with vinegar; and then baking the whole for 24 hours, or longer, in a well luted cucurbit.
