CHALCIDICE

CHALCIDICE, (anc. geog.) an eastern district of Macedonia, stretching northwards between the Sinus Toroneus and Singiticus. Formerly a part of Thrace, but invaded by Philip of Macedon. Named from the city Chalcis near Olynthus.
