CHORIAMBUS

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHORIAMBUS, in ancient poetry, a foot consisting of four syllables, whereof the first and last are long, and the two middle ones are short; or, which is the same thing, it is made up of a trocheus and iambus: such is the word nobilis.
