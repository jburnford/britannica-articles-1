CALENDER OF MONTEITH

CALENDER OF MONTEITH, a district in the
south-west corner of Perthshire in Scotland, from which
a branch of the ancient family of Livington had the
title of Earl. The chief seat of the family near Falkirk
is also called Calender. Both estate and title
were forfeited for being engaged in the rebellion
1715.
