CARCERES

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARCERES, in the ancient Circensian games, were inclosures in the circus, wherein the horses were restrained till the signal was given for starting, when, by an admirable contrivance, they all at once flew open.
