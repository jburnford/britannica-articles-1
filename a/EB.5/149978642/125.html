CÆSIA SYLVA

article · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CÆSIA SYLVA (anc. geog.), a wood in Germany, part of the great Sylva Hercynia, situated partly in the duchy of Cleves, and partly in Westphalia between Wesel and Kessfeld.
