CELEUSMA

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CELEUSMA, or CELEUMA, in antiquity, the shout or cry of the seamen, whereby they animated each other in their work of rowing. The word is formed from celestus, to call, to give the signal.
