CAMBIST

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMBIST, a name given in France to those who trade in notes and bills of exchange. The word cambist, though a term of antiquity, is even now a technical word, of some use among merchants, traders, and bankers. Some derive it from the Latin cambium, or rather cambio.
