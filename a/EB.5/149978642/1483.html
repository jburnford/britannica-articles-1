CERTOSA

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CERTOSA, a celebrated Carthusian monastery, in the territory of the Pavese, in the duchy of Milan, four miles from Pavia; its park is surrounded with a wall 20 miles in circumference; but there are several small towns and villages therein.
