CHAULIEU

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAULIEU (William Amfrye de), abbé d'Amale, one of the most polite and ingenious of the French poets, was born in 1639, and died at the age of 84. The most complete edition of his poems is that printed in 2 vols 8vo in 1733.
