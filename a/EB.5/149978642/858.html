CARLOWITZ

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARLOWITZ, a small town of Hungary, in Sclavonia, remarkable for a peace concluded here between the Turks and Christians in 1669. It is seated on the west side of the Danube, in E. Long. 19. 5. N. Lat. 45. 25.
