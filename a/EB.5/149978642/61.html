CACOPHONIA

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CACOPHONIA, in grammar and rhetoric, the meeting of two letters, or syllables, which yield an uncouth and disagreeable sound. The word is compounded of κακός evil, and φωνή voice.
