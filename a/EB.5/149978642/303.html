CALMAR

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALMAR, a strong sea-port of Sweden, in the province of Smaland, divided into two towns, the old and the new; but of the former there remains only the church and a few houses. The new town is built a little way from the other, and has large handsome houses. E. Long. 16. 15. N. Lat. 56. 48.
