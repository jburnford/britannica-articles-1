CELSUS

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CELSUS, an Epicurean philosopher, in the second century. He wrote a work against the Christians, entitled, The true Discourse; to which Origen, at the desire of Ambrose his friend, wrote a learned answer. To this philosopher Lucian dedicated his Pseudeomanies.
