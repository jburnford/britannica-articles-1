CELEUSTES

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CELEUSTES, in ancient navigation, the boatswain or officer appointed to give the rowers the signal, when they were to pull, and when to stop. He was also denominated epopeus, and by the Romans portifculus; sometimes simply hortator.
