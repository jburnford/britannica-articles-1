CAMPITÆ

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMPITÆ, in church history, an appellation given to the donatists, on account of their assembling in the fields for want of churches. For a similar reason, they were also denominated Montenses and Rupitani.
