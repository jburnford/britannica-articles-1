CALEFACTION

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALEFACTION, the production of heat in a
body from the action of fire, or that impulse impres-
sed by a hot body on others around it. This word is
used in pharmacy, by way of distinction from coction,
which implies boiling; whereas calefaction is only heat-
ing a thing.
