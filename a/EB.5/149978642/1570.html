CHALLON-SUR-MARNE

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHALLON-SUR-MARNE, a large episcopal town of France, in Champagne. It carries on a considerable trade in shalloons, and other woollen stuffs. It is seated

between two fine meadows on the rivers Marne, Mau, and Nau, in E. Long. 4. 37. N. Lat. 48. 57.
