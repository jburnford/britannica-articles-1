CERAMBYX, in zoology, a genus of insects of the beetle kind, belonging to the order of insecta coleoptera. The antennae are long and small; the breast is spinous or gibbous; and the elytra are linear. There are no less than 83 species enumerated by Linnæus, principally distinguished by the figure of the breast.
