CHARISIUS

CHARISIUS, in the heathen theology, a surname given to Jupiter. The word is derived from χάρη, gratia, "grace" or "favour;" he being the God by whose influence men obtain the favour and affection of one another. On which account the Greeks used at their meals to make a libation of a cup to Jupiter Charisius.
