CHARITY of St Hippolitus

sub_entry · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARITY of St Hippolitus, a religious congregation founded about the end of the 14th century, by one Bernardin Alvarez, a Mexican, in honour of St Hippolitus the martyr, patron of the city of Mexico; and approved by Pope Gregory XIII.
