CANDY, a town of Asia, and capital of a kingdom of

of the same name, in the island of Ceylon. It has been often burnt by the Portuguese, when they were masters of these coasts. The houses are very poor, low, and badly furnished. E. Long. 79. 12. N. Lat. 7. 35.
