CAORLO

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAORLO, a small island in the gulf of Venice, on the coast of Friuli, 20 miles south-west of Aquileia, subject to Venice. It has a town of the same name, with a bishop's see.
