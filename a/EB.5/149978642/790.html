CARDERS

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARDERS, spinners, weavers, fullers, sheermen, and dyers, not performing their duty in their occupations, shall yield to the party grieved double damages; to be committed until payment. One justice to hear and determine complaints.
