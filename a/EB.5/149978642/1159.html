CATAGOGION

article · 423 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATAGOGION, a heathen festival at Ephesus, celebrated on the 22d of January, in which the devotees run about the streets, dressed in divers antic and unseemly manners, with huge cudgels in their hands, and carrying with them the images of their gods; in which guise they ravished the women they met with, abused and often killed the men, and committed many other disorders, to which the religion of the day gave a sanction.
