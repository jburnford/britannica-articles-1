CELLS

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CELLS, in anatomy, little bags, or bladders, where fluids or other matters are lodged; called loculi, cellular, &c. Thus the cellula adiposa are the little cells where the fat is contained; cellula in the colon, are spaces wherein the excrements are detained till voided, &c.
