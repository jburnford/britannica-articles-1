CAROLUS

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAROLUS, a small copper coin, with a little silver mixed with it, struck under Charles VIII. of France. The carolus was worth 12 deniers when it ceased to be current. Those which are still current in trade in Lorraine, or in some neighbouring provinces, go under the name of French sols.
