CECROPS

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CECROPS, the founder and first king of Athens, about the time of Moses the lawgiver of the Hebrews. He was the first who established civil government, religious rites, and marriage among the Greeks; and died after a reign of 50 years. See Attica, no 4.
