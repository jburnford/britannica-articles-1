CAGE

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAGE, in carpentry, signifies an outer-work of timber, enclosing another within it. In this sense we say, the cage of a wind-mill. The cage of a stair-case denotes the wooden sides or walls which inclose it.
