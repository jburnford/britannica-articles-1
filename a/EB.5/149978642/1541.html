CHAJOTLI

article · 454 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAJOTLI, or CHAYOTI, a Mexican fruit of a round shape, and similar in the hulk with which it is covered to the chestnut, but four or five times larger, and of a much deeper green colour. Its kernel is of a greenish white, and has a large stone in the middle, which is white, and like it in substance. It is boiled, and the stone eat with it. This fruit is produced by a twining perennial plant, the root of which is also good to eat. See Plate CXXXVIII.
