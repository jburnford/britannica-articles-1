CARMONA, an ancient town of Spain in Andalusia. The gate towards Seville is one of the most extraordinary pieces of antiquity in all Spain. It is seated in a fertile country, 15 miles east of Seville. W. Long. 5. 37. N. Lat. 37. 24.
