CESPITOSÆ PLANTÆ

article · 167 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CESPITOSÆ PLANTÆ (from cespes, turf or sod), are those plants which produce many stems from one root, and thence form a close thick carpet on the surface of the earth.
