CALYCANTHEMÆ

CALYCANTHEMÆ, in botany, an order of plants in the Fragmenta methodi naturalis of Linnaeus, in which are the following genera, viz. epilobium, anthera, jussiaea, ludwigia, oldenlandia, isnarda, &c. See BOTANY, sect. vi. 17.
