CHIAUSI

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHIAUSI, among the Turks, officers employed in executing the vizirs, bashaws, and other great men: the orders for doing this, the grand signior sends them wrapped up in a black cloth; on the reception of which, they immediately perform their office.
