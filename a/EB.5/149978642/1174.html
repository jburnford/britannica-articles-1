CATAPHRACTÆ NAVES

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATAPHRACTÆ NAVES, ships armed and covered in fight, so that they could not be easily damaged by the enemy. They were covered over with boards or planks, on which the soldiery were placed to defend them; the rowers sitting underneath, thus screened from the enemy's weapons.
