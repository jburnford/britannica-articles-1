CERALIA, in botany, from Ceres the goddess of corn; Linnaeus's name for the larger esculent seeds of the grasses: these are rice, wheat, rye, barley, oats, millet, panic grass, Indian millet, holcus, zizania, and maize. To this head may be likewise referred dandel, (folium); which, by preparation, is rendered esculent.
