CEUTA

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CEUTA, a maritime town of Barbary in Africa, and in the kingdom of Fez, seated on the straits of Gibraltar, opposite that place, in W. Long. 6. 25. N. Lat. 36. 35. John king of Portugal took it from the Moors in 1415, but it now belongs to Spain. In 1697, it sustained a vigorous siege by the Moors.
