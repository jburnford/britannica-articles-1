CHERILUS

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHERILUS, of Samos, a Greek poet, flourished 479 years before Christ. He sung the victory gained by the Athenians over Xerxes, and was rewarded with a piece of gold for every verse. His poem had afterwards the honour of being rehearsed yearly with the works of Homer.
