CAMPDEN

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMPDEN, a small town of Gloucestershire in England, containing about 200 houses. It gives title of Viscount, by courtesy, to Earl of Gainborough his son. W. Long. 1. 50. N. Lat. 52.
