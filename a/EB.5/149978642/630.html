CANUSIUM

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANUSIUM (anc. geog.), a town of Apulia, on the right or south side of the Aufidus, to the west of Cannæ; whither the Romans fled after the defeat sustained there. It was famous for its red shining wool; whence those who wore clothes made of it were called Canusinati. Now called CANOSA; which see.
