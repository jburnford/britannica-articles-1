CARGO

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARGO denotes all the merchandises and effects which are laden on board a ship.

Super-Cargo, a person employed by merchants to go a voyage, oversee the cargo, and dispose of it to the best advantage.
