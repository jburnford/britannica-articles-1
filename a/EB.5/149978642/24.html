CABINDA

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CABINDA, the chief port of the kingdom of Angoy in Loango in Africa. It is situated at the mouth of a river of the same name about five leagues north of Cape Palmerino, on the north side of the mouth of the river Zaire. The bay is very commodious for trade, wooding, and watering.
