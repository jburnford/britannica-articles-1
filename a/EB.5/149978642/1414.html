CENTO

article · 306 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CENTO, in poetry, a work wholly composed of verses or passages promiscuously taken from other authors, only disposed in a new form and order.—Proba Falconia has written the life of Jesus Christ in centos taken from Virgil. Alexander Ross has done the like in his Christados, and Stephen de Pleure the same.
