CHRONOGRAM, a species of false wit, consisting in tois, that a certain date or epocha is expressed by numeral letters of two or more verses; such is that which makes the motto of a medal struck by Gustavus Adolphus in 1632:

ChrIstVs. DVX; ergo trIVMphVs.
