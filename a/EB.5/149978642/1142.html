CASUALTY

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASUALTY, in Scot's law. Casualties of a superior, are those duties and emoluments which a superior has right to demand out of his vassal's estate, over and besides, the constant yearly duties established

by the reddendo of his charter upon certain casual events.
