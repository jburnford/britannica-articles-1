CABALLINE

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CABALLINE, denotes something belonging to horses: thus caballine aloe is so called, from its being chiefly used for purging horses; and common brimstone is called sulphur caballinum for a like reason.
