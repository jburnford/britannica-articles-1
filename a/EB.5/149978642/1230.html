CATTI

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATTI, a people of Germany, very widely spread, on the east reaching to the river Sala, on the north to Westphalia; occupying, besides Hesse, the Wetterau, and part of the tract on the Rhine, and on the banks of the river Lohne. The Hercynian forest began and ended in their country.
