CAPITULUM

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPITULUM, in the ancient military art, was a transverse beam, wherein were holes through which passed the strings whereby the arms of huge engines, as balistæ, catapultæ, and scorpions, were played or worked.
