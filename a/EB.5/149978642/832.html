CARICATURA

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARICATURA, in painting, denotes the concealment of real beauties, and the exaggeration of blemishes, but still so as to preserve a resemblance of the object. The word is Italian; formed of carica, a load, burden, or the like.
