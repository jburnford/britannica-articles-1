CANIS Minor, the little dog, in astronomy, a constellation of the northern hemisphere; called also by the Greeks, Procyon, and by the Latins Antecanus and Canicula. The stars in the constellation canis minor, are in Ptolemy's catalogue 2; in Tycho's, 5; in Hevelius's, 13; and in the British catalogue, 14.
