CARRYING

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARRYING, in falconry, signifies a hawk's flying away with the quarry. Carrying is one of the ill qualities of a hawk, which she acquires either by a dislike of the falconry, or not being sufficiently broke to the lure.
