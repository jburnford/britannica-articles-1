CARDASSUS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARDASSUS, is also the name which, in the cloth manufactories of Languedoc, they give to a sort of large card, which is used for carding the dyed wool, designed for making cloth of mixed colours.
