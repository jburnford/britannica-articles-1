CANEPHORÆ

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANEPHORÆ, in Grecian antiquity, virgins who, when they became marriageable, presented certain baskets full of little curiosities to Diana, in order to get leave to depart out of her train, and change their state of life.
