CANETO

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANETO, a strong town of Italy in the duchy of Mantua, seated on the river Oglio, which was taken by the Imperialists in 1701, by the French in 1702, afterwards by the Imperialists, and then by the French in 1705. E. Long. 10. 45. N. Lat. 40. 55.
