CALLE

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLE (anc. geog.), a town of Hither Spain, situated on an eminence which hangs over the river Durus; whose port was at the mouth of the river. Now Porto, Oporto, or Port a Port.
