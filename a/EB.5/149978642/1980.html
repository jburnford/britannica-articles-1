CHRONICLE, in matters of literature, a species or kind of history disposed according to the order of time, and agreeing in most respects with annals. See ANNALS. Parian CHRONICLE. See ARUNDELIAN Marble. Since that article was printed, in which an abstract was given of Mr Robertson's doubts and observations respecting the authenticity of the Parian Chronicle, one or two publications have since appeared in answer, but none of them calculated to remove the objections or materially to affect the arguments that had been stated with so much learning and ingenuity against it.
