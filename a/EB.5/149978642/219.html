CALEA

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALEA, in botany: A genus of the polygamia aequalis order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Compositae. The receptacle is paleaceous, the pappus hairy, and the calyx imbricated.
