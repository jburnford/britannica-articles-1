CASTING

article · 354 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTING, in foundry, the running a metal into a mould, prepared for that purpose.

CASTING of Metals, of Letters, Bells, &c. See the article FOUNDRY.

CASTING in Sand or Earth is the running of metals between two frames, or molds, filled with sand or earth, wherein the figure that the metal is to take has been impressed en creux, by means of the pattern.
