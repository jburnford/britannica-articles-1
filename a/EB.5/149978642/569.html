CANONICAL HOURS

article · 345 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANONICAL HOURS, are certain stated times of the day, assigned, more especially by the Romish church, to the offices of prayer and devotion. Such are matins, lauds, sixth, ninth, vespers. In our country the canonical hours are from eight to twelve in the forenoon, before or after which marriage cannot be legally performed in any parish-church.
