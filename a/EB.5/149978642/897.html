CARPENTER

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARPENTER, a person who practises CARPENTRY. The word is formed from the French charpentier, which signifies the same, formed of charpente, which denotes timber; or rather from the Latin carpentarius, a maker of carpenta, or carriages.
