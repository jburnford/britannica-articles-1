CASAURINA

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASAURINA, in botany: A genus of the monandria order, belonging to the monoecia class of plants. The male has the calyx of the amentum; the corolla a bipartite small scale. The female has a calyx of the amentum, no corolla; the style bipartite.
