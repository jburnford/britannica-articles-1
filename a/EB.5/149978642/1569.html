CHALLON-SUR-SAONE

CHALLON-SUR-SAONE, an ancient town of France, in Burgundy, and capital of the Chalonnois, with a citadel and bishop's see. It is seated on the river Saone, in E. Long. 5. 7. N. Lat. 46. 47.
