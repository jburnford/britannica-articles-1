CHRISM PENCE

CHRISM PENCE, CHRISMATIS DENARI, or CHRISMATIS DENARI, a tribute anciently paid to the bishop by the parish-clergy, for their chrism, consecrated at Easter for the ensuing year: this was afterwards condemned as simoniacal.
