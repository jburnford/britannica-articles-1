CHAFFERCONNERS

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAFFERCONNERS, in commerce, printed linen manufactured in the Great Mogul's dominions. They are imported by the way of Surat; and are of the number of those linens prohibited in France.
