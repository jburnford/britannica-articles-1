CARNERO

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARNERO, in geography, a name given to that part of the gulph of Venice which extends from the western coast of Istria to the island of Grosso and the coast of Morachia.

CARNERO is likewise the name of the cape to the west of the mouth of the bay of Gibraltar.
