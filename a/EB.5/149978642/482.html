CANCER, in astronomy, one of the twelve signs, represented on the globe in the form of a crab, and thus marked (♋) in books. It is the fourth constellation in the zodiac, and that from which one quadrant of the ecliptic takes its denomination. The reason generally assigned for its name as well as figure, is a supposed resemblance which the sun's motion in this sign bears to the crab-fish.
