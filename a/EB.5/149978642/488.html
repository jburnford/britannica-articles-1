CANDELARES

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANDELARES (from candela a candle), the name of an order in the former editions of Linnaeus's Fragments of a natural method, consisting of these three genera, rhizophora, myssa, and minuspora. They are removed, in the latter editions, into the order HOLARACEÆ; which see.
