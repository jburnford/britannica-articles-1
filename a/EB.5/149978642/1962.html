CHRISOM

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHRISOM, CHRISMATIS, was anciently the facecloth or piece of linen laid over the child's head when it was baptized. Whence, in our bills of mortality, children who die in the month are called chrismoms. The time between the child's birth and baptism was also called chrismoms.
