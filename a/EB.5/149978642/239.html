CALES

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALES (anc. geog.), a municipal city of some note in Campania, at no great distance from Casilinum. The epithet Calenus is by Horace and Juvenal applied to a generous wine which the territory produced.
