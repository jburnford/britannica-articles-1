CENSIO hastaria

sub_entry · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CENSIO hastaria, a punishment inflicted on a Roman soldier for some offence, as laziness or luxury, whereby his hastia or spear was taken from him, and consequently his wages and hopes of preferment stopped.
