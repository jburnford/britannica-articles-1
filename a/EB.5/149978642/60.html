CACOCHYLIA

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CACOCHYLIA, or CACOCYMHIA, a vicious state of the vital humours, especially of the mass of blood; arising either from a disorder of the secretions or excretions, or from external contagion. The word is Greek, compounded of κακός ill, and χυμός juice.
