CARMAGNOLA

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARMAGNOLA, a fortified town of Italy, in Piedmont, with a good castle. It was taken by the French in 1691, and retaken by prince Eugene the same year. It is seated in a country abounding in corn, flax, and silk, near the river Po, in E. Long. 7. 32. N. Lat. 44. 43.
