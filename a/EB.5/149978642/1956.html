CHOUS

article · 449 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHOUS, in the eastern military orders, the title of the messengers of the divan of Janissaries. There are several degrees of honour in this post. When a person is first advanced to it, he is called a enchuk, or little chous; after this he is advanced to be the alloy chous; that is, the messenger of ceremonies; and from this, having passed through the office of petelwa, or procurator of the effects of the body, he is advanced to be the bus chous.
