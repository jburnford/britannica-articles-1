CARAGROUTH

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARAGROUTH, in commerce, a silver coin of the empire, weighing nine drachms. It goes at Constantinople for 120 aspers. There are four sorts of them, which are all equally current and of the same value.
