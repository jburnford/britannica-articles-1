JENCOPING

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JENCOPING, a town of Sweden, in the province of Smaland, seated on the south side of the lake Wetter, with a strong citadel. The houses are all built with wood. E. Long. 14. 20. N. Lat. 57. 22.
