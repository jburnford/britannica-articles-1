IRIS, in physiology, the rainbow. The word is Greek, iris, supposed by some to be derived from iris "I speak, I tell;" as being a meteor that is supposed to foretell, or rather to declare rain. See RAINBOW.

Lunar Iris, or Moon-rainbow. See RAINBOW (Lunar).
