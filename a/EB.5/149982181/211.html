ICOSAHEDRON

article · 335 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ICOSAHEDRON, in geometry, a regular solid, consisting of 20 triangular pyramids, whose vertexes meet in the centre of a sphere supposed to circum-

scribe it; and therefore have their height and bases equal; wherefore the solidity of one of these pyramids multiplied by 20, the number of bases gives the solid contents of the icosahedron.
