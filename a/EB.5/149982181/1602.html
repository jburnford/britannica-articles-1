LAVERNA

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAVERNA, in antiquity, the goddess of thieves and cheats among the Romans, who honoured her with public worship, because she was supposed to favour those who wished that their designs might not be discovered. Varro says, that she had an altar near one of the gates of Rome; hence called porta laevrnavis.
