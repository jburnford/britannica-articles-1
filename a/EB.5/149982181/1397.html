LAGUNES OF VENICE

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAGUNES OF VENICE, are marshes or lakes in Italy on which Venice is seated. They communicate with the sea, and are the security of the city. There are about 60 islands in these Lagunes, which together make a bishop's see. Eurano is the most considerable, next to those on which Venice stands.
