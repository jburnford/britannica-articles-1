LAGEMAN

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAGEMAN (lagamannus), homo habens legem, or homo legalis seu legitimus; such as we call now "good men of the jury." The word is frequently used in Domesday, and the laws of Edward the Confessor, cap. 38.
