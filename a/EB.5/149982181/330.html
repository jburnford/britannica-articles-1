IMBRICATED

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMBRICATED, is used by some botanists, to express the figure of the leaves of some plants, which are hollowed like an imbric, or gutter-tile, or are laid in close series over one another like the tiles of a house.
