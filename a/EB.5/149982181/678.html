INTESTINES, INTESTINA, in anatomy, the guts or bowels; those hollow, membranous, cylindrical parts, extended from the right orifice of the stomach to the anus; by which the chyle is conveyed to the lacteals, and the excrements are voided. See ANATOMY, no 93.
