LUBACH

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUBACH, a handsome and strong town of Germany, in the circle of Austria, and in Carniola, with a bishop's see, a castle, and very handsome houses. It is seated on a river of the same name, wherein are the largest craw-fish in Europe. E. Long. 14. 45. N. Lat. 46. 20.
