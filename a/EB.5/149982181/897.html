ITTIGIUS (Thomas), a learned professor of divinity at Leipzig, and son of John Ittigius, professor of physic in the same university. He first published A Treatise upon Burning Mountains; after which he became a minister, and exercised that function in various churches there. He furnished several papers in the Leipzig acts, besides publishing some historical works and dissertations. He died in 1710.
