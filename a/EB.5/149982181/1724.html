LEGIO VII

article · 461 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEGIO VII. GEMINA, (anc. geog.) a town or station of that legion in the Astures. Now Leon, capital of the province of that name in Spain. W. Long. 6. 5. Lat. 43.—Another LEGIO, a town of Galilee; from which Jerome determines the distances of the places in Galilee; not a bare encampment, though the name might originally be owing to that circumstance; it lay 15 miles to the west of Nazareth, between mount Tabor and the Mediterranean. Now thought to be Legone.
