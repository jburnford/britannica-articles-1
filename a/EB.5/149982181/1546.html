LARRIBUNDAR

LARRIBUNDAR, a sea-port town of Asia, in Indostan; seated at the mouth of the river Sinda, or Indus, with a harbour capable of receiving ships of 200 tons burden. It is but a small place, consisting of about 100 houses built with wood; but has a stone fort, with a few guns. E. Long. 67. 0. N. Lat. 25. 0.
