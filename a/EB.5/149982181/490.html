INDIGENOUS

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INDIGENOUS, of indigena, denotes a native of a country, or that which was originally born or produced in the country where it is found. In this sense, particular species of animals and plants are said to be indigenous in the country where they are native, in opposition to Exotic.
