LANFRANC

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LANFRANC, an Italian, born at Pavia, became archbishop of Canterbury in 1070. He disputed against Berengarius in the council held at Rome in 1059, and wrote against him concerning the real presence in the eucharist. He had other disputes, &c. and died in 1089.
