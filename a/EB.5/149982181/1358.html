LACONICUM

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LACONICUM, (whence our term laconic), a short pithy sententious speech, such as the Lacedæmonians were remarkable for: Their way of delivering themselves was very concise, and much to the purpose. See the preceding article.
