JUSTINIANI

article · 306 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUSTINIANI (Bernard), was born at Venice in 1408. He obtained the senator's robe at the age of 19, served the republic in several embassies, and was elected procurator of St. Mark in 1474. He was a learned man, and wrote the History of Venice, with some other works of considerable merit; and died in 1498.
