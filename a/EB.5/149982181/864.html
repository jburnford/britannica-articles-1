ISMARUS

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISMARUS (anc. geog.), a town of the Cicones in Thrace, giving name to a lake. In Virgil it is called Ismara. Servius supposes it to be a mountain of Thrace; on which mountain Orpheus dwelt.
