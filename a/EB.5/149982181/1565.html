LATHRÆA

LATHRÆA, in botany: A genus of the angiospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 40th order, Perfonata. The calyx is quadrifid; there is a depressed glandule at the base of the future of the germin. The capsule is unilocular.
