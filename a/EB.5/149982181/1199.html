KINGSBRIDGE

KINGSBRIDGE, a town of Devonshire, 217 miles from London. It is a pretty place, with a harbour for boats, a free school, a market, and a fair. This is a chapel of ease to Chelton, and has a bridge over the Salcomb to Dodbrook.
