LANTERN

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LANTERN, or LANTHORN, a device to carry a candle in; being a kind of cover usually made of white iron, with fashions of some transparent matter, as glass, horn, &c. to transmit the light.
