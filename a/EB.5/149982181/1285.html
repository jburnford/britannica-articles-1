KNOXIA

KNOXIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 47th order, Stellata. The corolla is monopetalous, and funnel-shaped; there are two furrowed seeds; the calyx has one leaf larger than the rest.
