LECTI

Cross-references: BEDS

LECTI, beds or couches, were of two kinds among the Romans, as being destined to two different uses, to lie upon at entertainments, and to repose upon for nightly rest. The first were called lecti tricliniari, the other lecti cubicularii. See BEDS.
