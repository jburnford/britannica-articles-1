LABIAU

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LABIAU, a small town of Ducal Prussia, in a circle of the same name, seated at the mouth of the river Deime, with a strong castle, two sides of which are surrounded with water, and the other defended by a wall and ditch. E. Long. 19. 56. N. Lat. 55. 17.
