JOIGNY

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JOIGNY, a town of France, in Champagne, and in the diocese of Sens, with a very handsome castle. It consists of three parishes, and is pleasantly situated on the river Yonne, in E. Long. 3. 25. N. Lat. 47. 56.
