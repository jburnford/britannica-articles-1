INCHOATIVE verbs

sub_entry · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INCHOATIVE verbs, denote, according to Priscian and other grammarians, verbs that are characterized by the termination scō or scor, added to their primitives: as augesco from augēo, calcesco from calēs, dulcesco from dulcēs, irascor from irā, &c.
