Honorary KNIGHTHOOD

sub_entry · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Honorary KNIGHTHOOD, is that which princes confer on other princes, and even on their own great ministers and favourites; such are knights of the Garter, Bath, St Patrick, Nova Scotia, Thistle, &c. See these articles; and for a representation of their different insignia, see Plate CCLVIII.
