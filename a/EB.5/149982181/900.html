ITZEHOA

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ITZEHOA, an ancient and handsome town of Germany, in the circle of Lower Saxony, and duchy of Holstein. It belongs to the king of Denmark, and is seated on the river Stoer, in E. Long. 9. 25. N. Lat. 54. 8.
