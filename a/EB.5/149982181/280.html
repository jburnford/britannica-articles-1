IGLAW

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IGLAW, a considerable and populous town of Germany, in Moravia, where they have a manufactory of good cloth, and excellent beer. It is seated on the river Iгла, 40 miles west of Brin, and 80 south-east of Prague. E. Long. 15. 5. N. Lat. 49. 10.
