LEONARD DE NOBLET

LEONARD DE NOBLET (St.) an ancient town of France, in the province of Guienne and territory of Limousin, with a considerable manufactory of cloth and paper. It is seated on the river Vienne, in E. Long. 1. 35. N. Lat. 45. 50.
