LAGURUS

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAGURUS, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the triandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 4th order, Gramina. The calyx is bivalved, with a villous awn; the exterior petal of the corolla terminated by two awns, with a third on its back retorted.
