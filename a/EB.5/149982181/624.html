INSUBRIUM AGER

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INSUBRIUM AGER, (anc. geog.), a district of the Transpadana; situated between the Ticinus to the west, the Addua to the east, the Padus to the south, and Orobii to the north. The people called Insabres by Livy, Insabri by Ptolemy, and Insombres by Strabo. Now the Duchy of Milan.
