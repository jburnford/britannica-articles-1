JUDICIUM CALUMNIE

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: INJUSTIO

JUDICIUM CALUMNIE, was an action brought against the plaintiff for false accusation. The punishment, upon conviction, was injusto frontis, or branding in the forehead. See INJUSTIO.
