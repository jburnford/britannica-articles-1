HYPERCRITIC

Cross-references: CRITICISM

HYPERCRITIC, an over-rigid censor or critic: one who will let nothing pass, but animadverts severely on the slightest fault. See CRITICISM. The word is compounded of υπερ super, "over, above, be-

yond;" and κριτικός, of κριτός, judex, of κρίνω, judicio, Hyperdulia.
"I judge."
