LANDAU

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LANDAU, an ancient, handsome, and very strong town of France, in Lower Alsace. It was formerly imperial, and belonged to Germany, till the treaty of Münster, when it was given up to France. It is seated on the river Zurich, in a pleasant fertile country. E. Long. 8. 12. N. Lat. 49. 12.
