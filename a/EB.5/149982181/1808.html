LEPTOPOLYGINGLIMI

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEPTOPOLYGINGLIMI, in natural history, a genus of fossil shells, distinguished by a number of minute teeth at the cardo; whereof we find great numbers at Harwich-cliff, and in the marine-pits of Sussex.
