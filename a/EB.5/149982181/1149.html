KIDDERS

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KIDDERS, those that badge or carry corn, dead victuals, or other merchandise, up and down to sell: every person being a common badger, kiddier, lader, or carrier, &c. says the stat. 5. Eliz. cap. 12. And they are called kidders, 13 Eliz. cap. 25.
