IMPALE

article · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: HERALDRY

IMPALE, in heraldry, is to conjoin two coats of arms pale-wise. Women impale their coats of arms with those of their husbands. See HERALDRY.

To impale cities, camps, fortifications, &c. is to inclose them with pallisades.

To IMPALS, or Empale, signifies also to put to death by spitting on a stake fixed upright.
