IKENILD STREET

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IKENILD STREET, one of the four famous ways which the Romans made in England, called Stratum Icenorum, because it began in the country of the Iceni, who inhabited Norfolk, Suffolk, and Cambridge-shire.
