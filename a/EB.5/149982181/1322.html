LABEL

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LABEL, in law, is a narrow slip of paper, or parchment, affixed to a deed or writing, in order to hold the appending seal.—Any paper annexed by way of addition or explication, to any will or testament, is also called a label or codicil.
