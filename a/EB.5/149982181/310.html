ILKUCH

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ILKUCH, a royal town of Poland, in the palatinate of Cracow, remarkable for its silver mines mixed with lead. It is seated in a barren and mountainous country, in E. Long. 20. 0. N. Lat. 50. 26.
