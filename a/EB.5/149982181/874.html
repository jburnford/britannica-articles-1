ISOPYRUM

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISOPYRUM, in botany: A genus of the polygynia order, belonging to the polyandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 26th order, Multiflora. There is no calyx, but five petals; the neçaria trifid and tubular; the capsules recurved and polyspermous.
