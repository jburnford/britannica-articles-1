LAGERSTROEMIA

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAGERSTROEMIA, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the polyandria class of plants. The corolla is hexapetalous, and curled; the calyx sexfid, and campanulated; there are many stamens, and of these the six exterior ones thicker than the rest, and longer than the petals.
