JUXTAPOSITION

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUXTAPOSITION, is used by philosophers to denote that species of growth which is performed by the apposition of new matter to the surface or outside of old. In which sense it stands opposed to intussuption; where the growth of a body is performed by the reception of a juice within it diffused through its canals.
