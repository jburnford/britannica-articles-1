KIRIATHAIM

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KIRIATHAIM, (anc. geog.), one of the towns built by the Reubenites; reckoned to the tribe of Reuben (Joshua, xiii.), 12 miles to the west of Midaba. The ancient residence of the giants called Emim.
