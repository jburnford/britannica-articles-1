KNIGHTON

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KNIGHTON, a well built town of Radnorshire in South Wales, 155 miles from London. It is pleasantly situated on an elevation rising from a small river, which divided this part of Wales from Shropshire. It carries on a considerable trade, and has a market and a fair.
