INDIVIDUAL

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INDIVIDUAL, a particular being of any species, or that which cannot be divided into two or more beings equal or alike.

The usual division in logic is made into genera, or into genus; those genera into species; and those species into individuals.
