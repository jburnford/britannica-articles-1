ITYS

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ITYS (fab. hist.), a son of Tercus king of Thrace, by Proene daughter of Pandion king of Athens. He was killed by his mother when he was about six years old, and served up before his father. He was changed into a pheasant, his mother into a swallow, and his father into an owl.
