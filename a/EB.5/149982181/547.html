INGROSSER

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FORESTALLING · ENGROSSING

INGROSSER, or ENGROSSER, in common law, is one who buys up corn growing, or any provisions by wholesale, before the market, to sell again. See FORESTALLING.

It also signifies a clerk who writes records or instruments of law on skins of parchment. See ENGROSSING.
