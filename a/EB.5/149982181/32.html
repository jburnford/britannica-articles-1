HYPERCATALECTIC

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPERCATALECTIC, in the Greek and Latin poetry, is applied to a verse that has one or two syllables too much, or beyond the regular and just measure; as,

Musa sorores sunt Minerva:

Also,

Musa sorores Palladis iugent.
