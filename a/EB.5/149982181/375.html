IMPETRATION, the act of obtaining any thing by request or prayer.

IMPETRATION was more particularly used in our statutes for the pre-obtaining of benefices and church-offices in England from the court of Rome, which did belong to the disposal of the king and other lay patrons of the realm; the penalty whereof is the same with that of provisors, 25 E. III.
