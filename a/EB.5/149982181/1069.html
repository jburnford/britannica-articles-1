KECKERMAN

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KECKERMAN (Bartholomew), a native of Dantzick, and professor of philosophy there about the beginning of the 17th century, composed systems of almost all the sciences, in which he shows more method than genius. He died in 1609, fairly worn out at the age of 38 with mere scholastic drudgery.
