JUGORA

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUGORA, a considerable province of Muscovy, depending on the government of Archangel. It has the title of a duchy; and is inhabited by a kind of Tartars, who are very savage, and much of the same disposition with the Samoiedes.
