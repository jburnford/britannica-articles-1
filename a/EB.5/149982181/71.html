HYRCANIA (anc. geog.), a country of the farther Asia, lying to the south-east of the Mare Hyrcanum or Caspium; with Media on the west, Parthia on the south, and Margiana on the west. Famous for its tygers (Virgil); for its vines, figs, and olives, (Strabo).
