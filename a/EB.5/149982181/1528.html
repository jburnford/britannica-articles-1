LARARIUM

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LARARIUM, was a chapel which the Romans frequently had in their houses for the household gods, called laræ. Spartan says, that Alexander the son of Mammus kept in his lararium the figure of our Saviour, together with his other idols.
