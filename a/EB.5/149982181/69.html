HYPOXIS

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPOXIS, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the hexandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 10th order, Coromariae. The corolla is divided into six parts, and peristylum, superior; the capsule narrowing at the base; the calyx a bivalved glume.
