INDEX

Cross-references: ALGEBRA · GLOBE

INDEX, in arithmetic and algebra, shows to what power any quantity is involved, and is otherwise called its exponent. See ALGEBRA, p. 412.

INDEX of a Book, is that part annexed to a book, referring to the particular matters or passages therein contained.

INDEX of a Globe, is a little style fitted on to the northpole, and turning round with it, pointing to certain divisions in the hour-circle. It is sometimes also called gnomon. See GLOBE.
