KANISCA

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KANISCA, a very strong town of Lower Hungary, capital of the county of Selawar. It was taken by the Imperialists in 1690. It is seated on the river Drave, in E. Long. 17. 37. N. Lat. 46. 23.
