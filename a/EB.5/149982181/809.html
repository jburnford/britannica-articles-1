IRON-Moulds

sub_entry · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IRON-Moulds, and spots of ink in linen, may be taken out by dipping the stained part in water, sprinkling it with a little of the powdered essential salt of wood-forel, then rubbing on a pewter plate, and washing the spot out with warm water.
