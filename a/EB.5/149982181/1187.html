KINCARDINESHIRE. See MEARNS.
KINDRED, in law, persons related to one another, whereof the law reckons three degrees or lines, viz. the descending, ascending, and collateral line. See CONSANGUINITY and DESCENT.

On there being no kindred in the descending line, the inheritance passes in the collateral one.
