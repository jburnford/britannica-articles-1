LACHNEA

LACHNEA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the octandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 31st order, Vesiculæ. There is no calyx; the corolla is quadrifid with the limb unequal; there is one seed a little resembling a berry.
