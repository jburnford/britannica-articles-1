KADMONÆI

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KADMONÆI, or CADMONÆI (anc. geog.), a people of Palestine, said to dwell at the foot of mount Hermon; which lies east, and is the reason of the appellation, with respect to Libanus, Phoenicia, and the north parts of Palestine. Called also Hevæi (Moses).
