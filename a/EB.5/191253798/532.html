OECONOMY, denotes the prudent conduct, or discreet and frugal management, whether of a man's own estate or that of another.

Animal Oeconom, comprehends the various operations of nature in the generation, nutrition, and preservation of animals†. The doctrine of the animal economy is nearly connected with physiology, which explains the several parts of the human body, their structure, use, &c. See ANATOMY and MEDICINE.
