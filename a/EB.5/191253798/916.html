OVIDEIO, a town of Spain, and capital of Asturias d'Oviedo, with a bishop's see, and an university; seated at the confluence of the rivers Ove and Deva, which form the Asta, 50 miles north-west of Leon, and 208 north-west of Madrid. W. Long. 5. 47. N. Lat. 43. 23.
