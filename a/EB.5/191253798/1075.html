PALLIATION

article · 282 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALLIATION, or a PALLIATIVE Cure, in medicine, is when, in desperate and incurable diseases, after predicting the fatal event, the physician prescribes some remedies for mitigating the pain or some other urgent symptoms, as in ulcerated cancers, or cancerous fistulas, and the like.
