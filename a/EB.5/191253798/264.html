NONNUS

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NONNUS, a Greek poet of the 5th century, and native of Panopolis in Egypt, was the author of an heroic poem in 48 books, intitled Dionysiacorum, and a paraphrase in verse of St John's Gospel, which may serve as a commentary upon it.
