OTHONNA

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OTHONNA, in botany: A genus of the polygamia necessaria order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Compositae. The receptacle is naked; there is almost no pappus; the calyx is monophyllous, multifid, and nearly cylindrical.
