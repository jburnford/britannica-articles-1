OFFA'S DYKE

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OFFA'S DYKE, an entrenchment cast up by Offa, a Saxon king, to defend England against the incursions of the Welch. It runs through Hertfordshire, Shropshire, Montgomeryshire, Denbighshire, and Flintshire.
