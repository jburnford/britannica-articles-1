OSWEIZEN

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OSWEIZEN, a town of Poland, in the palatinate of Cracovia, with the title of a duchy. It carries on a great trade in salt, and is seated on the river Vistula. E. Long. 19. 47. N. Lat. 50. 1.
