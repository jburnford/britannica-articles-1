NEREUS

article · 343 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEREUS, (fab. hist.), a marine deity, was the son of Oceanus and Thetis. He settled in the Ægean Sea, was considered as a prophet, and had the power of assuming what form he pleased. He married his sister Doris, by whom he had 50 daughters called the Nereids, who constantly attended on Neptune, and when he went abroad surrounded his chariot.
