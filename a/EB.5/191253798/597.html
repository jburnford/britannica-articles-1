OLEARIUS

OLEARIUS (Adam), minister to the duke of Holstein, and secretary to the embassy sent in 1633 to the great duke of Muscovy and to the king of Persia. He spent six years in this employment; and, on his return, published a relation of his journeys, with maps and figures, at Sleswic, 1636, in folio. He wrote an Abridgement of the Chronicles of Holstein from 1448 to 1663; and was appointed librarian to the duke of Holstein, in which capacity he probably died. He has the character of an able mathematician, an adept

Olearius of music, and a good orientalist, especially in the Persian language.
