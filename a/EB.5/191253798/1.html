NEHOW

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEHOW, one of the Sandwich Islands, discovered by Captain Cook in his last voyage to the Pacific Ocean: they are eleven in number, and are situated from 18^{\circ} 45' to 22^{\circ} 15' N. Lat. and from 154^{\circ} 56' to 160^{\circ} 24' W. Long. They are not very particularly described in any account that has hitherto appeared.
