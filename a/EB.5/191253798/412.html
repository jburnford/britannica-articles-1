NYMPHÆA

article · 328 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NYMPHÆA, in anatomy, two membranaceous parts, situated on each side the rima. They are of a red colour, and cavernous structure, somewhat resembling the wattles under a cock's throat. They are sometimes smaller, sometimes larger; and are contiguous to the præputium of the clitoris, and joined to the interior side of the labia.
