NOEOMAGUS LEXOVIORUM

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOEOMAGUS LEXOVIORUM, (Ptol.); thought to be the Civitas Lexoviorum of the lower age. Now Lisieux, a city in Normandy.—Another of the Tricastini; a town of Gallia Narbonensis; thought to be S. Pol de Trois Châteaux, six miles to the west of Nyons in Dauphiné.
