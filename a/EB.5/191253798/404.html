NYCTASTRATEGI

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NYCTASTRATEGI, among the ancients, were officers appointed to prevent fires in the night, or to give alarm and call assistance when a fire broke out. At Rome they had the command of the watch, and were called nocturni triumviri, from their office and number.
