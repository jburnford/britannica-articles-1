NIESTER

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIESTER, a large river of Poland, which has its source in the Lake Niester, in the palatinate of Lemberg, where it passes by Halicz. Then it separates Podolia and Oczakow Tartary from Moldavia and Budziac Tartary; and falls into the Black Sea at

Belgorod, between the mouths of the Nieper and the Danube.
