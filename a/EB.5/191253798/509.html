OCTOVAR Equus

sub_entry · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OCTOVAR Equus, a horse annually sacrificed to Mars in the month of October, either because the horse is a warlike animal, or to punish him for the taking of Troy. A race was run with chariots, drawn by two horses, previous to the sacrifices, and he that ran quickest was adjudged to be the victim.
