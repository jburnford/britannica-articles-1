NIWEGAL

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIWEGAL, in Pembrokeshire, South Wales, a small village and beach on the coast, remarkable only for the discovery of an immense quantity of the stumps of trees appearing below low-water-mark, after and during a storm in the year 1590, notwithstanding the country all round it is entirely barren of wood.
