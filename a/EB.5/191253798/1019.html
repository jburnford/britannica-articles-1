PALÆOLOGUS

Cross-references: CONSTANTINOPLE

PALÆOLOGUS (Michael), a very able man who was governor of Asia under the emperor Theodorus Lasarus; and who, by various stratagems and cruelties, procured the empire for himself and his posterity. See CONSTANTINOPLE, from no 145 to the end of that article.
