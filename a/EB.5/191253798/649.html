ONEGA

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ONEGA, a river and lake of the Russian empire, between Muscovite Carelia, the territory of Cargapol, and Swedish Carelia. It is 100 miles in length and 40 in breadth, having a communication with the lake Ladoga, and consequently with Peterburgh. The river has its source in Cargapol, and gives its name to a country full of woods.
