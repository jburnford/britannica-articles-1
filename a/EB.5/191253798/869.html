OSTRACION

OSTRACION, in zoology, a genus of the amphibians nantes class. It has ten long cylindrical obtuse teeth in each jaw; the aperture is linear; the body is covered with a bony substance, and it has no belly-fins. There are nine species; principally distinguished by the angles of their bodies, and number of fins near their tail.
