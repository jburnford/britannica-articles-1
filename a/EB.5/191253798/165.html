NIGHT

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIGHT, in scripture-language, is used for the times of heathenish ignorance and profaneness (Rom. xiii. 12.); for adversity and affliction (Is. xxi. 12.); and, lastly, for death (John ix. 4.)
