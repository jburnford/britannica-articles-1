PAMPELUNA

PAMPELUNA, the capital of the kingdom of Navarre in Spain, with a very strong citadel and rich bishopric. It is handsome and populous, and carries on a great trade, seated in a very fertile plain, in E. Long. 1. 25 N. Lat. 42. 42.
