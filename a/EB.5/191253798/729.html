ORCHIA LEX

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORCHIA LEX, instituted by Orchis the tribune in the year of Rome 566. Its intention was to limit the number of guests that were to be admitted at an entertainment; and it also enforced, that during supper, which was the chief meal among the Romans, the doors of every house should be left open.
