OPIATES

article · 411 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OPIATES, medicines of a thicker consistence than a syrup, prepared with opium scarcely fluid. They consist of various ingredients, made up with honey or syrup; and are to be used for a long time either for purgative, alterative, or corroborative intentions.

The word opiate is also used, in general, for any medicine given with an intention to procure sleep, whether in the form of electuaries, drops, or pills.
