NEUTRAL-Salts

sub_entry · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEUTRAL-Salts, among chemists, those compounded of an acid with any other substance capable of uniting with it and destroying its acidity. Those in which the acid is saturated with an earth or a metal are called imperfect, but those in which a pure alkali is employed are called perfect, neutrals.
