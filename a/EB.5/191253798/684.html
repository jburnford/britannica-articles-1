OPHIOMANCY

article · 364 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OPHIOMANCY, in antiquity, the art of making predictions from serpents. Thus Calchas, on seeing a serpent devour eight sparrows with their dam, foretold the duration of the siege of Troy: and the seven coils of a serpent that was seen on Anchises's tomb, were interpreted to mean the seven years that Aeneas wandered from place to place before he arrived at Latium.
