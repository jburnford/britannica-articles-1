PALAMBOANG, or PALAMBANG, a town of Asia, in the East Indies, and in the island of Java, capital of a kingdom; seated at the east end of the island, on the straits of Bally, and separated from the island of Bally by a narrow channel. E. Long. 115. 10. S. Lat. 7. 10.
