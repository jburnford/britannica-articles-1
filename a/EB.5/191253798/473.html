OCCULT, something hidden, secret, or invisible. The occult sciences are magic, necromancy, caballa, &c. Occult qualities, in philosophy, were those qualities of body or spirit which baffled the investigation of philosophers, and for which they were unable to give any reason: unwilling however to acknowledge their ignorance, they deceived themselves and the vulgar by an empty title, calling what they did not know occult.
