PARASEMON

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARASEMON, among the Greeks, was the figure carved on the prow of the ships to distinguish them from each other. This figure was generally that of a bull, lion, or other animal; sometimes the representation of a mountain, tree, flower, &c.
