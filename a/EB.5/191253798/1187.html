PAPISTS

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: POPE

PAPISTS, are those who believe the pope or bishop of Rome to be the supreme pastor of the universal church, who profess to believe all the articles of pope Pius's creed, and who promise implicit obedience to the edicts of the church, especially the decrees of the council of Trent. See POPE and TRENT.
