NETTINGS

article · 346 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NETTINGS, in a ship, a sort of grates made of small ropes seized together with rope-yarn or twine, and fixed on the quarters and in the tops; they are sometimes stretched upon the ledges from the waste-trees to the roof-trees, from the top of the forecastle to the poop, and sometimes are laid in the waste of a ship to serve instead of gratings.
