PANTALARIA

article · 343 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANTALARIA, an island in the Mediterranean sea, between Sicily and the main land of Africa, about 27 miles in circumference. It is near the coast of Tunis, and abounds in cotton, fruits, and wine; but the inhabitants are obliged to bring all their corn to Sicily, as it belongs to the king of the two Sicilies. E. Long. 12. 25. N. Lat. 36. 55.
