PANTALOON

article · 448 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANTALOON, on the theatre, is a buffoon or masked person, who performs high and grotesque dances, and shows violent and extravagant postures and airs. The word is likewise used for the habit or dress these
N° 258.

buffoons usually wear; which is made precisely to the form of their body, and all of a piece from head to foot.

And hence those who wear a habit of this kind, for convenience, under their other clothes, are called pantaloons of Venice.
