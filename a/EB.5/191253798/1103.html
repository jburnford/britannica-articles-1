PAMPHYLIA

article · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAMPHYLIA, the ancient name of a country of Natolia, in Asia, now called Carmania and Goy-bay, between Lycia and Cilicia, on the south coast, to the north of the Mediterranean sea.
