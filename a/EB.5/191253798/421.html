NYU-CHE, or KIN

Cross-references: CHINA

NYU-CHE, or KIN, an empire which arose in eastern Tartary in the beginning of the 13th century. From the founder of this empire the late Chinese emperor Kang-hi said that his family was descended. See CHINA and TARTARY.
