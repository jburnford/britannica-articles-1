ORISTAGNI

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORISTAGNI, an ancient town of the island of Sardinia, with an archbishop's see. It is pretty large and well fortified; but thinly inhabited, on account of the unhealthy air: it is seated on the western coast, in a bay of the same name, in E. Long. 8. 58. N. Lat. 39. 55.
