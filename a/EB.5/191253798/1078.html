PALLIUM

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALLIUM, in antiquity, an upper garment or mantle worn by the Greeks, as the toga was by the Romans. Each of these were so peculiar to the respective nations, that Pallius is used to signify a Greek, and Togas a Roman.
