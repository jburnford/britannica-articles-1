OSTALRIC

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OSTALRIC, a town of Spain, in Catalonia. It had a strong castle, but was taken by the French and demolished in 1695. It is seated on the river Lorders, in E. Long. 2. 45. N. Lat. 24. 44.
