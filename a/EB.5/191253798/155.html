NIENBURG

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIENBURG, a rich and strong town of Germany, in the duchy of Brunswick-Lunenburg, with a strong castle. It carries on a considerable trade in corn and wood, and is seated in a fertile soil on the river Weser. E. Long. 9. 26. N. Lat. 52. 44.
