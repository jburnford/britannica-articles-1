OTHRYADES

article · 653 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OTHRYADES, one of the 300 Spartans who fought against 300 Argives, when those two nations disputed their respective right to Thyrreus. Two Argives, Alcinoor and Cronius, and Othryades, survived

the battle. The Argives went home to carry the news of their victory; but Othryades, who had been reckoned among the number of the slain on account of his wounds, recovered himself, and carried some of the spoils of which he had stripped the Argives into the camp of his countrymen; and after he had raised a trophy, and had written with his own blood the word vict on his shield, he killed himself, unable or unwilling to survive the death of his countrymen.
