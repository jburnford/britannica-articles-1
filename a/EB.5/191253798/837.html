ORTNAU

article · 419 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORTNAU, a county of Germany, in the circle of Suabia, lying along the Rhine, and separating it from Alface. It is bounded on the south by Breslau, on the north by the margravate of Baden, and on the east by the duchy of Wirttemberg. It contains three imperial towns; namely, Offenburg, Gegenbach, and Zell. It belongs partly to the house of Austria, partly to the bishopric of Spire, and partly to the county of Hannau.
