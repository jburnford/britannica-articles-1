NOMBRIL POINT

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOMBRIL POINT, in heraldry, is the next below the fess-point, or the very centre of the escutcheon.

Supposing the escutcheon divided into two equal parts below the fess, the first of these divisions is the nombril, and the lower the base.
