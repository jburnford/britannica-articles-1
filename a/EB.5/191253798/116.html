NICE, an ancient town of Asia, in Natolia, now called Isnik, with a Greek archbishop's see. It is famous for the general council assembled here in 325, which endeavoured to suppress the doctrines of Arius. It was formerly a large, populous, and well-built place, and even now is not inconsiderable. See ISNIK.
