NEMAUSUS

NEMAUSUS, or NEMASUS, (anc. geog.) the capital of the Arcemici in Gallia Narbonensis; a colony, (Coin), with the surname Augusta, (Inscription). In it stands a Roman amphitheatre, which is still almost entire. Now Nimes in Languedoc.
