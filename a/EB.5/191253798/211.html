NIVernois

sub_entry · 525 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIVernois, an inland province of France, with the title of a duchy, lying on the west side of Burgundy, and between it Bourbonnois and Barri. It is pretty fertile in wine, fruit, and corn; except the part called Morvant, which is a mountainous country, and barren. There is a great deal of wood, and several iron-mines; as also mines of pit-coal, which serves to work their forges. This province is watered by a great number of rivers; of which the Allier, the Loire, and the Yonne, are navigable. Nevers is the capital city.
