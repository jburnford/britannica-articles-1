NOMBRE-DE-DIOS

NOMBRE-DE-DIOS, a town of Mexico, in the province of Darien, a little to the eastward of Porto-Bello. It was formerly a famous place; but it is now

abandoned, on account of its unhealthy situation. W. Nombril Long. 78. 35. N. Lat. 9. 43.
