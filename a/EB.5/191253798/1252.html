PARENCHYMA

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARENCHYMA, in anatomy, a term introduced by Erasistratus, signifying all that substance which is contained in the interstices betwixt the blood-vessels of the viscera, which he imagined to be extravasated and concreted blood.
