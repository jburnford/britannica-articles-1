ELATERIUM, in botany: A genus of the monandria order, belonging to the monoclea class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 34th order, Cucurbitaceae. There is no male calyx; the corolla is salver-shaped; there is no female calyx; the corolla salver-shaped; the capsule inferior, unilocular, and bivalved.
