EPIDaurus, Epidaurus, or Epitaurus, (anc. geog.), a town of Dalmatia, on the Adriatic, built the same year, as is said, with Dyrrachium, 430 years after the destruction of Troy: A considerable town formerly, but now reduced to a small village, called Ragusi Vecchio; distant six miles from the modern Ragusi. E. Long. 19°. Lat. 42°, 20'.
