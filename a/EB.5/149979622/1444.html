ESQUIMAUX

ESQUIMAUX, a people of North America inhabiting all that vast tract of land known by the name of Labrador, or New Britain.—They differ very considerably, both in aspect and behaviour, from the other American nations; agreeing in most respects with the inhabitants of West Greenland. See New Britain, and GREENLAND.
