ELEPHANTOPUS

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELEPHANTOPUS, in botany: A genus of the polygamia segregata order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Composite. The calyculus is quadriflorus, with hermaphrodite florets ligulated or plane; the receptacle is naked; the pappus bristly.
