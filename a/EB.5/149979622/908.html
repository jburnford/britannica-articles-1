ELAIS

ELAIS, in botany; a genus belonging to the natural order of Palme. The male calyx is hexaphyllous; the corolla sexfid; the stamens six: The female calyx is hexaphyllous; the corolla hexapetalous; the fig-mata three; the fruit a fibrous plum, with a three-valved nut or kernel.
