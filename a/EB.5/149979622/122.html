DIKE

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIKE, a ditch or drain, made for the passage of waters.—The word seems formed from the verb to dig; tho' others choose to derive it from the Dutch, dijk, a dam, sea-bank, or wall.
