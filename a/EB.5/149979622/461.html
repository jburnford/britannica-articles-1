DOUBLING

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOUBLING, in the manège, a term used of a horse, who is said to double his reins, when he leaps several times together, to throw his rider: thus we say, the ramming doubles his reins, and makes pontlevis.
