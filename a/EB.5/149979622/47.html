DICHOTOMY

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DICHOTOMY, a term used by astronomers for that phasis or appearance of the moon, wherein she is bisected, or shows just half her disk. In this situation the moon is said to be in a quadrature aspect, or to be in her quadrature.
