ENCLITICA

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENCLITICA, in grammar, particles which are so closely united with other words as to seem part of them, as in virumque, &c.—There are three enclitic particles in Latin. viz. que, ne, ve.
