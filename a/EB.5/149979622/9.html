DIANIUM

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIANIUM (anc. geog.), a town of the Conteñani, in the Hither Spain; famous for a temple of Diana, whence the name: Now Denia, a small town of Valencia, on the Mediterranean. Also a promontory near Dianium: Now El Cabo Martin, four leagues from Denia, running out into the Mediterranean.
