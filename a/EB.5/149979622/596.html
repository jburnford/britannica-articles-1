DUCAS

DUCAS, a learned Greek, who wrote an history of
what passed under the last emperors of Constantinople,
till the ruin of that city. This work, which is esteem-
ed, was printed at the Louvre in 1649, with the Latin
translation and notes of Bouillaud.
