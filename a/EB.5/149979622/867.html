EFFENDI

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EFFENDI, in the Turkish language, signifies master; and accordingly it is a title very extensively applied; as, to the musti and emirs, to the priests of mosques, to men of learning, and of the law. The grand chancellor of the empire is called reïs-effendi.
