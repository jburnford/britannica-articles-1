DICTYS

article · 336 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DICTYS (Cretenfis), a very ancient historian, who serving under Idomeneus king of Crete in the Trojan war, wrote the history of that expedition in nine books; and Tzetzes tells us, that Homer formed his Iliad upon the plan of that history. It is however maintained, that the Latin history of Dictys which we have at present is spurious.
