EQUIPOLLENCE

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EQUIPOLLENCE, in logic, is when there is an equivalence between any two or more terms or propositions; i. e. when they signify one and the same thing, though they express it differently. Such propositions, &c. are said to be equipollent.
