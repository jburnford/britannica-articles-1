DIONYSIACA

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIONYSIACA, in antiquity, was a designation given to plays and all manner of sports acted on the stage; because play-houses were dedicated to Dionysus, i. e. Bacchus and Venus, as being the deities of sports and pleasure.
