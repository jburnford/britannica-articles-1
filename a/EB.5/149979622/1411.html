ERYTHROXYLON

article · 353 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERYTHROXYLON, in botany: A genus of the trigynia order, belonging to the decandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking with those of the order of which is doubtful. The calyx is turbinated; the petals of the corolla have each a nectariferous emarginated scale at the base; the stamens are connexed at the base; the fruit a bilocular plum.
