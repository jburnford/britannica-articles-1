DIOSPOLITES NOMOS

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIOSPOLITES NOMOS (Ptolemy), a division of Thebæis or the Higher Egypt, to distinguish it from another of the Lower Egypt or the Delta; to the south of the Nomos Thebæis, on the west side of the Nile.
