ENOCH

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENOCH, the son of Cain (Gen. iv. 17), in honour of whom the first city taken notice of in scripture was called Enoch by his father Cain, who built it. It was situated to the east of the province of Eden.
