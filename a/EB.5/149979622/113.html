DIGNE

article · 163 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIGNE, an episcopal town of Provence in France, famous for the baths that are near it. It is seated on a river called Marderice, in E. Long. 5. 27. N. Lat. 44. 45.
