ESSENTIAL

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESSENTIAL, something necessarily belonging to a thing, from which it cannot be conceived distinct: thus the primary qualities of bodies, as extension, figure, number, &c. are essential or inseparable from them in all their changes and alterations.
