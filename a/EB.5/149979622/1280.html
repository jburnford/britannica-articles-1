EPITHEM

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPITHEM, in pharmacy, a kind of fomentation, or remedy of a spirituous or aromatic kind, applied externally to the regions of the heart, liver, &c. to strengthen and comfort the same, or to correct some intemperance thereof.
