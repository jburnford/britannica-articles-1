DODARTIA

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DODARIA, in botany: A genus of the angiospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 40th order, Perfonate. The calyx is quinquedentated; the under lip of the corolla twice as long as the upper; the capsule bilocular and globose.
