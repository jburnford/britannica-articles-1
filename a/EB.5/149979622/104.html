DIGESTIVE

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIGESTIVE, in medicine, such remedies as strengthen and increase the tone of the stomach, and assist in the digestion of foods. To this class belong all stomachics and strengtheners or corroborants.
