DIASTOLE

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIASTOLE, in grammar, a figure in prosody whereby a syllable naturally short is made long. Such is the first syllable of Priamides in the following verse of Virgil:

Atque hic Priamides! nihil o tibi, amice, reliquum.
