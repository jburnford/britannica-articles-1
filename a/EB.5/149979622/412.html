DONAX

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DONAX, a genus of insects belonging to the order of vermes tectaces. It is an animal of the oyster kind; and the shell has two valves, with a very obtuse margin in the fore-part. There are 10 species, principally distinguished by the figure of their shells.
