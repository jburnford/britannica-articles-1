EFFIGY

article · 401 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EFFIGY, to execute or degrade in, denotes the execution or degradation of a condemned contumacious criminal, who cannot be apprehended or seized. In France, they hang a picture on a gallows or gibbet, wherein is represented the criminal, with the quality or manner of the punishment: at the bottom is written the sentence or condemnation. Such persons as are sentenced to death are executed in effigy.
