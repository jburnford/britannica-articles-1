ERATOSTRATUS

article · 391 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERATOSTRATUS, an Ephesian who burnt the famous temple of Diana the same night that Alexander the Great was born. This burning, as some writers have observed, was not prevented or seen by the goddess of the place, who was then present at the labours of Olympias, and at the birth of the conqueror of Persia. Eratosthratus did this villany merely to eternize his name by so uncommon an action.
