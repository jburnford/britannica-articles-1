ECCOPROTICS

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ECCOPROTICS, in medicine, laxative or loosening remedies, which purge gently, by softening the humours and excrements, and fitting them for expulsion. The word is composed of the Greek particle ex, and excrement.
