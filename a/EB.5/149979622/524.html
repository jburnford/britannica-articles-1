DRAKENBORCH

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRAKENBORCH (Arnold), professor of eloquence and history at Utrecht, made himself known by several works, and particularly by his Notes on Titus Livius and Silius Italicus; his fine editions of which are highly esteemed.
