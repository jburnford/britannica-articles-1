EPIDaurus

EPIDaurus, with the surname Limera, to distinguish it from the Epidaurus of Argolis; called so, either from its meadows or its commodious harbours (Stephanus, Apollodorus): a town of Laconica, on the Ionian sea, to the south of the Sinus Argolicus, situated where now Molvasia stands, in the Morea. E. Long. 23. 30. Lat. 35. 40.
