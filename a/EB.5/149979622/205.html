DISANDRA

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISANDRA, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the heptandria class of plants. The calyx has seven leaves; the corolla parted into seven, and flat; the capsule two-celled.
