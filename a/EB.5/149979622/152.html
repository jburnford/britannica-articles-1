DIODIA

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIODIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 47th order, Stellata. The corolla is monopetalous and funnel-shaped; the capsule bilocular and disperous.
