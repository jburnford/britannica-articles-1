EPIDOTÆ

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPIDOTÆ, certain deities who presided over the growth of children. They were worshipped by the Lacedæmonians, and chiefly invoked by those who were persecuted by the ghosts of the dead, &c.
