ELOGY

article · 407 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELOGY, a praise or panegyric bestowed on any person or thing, in consideration of its merit. The beauty of elogy consists in an expressive brevity. Eulogiums should not have so much as one epithet, properly so called, nor two words synonymous: they

VOL. VI. Part II.

should strictly adhere to truth; for extravagant and improbable elogies rather lessen the character of the person or thing they would extol.
