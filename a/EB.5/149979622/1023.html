EMANATION, the act of flowing or proceeding from some source or origin. Such is the emanation of

Emanation light from the sun; or that of effluvia from odorous, &c. bodies; of wisdom from God, &c.—The word is formed of the Latin e "out of," and manere "to flow or stream."
