DIFF

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIFF, is the name of an instrument of music among the Arabs, serving chiefly to beat time to the voice: it is a hoop, sometimes with pieces of brass fixed to it to make a jingling, over which a piece of parchment is stretched. It is beat with the fingers, and is the true tympanum of the ancients.
