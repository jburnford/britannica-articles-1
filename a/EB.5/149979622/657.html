DUO

article · 329 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUO, in music, a song or composition, to be performed on two parts only, one sung, the other played on an instrument, or by two voices.

Duo is also when two voices sing different parts, as accompanied with a third, which is a thorough bass. It is seldom that unisons and octaves are used in duos, except at the beginning and end.
