DIAPASON

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIAPASON, among the musical instrument-makers, a kind of rule or scale whereby they adjust the pipes of their organs, and cut the holes of their hautboys, flutes, &c. in due proportion for performing the tones, semitones, and concords, just.
