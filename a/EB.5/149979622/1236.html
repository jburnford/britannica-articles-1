EPIDICASIA, among the Athenians. Daughters inheriting their parents estate, were obliged to marry their nearest relation; which gave occasion to persons of the same family to go to law with one another, each pretending to be more nearly allied to the heirs than the rest. The suit was called epidicasia dis; and the virgin, about whom the relations contended, epidice.
