EIA, or Ey

sub_entry · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EIA, or Ey, in our old writers, are used for an island. Hence the names of places ending in ey, denotes them to be islands. Thus, Ramsey, the isle of rams; Shepey, the isle of sheep, &c.
