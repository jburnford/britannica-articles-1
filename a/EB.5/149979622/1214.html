EPIBATAI

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPIBATAI, Επιβαται, among the Greeks, marines or soldiers who served on board the ships of war. They were armed in the same manner as the land-forces, only that more of them wore full or heavy armour.
