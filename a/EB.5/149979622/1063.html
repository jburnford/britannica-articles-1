EMMENAGOGUES

EMMENAGOGUES, ΕΜΜΕΝΑΓΟΓΟΥΣ, in medicine, such remedies as promote the menstrual discharge. They are thus called from "in," in, "month," μήνας duco, "I lead," because their natural periods of flowing are once a month.
