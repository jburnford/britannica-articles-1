ERGASTULUM

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERGASTULUM, among the Romans, was a prison, work-house, or house of correction, where slaves by the private authority of their masters were confined and kept for their offences to hard labour. The Greeks had a place of confinement of this sort called Zoegonapôn.
