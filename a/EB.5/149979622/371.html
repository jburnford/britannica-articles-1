DOMAT

article · 376 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOMAT (John), a celebrated French lawyer born in 1625, who observing the confused state of the laws, digested them in 4 vols 4to, under the title of The Civil Laws in their natural order: for which undertaking, Louis XIV. settled on him a pension of 2000 livres. Domat was intimate with the famous Pascal, who left him his private papers at his death: he himself died in 1696.
