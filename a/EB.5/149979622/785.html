ECHINOPHORA

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ECHINOPHORA, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 45th order, Umbellatæ. The male florets are lateral, with the central one hermaphrodite; there is one seed, sunk into an indurated involucre.
