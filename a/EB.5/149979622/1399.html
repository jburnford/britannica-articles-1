ERYMANTHUS

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERYMANTHUS, a mountain, river, and town of Arcadia, where Hercules killed a prodigious boar, which he carried on his shoulders to Eurytheus; who was so terrified at the sight, that he hid himself in a brazen vessel.
