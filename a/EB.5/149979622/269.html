DISTANCE

Cross-references: METAPHYSICS · GEOMETRY

DISTANCE, in general, an interval between two things, either with regard to time or place. See METAPHYSICS.

Accessible Distances, in geometry, are such as may be measured by the chain, &c. See GEOMETRY.

Inaccessible Distances, are such as cannot be measured by the chain, &c. by reason of some river, or the like, &c. which obstructs our passing from one object to another. See GEOMETRY.
