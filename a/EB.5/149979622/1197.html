EPENTHESIS

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPENTHESIS, in grammar, the interposition or insertion of a letter or syllable in the middle of a word; as alium, for alium; velligio, for religio; induperator, for imperator, &c.
