DIANÆ ARBOR

DIANÆ ARBOR, or ARBOR LUNÆ, in chemistry, the beautiful crystallizations of silver, dissolved in aqua-fortis, to which some quicksilver is added: and so called from their resembling the trunk, branches, leaves, &c. of a tree. See CHEMISTRY, no 754.
