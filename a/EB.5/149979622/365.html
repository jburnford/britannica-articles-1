DOLE

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOLE, in Scots law, signifies a malevolent intention. It is essential in every crime, that it be committed intentionally, or by an act of the will: hence the rule, Crimen dolo contrahitur.
