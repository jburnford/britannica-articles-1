EASEL PIECES

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EASEL PIECES, among painters, such smaller pieces, either portraits or landscapes, as are painted on the easel, i. e. the frame wherein the canvas is laid.—They are thus called, to distinguish them from larger pictures drawn on walls, ceilings, &c.
