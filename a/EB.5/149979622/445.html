DOSE

article · 176 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOSE, in pharmacy, &c. the quantity of a medicine to be taken at one time. The word is formed from the Greek dos, which signifies gift, or a thing given; from didōmi, "I give."
