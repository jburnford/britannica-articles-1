DIBIO, or DIVIO (anc. geog.), the Divionense Castrum, and the Divionum of the lower age; a town of the Lingones, in Gallia Belgica: Dibionensis, the people. Now Dijon, the capital of Burgundy. E. Long. 5. 5. N. Lat. 47. 15.
