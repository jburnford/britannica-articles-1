EHRETIA

EHRETIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 41st order, Asperifolia. The fruit is a bilocular berry; the seeds solitary and bilocular; the stigma emarginated.
