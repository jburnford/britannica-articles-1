DIAPASON-Diapente

sub_entry · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIAPASON-Diapente, in music, a compound consonance in a triple ratio, as 3:9. This interval, says Martianus Capella, consists of 9 tones and a semitone; 19 semitones, and 38 dieses. It is a symphony made
No 101.

when the voice proceeds from the first to the twelfth found. Diapason
