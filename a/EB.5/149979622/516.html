DRAGONNE

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRAGONNE, in heraldry. A lion dragonnée is where the upper half resembles a lion, the other half going off like the hinder part of a dragon. The same may be said of any other beast as well as a lion.
