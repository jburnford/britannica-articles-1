DIPTERA

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIPTERA (from dis, and pteron wing), in zoology, an order of insects, which have only two wings, and under each wing a style or oblong body, terminated by a protuberance or head, and called a halter.
