ESK

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESK, the name of several rivers both in England and Scotland, particularly of one which forms part of the boundary between the two kingdoms. It runs from north-east to south-west, and gives name to the county of Eskdale.
