DOUBLE Fitchy

sub_entry · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOUBLE Fitchy, or Fiché, in heraldry, the denomination of a cross, when the extremity has two points; in contradistinction to fiché, where the extremity is sharpened away to one point.
