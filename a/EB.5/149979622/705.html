DWINA, the name of two large rivers; one of which rises in Lithuania, and, dividing Livonia from Courland, falls into the Baltic Sea a little below Riga: the
No 105.

other gives name to the province of Dwina in Russia, discharging itself into the White Sea a little below Archangel.
