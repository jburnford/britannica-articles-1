DIDYNAMIA

Cross-references: BOTANY

DIDYNAMIA (from dis twice, and dynamic power), the name of the 14th class in Linnæus's sexual method; consisting of plants with hermaphrodite flowers, which have four stamens or male organs, two of which are long and two short. See BOTANY, the Scheme, and Plate CII. fig. 14.
