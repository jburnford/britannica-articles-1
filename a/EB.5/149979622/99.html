DIFFUSION, the dispersion of the subtle effluvia of bodies into a kind of atmosphere all round them.

Digastricus Thus the light diffused by the rays of the sun, issues all round from that amazing body of fire.

Digest. DIGASTRICUS, in anatomy, a muscle of the lower jaw, called also Biventer. See ANATOMY, Table of the Muscles.
