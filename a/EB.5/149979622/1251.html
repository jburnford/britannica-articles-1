EPIMETHEUS

article · 574 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPIMETHEUS, a son of Japetus and Clymene, one of the Oceanides, who inconsiderately married Pandora, by whom he had Pyrrha, the wife of Deucalion. He had the curiosity to open the box which Pandora had brought with her, and from thence issued a train of evils, which from that moment have never ceased to afflict the human race. Hope was the only one which remained at the bottom of the box, not having a sufficient time to escape, and it is the alone which comforts men under misfortunes. Epimetheus was changed into a monkey by the gods, and sent into the island Pitheusa.
