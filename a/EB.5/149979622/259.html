DISSEPIMENTUM

article · 178 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISSEPIMENTUM, in botany, the name by which Linnaeus denominates the partitions which in dry seed-vessels, as capsules and pods (filiqua), divide the fruit internally into cells.
