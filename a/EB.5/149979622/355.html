DOESBURG, a town of the united provinces, in the county of Zutphen and province of Guelderland. It is small, but well peopled, and very strong both by art and nature, having the river Yssel on one side, and a morass on the other, and is only to be approached by a narrow neck of land. E. Long. 5. 55. N. Lat. 52. 3.
