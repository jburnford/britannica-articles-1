EPAULEMENT

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPAULEMENT, in fortification, a work raised to cover sidewife, is either of earth, gabions, or fascines loaded with earth. The epaulements of the places of arms for the cavalry, at the entrance of the trenches, are generally of fascines mixed with earth.
