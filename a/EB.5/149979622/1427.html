ESCOUADE

Cross-references: BRIGADE

ESCOUADE, or SQUAD, is usually the third or fourth part of a company of foot; so divided for mounting guards, and for the more convenient re-

lieving of one another. It is equivalent to a brigade of a troop of horse. See BRIGADE.
