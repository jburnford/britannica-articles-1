ERIOCAULON

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERIOCAULON, in botany: A genus of the trigynia order, belonging to the triandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking with the sixth order, Enfata. The common calyx is an imbricated capitulum or knob; there are three equal petals; and the stamens are on the germens.
