DONATORY 2. cap. 10. The ordinary cannot visit a donative, and therefore it is free from procurator, and the incumbent is exempted from attendance at visitations.

All bishoprics in ancient time were donative by the king. Again, where a bishop has the gift of a benefice, it is properly called a donative, because he cannot present to himself.
