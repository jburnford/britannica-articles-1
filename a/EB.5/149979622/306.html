DIVIDEND of Stocks

sub_entry · 172 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIVIDEND of Stocks, is a share or proportion of the interest of stocks erected on public funds, as the south-sea, &c. divided among and paid to the adventurers half-yearly.
