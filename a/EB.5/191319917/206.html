REGENERATION

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REGENERATION, in theology, the act of being born again by a spiritual birth, or the change of heart and life experienced by a person who forsakes a course of vice, and sincerely embraces a life of virtue and piety.
