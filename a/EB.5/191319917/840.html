SAGUNTUM

SAGUNTUM, an ancient town of Spain, now called Morvedro, where there are still the ruins of a Roman amphitheatre to be seen. The new town is seated on a river called Morvedro, 15 miles to the north of Valencia, in E. Long. 0. 10. N. Lat. 39. 38. It was taken by Lord Peterborough in 1756.
