SANTERRE

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SANTERRE, a small territory of France, in Picardy; bounded on the north by Cambreis, on the east by Vermandois, on the west by Amiens, and on the south by the river Somme. It is very fertile, and the capital town is Peronne.
