REPEALING, in law, the revoking or annulling of a statute or the like.

VOL. XVI. Part I.

No act of parliament shall be repealed the same session in which it was made. A deed or will may be repealed in part, and stand good for the rest. It is held that a pardon of felony may be repealed on disproving the sufficiency thereof.
