SARRACONIA

SARRACONIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the polyandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 54th order, Miscellanea. The corolla is pentapetalous; the calyx is double, and triphyllous below; pentaphyllous above; the capsule quinquilocular; the style has a stigma of the form of a shield.
