ROMNEY

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROMNEY, a town of Kent in England. It is one of the cinque-port towns, and is seated on a marsh of the same name, famous for feeding cattle; but the air is very unhealthy. It was once a large and populous place, but the retiring of the sea has reduced it very much; however, it sends two members to parliament.
