RUPPIA

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RUPPIA, in botany: A genus of the tetragynia order, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 15th order, Inundata. There is neither calyx nor corolla; but four pedicellated seeds.
