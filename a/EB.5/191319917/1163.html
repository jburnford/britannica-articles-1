SCHEMNITZ, a town of Upper Hungary, with three castles. It is famous for mines of silver and other

metals, as also for hot baths. Near it is a rock of Scherardia a shining blue colour mixed with green, and some spots of yellow. E. Long. 19. o. N. Lat. 48. 40.
