SAROTHRA

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAROTHRA, in botany: A genus of the trigynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 20th order, Rosaceæ. The corolla is pentapetalous; the capsule unilocular, trivalved, and coloured.
