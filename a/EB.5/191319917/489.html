RICHARDIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the hexandria class of plants; and

Richardson, and in the natural method ranking under the 47th order, Stellata. The calyx is sexpartite; the corolla monopetalous, and subcylindrical; and there are three seeds.
