RAVENGLAS

RAVENGLAS, a town of Cumberland in England, situated between the rivers Irn and Esk, which, with the sea, encompasses three parts of it. It is a well built place, and has a good road for shipping, which brings it some trade. E. Long. 0. 5. N. Lat. 51. 20.
