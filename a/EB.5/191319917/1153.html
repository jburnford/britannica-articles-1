SCENOGRAPHY, (from the Greek, σκηνὴ scene, and γραφὴ description), in perspective, a representation of a body on a perspective plane; or a description thereof

in all its dimensions, such as it appears to the eye. See SCEPTIC PERSPECTIVE.
