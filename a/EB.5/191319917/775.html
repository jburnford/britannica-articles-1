RYNCHOPS

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RYNCHOPS, in ornithology, a genus belonging to the order of anseres. The bill is straight; and the superior mandible much shorter than the inferior, which is truncated at the point. The species are two, viz. the nigra and fulva, both natives of America.
