REAUMURIA

REAUMURIA, in botany: A genus of the pentagynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 13th order, Succulentæ. The calyx is hexaphyllous, and there are five petals; the capsule is unilocular, quinquelvalved, and polyspermous.
