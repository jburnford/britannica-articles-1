RHINANTHUS

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHINANTHUS, in botany: A genus of the angiospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 40th order, Personatæ. The calyx is quadrifid, and veatricose; the capsule bilocular, obtuse, and compressed.
