SAMOLUS

SAMOLUS, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 21st order, Pricie. The corolla is falver-shaped, the flamina surrounded by small scales at its throat. The capsule is unilocular inferior.
