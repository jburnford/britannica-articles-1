SAY, or SAYE

sub_entry · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAY, or SAYE, in commerce, a kind of serge much used abroad for linings, and by the religious for shirts; with us it is used for aprons by several sorts of artificers, being usually dyed green.
