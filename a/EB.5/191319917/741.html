RUNG-HEADS

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RUNG-HEADS, in a ship, are made a little bending to direct the sweep or mold of the futtocks and navel-timbers; for here the lines begin which make the compass and bearing of the ship.
