SCOLYMUS

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCOLYMUS, in botany: A genus of the polygamia equalis order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Composita. The receptacle is palæaceous; the calyx imbricated and prickly, without any pappus.
