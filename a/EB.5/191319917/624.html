ROOT

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROOT, in algebra and arithmetic, denotes any number which, multiplied by itself once or oftener, produces any other number; and is called the square, cube, biquadrate, &c. root, according to number of multiplications. Thus, 2 is the square-root of 4; the cube-root of 8; the biquadrate-root of 16, &c.
