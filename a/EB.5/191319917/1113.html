SCAFFOLD

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCAFFOLD, also denotes a timber-work raised in the manner of an amphitheatre, for the more commodious viewing any show or ceremony: it is also used for a little stage raised in some public place, whereon to behold criminals.
