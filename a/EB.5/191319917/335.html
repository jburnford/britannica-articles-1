Body of RESERVE

sub_entry · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Body of RESERVE, or Corps de RESERVE, in military affairs, the third or last line of an army, drawn up for battle; so called because they are reserved to sustain the rest as occasion requires, and not to engage but in case of necessity.
