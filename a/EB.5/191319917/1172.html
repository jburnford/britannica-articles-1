SCHMIEDELIA

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCHMIEDELIA, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the octandria class of plants. The calyx is diphyllous; the corolla tetrapetalous; the germina pedicellated, and longer than the flower.
