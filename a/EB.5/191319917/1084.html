SAUL

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAUL, the son of Kish, of the tribe of Benjamin, was the first king of the Israelites. On account of his disobedient conduct, the kingdom was taken from his family, and given to David. See the First Book of Samuel.
