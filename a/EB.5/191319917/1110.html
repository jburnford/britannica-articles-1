SCÆVOLA

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCÆVOLA (C. Mucius), a young Roman of illustrious birth, is particularly celebrated in the Roman history for a brave but unsuccessful attempt upon the life of Porfena king of Hetruria, about the year before Christ 504. See the article ROME, no 71.
