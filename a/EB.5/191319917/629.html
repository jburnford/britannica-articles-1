RORIDULA

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RORIDULA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. The corolla is pentapetalous; the calyx pentaphyllous; the capsule trivalved; the anthers scrotopiform at the base.
