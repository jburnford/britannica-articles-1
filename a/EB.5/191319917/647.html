ROSIERS-AUX-SALINES

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROSIERS-AUX-SALINES, a town of France, in Lorraine, and in the bailiwick of Nancy, famous for its salt-works. The works that king Stanislaus made here are much admired. It is seated on the river Murt, in E. Long. 6. 27. N. Lat. 48. 32.
