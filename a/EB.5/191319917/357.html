REST, in poetry

sub_entry · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: POETRY

REST, in poetry, is a short pause of the voice in reading, being the same with the cæsuræ, which, in Alexandrine verses, falls on the sixth syllable; but in verses of 10 or 12 syllables, on the fourth. See POETRY, Part III.
