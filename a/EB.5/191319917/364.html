RESTORATION, the same with restaura-
tion. See RESTAURATION.

In England, the return of king Charles II. in 1660, is, by way of eminence, called the Restoration; and the 29th of May is kept as an anniversary festival, in commemoration of that event, by which the regal and episcopal government was restored.
