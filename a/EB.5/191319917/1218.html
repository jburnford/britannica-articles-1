SCLERIA, in botany: A genus of the tetrandria order, belonging to the monoclea class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 4th order, Gramina. The calyx has a gluma, with from two to six valves; the flowers numerous; the seed a sort of nut, small, oblong, and shining. There are six species, all of them natives of the West Indies.
