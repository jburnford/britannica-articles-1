ROSBACH

ROSBACH, a town of Germany, in Saxony, famous for a victory obtained here by the king of Prussia over the French, on November 5. 1757, in which 10,000 of the French were killed or taken prisoners, with the loss of no more than 500 Prussians. See PRUSSIA, no 30.
