SCHOTIA

SCHOTIA, in botany: A genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the decandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 33d order, Lomentaceæ. The calyx is semiquinquefid; the corolla has five petals, which are equal; the tube is turbinated, carinous, and persistent. The legumen pedicellated, and contains two seeds; there is only one species, viz. the speciosa, or African lignum vite.
