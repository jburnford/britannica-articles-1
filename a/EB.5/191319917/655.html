ROS-SOLIS

Cross-references: DROSERA

ROS-SOLIS, Sun-dew, an agreeable spirituous liquor, composed of burnt brandy, sugar, cinnamon, and milk-water; and sometimes perfumed with a little musk. It has its name from being at first prepared wholly of the juice of the plant ros folia, or drosera. See DROSERA.
