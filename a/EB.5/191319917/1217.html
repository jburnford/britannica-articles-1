SCLERANTHUS

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCLERANTHUS, in botany: A genus of the digynia order, belonging to the dodecandria class of plants, and in the natural method ranking under the 22d order, Caryophylli. The calyx is monophyllous; there is no corolla; there are two seeds contained in the calyx.
