SACRE, or SAKER, in ornithology, the name of a species of falcon, called by authors falco sacer, and differently

Sacred, Sacrifice. ently described by different authors, but by all agreed to be an extremely bold and active bird. It is a native of the northern regions of Europe; and a variety called by some writers the speckled partridge hawk is found at Hudson's bay, North America.
