REPERTORY

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REPERTORY, a place wherein things are orderly disposed, so as to be easily found when wanted. The indices of books are repertories, showing where the matters sought for are treated of. Common-place books are also kinds of repertories.
