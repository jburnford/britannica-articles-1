SARPLAR of WOOL

sub_entry · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SARPLAR of WOOL, a quantity of wool, otherwise called a pocket or half-sack; a sack containing 80 tod; a tod two stone; and a stone 14 pounds. In Scotland it is called sarpliath, and contains 80 stone.
