SANIDIUM

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SANIDIUM, in natural history, the name of a genus of fossils of the class of the selenite, but neither of the rhomboidal nor columnar kinds, nor any other way distinguishable by its external figure; being made up of several plain flat plates.
