SARCOCELE

SARCOCELE, in surgery, a spurious rupture or hernia, wherein the testicle is considerably tumefied or indurated, like a scurrius, or much enlarged by a fleshy excretion, which is frequently attended with acute pains, so as to degenerate at last into a cancerous disposition. See SURGERY.
