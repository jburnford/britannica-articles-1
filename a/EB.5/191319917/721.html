RUELLIA

RUELLIA, in botany: A genus of the angiospermia order, belonging to the didynamia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 40th order, Personata. The calyx is quinquepartite; the corolla sub-campanulated; the stamens approaching together in pairs; the capsule springing afunder by means of its clastic segments.
