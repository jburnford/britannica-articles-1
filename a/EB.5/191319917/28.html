RAPPOLSTEIN

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RAPPOLSTEIN, a town of France in Upper Alsace, which, before the Revolution, had the title of a barony. All the musicians of Alsace likewise depended upon this baron, and were obliged to pay him a certain tribute, without which they could not play upon their instruments. E. Long. 7. 28. N. Lat. 48. 15.
