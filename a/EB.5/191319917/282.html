RENEALMIA

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RENEALMIA, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the monandria class of plants. The corolla is trifid; the nectarium oblong; the calyx monophyllous; the anthera sessile, opposite to the nectarium; the berry is fleshy. There is only one species, which is a native of Surinam.
