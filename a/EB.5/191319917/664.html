ROTALA

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROTALA, in botany; a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the triandria class of plants. The calyx is tridentate; there is no corolla; the capsule is trilocular and polyspermous.
