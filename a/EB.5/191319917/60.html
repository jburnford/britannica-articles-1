RAVELIN

Cross-references: FORTIFICATION

RAVELIN, in fortification, was anciently a flat

bastion placed in the middle of a curtain; but now a detached work composed only of two faces, which make a salient angle without any flanks, and raised before the counter-carp of the place. See FORTIFICATION.
