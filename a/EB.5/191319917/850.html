THE SAINTS

sub_entry · 842 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THE SAINTS, three leagues distant from Guadalupe, are two very small islands, which, with another yet smaller, form a triangle, and have a tolerable harbour. Thirty Frenchmen were sent thither in 1648, but were soon driven away by an excessive drought, which dried up their only spring before they had time to make any reservoirs.
