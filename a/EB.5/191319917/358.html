REST-HARROW

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REST-HARROW, or САММОС, the Ononis Arvensis. A decoction of this plant has been much recommended to horses labouring under a stoppage of urine. It is the pest of some corn-fields; but in its younger state, before the plant has acquired its thorns, it is a most acceptable food to sheep.
