RHUMB

article · 324 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHUMB, in navigation, a vertical circle of any given place, or intersection of such a circle with the horizon; in which last sense rhumb is the same with a point of the compass.

Rhumb-Line is also used for the line which a ship describes when sailing in the same collateral point of the compass, or oblique to the meridians.
