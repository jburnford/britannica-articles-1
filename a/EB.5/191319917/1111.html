SCÆVOLA

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCÆVOLA, in botany: a genus of the monogynia order, belonging to the pentandria class of plants. The corolla is monopetalous; the tube slit longitudinally; the border quinquefid and lateral. The fruit is a plum inferior and monospermous; the nucleus bilocular.
