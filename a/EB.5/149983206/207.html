MERIDIONAL DISTANCES

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MERIDIONAL DISTANCES, in navigation, the same with departure, or easting and westing; being the difference of longitude between the meridian, under which the ship now is, and any other meridian which she was under before.
