MENZINI

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MENZINI (Benedict), a celebrated Italian poet, born at Florence, was professor of eloquence at the college Della Sapienza at Rome, where he died in 1704. He wrote, 1. The art of poetry. 2. Satires, elegies, hymns, and the Lamentations of Jeremiah. 3. Academia Tusculana, a work in verse and prose, which passes for his masterpiece.
