MELAMPODIUM, in botany: A genus of the polygamia necessaria order, belonging to the syngenesia class of plants; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Compositæ. The receptacle is paleaceous and conical; the pappus is monophyllous and valve-like; the calyx pentaphyllous.
