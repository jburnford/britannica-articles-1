MEDIOLANUM Santonum

sub_entry · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEDIOLANUM Santonum (anc. geog.), which afterwards taking the name of the people, was called Santonica Urbs; also Santones and Santoni. A town of Aquitain. Now Saintes, capital of Saintonge in Guienne, on the river Charente.
