MEPHITIS FANUM

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEPHITIS FANUM, a temple erected to the goddess Mephitis, near Lacus Amfanti; who was worshipped also at Cremona. Figuratively, Mephitis denotes a noisome or pestilential exhalation, (Virgil.)
