MENTOR

MENTOR (fab. hist.), a faithful friend of Ulysses; a son of Hercules; a king of Sidonia, who revolted against Artaxerxes Ochus, and afterwards was restored to favour by his treachery to his allies, &c. Diod. 16. An excellent artist in polishing cups and engraving flowers on them. Plin. 33. c. 11.—Mart. 9. ep. 6c. v. 16.
