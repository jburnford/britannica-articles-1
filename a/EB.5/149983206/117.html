MELTON-MOUBRAY

article · 414 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELTON-MOUBRAY, a town of Leicestershire, 108 miles from London. It is a large built place, in a fertile soil; with a market on Tuesday, the most considerable for cattle of any in this part of the island. It is almost encompassed with a little river called the Eye, over which it has two fine bridges; and has a large handsome church, with a free-school. Here are frequent horse-races, and three fairs in the year.
