METRONOMII

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

METRONOMII, the name given by the Athenians to five officers in the city and ten in the piraeus, whose duty it was to inspect all sorts of measures except those of corn. The piraeus was the greatest mart in Attica.
