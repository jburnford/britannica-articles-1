METRODORUS

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

METRODORUS, a Greek physician, born at Chios, was the disciple of Democritus the philosopher, and the master of Hippocrates the physician and Anaxarchus the philosopher. He maintained that the universe is infinite and eternal: but his works are lost. He lived about 444 B. C.
