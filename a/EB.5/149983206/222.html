MEROVINGIAN CHARACTER

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEROVINGIAN CHARACTER, derives its name from Merovée, the first king of France of that race, which reigned 333 years, from Pharamond to Charles Martel. This race is said by some to have terminated in Childeric III. A. D. 751. There are many MSS. in the French libraries still extant in this character.
