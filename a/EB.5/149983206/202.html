MERIDA, a town of North America, in New Spain, and capital of the province of Yutacan, where the bishop and the governor of the province reside. It is inhabited by Spaniards and native Americans; is 30 miles south of the sea, and 120 N. E. of Campeachy. W. Lon. 89. 25. N. Lat. 20. 15.
