MEDINA-del-Campo

MEDINA-del-Campo, a large rich, and ancient town of Spain, in the kingdom of Leon. The great square is very fine, and adorned with a superb fountain. It

is a trading place, enjoys great privileges, and is seated in a country abounding with corn and wine. W. Long. 4. 20. Nat. Lat. 41. 22.
