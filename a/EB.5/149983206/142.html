MENE

Cross-references: BELSHAZZAR

MENE, a Chaldean word, which signifies "he has numbered or counted;" being one of the three words that was written upon the wall by the hand that appeared to Belsazzar, the last king of Babylon, the night that he was put to death. See BELSHAZZAR.
