MELPOMENE

article · 382 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELPOMENE (fab. hist.), one of the muses, daughter of Jupiter and Mnemosyne. She presided over tragedy. Horace has addressed the finest of his odes to her, as to the patroness of lyric poetry. She was generally represented as a young woman with a serious countenance. Her garments were splendid; she wore a buskin, and held a dagger in one hand and in the other a sceptre and crown.
